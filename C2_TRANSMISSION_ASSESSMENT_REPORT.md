## ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

**Transmissive Potency Grading:**

**A+ (Excellent Transmission)**
- Direct pointing-out instructions (ngo sprod) present
- Language induces immediate recognition (rigpa)
- Snying po (heart essence) unmistakably conveyed
- Use of "you," "this," "here" - urgent pointing
- Imperative recognition commands: "Look!" "Recognize!" "See!"

**A (Good Transmission)**
- Clear Dzogchen view with some pointing elements
- Doctrinally precise, moderate recognition capacity
- May require commentary but has transmission DNA
- Embedded recognition moments in technical text

**B+ (Adequate Transmission)**
- Doctrinally sound, primarily informational
- Minimal recognition-inducing capacity
- More descriptive than pointing
- Prepares vessel but doesn't pour essence

**B (Pedagogical Scaffolding)**
- Academic/technical presentation
- No direct pointing
- Background material requiring oral commentary
- Would not induce recognition without teacher

**C or below (Weak Transmission)**
- Purely descriptive or procedural
- No recognition capacity
- Reference material only
