## The Treasury of Direct Seeing  
*Blta ba'i mdzod*  

Root text: *Kun mkhyen klong chen rab 'byams pa'i gsung rab mdzod bdun la blta bar bskul ba*  
Author: Longchen Rabjampa (Klong chen rab 'byams pa dri med 'od zer)  
Translator: litepresence  
Copyleft: May this merit benefit all beings.  
Publication Status: Final draft per Nyingma editorial conventions  
Tibetan Source: BDK UT22920_005_0004 (vol. 5, pod 5 "pa", text 4)  

HOMAGE  
༄༅།  
ན་མཿསརྦ་ཛྙཱ་ཡ།  
na maḥ sarva jñāya  

Homage to all-encompassing wisdom  

WARNING: RESTRICTED TEXT  
Publisher's Note: This text functions as mirror rather than map. Without receiving lung from a qualified lineage holder, these words remain conceptual objects—the very obscuration Paltrül cuts. Receive this mirror only when the face appears before it.  

CONTENTS  
PART ONE: HOMAGE  
Stanzas 1–10  
Prostration lands where the bowing one already rests. Field unbroken from the beginning.  

PART TWO: DHARMA-BODY'S DISPLAY  
Stanzas 11–27  
Scripture as actual dharma-body. Abandon and adopt collapse in single expanse. Liberation through seeing without interval.  

PART THREE: EFFORTLESS ABIDING  
Stanzas 28–40  
Relaxed equal abiding. Mind's thread cuts itself when seen. Devotion blazes realization-awareness.  

PART FOUR: DEVOTION AND LINEAGE  
Stanzas 41–54  
Single bridge of hundred pith instructions. Ancestors placed the path exactly where recognition dawns.  

COLOPHON  
Paltrül Rinpoche's exhortation. Mirror rather than map.
