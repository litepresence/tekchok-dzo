[9404] <ornament> First:
[9405] <prose> The practice chamber, its direction determined, together with sunbeams entering, pure crystal without stain, prepared as the foundation.
[9406] <tantra> From the Tantra of Clear Introduction:
[9407] <prose> The practice chamber's direction should be determined;
[9408] <prose> Having apertures admitting sunbeams,
[9409] <prose> By extending the mirror that clarifies,
[9410] <prose> Light's appearance is seen emerging outward.
[9411] <prose> Thus it is spoken.
[9412] <prose> Through this, by Vajrasattva's blessing of mirror-introduction shown in former times, the blessing of the Teacher.
