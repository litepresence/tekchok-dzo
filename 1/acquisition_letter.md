**About the Text**

Longchen Rabjam (1308–1364) composed *The Treasury of the Supreme Vehicle* as his mature masterpiece—a comprehensive 25-chapter autocommentary that synthesizes the Seventeen Tantras of the Innermost Essence (Nyingthig). This work stands as one of the core scholarly expositions of the Great Perfection (Dzogchen), covering:

- Cosmology and subtle-body science
- The nine vehicles (yānas) framework
- Direct pointing to the nature of mind (rigpa)
- Complete path from beginning to full realization

At approximately 900 pages of Tibetan text, it is one of the most extensive and technically sophisticated works in the Nyingma tradition. Until now, no complete English translation has been available. This edition fills that longstanding gap.
