1. mtshan sum cu rtsa gnyis grub pa ni dbang sum cu rtsa gnyis las byung ba'o/
2. /de nas shes rab kyi mthong ba rnam par dag pa'i mngon par shes pa drug 'char te/
3. sbyangs pa'i shes rab kyi mthong ba rnam par dag pa'i ye shes drug shar bas tshig thams cad kyi brda don khong du chud pa'o/
4. /bslab pa'i shes rab kyi mthong ba dag pa'i mngon shes drug gis sems nyid chos nyid la 'jug pa khyad par can skye ba'o/
5. /mthong ba rnam par dag zhes kun la sbyar te/
6. thos pa'i shes rab kyi mngon shes drug gis sgra dang don thams cad shes so/
7. /bsam byung gi shes rab kyi mngon shes drug gis chos thams cad kyi 'du mched rtogs so/
8. /sgom byung gi shes bar kyi mngon shes drug gis chos dang sems gnyis 'dres te nam mkha' ltar shes so/
9. /de ltar sum cu ni dbang gi 'gyur byed sum cu rtsa gnyis kyis sangs rgyas kyis sbyangs pa'i yon tan sum cu rtsa gnyis mthong ba las byung ba'o/
10. /de nas ye shes kyi mthong ba rnam par dag pa'i mngon par shes pa drug 'char te/
11. 'di'i dus na ye shes kyi mig rdul med cing dri ma dang bral ba rnyed pa'o/
12. /de yang me long lta bu'i ye shes kyi mthong ba dag pa'i mngon shes drug gis chos kyi mtshan nyid dang dgongs pa mngon sum du shes so/
13. /mnyam pa nyid kyi drug gis 'khor 'das gnyis su med par shes so/
14. /so sor rtog pa'i drug gis 'gro ba drug gi dbang po la sogs pa shes so/
15. /bya ba grub pa'i drug gis chos thams cad rtsol ba dang bral ba dang sangs rgyas kyi zhing khams thams cad shes so/
16. /chos kyi dbyings kyi drug gis sangs rgyas kyi yon tan thams cad shes so/
17. /mi gnas pa'i ye shes kyi drug gis chos gang la'ang mi gnas pa shes so/
18. /de yang dbye gzhi re phri bas mngon shes lnga tshan drug tu 'gyur te/
19. ye shes kyi mngon par shes pa sum cu ni dbang sum cu rtsa gnyis las byung ba'o/
20. /de rnam par grol ba'i mngon shes drug 'char te/
