1. sde tshan dang po rgyud kyi snying po gsum ni rig pa rang shar/
2. rang grol yi ge med pa gsum ste/
3. 'di dag gsum shes na rgyal po dmangs la dbang bsgyur ba ltar/
4. rgyud spyi'i don la dbang bsgyur ba'o/
5. /de yang yi ge med pas rgyud spyi'i man ngag ston/
6. de la blon pos kha lo bsgyur ba dang 'dra ba/
7. rang shar gyis lta sgom spyod pa'i gzhi 'dzin/
8. 'bangs kyis g.yog dang dpya la sogs byed pa ltar/
9. rang grol gyis de nyid kyi don 'grol ba'o/
10. /de gsum 'dzom pas rgyal khams la mnga' bsgyur pa ltar/
11. rgyud sde kun la dbang bsgyur ba'o/
12. /gnyis pa rgyud kyi dngas ma gsum ni/
13. kun tu bzang po thugs kyi me long*/
14. rdo rje sems dpa' snying gi me long*/
15. nor bu 'phra bkod gsum ste/
16. 'di gsum shes na ri gsum gyi rtser phyin pas/
17. dma' ba'i lung pa rnams mthong ba ltar rgyud sde kun gyi khong don shes pa'i dgos pa yod do/
18. /gsum pa rgyud kyi me tog gsum ni/
19. mu tig phreng ba/
20. bkra shis mdzes ldan/
21. seng ge rtsal rdzogs te/
22. 'di gsum shes na nam mkha' la nyi ma gsum shar bas 'jig rten gyi mun pa sangs pa ltar/
23. rgyud kyi don dang tshig la rmongs pa med par rtogs pa'i dgos pa yod do/
24. /bzhi pa rgyud kun gyi sdom ni/
25. rdzogs pa rang byung ste/
26. mkhar gyi rmang 'gram legs par tshud na steng du thog rim pa mang po brtsigs btub pa ltar/
27. dbang dang ldan pas thar pa'i gzhi things par shes pa'i dgos pa yod do/
28. /lnga pa rgyud kyi snang ba gting rdzogs ni/
29. ngo sprod spras pa ste mkhar rtse mor 'byangs nas dmag gis mi tshugs pa ltar 'khor ba'i gnas la mi 'jigs pa'i dgos pa yod do/
30. /drug pa ye shes g.yul ldog gi rgyud ni/
31. nyi zla kha sbyor te/
32. bar dor 'khrul pa ldog pa'i dgos pa yod do/
33. /bdun pa yan lag gi rgyud gnyis ni/
34. rin po che spungs pa dang*/
35. sku gdung 'bar ba ste/
