1. 'khor mkha' 'gro ma 'bum phrag gnyis la/
2. chos phun sum tshogs pa sku gsung thugs yon tan 'phrin las kyi rgyud lnga/
3. tshe lo bye ba gcig thub pa'i dus su gsungs so/
4. /gsum pa ni/
5. gnas phun sum tshogs pa drod gsher 'dus pa 'od kyis spungs par/
6. ston pa phun sum tshogs pa 'jigs pa skyob pa'i yid ces bya ba/
7. 'khor byang chub sems dpa' 'bum phrag drug la/
8. chos phun sum tshogs pa 'khor ba dong sprugs/
9. rma bya mjing bsnol/
10. /'byung bzhi zad pa'i rgyud rnams tshe lo 'bum pa'i dus su gsungs so/
11. /bzhi pa ni/
12. gnas phun sum tshogs pa chags 'byung mngal du snang bar/
13. ston pa gzhon nu rol pa rnam par brtse bas/
14. 'khor gnod sbyin dang brag srin stong phrag gcig la/
15. chos sems sde rtsa ba'i rgyud lnga/
16. yan lag gi rgyud drug ste bcu gcig tshe lo brgyad khri pa'i dus su gsungs so/
17. /lnga pa ni/
18. gnas phun sum tshogs pa 'tsho byed gzhon nu'i ldum rar/
19. ston pa drug pa rdo rje 'chang gis/
20. 'khor sangs rgyas rabs bdun du snang ba rang bkod nas/
21. chos pha rol tu phyin pa drug
22. tshe lo bdun khri pa'i dus su gsungs so/
23. /drug pa ni/
24. gnas phun sum tshogs pa ri rab kyi byang phyogs dur khrod mi re 'bar bar/
25. ston pa phun sum tshogs pa gzhon nu dpa' bo stobs ldan chen pos/
26. 'khor phun sum tshogs pa byang chub sems dpa' sprin gyi shugs can bdun dang*/
27. mkha' 'gro ma dang*/
28. lha dang klu la sogs pa dpag tu med pa la/
29. chos phun sum tshogs pa rig pa rang shar chen po'i rgyud la sogs pa mngon sum 'od gsal gyi rgyud rnams dang*/
30. gzhan yang dpag tu med pa rnams tshe lo drug khri pa'i dus na gsungs so/
31. /bdun pa ni/
32. gnas phun sum tshogs pa srin po'i yul ru lu'i sgra dang ldan pa'i phug tu/
33. ston pa drang srong khros pa'i rgyal pos/
34. 'khor srin po bye ba gcig la/
35. chos rags pa 'dul ba'i rgyud bcu la sogs pa/
