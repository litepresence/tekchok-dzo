12. gzugs can skyed cing gnas pa dang*/
13. 'jig pa'i rgyu rkyen dang rten dang ngo bo byed pa yin no/
14. /de yang sa'i mtshan nyid nyid sra zhing 'thas pa/
15. chu'i mtshan nyid brlan zhing gsher ba/
16. me'i mtshan nyid tsha zhing bsreg pa/
17. rlung gi mtshan nyid g.yo zhing 'degs pa/
18. nam mkha'i mtshan nyid yangs shing go 'byed pa'o/
19. /de dag gis snod bcud skyed cing 'dzin pa ste/
20. de nyid las phyi yul gyis bsdus pa'i khams ni lnga ste/
21. sa'i khams kyis lus kyi gzhi skyed pas na khams so/

25. /nam mkha'i khams kyis dbang po rnams 'dzin par byed pas na khams so zhes so/
26. /nang sems kyis bsdus pa'i khams ni snod dang bcud du snang ba gnyis las/
27. snod ni las kyis bsags pas yul ltar snang ba ste/
28. dbye na/
29. phyi yul snang sems kyi 'khrul pa las byung ba dang*/
30. nang sems snang rtsal las rang bzhin du shar ba gnyis so/
31. /dang po ni yul snang sems kyi bag chags te rmi lam bzhin no/
32. /dbye na/
33. 'dod pa'i yul gling bzhi la sogs pa dang*/
34. gzugs kyi yul 'od lnga rang gsal 'ja' tshon lta bu dang*/

36. nyi zla kha sbyor las/
37. nang sems kyis bsdus pa'i khams la gnyis te/
38. sems can sdud par byed pa'i snod kyi khams dang*/
