2. rlung bzung ba la ma ltos pa dang*/
3. nyon mongs dang rtog pa rang 'gags pa dang*/
4. dngas la rang gnas kyi bsam gtan gyis brgyan la/
5. rtsa rlung gi rtags bcu rnam dag gi rlung gi rtsol ba'i rjes su byed pas 'phel 'grib mang zhing mi brtan pa dang*/
6. mi dngas pa dang*/
7. cha'i 'grib pa dang*/
8. rtog pa dang nyon mongs rags pa rjes la gsal por skye ba dang*/
9. gnas cha'i brlan chung ba la sogs pa ste/
10. khyad shin tu che'o/
11. /'di dag lam 'bras dang*/
12. sbyor drug dang*/
13. gsang 'dus kyi phra tig la sogs pa bsgom dus 'char yang*/
14. snying tig gi mngon sum dang gnyis gser dang ra gan gyi khyad bzhin yod do/
15. /bcu gnyis pa grol sa'i dbyings ka dag dang*/
16. nyams su len dus kyi phyi nang gi dbyings ka dag gnyis rig pa rgya chad med pa dang*/
17. yul snang dag tsam du 'dra yang*/
18. grol sa rlung sems dag pas 'khor 'das ka dag tu gnas la/
19. da lta rlung sems ma dag pas rkyen dbang res 'ga' ba dang/
20. mi gsal la dri ma dang bcas pa'i khyad yod do/
21. /bcu gsum pa rig pa nang gsal rjen la bud dus sna tshogs snang ba dang rig pa mi 'gag par 'char ba dang*/
22. sems byung sna tshogs rtog tshogs su 'phro ba gnyis snang sems 'char bar 'dra 'dra la/
23. rig pa rkyen dbang du mi 'gro/
24. /ngang dngas dang mi 'bral/
25. thal byung du shar ba dang grol ba dus mnyam ris med du gnas la/
26. byung tshor rtog pa yul la yengs nas tha mal rang ka'i khyad yod do/
27. /bcu bzhi pa shes rab rtsal du rdzogs pa dang*/
28. rnam rtog yul la 'phro ba gnyis phyir 'char ba la 'dra yang*/
29. shes rab zang nge thal le thad kar zhig nas phar tshur 'khor rgyu med la/
30. rtog pa phyir yul 'dzin/
31. tshur log nang du gcig phyir gcig mthud nas gzung 'dzin rang ka mar song ba'i khyad yod do/
32. /bco lnga pa sngags kyi rtse mo 'dir thad drang du bstan pa'i don dam ma skyes pa la sogs pa dang*/
