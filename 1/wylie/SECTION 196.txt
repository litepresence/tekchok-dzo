1. bla ma grub pa thob pa rnams kyis sil bu dum bu gang rung re res kyang slob ma la dbang rdzogs pa 'thob pas/
2. ji skad gsungs pa des 'grub la/
3. grub pa ma thob pa rnams kyis de thams cad dril nas bskur bas don 'grub pa'i phyir/
4. /de'i rnam par gzhag pa la dbang bzhi las/
5. dang po rdul tshon gyi dkyil 'khor du lha dang*/
6. bum pa dang*/
7. slob ma sta gon du bya ba rdzogs nas/
8. bum pa'i byed pa sngon du 'gro bas bkra shis rdzas brgyad/
9. rgyal srid sna bdun/
10. zhi ba dang khro bo'i sku tsa ka li'am 'bur ram lugs mas dbang bskur zhing rdo rje byin la ming gi dbang bskur te/
11. bka' rtags phyag rgya'i dbang po tis bskur nas/
12. dpal gyi gtor ma'i dbang gis 'phrin las rdzogs par byed pa yan chad bum dbang zhes bya'o/
13. /de nas byang sems gnyis sbyin pa ni gsang dbang ngo*/
14. /de nas chags bral la phyag rgya'i dmigs pa dang*/
15. phyag rgya 'tshal ba tsam gyis bskur la/
16. chags can las kyi mu dra dngos la bskur ba gnyis ni/
17. shes rab ye shes so/
18. /de yan chad ma rtogs rgyu mtshon gyi dbang zhes bya'o/
19. /de nas bzhi pa tshig dbang ni/
20. /rtogs pa ye shes kyi dbang ste/
21. phyi lta ba/
22. nang sgom pa/
23. gsang ba rig pa gsum gyis bskur ba'o/
24. /rig pa la'ang ngo sprod kyis rang ngo sprad pa/
25. /mngon sum gyis don la lta ba/
26. shar grol gyis gnad la bsger ba dang gsum du phye nas ngo 'phrod ngo shes par sprad pa ni rig pa rtsal gyi dbang zhes bya'o/
27. /de ltar dbang bzhi nges pa ni/
28. spros bcas 'bring gi rnam par gzhag pa'o/
29. /spros bcas bsdus pa ni/
30. don spros zhes bya ste/
31. dang po rig pa'i ye shes lnga mtshon pa rdul tshon gyi dkyil 'khor du bdag dang slob ma zhugs nas/
32. bum pa dang*/
33. gsang ba dang*/
34. shes rab ye shes dang*/
35. tshig dbang rdzogs pa chen po brda bzhis bskur nas dga' ston gyi bar du byed pa'o/
36. /'di ni bi ma las lce btsun la dngos su bskur ba'i rim pa ste/
