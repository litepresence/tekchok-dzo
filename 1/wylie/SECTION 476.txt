1. yi dam lha'i sgyu lus gnyis lha sku snang tsam du 'dra yang*/
2. snang ba rgya che chung dang rang snang du shes dus grol mi grol gyi khyad yod de/
3. chos nyid bar do rig gdangs snang la/
4. lha'i sgyu lus rtog pa goms rjes su byas pa'i phyir ro/
5. /drug pa rang bzhin sprul sku'i zhing snang dang*/
6. mas bgrod sangs rgyas nas dag pa'i zhing du mdzad pa ston pa gnyis/
7. longs sku'i snang cha dag pa'i zhing bcas su 'dra 'dra 'dra yang*/
8. /rang bzhin sprul pa rang snang lhun grub kyi sgo las shar ba yin la/
9. mas bgrod kyi zhing 'khrul snang reng bu ba mthun par snang ba'i khyad yod de/
10. don la rang snang dang gzhan snang gi khyad yod do/
11. /bdun pa mthar thug gi grol sa ka dag dang*/
12. nges med gzhi'i lhun grub gnyis rig pa'i yon tan ngo bo rang bzhin thugs rje la sogs pa 'dra yang*/
13. dri ma dag ma dag dang 'khrul gzhi byed mi byed kyi khyad yod do/
14. /rang gi sde bas khyad 'di shes pa gal che bas/
15. 'bras bu gzhi thog tu grol zer nas rtsing por ma smra cig
16. /brgyad pa 'og ma'i yid dpyod kyi lta sgom dang*/
17. rig pa mngon sum gyi lta sgom gnyis ming du 'dra yang*/
18. dbang po dang rig pa la snang mi snang gi khyad che'o/
19. /dgu pa rig pa'i sku dang*/
20. rtog pa'i lha sku gnyis lha mthong tsam du 'dra yang*/
21. rgya che chung dang rtag mi rtag dang rtog pa goms rjes su byed dgos mi dgos kyi khyad yod do/
22. /bcu pa rig pa'i snang yul rang shar dang*/
23. 'khrul snang sna tshogs su shar ba gnyis ma dag pa'i dngos po 'di snang tsam du 'dra yang*/
24. /rnal 'byor pa la chu zla dang rmi lam ltar ngang gis bden med du shar bas mi 'ching la/
25. phal pa rnams 'ching ba'i khyad yod do/
26. /bcu gcig pa 'od gsal ye gdangs kyi thig le dang*/
27. rlung sems bzung ba'i 'ja' 'od kyi thig le gnyis stong gzugs shar tsam du 'dra yang*/
28. rang bzhin rnam dag gi thig le dbyings rig gi snang ba yin pas dngas pa dang*/
