1. 'di'i bstan bcos rnams kyang bka' dang khyad par med pa yin no/
2. /don bzhi pa bya ba rgyud kyi theg pa gtan la dbab pa la gnyis te/
3. rgyu'i theg pa las khyad par du 'phags tshul bstan pa dang*/
4. khyad par can gyi theg pa nyid bshad pa'o/
5. /dang po ni mtshan nyid kyi theg pa rgyu la 'bad pa cher byas nas tshegs chen pos bsgrub dgos pa las/
6. sngags ni rgyu la ltos nas 'bras bu smin zin pas 'bad rtsol chung ngus sangs rgyas thob pas 'phags pa dang*/
7. rgyu nas lam lnga sa bcu dka' ba chen pos bskal pa grangs med mang po na sangs rgya la/
8. sngags ni rab tshe gcig
9. 'bring bar do/
10. tha mas kyang skye ba brgyud nas sangs rgyas bas dus myur bas kyang 'phags pa dang*/
11. rgyus dang po bden pa mthong ba la'ang tshogs dpag med sogs dgos la/
12. sngags kyi dkyil 'khor tshogs mchod la sogs pa phra mos 'grub pas kyang 'phags pa dang*/
13. rgyus lam thad drang du stong par mi byed de/
14. drang don dang nges don/
15. dgongs pa dang ldem dgongs su bstan nas  rnam par 'jog la/
16. sngags ni lam cig car thang drang du bstan pas nye zho dang bar chad med pa'i phyir 'phags pa yin no/
17. /mdor bsdu na/
18. rgyu'i theg pa bas sngags kyi theg pa 'dod pa'i don bsgrub pa la/
19. thabs mang ba dang*/
20. dka' ba med pa dang*/
21. ma rmongs pa dang*/
22. dbang po rnon pos myur du 'grub pa'i tshul gyis 'phags te/
23. tshul gsum pa'i sgron me las/
24. don gcig na yang ma rmongs dang*/
25. /thabs mang dka' ba med pa dang*/
26. /dbang po rnon po'i dbang byas nas/
27. /sngags kyi theg pa khyad par 'phags/
28. /zhes so/
29. /gnyis pa la lnga las/
30. ngo bo ni rang byung gi ye shes dbyings 'od gsal gyi bden pa thad drang du rtogs pa'i thabs mchog tu gsang ba/
31. bskyed rdzogs lhun grub kyi dbyings la 'jal ba'i rnam grangs so/
32. /nges tshig ni nyon mongs pa las skyob pa dang byang chub myur du 'grub pa la mchog tu bsngags pas na sngags so/
33. /dgos pa ni dbang po rnon po'i rnon po myur du grol bar byed pa'o/
34. /'bras bu ni tshe gcig gam bar do'am rgyud nas mgon po rdo rje 'chang gis mchog 'grub pa'o/
35. /dbye na gnyis te/
