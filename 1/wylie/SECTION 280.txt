1. de ltar rang rang la snang ba 'di yin na'ang*/
2. thun mong mthun pa'i 'jig rten gyi cha ni spyi mthun gyi las dang*/
3. ston pa'i mdzad pas bkod la/
4. rang la snang rung 'khrul par snang ba'i cha ni rang snang 'khor ba'i sgos bkod de/
5. rang snang gi cha ni spubs 'pho dus rang log la 'gro la/
6. thun mong pa ni gzhan ngor da dung shol na yod pa'o/
7. /'di gnyis phyed pa shin tu gal che'o/
8. /rang bzhin du rig pa la rang 'khrungs lhun grub kyi sgo las 'gro drug gi snang ba yod pas/
9. 'khrul snang rmi lam 'dra ba ka dag gi sa la snang med du rang log la gtong dgos pa'i gnad de yin la/
10. bar dor yang rang ngo'i 'gro drug tu rang snang gi sprul pas don byed pa dang*/
11. mi byed pa cha gnyis su bshad pa ni/
12. ma dag pa 'khor ba'i sgo ma shar ba'i gong du rang snang rang ngo shes na don mi byed de/
13. bya ba'i yul mi snang ba'i phyir ro
14. /shar nas don byed dgos te/
15. 'khrul snang ma stongs na grol bar mi nus pa'i phyir ro/
16. /gnad 'di yang phra la gal po che cig yin no/
17. /gnyis pa skyon yon gyi 'thad pa bye brag tu bshad pa la gsum ste/
18. yon tan gyi cha brgyad spyir bstan pa/
19. rkyen gyis skyon yon gyi char dbye ba/
20. 'char nub snang tshul gyi 'thad pa dgod pa'o/
21. /dang po ni/
22. thog ma'i gzhi la lhun gyis ma grub na/
23. gzhi snang 'char ba'i don ma mchis la/
24. gzhi snang lhun grub kyi 'char tshul med na/
25. 'khor 'das 'byung ba'i don med pas/
26. gzhi dang gzhi snang la nang dbyings phra ba dang*/
27. phyi dbyings rags par 'char sgo bstan pa'o/
28. /de yang sems nyid 'od gsal gyi dbyings/
29. lhun  grub kyi rang gdangs la thugs rje 'byung byed du med na/
30. 'khor 'das gnyis sde chad de gcig gis gcig gdul bya dang 'dul byed du mi 'thad do/
31. /sems can gyi rang snang 'khrul ba/
32. sangs  rgyas kyi rang ngo rnam dag yin pas/
33. snang ba mi gcig pa'i phyir ro/
34. /snang ba mi gcig kyang sems can gyi don 'byung ba ni gzhi la thugs rje yod pa gzhi snang nas shar te/
