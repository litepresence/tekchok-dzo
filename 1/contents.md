# *Theg mchog rin po che'i mdzod* (Treasury of the Supreme Vehicle), 

This autocommentary represents Longchenpa's mature synthesis of Nyingma cosmology, subtle-body science, and Dzogchen view—written by the master himself to elucidate his own root verses for disciples of varying capacity.
