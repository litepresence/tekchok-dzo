## STRUCTURE

**File naming:** VV-CC-SS-SS.txt
- **VV** = Volume (01 or 02)
- **CC** = Chapter (01-25)
- **SS** = Section
- **SS** = Subsection

**Example:** `01-01-01-01.txt` = Volume 1, Chapter 1, Section 1, Subsection 1

**Format:**
- Clean Unicode Tibetan
- Line numbers: `[1]`, `[2]`, `[3]`... continuous across entire text
- No PAGE markers
- No blank lines
- No BDRC metadata
