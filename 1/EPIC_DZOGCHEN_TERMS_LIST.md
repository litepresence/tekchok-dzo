## TERMS MISSING FROM GLOSSARY ⚠️

### HIGH FREQUENCY (Need immediate addition)

**1. Channels, Winds, and Bindus (Subtle Body)**
- **rtsa** (channel) - 100 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Subtle energy channels (nāḍī), not anatomical
  - *Context:* Chapter 11 on subtle body
  - *English:* channel, nāḍī
  
- **rlung** (wind/prāṇa) - 58 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Vital energy winds, not breath
  - *Context:* Five winds (life, fire, pervasive, upward, downward)
  - *English:* wind, prāṇa, vāyu
  
- **thig le** (bindu/drop) - 6 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Energy drops, essences, seminal points
  - *Context:* White/red bindus, four lamps
  - *English:* bindu, drop, essence

**2. Appearance/Display**
- **snang ba** (appearance) - 68 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Phenomenal appearance, display, manifestation
  - *Context:* snang stong (appearance-emptiness), rang snang (self-appearance)
  - *English:* appearance, display, manifestation
  - *Note:* Distinct from 'perception' - refers to phenomena not the act of perceiving

**3. Empowerment/Initiation**
- **dbang** (empowerment) - 38 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Tantric initiation, authorization, power
  - *Context:* Four empowerments (vase, secret, wisdom, word)
  - *English:* empowerment, initiation, abhiṣeka

**4. Bardo (Intermediate State)**
- **bar do** (bardo) - 30 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Intermediate state between death and rebirth
  - *Context:* Three bardos (clear light, dharmatā, becoming)
  - *English:* bardo, intermediate state
  - *Note:* Also moment-to-moment bardo (this life)

**5. Liberation/Freedom**
- **grol ba** (liberation) - 16 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Freedom, release, liberation
  - *Context:* Self-liberation (rang grol), liberation upon arising (char grol)
  - *English:* liberation, freedom, release
  - *Note:* Not something achieved but recognized

**6. Method/Means**
- **thabs** (method/means) - 11 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Skillful means, method, upāya
  - *Context:* Thabs shes (method and wisdom), thabs kyi theg pa (method vehicle)
  - *English:* method, means, skillful means, upāya
  - *Note:* Paired with wisdom (shes rab)

**7. Purity/Purification**
- **dag pa** (pure/purity) - 16 occurrences in literal
  - *Meaning:* Pure, cleansed, purified
  - *Context:* Primordial purity (ka dag), twofold purity (dag pa gnyis)
  - *English:* pure, purity, cleansed
  
- **ma dag** (impure) - 2 occurrences
  - *Meaning:* Impure, unpurified, defiled
  - *Context:* Impure perception vs pure perception
  - *English:* impure, unpurified
