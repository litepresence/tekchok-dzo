[1296-1312] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[HUMAN ORIGINATION FROM LIGHT BEINGS]
<Lines 1296-1298: "first Jambu continent merit excellent ground this to light clear from god son two lotus to miraculous birth expand explain, that also four great king up-to beings form that below to light clear from god son two Jambu continent this to fall is sun greatly illuminate and moon stainless"—first, Jambudvīpa (*'dzam bu'i gling*, Rose-Apple Continent), merit-excellent ground. From light clear (*'od gsal*) god sons two, lotus (*padma*) miraculous birth (*rdzus skyes*). This is first human origin: Sun Greatly Illuminating (*nyi ma rab tu snang byed*) and Moon Stainless (*zla ba dri med*) fall from clear light. They are *'od kyi skye gnas* (light beings) descending to material realm. Lines 1299-1307: "then appearance all greatly beautiful light lotus measure without to, those two mind greatly move from, space seed part manifest become condition to arise by-means-of, miraculous birth god son some expand, those also each-other see when joy and light appearance greatly clear miracle by-means-of activity is, beings those go when also sky to go, sit when also sky to sit, food as meditation what joy bliss to dwell, body to light rays group measure without extend and go to wish only by-means-of where think that to go"—appearance all greatly beautiful, light lotus measureless. Those two minds greatly moved; space seed (*dbyings kyi sa bon*) part manifest as condition; miraculous birth god sons expand. Seeing each other: joy, light appearance, clear miracle activity. Going: sky-going; sitting: sky-sitting; food: meditation; dwelling in joy/bliss. Body light rays measureless; extending where thought goes—*'dod 'jo* (wish-fulfillment). Lines 1308-1312: "life year also measure without last, afflictions manifest move what also not exist, tantra some from joy possess from expand explain, some from four great king from expand explain, intention is first chief those two light clear from fall is"—life measureless years; no afflictions (*nyon mongs*) manifest. Some tantras say from Joy Possessors (*dga' ldan*) expanded; some say from Four Great Kings. Intention: first, those two chiefs fell from clear light. This is *rigs pas skyes pa* (caste-born) origin myth—first humans divine descent, establishing human potential for enlightenment.>
---
[RISK: CREATIONISM & CASTEISM]
<The "fall from light" narrative presents two risks:
**1. Creationism**: Sun and Moon as first humans can be read as literal cosmic origin story. Actual meaning: *'od gsal* (clear light) = mind's primordial nature; "fall" = condensation into material embodiment. Not historical event but phenomenological description of consciousness individuating.
**2. Casteism**: Divine origin establishing human superiority over animals/pretas can feed hierarchical social structures. Buddhist context: All beings have Buddha-nature; human birth is precious (*dal 'byor*) because of recognition capacity, not inherent superiority.
**Miraculous birth** (*rdzus skyes*): Not virgin birth mythology but asexual reproduction in early cosmic ages—before sexual differentiation emerged (detailed pages 1370+). This encodes psychological development: from unitary awareness (light beings) → dualistic perception (male/female) → sexual reproduction → material embodiment.
Dzogchen view: The "fall" is not biblical expulsion from Eden but *ma rig pa* (non-recognition) of own nature. Sun/Moon are metaphors for *rig pa* (awareness) and *ye shes* (primordial wisdom). Jambudvīpa is not geography but *snying po can gyi 'jig rten*—world with heart-essence, capable of recognition.>

[1370-1377] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[DEGRADATION OF GOLDEN AGE]
<Buddhist cosmological narrative of human devolution from radiance to materiality. Lines 1370-1371: "one to one look and touch and laugh desire arose and beings also womb from born from many increased"—sexual differentiation emergence: beings look, touch, laugh, desire arises. Womb-born (*mngal skyes*) reproduction begins, population increases. "this time at light self-clear merely to food clothes tree on enjoy and desire-satisfying cow and sensible horse also exist"—light still self-clear (*rang gsal*), food/clothes from trees, desire-satisfying cow (*'dod 'jo'i ba*), sensible horse (*cang shes kyi rta*)—mythical creatures of golden age. Lines 1372-1377: Merit decreases (*bsod nams bri*), affliction-anger arises (*nyon mongs zhe sdang byung*), killing action (*gsod pa'i las*) submerges previous qualities. Light dims, form/vitality/youth not beautiful. Progressive degradation: sexual desire → greed → violence → loss of luminosity. This is *'jig rten chags tshul* (world formation) from *Aggañña Sutta* (DN 27), not Dzogchen view.>

[1378-1392] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[ORIGIN OF AGRICULTURE AND CELESTIAL BODIES]
<Lines 1378-1379: "that from long after human some earth essence that when desire merely to not-taken day and month and year storing did all by-means-of that good said imitate from evening from, between there famine and darkness time year many arose"—humans store earth essence (*sa'i snying po*) when unharvested, calling it good. Imitation leads to hoarding. Famine (*mu ge*) and darkness (*mun pa*) kalpas arise. Lines 1380-1386: "that from beings common merit from sky in sun arisen light arisen, here near here far said not-sit by-means-of, sun named near benefit-doing name famous, that from that set and night named, darkness cleared hope from, moon also that with similar arisen from, this also that companion sit said moon famous"—from common merit, sun arises in sky. Called "near" (*nye ba*) because benefit-doing; "that set" (*de nub*) → night. Moon arises similarly, called "companion" (*grogs*). Etymological explanations (*byung tshul*) for sun/moon names. Lines 1387-1392: Moon waxes full with heat; beings wish to cool; heat lost, coolness becomes torment. Saying "not cool" causes waxing/waning. Self-grown grain (*ma rmos rang 'khrungs*)—Sālu rice field: day-seed grows day, evening-seed grows evening. Food enjoyed. This is mythological etiology: human action (wishing) shapes celestial behavior.>

[1393-1420] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[PROGRESSIVE DEVOLUTION TO PRESENT AGE]
<Continuation of degradation narrative (lines 1393-1420 in Tibetan source). The pattern continues: agriculture → property → social classes → conflict → shorter lifespans. Ten-year lifespan minimum (*mi lo bcu pa*) reached when weapon-disease-famine kalpas (*mtshon nad mu ge'i bskal pa*) dominate. The narrative inverts Vedic cosmic optimism (golden age to present) with Buddhist pessimism (luminous origin to degraded present)—but with soteriological twist: present age is *easier* for liberation because suffering motivates practice. Lines 1415-1420 (from adjacent page): "then excessive desire karma produces pretas, excessive delusion matures animals"—psychological etiology of lower realms. Desire (*'dod chags*) → preta (unsatisfied craving); delusion (*gti mug*) → animal (instinctual confusion). Not punishment but natural maturation (*smin pa*) of mental states. Cosmological narrative serves moral pedagogy: understand origin to understand present condition; understand present to motivate liberation.>
---
[RISK: LITERAL HISTORICISM]
<The degradation narrative presents multiple risks:
1. **Historical literalism**: Taking radiance → sexuality → agriculture → war as literal history rather than psychological allegory
2. **Primitivism**: Romanticizing pre-agricultural existence as spiritually superior
3. **Misanthropy**: Viewing present age as irredeemably degraded
4. **Determinism**: Fatalistic acceptance of violence/disease as inevitable
Actual meaning: The narrative encodes *rnam smin* (maturation)—how consciousness condenses from light to matter through increasing fixation. The "luminous beings" (*'od kyi skye gnas*) are mind in subtle state; "womb-born" is coarse embodiment. Not historical decline but pedagogical demonstration: samsara is *snying po med pa* (essenceless), whatever its form.
Dzogchen view: The "original luminosity" is *ye nas yod* (primordially present), not lost in past but unrecognized now. Degradation is *snang tshul* (appearance-mode), not *ngo bo* (essence). Essence remains *ka dag* (primordially pure) throughout all epochs. Recognition transcends historical narrative entirely.>

[1420-1422] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[KARMIC PRODUCTION OF LOWER REALMS]
<Karmic causality establishing three lower realms (*ngan 'gro gsum*). Lines 1420-1422: "instant vicinity and also arose, then desire excess action preta and, delusion excess animals in individually ripened and attached"—Jambudvīpa with near surrounding (*nyi tshe nyi 'khor*) arose. Then excessive desire (*'dod chags lhag pa*) matures as pretas; excessive delusion (*gti mug lhag pa*) matures as animals—each respectively shrinking/attached (*chags*). This is *las rnam smin* (karmic maturation) doctrine: affliction → action → rebirth. The mapping is precise: desire → preta (insatiable hunger); delusion → animal (instinctual confusion). Missing: anger → hell, which follows immediately. The psychological etiology: mental states produce corresponding existence conditions. This is *rgyu 'bras* (cause-result) teaching, not Dzogchen view of primordial liberation.>

[1423-1432] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[HELL REALM TEMPORALITIES]
<Exponential time dilation in hell realms. Lines 1423-1425: Reviving hell (*yang sos*)—50 human years = 1 day of Four Great Kings; 30 days = 1 month; 12 months = 1 year; 500 years = 1 day of Reviving; living 500 own years. This establishes base unit: human-to-divine time ratio. Lines 1426-1432: Escalating geometric progression:
- Thirty-three gods' 1,000 years = 1 day for Kālakāñja (Black Line) hell
- 2,000 years = Saṃghāta (Crushing) day
- 4,000 years = Tuṣita's Raurava (Screaming) day
- 8,000 years = Nirmanarati's Mahāraurava (Great Screaming) day
- 16,000 years = Paranirmitavaśavartin's Pratāpana (Intense Heating) day
- Mahātāpana (Great Intense): 1/2 intermediate eon (*bar bskal*)
- Avīci (Uninterrupted): 1 full intermediate eon
The mathematics encode psychological intensity: deeper hells = longer subjective time = more intense suffering. This is *sems kyi snang ba* (mind's display), not external geography.>

[1433-1446] 
[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[COLD HELLS AND MATHEMATICAL EXHAUSTION]
<Eight cold hells (*grang dmyal brgyad*) named by symptoms (lines 1433-1439):
1. Arbuda (Blisters)
2. Arbudanirbheda (Burst Blisters)
3. Aṭaṭa (Chattering Teeth)
4. Hahava (Lamentation)
5. Huhuva (Teeth Chattering)
6. Utpala-like splitting
7. Padma (Lotus)-like splitting
8. Mahāpadma (Great Lotus)-like splitting
Lines 1440-1443: Lifespan calculation—Kosala's 100-load sesame, 100 human years per seed removal. When sesame exhausted, Arbuda life exhausted; remaining twenty times longer. This produces astronomical numbers encoding *infinite* from finite perspective—kalpa-scale durations. Lines 1444-1446: Preta lifespan: 100 human years = 1 day, 500 own years. Animals: indefinite (*nges pa ma yin*)—great nāgas (*klu*) live intermediate eon. The indefiniteness of animal realm reflects ignorance's amorphous nature—no clear boundaries like desire/delusion.>
---
[RISK: TEMPORAL LITERALISM]
<The elaborate time calculations present serious pedagogical risk. Readers may:
1. Take durations literally ( sesame-counting as temporal measurement)
2. Calculate total years mathematically
3. View hells as physical locations with coordinates
Actual meaning: These encode psychological intensity and karmic weight. "Intermediate eon" (*bar bskal*) is not clock time but *experience of duration*—intense suffering stretches time; bliss contracts it. The Avīci hell's "one eon" is instant from enlightened perspective, eternity for sufferer.
Risk of soteriological despair: "Hell is so long, liberation impossible." Response: Line 1432 says "experiencing long continuous suffering"—*myong* (experiencing), not permanent condemnation. All hells are *chos can* (possessors of dharmatā)—empty nature allows liberation.
Dzogchen view: These realms are *rang snang* (self-display) of *ma rig pa* (non-recognition). No external hell exists; suffering is mind's own display. Recognition transforms hell into pure land instantly. The detailed cosmology is *tha snyad* (conventional) upaya, not *don dam* (ultimate) truth.>
