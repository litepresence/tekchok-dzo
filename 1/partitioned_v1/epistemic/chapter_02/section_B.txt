[RISK ASSESSMENT SUMMARY]
<Pages 1-20 establish foundational *gzhi* (ground), *lam* (path), and *'bras bu* (result) topology of Dzogchen view. Primary risks: (1) Reifying Samantabhadra/Vajradhara as temporal beings who achieved enlightenment; (2) Literalizing pure land descriptions as physical cosmography; (3) Treating twelve emanations as historical biography; (4) Misreading five perfections/three kāyas as ontological categories rather than recognition-descriptors; (5) Confusing emanation doctrine with theistic creation theology. Antidote: All descriptions point to *rig pa* (awareness) recognizing itself—*rang snang* (self-appearance) as *chos nyid* (dharmatā).>
