#### VERSE SECTIONS ANALYSIS

**Lines [12-27] - Homage Verses**
**Meter Pattern:** Free verse with extended lines (15-25 syllables)
**Breath Analysis:**
- [12] "Samantabhadra, whose innate nature holds the five perfections, complete in dominion," 
  - CAESURA after "perfections," - natural breath point
  - Line length: Manageable for single breath with pause
  
- [13] "Together with the regent of the five families, the Great Ocean, the sublime guide, the Teacher,"
  - Multiple caesuras: after "families," "Ocean," "guide,"
  - LINE BREAK ISSUE: Too many appositives without sufficient rhythm
  - CADENCE: Starts strong but loses momentum at end
  
- [14] "The five vidyādharas, hosts of the aural lineage, ornament of the three worlds together with the gods,"
  - BREATH ISSUE: Line 38 words, difficult single breath
  - Multiple list items without variation
  
- [15] "To that crown of all joy, the glorious source of excellence and auspiciousness, I offer homage."
  - EXCELLENT CADENCE: Builds to strong closure
  - "I offer homage" lands with weight

**Lines [16-19] - Second Verse Group**
- [16] "That which from the primordial is pure, where all elaborations cease into space, beautifully arrayed,"
  - DACTYLIC OPENING: "That which from the pri-" creates momentum
  - CAESURA after "pure," then "where all elaborations..."
  
- [17] "Nature ever-abiding, clear light, seven horses, pervading all three bodies and qualities,"
  - LIST FATIGUE: "clear light, seven horses" - too rapid
  - Missing conjunction or transition
  
- [18] "Not dwelling in existence or peace, spontaneously entered, uncompounded and spontaneously accomplished—"
  - STRONG: Parallel structure "spontaneously... spontaneously..."
  - DASH creates pause before final prostration
  
- [19] "To that Vajra-point, supreme possessor of all, the Sugata-essence, I prostrate."
  - EXCELLENT: Triple cadence landing

**Lines [20-27] - Third Verse Group**
- [20-23]: Generally strong with good variation
- [21] "The unsurpassed secret vajra vehicle, supreme among all, glorious as the wish-fulfilling jewel,"
  - Good alliteration: "supreme... glorious"
  - CAESURA well-placed
  
- [23] "Revealing the essence of suchness directly—by that, this day, protect the lotus-throne of my mind."
  - DASH creates dramatic pause
  - "protect the lotus-throne" has strong rhythm
  - "of my mind" slightly weak ending
