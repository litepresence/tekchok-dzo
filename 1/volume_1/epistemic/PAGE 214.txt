- Key Wylie terms (*italicized*): (*phyi nang gsang ba yongs rdzogs* — outer inner secret utterly complete, *spros bcas spros med* — with/without elaboration, *dbang bzhi ngos* — four empowerments certainty, *smim so* — ripening signs)
- Hermeneutical precision: Systematic justification (*'thad pa*) for four empowerments based on necessity of cutting elaborations: outer elaboration not cut → appearance-perception elaborations not severed; inner not gathered → mind not enter path; secret not cut → lamp not illuminate path; utterly secret not pointed out → seeing path not clear. - Temporal warnings: The "ripening" is recognition-maturation, not temporal growth.

- Pedagogical purpose: The systematic justification provides logical framework for practitioners needing rational support. However, ultimate recognition transcends all logic—*rnam par mi rtog* (non-conceptual).
