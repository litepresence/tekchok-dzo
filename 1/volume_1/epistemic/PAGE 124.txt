[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW]
[ORDER SECTIONS 1-4]
[PEDAGOGICAL FRAMEWORK]

<Eight order (*go rim*) sections establishing tantric curriculum: (1) Two root tantras—*Rdzogs pa rang byung* with eight root scriptures ripening beings who like elaboration, and *Yi ge med pa* six chapters with instructions liberating ripened beings; (2) Two explanation tantras—mother-child for those needing progressive elaboration; (3) Four instruction tantras—ripening different capacities: precious heap for faith, blazing lamp for diligence, six spaces for wisdom, sun-moon union for four bardos; (4) Two entry-self-liberation tantras—*Seng ge rtsal rdzogs* for view-formulation and *Nor bu 'phra bkod* for yogin entry. These are curriculum organization, not ontological hierarchies.>
