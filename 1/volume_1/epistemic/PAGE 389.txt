<Emptiness light's appearing basis mirror-like wisdom: "Emptiness light's appearing basis mirror-like wisdom clearly" (*stong nyid 'od gsal kyi 'char gzhi me long lta bu'i ye shes su gsal*)—Five Wisdoms framework in Dzogchen context. Mirror-like wisdom (*me long lta bu'i ye shes*) is dharmakāya's natural expression—reflecting all without grasping. "Direction, class, center, limit not observed, empty, experience itself wisdom abides" (*phyogs dang ris dang dbus dang mtha' mi dmigs par stong pas nyams pa nyid kyi ye shes su gnas*)—wisdom beyond spatial distinctions. This is *chos kyi dbyings kyi ye shes* (dharmadhātu wisdom)—recognizing all phenomena as dharmakāya. Not different wisdoms but one wisdom's facets, like one sun reflecting in many mirrors.>
