[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW]
[PURPOSE SECTIONS ONE-SIX]
[UPĀYA STATEMENT]

<Eleven purpose (*dgos ched*) sections on tantra essence: (1) Three tantra essences—*rig pa rang shar*, *rang grol*, *yi ge med pa* (self-arisen, self-liberated, letterless awareness)—king controlling kingdom analogy; (2) Three tantra clarities (*dangs ma*)—mind-mirrors revealing all tantras' meaning; (3) Three tantra flowers (*me tog*)—three suns dispelling darkness; (4) All tantras' summary—empowerment (*dbang*) for liberation basis; (5) Appearance complete depth (*snang ba gting rdzogs*)—view recognition for fearlessness in samsara; (6) Wisdom battle-return tantra—bar-do delusion reversal. These are pedagogical purposes, not ontological stages.>
