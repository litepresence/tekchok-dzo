[11223-11242]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – SPONTANEOUSLY ACCOMPLISHED PRIMORDIAL WISDOM]
[PLAY OF APPEARANCE]

<Nature as spontaneously accomplished primordial wisdom. Lines 11223-11226: "confusion-thought ceased-by-means-of what-to not-done, not-born by-means-of ceased empty, nature spontaneously-accomplished primordial-wisdom called, not-born not-ceased what-to not-think"—confusion-thoughts ceased without doing anything; ceased by means of not-born, empty; nature spontaneously accomplished called primordial wisdom; not born, not ceased, nothing to think. Lines 11227-11242: "object pure definite not because, capacity and quality ceased not-by-means-of, play only-to appearing from, various perfected's ground-base, not because appearance-to appearance because empty, appearance empty entering's branch having, buddha sentient-being pure object, thus ground called manifestly abides, nature only-to abiding from, entity's portion-from examined by-means-of empty, branch elaboration because knowing perfected, self-abiding spontaneous accomplished because, this-to accept-reject two not, compassion all-pervading primordial-wisdom from, not-do various arising's door, deed like-appearing entity perfected"—object not definitely pure; capacity/quality not ceased; appearing from play only; various perfected ground-base; not because appearance, appearance because empty; appearance-emptiness having branches; buddha/sentient being pure object; thus ground manifestly abides; from nature-only abiding; entity portion examined empty; knowing perfected because elaboration branches; self-abiding spontaneous accomplishment; no accept/reject two; compassion all-pervading from primordial wisdom; various arising's door without doing; deed like-appearing perfected entity. This is *lhun grub ye shes*—spontaneously accomplished primordial wisdom.>

[11243-11266]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – DHARMAKĀYA EMPTY NATURE]
[FORCE OF COMPASSION]

<Lines 11243-11262: "dharma-body empty's nature from, primordial-wisdom knowing perfected's portion, force-by-means-of sentient-beings to arise, that not samsara-nirvana class broken by-means-of, knowing-by-means-of aware and clear, self-aware clear's identity from, nature force-by-means-of compassion itself, not-ceased ceased not, element pure's portion from, karma-to not one perfected's, sun from light like, self-possessed obstruction not, that-from characteristic holding's, enjoyment-body's primordial-wisdom five arise, mirror primordial-wisdom form-reflection from, shape and color appearance-portion perfected, white stain pure because light, self-possessed appearing samsara-nirvana connect, dharmas all-by-means-of form appearance-by-means-of, self-aware primordial-wisdom called"—from dharmakāya empty nature; primordial wisdom knowing perfected portion; force arising to sentient beings; not broken by samsara-nirvana class; knowing aware and clear; from self-aware clear identity; compassion itself by force of nature; not ceased, not ceased; from pure element portion; karma not one perfected; like light from sun; self-possessed without obstruction; from that characteristic holding; five primordial wisdoms of enjoyment body arise; mirror primordial wisdom from form-reflection; shape/color appearance portion perfected; white pure because stain-free light; self-possessed appearing samsara-nirvana connection; all dharmas by form appearance; called self-aware primordial wisdom. Five primordial wisdoms (*ye shes lnga*) of saṃbhogakāya arise from dharmakāya empty nature. Lines 11263-11266: "clear and empty-to empty-by-means-of liberate, liberation hold extreme's thought exhausted, placed-not liberation-ground perfected, equal called is"—clear and empty liberated by emptiness; thought of holding liberation exhausted; perfected liberation-ground not placed; called equal. This is *mnyam pa nyid* (equality)—beyond liberation/bondage dichotomy.>
