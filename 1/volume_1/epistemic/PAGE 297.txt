Details twelve links from contact through aging-dying. From ego, twelve-wheel delusion; even one life, light-clear sets, samsara-bardo appearance-moment first self-face not-knowing is ignorance. Outer breath cut time dying, dependent-origination enters interval. Then coarse-subtle dissolving manner, channels transfer stages reverse dependent-origination. Deluded-appearance empty, samsara turns; nirvana self-arisen manner first primordially-pure appearance arises. Dharmata bardo appearances are nirvana's dependent-origination manifest. Self-face not-know: wander; know: liberate—samsara-nirvana liberation. Pearl Garland: thus abiding dharmata, delusion from-start none; dharmakaya sky-like, adventitious beings cloud-obscured.
