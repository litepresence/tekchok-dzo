<Twelve links of dependent origination (*rten 'brel yan lag bcu gnyis*). Lines 12331-12338: "that from object from utilize is touch, that from joy sorrow middle three arise is feeling, that from bliss to attached and suffering not-desiring knowing is craving, that from object accept grasping is grasping, that from karma and afflictions spread future birth in propel karma accomplish is becoming, that from realms various in birth is birth, that from mature and aged and die is aging-dying, that also beginning from ego up-to wheel twelve by-means-of delusion"—touch is utilizing from object from that; feeling is three arise middle sorrow joy from that; craving is knowing not-desiring suffering and attached to bliss from that; grasping is grasping accept object from that; becoming is accomplish karma propel in future birth spread afflictions and karma from that; birth is birth in various realms from that; aging-dying is die and aged and mature from that; delusion by means of twelve wheel up-to ego from beginning that also. **Twelve links**:
1. Ignorance (ma rig pa)
2. Formation ('du byed)
3. Consciousness (rnam shes)
4. Name-form (ming gzugs)
5. Six sense bases (skye mched drug)
6. Contact (reg pa)
7. Feeling (tshor ba)
8. Craving (sred pa)
9. Grasping (len pa)
10. Becoming (srid pa)
11. Birth (skye ba)
12. Aging-dying (rga shi)

Lines 12339-12345: "life one even light clear sets samsara bardo appearance-moment first self face not-know is ignorance, outer breath cut time die by, that interval dependent-origination manner enters, then coarse subtle dissolving manner channels transfer stages dependent-origination reverse, deluded-appearance empty from samsara turn when, nirvana self-arisen manner first primordially-pure appearance arise from, dharmata bardo appearances are nirvana dependent-origination manifest appearance"—ignorance is not know self face first moment appearance bardo samsara sets clear light even one life; by die time cut outer breath; enters manner dependent-origination interval that; reverse dependent-origination stages transfer channels manner dissolving subtle coarse then; when turn samsara from empty deluded-appearance; from arise appearance primordially-pure first manner self-arisen nirvana; manifest appearance dependent-origination nirvana are appearances bardo dharmatā. **Bardo process**: death (breath cut) → dependent origination interval → coarse/subtle dissolving → channels transfer → reverse dependent origination. Deluded appearance empty = turn samsara. Dharmatā bardo appearances = nirvana dependent origination.>
