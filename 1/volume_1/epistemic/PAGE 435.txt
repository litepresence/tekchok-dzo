<Dharmadhātu wisdom (*chos dbyings ye shes*): awareness's empty essence, pervading all like space. Not a wisdom that knows objects but the ground of all knowing. When ignorance is recognized as never having existed, this wisdom naturally manifests—not achieved but revealed.>

<Mirror-like wisdom (*me long lta bu'i ye shes*): awareness's clear nature, reflecting all without grasping. Like a mirror reflecting forms without judgment or attachment—this is awareness's natural clarity, not a cultivated state.>
