[DZOGCHEN VIEW – SAMSARA ENTERING & REVERSE METHODS]
[SIX LIBERATION DOORS]

<Samsara entering and delusion reverse methods. Lines 11819-11821: "Samsara enter method like if impure delusion-as appear, Delusion reverse method like if primordially pure display complete liberate-from Buddha, Also spontaneous appear-method-of aspect from impure samsara-of door arise"—if like samsara entering method, appearing as impure delusion; if like delusion reverse method, liberating from Buddha with complete primordially pure display; also from spontaneous appear-method aspect, impure samsara door arises. **Two methods**:
1. Entering samsara: delusion appearance
2. Reverse delusion: liberation to Buddha

and as nature spontaneous quality complete method; spontaneous arise defect in base non-realizing plural; although liberating from sense sharp at top of six one-one actual base plural, existence non-ceasing, door and seven in liberate method at top of six;
