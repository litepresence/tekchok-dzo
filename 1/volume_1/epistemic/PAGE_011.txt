[323-335]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – BODY ACTIVITIES COMPLETE]
[QUALITY MANIFESTATION]

<Perfecting body with wisdom face-hand (*ye shes kyi zhal phyag rdzogs pas sku*), perfecting body-nature with light face-hand (*'od kyi zhal phyag gsal bas rang snang grol bar mdzad*), perfecting knowledge with discerning face-hand (*shes rab kyi zhal phyag gsal bas mkhyen pa rdzogs par mdzad*)—inner four: thirty-two marks (*mtshan sum cu rtsa gnyis*) perfecting body's qualities, eighty characteristics (*dpe byad brgyad cu*) perfecting Dharmatā, light-ray mass emanating (*'od zer gyi tshogs 'phro ba*) perfecting special qualities, face pervasively residing (*zhal kun du thal ler bzhugs pa*) perfecting enjoyment-characteristic. Secret four: front-back without residing (*mdun dang rgyab med par bzhugs*) perfecting appearance-aspect, inside clear outside empty (*nang nas bltas na phyi gsal*), outside clear inside (*phyi nas bltas na nang gsal*) perfecting non-dual quality, tip pervading all directions (*rtse mo phyogs kun tu zang thal*) perfecting non-dwelling without cessation.>

[336-349]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – SPEECH ACTIVITIES]
[TWELVE DIVISIONS MANIFEST]

<Outer four speech activities: inexpressible (*brjod du med pa*), unable to be shown (*bstan du med pa*), unseeable (*bltar med pa*), without trace (*cung zad med pa*)—inner four: self-nature (*rang bzhin*), transcending (*gyin 'da'*), non-subject (*yul can ma yin*), non-multiple (*du ma med*)—secret four: fresh (*so ma*), innate (*gnyug ma*), natural (*rnal ma*), unaltered (*ma bcos*). This describes *gsung* (speech) as *sgra* (sound) nature—beyond expression yet expressing all.>

[DZOGCHEN VIEW – FACE PRACTICE COMPLETION]
[ALL-KNOWING ENJOYMENT]

<Face practice pure (*zhal gyi sbyor ba dag pas*) engaging all-knowing Dharma (*thams cad mkhyen pa'i chos la longs spyod par mdzad*)—completing the fivefold structure of body, speech, mind, qualities, activities. This "perfection" (*rdzogs pa*) indicates recognition-completeness, not achievement of separate faculty.>
