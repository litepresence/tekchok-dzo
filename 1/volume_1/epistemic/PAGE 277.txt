[DZOGCHEN VIEW – SEMS SIDE]
[DECLARATIVE FINALITY]
[RISK: REIFICATION]

Introduces three cavities (*sbubs gsum*) of spontaneous presence: base jewel cavity, path jewel cavity, and result secret cavity. From the eight spontaneous presence appearing-doors, all samsara-nirvana dharmas arise with non-ceasing door. The Enjoyment Body mandala of five elements—wisdom warmth, compassion water, space earth, wind movement—appears from body-wisdom own-light. From appearance's tone/display, nature emanation fields and taming emanation bodies manifest. Bkra-shis-mdzes-ldan states all phenomena arise from spontaneous presence: buddhas, sentient beings, wisdom, light, awareness, non-awareness, karma, compassion, existence-appearances.
