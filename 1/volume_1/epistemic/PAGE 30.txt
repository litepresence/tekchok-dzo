[UPĀYA STATEMENT]
[RISK: SPATIAL REIFICATION]

<The cosmogonic sequence of contained beings (*bcud kyi 'jig rten*)—nectar rain (*bdud rtsi'i char*) to jewel-trees to lotus-born beings—presents standard Buddhist world-formation narrative. Operating within temporal framework of *vivartakalpa*, this sequential genesis is conventional description awaiting Dzogchen transposition.>
