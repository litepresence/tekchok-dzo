[WISH-FULFILLING TREE & FOUR CONDITIONS]

<Wish-fulfilling tree and four conditions. Lines 12418-12421: "Wish-fulfilling tree spread called, Buddha youth vase body-by-means-of blessing from arise-by-means-of tree and, Egg from arise-by-means-of heat and moisture from hatch one exist from, That from mind self arise-by-means-of wisdom called agitate from Jambudvipa-by-means-of world arise"—called spread wish-fulfilling tree; and tree from arise by means of blessing from Buddha youth vase body; from exist one hatch from moisture and heat from arise egg; arise world from Jambudvipa by means of agitate called wisdom from self arise mind from that. **Three birth types**:
1. Wish-fulfilling tree (blessing from Buddha)
2. Egg birth (heat + moisture)
3. Mind birth (wisdom agitation)

Lines 12422-12432: "That delusion object-by-means-of ignorance called, Delusion-basis basis-by-means-of ignorance, Delusion-basis self-by-means-of self not-pure from arise and, First cause condition called ignorance actual arise and, Basis not-know ignorance, That from object condition called delusion object-by-means-of ignorance like, That from master condition called tree on I grasp arise, That from equal immediate condition called, Consciousness coarse go-by-means-of grasp-by-means-of grasp, That from affliction coarse go, That delusion basis-by-means-of ignorance called"—called ignorance by means of delusion object; ignorance by means of basis delusion-basis; and arise from not-pure self by means of self delusion-basis; and arise actual ignorance called first cause condition; ignorance not-know basis; like ignorance by means of delusion object called object condition from that; arise grasp I on tree called master condition from that; called equal immediate condition from that; grasp by means of grasp by means of coarse go consciousness; coarse go affliction from that; called ignorance by means of basis delusion from that. **Four conditions**:
1. Cause condition (*rgyu rkyen*): ignorance
2. Object condition (*dmigs rkyen*): delusion object
3. Master condition (*bdag rkyen*): grasping "I" on tree
4. Immediate condition (*de ma thag rkyen*): equal

Consciousness grasps coarsely → afflictions arise.>
