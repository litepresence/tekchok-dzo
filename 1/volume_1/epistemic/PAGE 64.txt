[DECLARATIVE FINALITY]
[POLEMICAL DISTINCTION]

<The transition to Buddhist vehicle presentation (*theg pa rnam gzhag*) marks Dzogchen's definitive hermeneutical positioning. The distinction between Buddhist (*nang pa*) and non-Buddhist (*phyi rol pa*) views operates at doxographical apex—asserting that Dzogchen transcends both through direct recognition of awareness-nature (*rig pa*).>
