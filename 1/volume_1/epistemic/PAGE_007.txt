[192-206]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – GANDAVYŪHA CITATION]
[BODHISATTVA EMANATION]

<Citations from *yab sras mjal ba* (Gandavyūha Sutra) and *phal po che* (Avatamsaka) establish bodhisattva capability: "great hero skillful means" (*dpa' bo chen po thabs mkhas*) manifesting as "ten million Buddhas" (*bye ba phrag brgyar rgyal ba*) to ripen sentient beings (*sems can yongs su smin pa*). Even after obtaining supreme enlightenment (*byang chub rgya mtsho rab brnyes*), they continue generating bodhicitta (*byang chub sems bskyed*) and showing "ocean of non-diminishing conduct" (*mi 'grib spyod pa rgya mtsho*). This describes *thugs rje* (compassion) as inexhaustible display, not attainment-status.>

[207-210]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – PRIMORDIAL BODHICITTA]
[ATEMPORAL RESOLUTION]

<The "first protector" (*thog ma'i mgon po*) contemplating taming this field (*zhing 'di gdul bar dgongs nas*) manifests emanations demonstrating bodhicitta-generation—referenced to Fortunate Eon (*bskal pa bzang po*) regarding (*ltos nas*) immeasurable eons before (*bskal pa dpag tu med pa'i sngon rol*). The offering of golden vajra (*gser gyi rdo rje*) to Buddha Flower-Beautiful Great (*me tog mdzes pa chen po*) and "first mind generating supreme enlightenment" (*thugs dang po byang chub mchog tu sems bskyed*) describes timeless recognition-event, not historical occurrence.>

[211-216]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – EMANATION BIOGRAPHY]
[PEDAGOGICAL NARRATIVE]

<Second birth (*skye ba gnyis*) as householder Good-Give's son Jewel-Garland (*khyim bdag legs sbyin gyi sras nor bu'i phreng ba*) hearing Dharma near Buddha Wheel-Well-Conduct (*'khor lo legs par spyod pa*); then birth as Lightning-Light (*glog gi 'od*) the brahmin youth Good-Give (*bram ze'i khye'u legs sbyin*); five years hearing Dharma from Buddha Good-Intelligence (*dge ba'i blo gros*), seven years in single-pointed samādhi (*ting nge 'dzin rtse gcig*), awakening at age seventy-five on Jewel-Precious-Heap Mountain (*nor bu rin po che'i phung po*)—this is *rnam thar* (liberation-story) as teaching-device, not historical record.>

[217-218]
[DZOGCHEN VIEW – SELF-ARISEN MANIFESTATION]
[NON-DEPENDENT DISPLAY]

<"From that state... self-arising enjoyment-body ornament and color wheel" (*de'i ngang las... longs spyod rdzogs pa'i sku rgyan dang kha dog gi 'khor lo rang byon*) and "thirty-six tantras self-arisen" (*mdzad pa sum cu rtsa drug gi rgyud rang byung*) from light-ray gathering and spreading (*'od zer 'phro 'du*) describe *lhun grub* (spontaneous accomplishment). This is not creation but natural display (*rang snang*) of awareness-nature. The enumeration (36) is symbolic completeness, not catalog.>
