<The summary citation from *Thal 'gyur* (Consequence/Prasanga) presents Dzogchen textual statistics: nine spaces (*klong dgu*), 21,000 sections (*bam po*), three categories (*sde gsum*), 35,000 chapters (*le'u*), etc. These numbers encode completeness, not literal bibliographic data. The reconvergence into three series (*sems sde*, *klong sde*, *man ngag gi sde*) demonstrates Longchenpa's hermeneutical framework: all Dzogchen teachings organize by pedagogical approach—mind (*sems*), space (*klong*), or instruction (*man ngag*) as entry points. The phrase *rdzogs pa chen po de dag* (those great perfections) indicates all enumerations point to single *chos nyid*, not competing systems.>
