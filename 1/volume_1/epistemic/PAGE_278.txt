[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW]
[FIVE FOLD STRUCTURE OF ENJOYMENT BODY]

<Path manifestation as enjoyment body. Line 11547: "That path manifestly appear-of base say"—say base of path manifest appearance. Lines 11548-11552: Five qualities: "That also clear, Warm, Cool, Light, Move-of self hold"—clear, warm, cool, light, self-holding movement. These correspond to five elements as five wisdoms: clear = space/ether, warm = fire, cool = water, light = earth, movement = wind. Lines 11553-11568: Extensive fivefold classification: "Body five and possess, Primordial-wisdom five and possess, Families five and, Color five and, Light five and, Father five and, Mother five and, Form five and, Sound five and, Smell five and, Taste five and, Touch five and, Dharma five and, Appear five and, Chief five and, Retinue five and possess"—possessing five bodies, five primordial wisdoms, five families, five colors, five lights, five fathers, five mothers, five forms, five sounds, five smells, five tastes, five touches, five dharmas, five appearances, five chiefs, five retinues. **Pentadic classification** (*lnga ldan*) of saṃbhogakāya: everything structured as five—corresponding to five Buddha families (*rigs lnga*), five wisdoms, five elements, five senses. This is mandala architecture (*dkyil 'khor*) of enjoyment body.>

[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW]
[EMANATION BODY MANIFESTATIONS]

<Lines 11569-11572: "That also marks good thirty two and possess, Marks good eighty and possess, Vast dharma six and possess, That enjoyment body-of appear-way"—also possessing thirty-two good marks, eighty good marks, six vast dharmas; that enjoyment body appear-way. Major and minor marks (*mtshan/dpe byad*) of Buddha body; six vast dharmas (*chos mtha' yas pa drug*)—enjoyment body qualities. Lines 11573-11590: Three emanation bodies: "That and that from arise-of relate, That-of appear from nature emanation body-of appear and, Beings tame emanation body-of appear-way and, Impure emanation body-of appear-way attach, That also nature emanation-of body as-for, Nature spontaneous from arise, Nature emanation-of body that-of appear-way as-for, Enjoyment non-exhaust, Retinue non-exhaust, Quality exhaust non-know, Power exhaust non-know, Display exhaust non-know, Miracle exhaust non-know, Life-of measure number non-existent, Sickness-of suffering all and free, Palace mind-by-means-of non-conceive, Dharma mind-by-means-of non-conceive teach, That nature emanation body-of appear-way say"—relate arising from that and that; from that appearance, nature emanation body appears; beings-tame emanation body appear-way; impure emanation body appear-way attached; as for nature emanation body: from spontaneous nature arise; as for nature emanation body appear-way: enjoyment non-exhausted, retinue non-exhausted; quality/power/display/miracle unknowably exhausted; life measure number non-existent; free from all sickness suffering; palace inconceivable by mind; teach dharma inconceivable by mind; say nature emanation body appear-way. Nature emanation body (*rang bzhin sprul sku*) characteristics: inexhaustible enjoyment/retinue, inconceivable qualities, measureless life, free from sickness. Lines 11591-11606: Beings-tame and impure emanation bodies: "That from beings tame emanation-of body arise, That also various in appear in various-of object know, Appear although follow not cut, Appear in follow not attach, Eye completely pure possess, Ear completely pure possess, Nose completely pure possess, Tongue completely pure possess, Body completely pure possess, Mind completely pure possess, Compassion-of deed various show, Primordial-wisdom special possess, Knowledge non-cease as all-to appear, That beings tame emanation-of body, That from impure world-of realm attach, That also thus appear great is"—from that arise beings-tame emanation body; also variously appearing, knowing various objects; appearing although not cutting following; not attaching in appearance; possessing completely pure eye/ear/nose/tongue/body/mind; showing various compassion deeds; possessing special primordial wisdom; appearing as all with non-ceasing knowledge; that beings-tame emanation body; from impure world realm attached; also thus great appearance. Beings-tame emanation body: pure sense powers, special wisdom, compassionate deeds. Impure emanation body: attached to impure world realm. Both "great appearance"—vast manifestation for benefit of beings.>
