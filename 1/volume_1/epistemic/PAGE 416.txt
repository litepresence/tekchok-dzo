[DZOGCHEN VIEW – RIGPA SIDE]
[DECLARATIVE FINALITY]
[RISK: VIEW COLLAPSE]

<Kun gzhi is not the ultimate ground but the basis of delusion. The actual ground is dharmakāya—primordially pure, spontaneously perfect. Kun gzhi is the play of that ground when not recognized. The distinction is crucial: without it, one might mistake alaya for the ultimate, leading to subtle eternalism.>
