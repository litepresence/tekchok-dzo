[SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW]
[SIX IGNORANCES & WISDOM PATH]

<Six ignorances and wisdom path. Lines 12455-12461: "Vow break action many do, Definitely place mind, That all-by-means-of appearance to I-by-means-of be and, Other-by-means-of be think that definitely place mind, That grasp thought-by-means-of ignorance called, Remedy path-by-means-of ignorance, Mind six gather-by-means-of wisdom-by-means-of path obscure and"—do many action break vow; mind place definitely; and be I to appearance by means of all that; mind place definitely that think be by means of other; called ignorance by means of thought grasp; ignorance by means of remedy path; and obscure path by means of wisdom by means of gather six mind. **Ignorance of self/other**: grasping "I" in appearance, thinking "other." Lines 12462-12469: "That also mind-by-means-of wisdom not-see and clear not-give, Wisdom to thought not-possess, Mind from move-maker exist-by-means-of cause, Buddha-by-means-of path obscure is, That artificial path-by-means-of ignorance called, Not-know confuse-by-means-of ignorance self-by-means-of appearance-by-means-of light-in exist not-know-by-means-of again again samsara cause samsara-in samsara, That not-know confuse-by-means-of ignorance, Thus ignorance six-as arise-by-means-of wisdom-by-means-of appearance not-know said"—also that: not see wisdom by means of mind and not give clear; not possess thought to wisdom; cause exist by means of move-maker from mind; is obscure path by means of Buddha; called ignorance by means of artificial path; cause samsara again again by means of not know exist in light by means of appearance by means of self ignorance confuse not-know; ignorance by means of confuse not-know; said not know appearance by means of wisdom by means of arise six-as ignorance thus. **Six ignorances**:
1. Grasping thought ignorance
2. Remedy path ignorance
3. Mind-wisdom not seeing
4. Move-maker cause ignorance
5. Buddha-path obscure ignorance
6. Artificial path ignorance

Wisdom does not see appearance—mind obscures. Not knowing light in appearance = samsara cause.>
