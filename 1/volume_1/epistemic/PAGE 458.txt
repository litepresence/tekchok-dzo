<Ground: thirteen kāyas as potential (for *sems*-side), as actual (for *rigpa*-side). Path: glimpses of kāyas, unstable recognition. Fruition: continuous kāya-display, never separate from rigpa. These are not three phases but three ways of describing one recognition.>

[RISK: REIFICATION]

<The crucial instruction: do not think "I will perfect the thirteen kāyas in the future." Think "the thirteen kāyas are perfect now." The difference between these two thoughts is the difference between samsara and nirvāṇa—between wandering and liberation.>
