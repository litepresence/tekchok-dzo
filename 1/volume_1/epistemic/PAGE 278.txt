[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW]
[DECLARATIVE FINALITY]
[RISK: REIFICATION]

Enumerates the Enjoyment Body (*longs sku*) appearance-mode with fivefold structures: five bodies, five wisdoms, five families, five colors, five lights, five fathers, five mothers, five forms, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, dharmas, appearances, chiefs, retinues. Possessing thirty-two marks (*mtshan bzang po sum cu rtsa gnyis*), eighty marks (*dpe byad bzang po brgyad cu*), and six vast dharmas. From this appear nature emanation body, beings-taming emanation body, and impure emanation body appearances. These tantric taxonomies describe the complete manifestation structure from spontaneous presence.
