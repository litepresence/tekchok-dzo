[PRATYEKABUDDHA PATH TYPES]

<Dull faculties (*dbang po rtul po*) and medium diligence (*'bring brtson 'grus*) some obtain results in that very life. Sharp faculties and bodhisattvas who previously traversed five paths and accumulated much collection—on such basis, completing five paths in single sitting, liberation sought. From Śrāvaka vehicle, their path small, medium, great—accomplishing Śrāvaka, Pratyekabuddha, and Buddha's enlightenment. Not called Great Vehicle or Mantra path. For Śrāvaka-Pratyekabuddha, eight inferior grounds (*sa brgyad*) like white-clarity vision ground explained. Depending on that, stream-enterer, once-returner, non-returner, arhat—each with entry and abiding, two each, called eight great person stages. Bodhisattvas' ten grounds from supreme joy also have four śramaṇa fruits. [SŪTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW] gradualist framework.>

[ŚRĀVAKA DOXOGRAPHY]

<Citation from *Rigpa Rangshar* (*rig pa rang shar*): Śrāvaka vehicle entry-door is four truths—common Śrāvaka entry-door. Two divisions accepted: Vaibhāṣika (*bye brag pa*) and Sautrāntika (*mdo sde pa*). Vaibhāṣika view: aggregates, elements, sense-bases like yak-horn rabbit-horn manner. Self's mind becoming sign, accepts atoms and particles. Thus analyzing, meditate emptiness. Sautrāntika view: nature of recitation, and three—hearing, thinking, meditation—meditating three prajñās, obtain ten-ground result. Śrāvaka common manner: meditating four truths, accept four results. Meditation system: wanting to meditate cessation, wanting to abandon origin. Cessation meditation system: entering abode of non-attachment to external elements, first becoming accustomed to food; becoming accustomed to food, meditate samādhi concentration; meditate samādhi non-conceptual single-pointed. Wanting to obtain result in Victor Maitreya's teaching. Origin abandonment: separating food perception, wanting to decide origin continuum. That also meditate anger antidote, abandon all afflictions.>
