[4519-4521]
[ETERNALISTIC ERROR] [REIFICATION ERROR]

MISREADING:
"Snake coil like text word self-liberated shown" and "great bird sky expanse unobstructed" establish text and awareness as snake-like and bird-like eternal qualities that are self-liberated and unobstructed.

WHY IT ARISES:
The animal metaphors suggest naturalness. The self-liberation implies automaticity.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Text and awareness are substantialized as animal-nature. The practitioner seeks these qualities.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Animal fixation. Naturalness chasing. Self-liberation confusion.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Naturalism → anti-civilization → regression → social dysfunction.
