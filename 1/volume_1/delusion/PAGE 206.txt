MISREADING:
The four mudras (karma mudra, samaya mudra, dharma mudra, mahamudra) are interpreted as physical hand gestures and sexual positions that mechanically produce realization when performed correctly.

WHY IT ARISES:
Performative frameworks project onto mudra practice. The detailed physical instructions suggest choreographed causation. Desire for concrete techniques supports literal interpretation.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
The teaching that mudras are expressions of realized view, not causes of realization, is entirely lost. Practice becomes physical performance rather than recognition.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Practitioners obsess over precise positioning while neglecting view. Sexual technique replaces wisdom inquiry. The symbolic dimensions are reduced to mechanics.
