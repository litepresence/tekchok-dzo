[4393-4395]
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [VIEW COLLAPSE]

MISREADING:
"Realized Buddha tantra-to entered" and "unrealized sentient-being tantra-to entered" establish two distinct ontological categories of beings who either enter the tantra or don't, based on realization.

WHY IT ARISES:
The spatial metaphor (entered) suggests container. The binary (realized/unrealized) implies categories.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Tantra is substantialized as container for realized beings. The practitioner seeks entry.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Entry fixation. Category elitism. Exclusion of "unrealized."

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Elitism → sectarianism → discrimination → institutional abuse.
