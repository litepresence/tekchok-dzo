[5072-5074]
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR] [PEDAGOGICAL ERROR]

MISREADING:
"K+kh+gha g+ha j+ha d+ha b+ha is" and "place thirty-four on letter all gathered presented" present thirty-four letters as gathered, presented system to be studied.

WHY IT ARISES:
The enumeration suggests comprehensiveness. The presentation implies pedagogy.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Letters are substantialized as 34-element curriculum. The practitioner memorizes.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Memorization obsession. Letter-cataloging. System mastery.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Mastery → scholastic pride → no realization → cynicism.
