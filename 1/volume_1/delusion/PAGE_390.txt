[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [AFFLICTION AS ENTITY]

MISREADING:
Mental afflictions (nyon mongs) are real forces or entities that obscure awareness.

WHY IT ARISES:
Vivid experience of emotional turbulence seems substantial.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Battling afflictions as if they were enemies.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Strengthening the very dualism that sustains them.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SUTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW] applied to [DZOGCHEN VIEW].

[PRACTICE ERROR] [SAMSARA AS PLACE]

MISREADING:
Samsara is a place, realm, or condition one must escape from.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Wheel" (khor lo) suggests location. Three realms described spatially.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking exit from here to somewhere else.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Rejecting present experience as samsaric.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [ETERNALISTIC ERROR] of liberation.
