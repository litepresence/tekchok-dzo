[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [VIEW COLLAPSE]

MISREADING:
"Word non-depending because wisdom depending completely" establishes a dichotomy where wisdom transcends words entirely, making language not just limited but actively obstructive.

WHY IT ARISES:
The contrast between word-non-dependence and wisdom-dependence suggests language prevents wisdom. The absoluteness of "completely" implies total rejection.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
All conceptual understanding is rejected as obstructive. The practitioner cannot use language to navigate or communicate about the view.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Inability to teach or discuss. Conceptual nihilism. Inarticulate mysticism.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Language rejection → inability to clarify → isolation → vulnerability to wrong views.
