[4560-4563]
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR] [PEDAGOGICAL ERROR]

MISREADING:
"Categories first tantra essence three awareness self-arise" and "self-liberated letter non-existent three" present a categorical system with three essences and three self-liberations to be understood.

WHY IT ARISES:
The enumeration suggests structure. The "categories" implies classification.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Dzogchen becomes categorical study. The practitioner masters the three-by-three system.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Categorical fixation. System obsession. Structural pride.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
System mastery → scholastic pride → sectarian elitism → conflict.
