[16391-16410]
[PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [PATH AS JOURNEY]

MISREADING:
The path (lam) is a progression from ignorance to enlightenment over time.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Path" metaphor implies travel. Narrative structure of spiritual journey.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Viewing oneself as traveler on path to destination.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Measuring progress and comparing states.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR] [SUTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW].

[16411-16430]
[REIFICATION ERROR] [DISPLAY AS PROJECTION]

MISREADING:
The display (rol pa) is something projected by or arising from the ground.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Arising" suggests production. Display/ground grammar implies duality.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Investigating relationship between ground and display.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Philosophical elaboration of non-dual relationship.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR].

[16431-16450]
[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [DZOGCHEN AS HIGHEST VIEW]

MISREADING:
Dzogchen is the highest, best, or most advanced Buddhist teaching.

WHY IT ARISES:
Text presents Dzogchen as supreme vehicle. Competitive spirituality.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Spiritual elitism and looking down on other traditions.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that vehicles are pedagogical, not hierarchical.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[POLEMICAL DISTINCTION] hardened into identity.
