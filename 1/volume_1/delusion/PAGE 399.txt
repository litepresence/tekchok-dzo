[16991-17010]
[PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [SAMENESS AS IDENTITY]

MISREADING:
Samsara and nirvana are the same (mtshungs) in the sense of being identical or one thing.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Same" suggests identity. Unity mysticism conflates sameness with non-duality.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Concluding that samsara IS nirvana without transformation.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Spiritual bypassing and unethical behavior.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[NIHILISTIC ERROR] "nothing matters."
