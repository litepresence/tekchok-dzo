[16511-16530]
[REIFICATION ERROR] [SPACE AS CONTAINER]

MISREADING:
Space (dbyings) is a container or field in which phenomena appear.

WHY IT ARISES:
Spatial metaphors suggest containment. Physics conception of space.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking spacious awareness as experience.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Confusing openness with spatial expansion.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR] seeking particular state.

[16531-16550]
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [FIVE COLORS SUBSTANTIALISM]

MISREADING:
The five colors (dkar, ser, dmar, ljang, mthing) are actual visual colors appearing in space.

WHY IT ARISES:
Color language suggests visual phenomenon. Meditation experiences produce colors.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Looking for or cultivating colored lights.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Fixation on visual phenomena as signs of progress.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW] fixation on signs.

[16551-16570]
[PRACTICE ERROR] [VAJRA CHAIN OBSTRUCTION]

MISREADING:
The vajra chain (rdo rje lu gu rgyud) is an obstacle or boundary to be crossed.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Chain" suggests bondage. "No place to grasp" sounds like difficulty.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Struggling to break through or transcend.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Aggressive approach to practice.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR] effortful striving.
