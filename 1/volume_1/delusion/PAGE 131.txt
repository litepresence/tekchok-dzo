[4953-4958]
[REIFICATION ERROR] [ETERNALISTIC ERROR]

MISREADING:
"Vajra-holder" and "vajra mind-essence" and "dharma's light-ray space pervaded" establish vajra-holder as possessor of eternal mind-essence with pervading light-rays.

WHY IT ARISES:
The mineral metaphor suggests permanence. The spatial metaphor suggests expansion.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Vajra-holder is substantialized as eternal possessor. The practitioner identifies as holder.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Identity fixation. Possession obsession. Expansion-chasing.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Identity inflation → megalomania → psychosis → institutionalization.
