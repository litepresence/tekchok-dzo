[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [FACULTY PHYSICALISM]

MISREADING:
Sense faculties (eye, ear, etc.) are being reduced to or identified with physical organs.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Eye faculty" invites conflation with physical eye. Western physiology.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Missing the distinction between physical organ and cognitive capacity.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Reduction of mind to brain function.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR].
