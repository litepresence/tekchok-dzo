[PRACTICE ERROR] [APERTURE OPENING]

MISREADING:
The aperture (sgo) must be opened or cleared to allow vision.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Gate" suggests obstruction. Spiritual practice often framed as removing blocks.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Trying to open or clear the aperture.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Manipulation of attention and perception.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR] effortful doing.

[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [SENSE-PERCEPTION AS REALITY]

MISREADING:
Sense perceptions (dbang po, yul) accurately represent external reality.

WHY IT ARISES:
Naive realism default. Perceptions seem to reveal objects.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Taking perceptions as evidence of external reality.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Reification of perceived objects.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [ORDINARY COGNITION].

[16971-16990]
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [HOOK AS INSTRUMENT]

MISREADING:
The hook (lcags kyu) is an actual instrument that grasps or pulls objects.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Hook" metaphor invites literalization. Agency attributed to tool.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Looking for hook as subtle structure.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that hook represents grasping nature itself.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[REIFICATION ERROR] metaphor literalism.
