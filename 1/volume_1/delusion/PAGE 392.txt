[PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [BARDO RECOGNITION TECHNIQUE]

MISREADING:
Bardo recognition is a technique to be applied at the moment of death.

WHY IT ARISES:
Bardo literature focuses on death. Fear of death motivates preparation.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Deferring practice to hypothetical future moment.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that bardo is every moment of non-recognition.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] temporal displacement.

[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [APPEARANCE AS OBSCURATION]

MISREADING:
Delusory appearances ('khrul snang) are obstacles that prevent recognition.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Delusory" has negative valence. Meditation seeks to clear obstacles.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Trying to eliminate or see through appearances.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that appearances are self-liberated display.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SUTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW] applied to [DZOGCHEN VIEW].

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [WISDOM AS RESULT]

MISREADING:
Wisdom (ye shes) is what remains after delusion ceases or is cleared away.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Self-ceased" suggests causal result. Purification metaphors.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Attempting to remove delusion to reveal wisdom.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Endless purification project.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SUTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW] causal framework.
