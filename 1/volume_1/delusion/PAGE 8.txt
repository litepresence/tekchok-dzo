[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [KAYA TRIPARTITION LITERALISM]

MISREADING:
Three kayas are three different bodies or states that one progresses through.

WHY IT ARISES:
Number "three" suggests sequential or distinct entities. Ordinary experience of having one body.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Dharmakaya seen as goal to reach, rather than always present ground.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Meditation becomes attempt to shift from one kaya to another.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
