[16811-16830]
[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [WISDOM LAMP AS OBJECT]

MISREADING:
The wisdom lamp (shes rab kyi sgro ma) is a special insight or understanding to be gained.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Wisdom" suggests knowledge. "Lamp" implies illuminating object.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking wisdom as acquisition.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Intellectual pride in understanding.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR] [PRACTICE ERROR].

[16831-16850]
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [GENERATION AS CREATION]

MISREADING:
Generation (bskyed tshul) means creating something that did not previously exist.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Birth" (skye) suggests production. Developmental view of realization.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Waiting for realization to be born in time.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that nature is already complete.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SUTRIC PROVISIONAL VIEW] temporal framework.

[16851-16870]
[PRACTICE ERROR] [NAVEL PRODUCTION]

MISREADING:
The bindu is produced or arises from the navel (lte ba) as a physical location.

WHY IT ARISES:
"From" (nas) suggests origin. Anatomical specificity invites literalism.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Focusing attention on navel to produce bindu.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Localized fixation missing non-local nature.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[REIFICATION ERROR] [MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
