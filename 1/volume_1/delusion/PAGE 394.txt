[PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [FOUR LAMPS ENUMERATION]

MISREADING:
The four lamps are four separate, distinct things to be identified individually.

WHY IT ARISES:
Enumeration suggests separation. Analytical mind categorizes.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Investigating each lamp as separate object.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that four are aspects of one nature.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] fragmentation.
