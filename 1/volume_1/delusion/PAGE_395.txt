[PRACTICE ERROR] [FIVE LIGHTS PRODUCTION]

MISREADING:
The five lights ('od lnga) are to be generated or made to appear through practice.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Gather" ('dus pa) suggests collection or production. Visualization practices.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Attempting to manifest lights through effort.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Straining for experience and signs.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR] [TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW].

Missing that space is ungraspable openness.

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] spatial reification.
