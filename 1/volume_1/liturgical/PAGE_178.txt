[7132] "If signs of improvement arise,
[7133] Primordially, exert the empowerment ritual.
[7134] If the substance of accomplishment increases,
[7135] Having obtained supreme siddhi,
[7136] Make faithful to the meaning of equality.
[7137] Having entered the empowerments,
[7138] If good signs are always seen,
[7139] Common siddhi is accomplished by that.
[7140] Even possessing experience and knowledge,
[7141] By this also, engage in bliss and happiness.
[7142] To the guru supreme,
[7143] Whoever born with good faith,
[7144] By this also, apply to the supreme mudrā.
[7145] If self sees body and speech blissful,
[7146] This is the pure Buddha field.
[7147] And the time of empowerment of elaborations
[7148] And signs are well explained."
[7149] Thus it is spoken.
[7150] Second, time and signs of without elaboration:
[7151] Also from that:
[7152] "Now, without elaboration,
[7153] Time and signs should be shown nearby.
[7154] In this, there is no distinction of year;
[7155] Whoever knows.
[7156] In this time, summer and winter are good;
[7157] Date on the waxing fortnight;
[7158] Empowerment time at dawn when sounds clear.
[7159] In this, five or six faithful ones,
[7160] If more than seven, purpose is not accomplished.
[7161] Thus, son possessing faith,
[7162] To mind-deed virtuous,
[7163] To speech possessing guru's praise,
[7164] At empowerment time for such,
[7165] If body is not blissful,
[7166] Make body-sin confession afterwards;
[7167] Thus for speech and mind also,
[7168] Begin the deed of sin confession.
[7169] Time and signs
[7170] Of empowerment without elaboration explained."
[7171] Thus it is spoken.
[7172] Third, time and signs of extreme without elaboration also:
[7173] From that:
[7174] "Now, extreme without elaboration,
[7175] Supreme secret empowerment,
[7176] Time and signs should be explained.
[7177] That also, not a distinction of year;
[7178] Accomplished by doing self-suitable.
[7179] Outer time, time of seed entering,
[7180] For supporting the seed of awakening,
[7181] If empowered at this time,
[7182] Explained as increasing self-arisen seed.
[7183] At the time of gathering grain and fruit,
[7184] If extremely without elaboration empowerment,
[7185] For completing the fruit of wisdom,
[7186] Definitely go to the ground of three kāyas.
