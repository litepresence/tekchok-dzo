[4639] FIRST: TWO ROOT TANTRAS
[4640] Relying on the eight root scriptures of perfect self-arisen:
[4641] Empowers and ripens those who enjoy elaborations through outer elaborations.
[4642] Relying on the six chapters of letterless tantra:
[4643] Shows the complete point of upadesha that ripens and liberates.
[4644] SECOND: EXPLANATORY MOTHER AND CHILD TANTRAS
[4645] Relying on the eighty-six chapters of explanatory tantra of rigpa self-arisen:
[4646] Shows the special clarity of the four: view, meditation, conduct, and result.
[4647] Relying on the nine chapters of rigpa self-liberated:
[4648] Shows the elimination of the mother's ground's object.
[4649] THIRD: TWO LIMB TANTRAS
[4650] Relying on the five chapters of jewel heap:
[4651] Shows qualities complete in themselves.
[4652] Relying on the three chapters of blazing body-relic:
[4653] Shows signs of body, speech, and mind, past and present.
[4654] FOURTH: TWO SCRIPTURE-LOGIC CLEAR TANTRAS
[4655] Relying on the six chapters of sudden sound:
[4656] Shows the root of all scriptural collections.
[4657] Relying on the seven chapters of auspicious beauty:
[4658] Identifies delusion's time, etc.
[4659] FIVE: FOUR UPADESHAS TANTRAS
[4660] Relying on the eight chapters of vajra-sattva heart-mirror:
[4661] Shows introduction and meeting,
[4662] Shows empowerment, samaya, etc.
[4663] Relying on the seven chapters of samantabhadra mind-mirror:  Shows the distinction and elimination of deviation and obscuration.  Relying on the three chapters of introduction adorned:  Shows examples, meanings, and signs with faith.  Relying on the eight chapters of pearl garland:  Shows instruction collected, causing awakening.  SIX: THREE INTENTION SELF-POSITION TANTRAS  Relying on the six chapters of six spaces:  Shows the purification of the six consciousnesses' birth-places and the emptying of the three realms.  Relying on the four chapters of blazing lamp:  Shows the four actual lamps.
