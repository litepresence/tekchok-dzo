FIRST: TWO ROOT TANTRAS

Relying on the eight root scriptures of perfect self-arisen:

Empowers and ripens those who enjoy elaborations through outer elaborations.

Relying on the six chapters of letterless tantra:

Shows the complete point of upadesha that ripens and liberates.

SECOND: EXPLANATORY MOTHER AND CHILD TANTRAS

Relying on the eighty-six chapters of explanatory tantra of rigpa self-arisen:

Shows the special clarity of the four: view, meditation, conduct, and result.

Relying on the nine chapters of rigpa self-liberated:

Shows the elimination of the mother's ground's object.

THIRD: TWO LIMB TANTRAS

Relying on the five chapters of jewel heap:

Shows qualities complete in themselves.

Relying on the three chapters of blazing body-relic:

Shows signs of body, speech, and mind, past and present.

FOURTH: TWO SCRIPTURE-LOGIC CLEAR TANTRAS

Relying on the six chapters of sudden sound:

Shows the root of all scriptural collections.

Relying on the seven chapters of auspicious beauty:

Identifies delusion's time, etc.

FIVE: FOUR UPADESHAS TANTRAS

Relying on the eight chapters of vajra-sattva heart-mirror:

Shows introduction and meeting,

Shows empowerment, samaya, etc.

Relying on the seven chapters of samantabhadra mind-mirror:

Shows the distinction and elimination of deviation and obscuration.

Relying on the three chapters of introduction adorned:

Shows examples, meanings, and signs with faith.

Relying on the eight chapters of pearl garland:

Shows instruction collected, causing awakening.

SIX: THREE INTENTION SELF-POSITION TANTRAS

Relying on the six chapters of six spaces:

Shows the purification of the six consciousnesses' birth-places and the emptying of the three realms.

Relying on the four chapters of blazing lamp:

Shows the four actual lamps.
