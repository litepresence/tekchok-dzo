[2799] Third, establishing the Bodhisattva vehicle has two aspects: how it surpasses the lesser vehicle, and explaining the especially superior vehicle itself.

[2802] First: made special through great compassion as method and wisdom realizing both selflessnesses. From the Ratnavali: "Emptiness with compassion as essence—this accomplishes enlightenment for some." Thus it is said, and: "Through compassion alone, connection to existence continues until enlightenment. From that hearer vehicle, without explaining the bodhisattva's aspiration and dedication of conduct, how could it become the Great Vehicle?" Thus it is said, and from the Compendium: "Without method, separated from wisdom, one falls into the hearer state"—thus extensively taught.

[2818] Second, five aspects: the essence of the Bodhisattva vehicle: realizing all dharmas as space-like, and through great compassion becoming the supreme method or cause for vast conduct benefiting others. From that itself: "This vehicle, like space, is a great measureless palace, supreme for manifestly obtaining joy and bliss. Mounting that, all beings reverse suffering. Therefore by that cause it is called the Great Vehicle." So it is said. Definition: depending on whatever method, leading to the supreme great city of liberation—thus the Great Vehicle.
