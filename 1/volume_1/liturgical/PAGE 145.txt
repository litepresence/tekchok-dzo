[5585] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter seven, annotation.

[5586] Thus it is: SHAM PADZRA MU TAD YA BYA NYA PRA PA RA DUS STAN KA HIH—its meaning:

[5587] By wisdom's meaning, realized; time-unchanging wisdom, ignorance by nature conquered. Dharmadhātu's equality, surrounded and endowed. In self always existing, pervasion threefold endowed. Equality-day's wisdom, self-arisen.

[5588] Further:

[5589] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter eight, annotation.

[5590] Thus it is: MAṂ GAM HA SA HŪM—accomplishing, MA LA MA LA DHA THIM DHA THIM—its meaning:

[5591] Self's rigpa, equal-pure body's form, realized. That too, by self-arisen emptiness-wisdom, pervaded, realized. That too, to self-entity's ground, complete parinirvāṇa, realized. Three kāyas, gathering-separating without, meaning realized. All dharmas, equality, non-dual, realized. From that, compassion unceasing, like sun-rays, shines. Conceptual wisdom, self-place grasped.

[5592] Further:

[5593] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter nine, annotation.

[5594] Thus it is: TA KA YU MU SHA SU MA HĀ DU BHĀRI KE HĀ KE HĀ—

[5595] Unchanging, three kāyas' ground, pressed. All-times wisdom, always abiding, dharmic pure wisdom-heart possessed; equality-wisdom's self-nature become. To emptiness, three compassions, one time arising—that too, unceasing manner, equality-wisdom's kāya, not gone beyond. Unchanging wisdom, self-constituent, explained.

[5596] Sound's place, many letters stacked: vowel and NA RO below, in order given, with GU GU, thus read; head-circle below's top, placed. This letter-place is the bind of seventeen tantras' internals; wherever tantra's place arises, thus understand. These stacked letter examples, from Samantabhadra's mind-mirror arisen, like. This letter-wheel's order, tantra's difficult place, is.
