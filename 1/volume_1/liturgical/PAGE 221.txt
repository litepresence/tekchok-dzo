Below is shown the empowerment of that which purifies.

Third, showing the means of purifying body and afflictions through divisions of number:

From the four types of empowerment,
When the first — elaborated, based on elaboration — is divided into four:
Elaborated's elaborated external empowerment: preparation assembly, offering tooth-stick;
Its internal: offering maṇḍala;
Its secret: tying protection cord, giving kuśa;
Its complete: examining dreams.

Its unelaborated external empowerment: performing ablution,
Making request,
Vajra calling within — these three.

Its internal: drinking samaya oath-water,
Oath of abandoning saṃsāra,
Oath of accepting nirvāṇa,
Oath of not doing beings' benefit — four.

Its secret: samaya,
Giving vows,
Entering maṇḍala,
Offering mind-flower.

Its complete: entering maṇḍala, showing deity-face,
Throwing flower,
Name empowerment.

Its utterly unelaborated external: entering śrāvaka maṇḍala,
Showing measure of ground and path,
Giving upper and lower covers,
Giving bowl and fan, etc. —
Discipline's edge,
Ground, etc.,
Action's distinction,
Indicating dependent arising,
Giving meditation,
Prophecy, and
Entering bodhisattva maṇḍala,
Generating mind,
Giving vows,
Holding distinction-mind, etc.

Its internal empowerment: entering kriyā maṇḍala,
Generating mind,
Holding vows,
Throwing flower, showing deity-face,
Name empowerment,
Prophecy,
Encouraging,
Emitting breath,
Conferring empowerment.

Likewise, entering upa and yoga maṇḍalas: nine types each — twenty-seven.
