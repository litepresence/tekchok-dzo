[5585] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter seven—annotation.
[5586] Thus it is: SHAM PADZRA MU TAD YA BYA NYA PRA PA RA DUS STAN KA HIH—its meaning:
[5587] By wisdom's meaning, realized;
[5588] Time-unchanging wisdom, ignorance by nature conquered.
[5589] Dharmadhātu's equality, surrounded and endowed.
[5590] In self always existing, pervasion threefold endowed.
[5591] Equality-day's wisdom, self-arisen.
[5592] Further:
[5593] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter eight—annotation.
[5594] Thus it is: MAṂ GAM HA SA HŪM—accomplishing, MA LA MA LA DHA THIM DHA THIM—its meaning:
[5595] Self's rigpa, equal-pure body's form, realized.
[5596] That too, by self-arisen emptiness-wisdom, pervaded, realized.
[5597] That too, to self-entity's ground, complete parinirvāṇa, realized.
[5598] Three kāyas, gathering-separating without, meaning realized.
[5599] All dharmas, equality, non-dual, realized.
[5600] From that, compassion unceasing, like sun-rays, shines.
[5601] Conceptual wisdom, self-place grasped.
[5602] Further:
[5603] "Of these letter-classes, aside observe, I pray." Stacked letter nine—annotation.
[5604] Thus it is: TA KA YU MU SHA SU MA HĀ DU BHĀRI KE HĀ KE HĀ—
[5605] Unchanging, three kāyas' ground, pressed.
[5606] All-times wisdom, always abiding, dharma-pure wisdom-heart possessed;
[5607] Equality-wisdom's self-nature become.
[5608] To emptiness, three compassions, one time arising—that too, unceasing manner, equality-wisdom's kāya, not gone beyond.
[5609] Unchanging wisdom, self-constituent, explained.
[5610] Sound's place, many letters stacked: vowel and NA RO below, in order given, with GU GU, thus read;
[5611] Head-circle below's top, placed.
[5612] This letter-place is the bind of seventeen tantras' internals;
[5613] Wherever tantra's place arises, thus understand.
[5614] These stacked letter examples, from Samantabhadra's mind-mirror arisen, like.
[5615] This letter-wheel's order, tantra's difficult place, is.
