Able to demonstrate six realms' birth-places — nineteen:
Arisen from completing empowerments.

Thus hundred miracles arising on sixteen grounds — called knowledge-holder ground.

Other name-synonyms: ripened,
Life control,
Mahāmudrā,
Spontaneous accomplishment —
Yoga,
Mahāyoga,
Distinguished yoga,
Realized yoga — in sequence.

Ripened knowledge-holder: mind ripened as deity-body, but body not ripened — liberated in bardo.
Life control: liberated in that itself.
Mahāmudrā: body and mind ripened as deity.
Spontaneous accomplishment: completing self and other's two benefits.
Apply these sixteen as before; know the qualities.

Ripened: if uninterrupted, obtaining life control in that life, obtaining result.
Life control: training miracles in one great kalpa, obtaining Vajra-holder ground.
Mahāmudrā: in one hundred years, obtaining Vajra-holder ground.
Spontaneous accomplishment: in one thousand years, obtaining Vajra-holder ground — doing two benefits through four activities.

Four activities:
Pacifying,
Increasing,
Empowering,
Wrathful.

There, elaborated does pacifying activity — manifest body-mind obstacles,
Pacifying non-entity subtle and gross defilements.
Divided: pacifying's pacifying — consciousness and wind;
Pacifying's increasing — life and merit;
Pacifying's empowering — body-speech power;
Pacifying's wrathful — two pacified, abiding as one.

Unelaborated accomplishes increasing activity.
Divided: increasing's pacifying — exhausting bad conceptions;
Increasing's increasing — life, merit, retinue, enjoyments, decline, blessings;
Increasing's empowering — increasing however-wished accomplishment object.
