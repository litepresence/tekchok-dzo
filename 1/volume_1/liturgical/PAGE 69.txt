[2430] Second: extensive division of vehicles transcending the world—three parts:
[2431] Method superior to outsiders,
[2432] General general explanation,
[2433] Extensive particular explanation.
[2434] First: accepting the four Dharma-seal mudras, taking refuge in the Three Jewels—superior,
[2435] For outsiders' paths lack this.
[2436] The four mudras: all compounded things impermanent,
[2437] All with defilements suffering,
[2438] All dharmas empty, selfless,
[2439] Liberation from suffering peaceful.
[2440] Second: five aspects—
[2441] Essence,
[2442] Definition,
[2443] Division,
[2444] Purpose,
[2445] Effect.
[2446] Essence of the vehicle transcending the world:
[2447] Supreme method of definitely exiting samsara.
[2448] Definition: that which, entered, produces happiness—
[2449] From the Compendium:
[2450] Mounting that, all beings reverse suffering,
[2451] Joy, happiness, bliss, excellence accomplishing virtue,
[2452] This vehicle like space, measureless great palace.
[2453] Thus.
[2454] Division:
[2455] Small and great, two;
[2456] Or listener, self-enlightened, bodhisattva vehicles, three;
[2457] Inconceivable, regarding disciples' minds—
[2458] From Lankavatara:
[2459] As long as mind engages,
[2460] Vehicle limits are endless.
[2461] Thus.
[2462] Purpose: accomplishing temporary and ultimate meaning.
[2463] Effect: definitely exiting three realms, obtaining liberation.
[2464] Third: extensive particular explanation—
[2465] From Rigpa Rangshar:
[2466] Vehicle divisions thus:
[2467] Sutra, extensive great,
[2468] And very extensive great.
[2469] Very extensive great:
[2470] Eighty-four thousand inconceivable,
[2471] Extensively number many great,
[2472] Inconceivable, measureless,
[2473] Inexpressible, transcending thought, great.
