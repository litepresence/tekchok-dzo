[2581] Herein, explaining the three vows:
[2582] Prātimokṣa, bodhisattva, and mantra vows.
[2583] Thus explaining the three vows in brief.
[2584] Herein, Prātimokṣa vows:
[2585] Eight types: bhikṣu, bhikṣuṇī, śikṣamāṇā, śrāmaṇera, śrāmaṇerikā,
[2586] Upāsaka, upāsikā, and one-day upavāsa.
[2587] Thus explaining Prātimokṣa.
[2588] Herein, bodhisattva vows:
[2589] Aspiring and engaging bodhicitta.
[2590] The twenty root downfalls and forty-six branch faults to be abandoned.
[2591] Restoring through the four powers.
[2592] Thus explaining bodhisattva vows.
[2593] Herein, mantra vows:
[2594] The fourteen root downfalls and eight thick branches.
[2595] Samaya of body, speech, and mind.
[2596] Restoring through recitation and meditation.
[2597] Thus explaining mantra vows.
[2598] Herein, the summary of vehicles and vows:
[2599] The three vehicles for three types of beings,
[2600] The three vows for three types of practitioners.
[2601] Thus explaining vehicles and vows.
[2602] Herein, the vehicle of characteristics:
[2603] Established through causes and conditions,
[2604] Gradually traversed over countless aeons.
[2605] Thus explaining the vehicle of characteristics.
[2606] Herein, the vehicle of mantra:
[2607] Established through the methods of channels, winds, and drops,
[2608] Traversed swiftly through profound skillful means.
[2609] Thus explaining the vehicle of mantra.
[2610] Herein, the vehicle of Ati:
[2611] Established through directly recognizing the nature,
[2612] Instantaneous liberation without gradual stages.
[2613] Thus explaining the vehicle of Ati.
[2614] Herein, distinguishing the vehicles:
[2615] By method, by view, by conduct, by effect.
[2616] Thus explaining the distinctions.
[2617] Thus the explanation of the common vehicle is completed.
