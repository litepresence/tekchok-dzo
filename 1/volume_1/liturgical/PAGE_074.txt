[2684] [2581] Herein, explaining the three vows:
[2685] [2582] Prātimokṣa, bodhisattva, and mantra vows.
[2686] [2583] Thus explaining the three vows in brief.
[2687] [2584] Herein, Prātimokṣa vows:
[2688] [2585] Eight types: bhikṣu, bhikṣuṇī, śikṣamāṇā, śrāmaṇera, śrāmaṇerikā,
[2689] [2586] Upāsaka, upāsikā, and one-day upavāsa.
[2690] [2587] Thus explaining Prātimokṣa.
[2691] [2588] Herein, bodhisattva vows:
[2692] [2589] Aspiring and engaging bodhicitta.
[2693] [2590] The twenty root downfalls and forty-six branch faults to be abandoned.
[2694] [2591] Restoring through the four powers.
[2695] [2592] Thus explaining bodhisattva vows.
[2696] [2593] Herein, mantra vows:
[2697] [2594] The fourteen root downfalls and eight thick branches.
[2698] [2595] Samaya of body, speech, and mind.
[2699] [2596] Restoring through recitation and meditation.
[2700] [2597] Thus explaining mantra vows.
[2701] [2598] Herein, the summary of vehicles and vows:
[2702] [2599] The three vehicles for three types of beings,
[2703] [2600] The three vows for three types of practitioners.
[2704] [2601] Thus explaining vehicles and vows.
[2705] [2602] Herein, the vehicle of characteristics:
[2706] [2603] Established through causes and conditions,
[2707] [2604] Gradually traversed over countless aeons.
[2708] [2605] Thus explaining the vehicle of characteristics.
[2709] [2606] Herein, the vehicle of mantra:
[2710] [2607] Established through the methods of channels, winds, and drops,
[2711] [2608] Traversed swiftly through profound skillful means.
[2712] [2609] Thus explaining the vehicle of mantra.
[2713] [2610] Herein, the vehicle of Ati:
[2714] [2611] Established through directly recognizing the nature,
[2715] [2612] Instantaneous liberation without gradual stages.
[2716] [2613] Thus explaining the vehicle of Ati.
[2717] [2614] Herein, distinguishing the vehicles:
[2718] [2615] By method, by view, by conduct, by effect.
[2719] [2616] Thus explaining the distinctions.
[2720] [2617] Thus the explanation of the common vehicle is completed.
