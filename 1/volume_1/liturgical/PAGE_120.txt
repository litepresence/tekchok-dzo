4488. From nature: basis and essence.

4489. Three quintessences, though designated with five different names,

4490. Are not beyond one meaning-essence.

4491. From that itself:

4492. From the distinction of the single basis's essence,

4493. Are called method and nature tantra.

4494. Though they appear merely as designations,

4495. The essence: single basis, empty nature,

4496. Are not different, have no variation.

4497. For example, a person—

4498. If many names exist,

4499. However called, appears so,

4500. Meaning unchanged, likewise.

First:

4507. The self-arisen rigpa's tantra: shown deep and vast as an ocean of expressions.

4508. The vajra-sattva heart-mirror tantra: shown clear as the sun.

4509. The great dzogchen tantra of lion's power: shown overwhelming all lower vehicles.

4510. The glorious letterless tantra of the mountain-king: shown as the unchanging dharmatā.

4511. The beautiful-auspicious tantra of the wheel: shown cutting the wheel of delusion.

4512. The suddenly-sounding tantra of the key: shown opening all scriptural doors.

4513. The universal-goodness mind-mirror tantra of the sword: shown cutting wrong places.

4514. The blazing-lamp tantra: shown making the lamp's appearance empty yet bright.

4515. The jewel-spread tantra of gold-purity: shown as gold adorned with turquoise.

4516. The sun-and-moon-joining tantra of the mother-child connection: shown clearly indicating with faithful, definite words.

4517. The introduction-adorning tantra of the mirror: shown applying meaning to the example of images within a mirror.

4518. The pearl-garland tantra: shown as instruction collected like a beautiful pearl garland.
