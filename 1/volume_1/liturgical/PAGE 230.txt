Transformation yoga — four.

Great yoga: very yoga ground,
Self-ground definite yoga ground,
Elaboration yoga ground,
Action-free complete yoga ground — four.

Distinguished yoga ground:
Sign ground,
Meaning ground,
Sign-meaning non-different ground,
Non-mixed ground — four.

Realized yoga ground:
Miracle-display ground,
Distinction-realized ground,
Manifest-arisen ground,
Hero family-distinguished ground — four;
Gathered: sixteen grounds.

Thus from sixteen grounds:
First ground's seven knower-miracles:
Emanating own body hundred-thousand,
Those limbs hundred-thousand,
One body hundred-thousand heads,
Hundred-thousand bodies one head,
One head emanating hundred-thousand different bodies, then scattering emanation — five;
Emanating immeasurable different heads and limbs on body — six;
Emanating various births — seven:
Arisen from realizing power-attained ground.

Second ground: opening own belly mouth, showing lung-heart to others;
Burning fire from body's upper, bubbling water from lower;
Cutting limbs, scattering four directions;
Five senses etc. falling to plain;
Throwing mountain through body strength;
Lifting outer great ocean to sky;
Planets, stars etc. falling to plain — seven:
Arisen from seven limbs preceding at empowerment time.
