[192] From the Meeting of Father and Son:
[193] "Great hero, skillful in means,
[194] For the complete ripening of sentient beings,
[195] Though you became Buddha ten million times,
[196] Still you guide, O Teacher,
[197] Demonstrating many Buddhas,"
[198] Thus proclaimed;
[199] From the Flower Ornament:
[200] "Having attained the ocean of enlightenment supreme,
[201] For the complete ripening of beings' ocean vast,
[202] Demonstrating the ocean of bodhicitta generation,
[203] Showing the ocean of conduct undiminished through all time,
[204] Such is the emanation of the Sugatas,"
[205] Thus spoke the Lord.

[208] In the field called "Self-Nature Completely Arranged,"

[211] Dwelt near Buddha Wheel-Well-Conduct, hearing Dharma.
[212] Then passing beyond, born as "Lightning-Light" in Bliss-Intoxicated Island, the brahmin youth Good-Give.
[213] At that time heard Dharma from Buddha Good-Intelligence for five years, and for seven years abiding in single-pointed samadhi,
[214] At seventy-five years demonstrated manifest complete Buddhahood upon Jewel-Precious-Heap-Arranged Mountain peak,
[215] Abiding in dharmakaya's meaning for one great eon,
