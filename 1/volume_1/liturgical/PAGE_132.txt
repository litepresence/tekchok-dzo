That also: each consonant with vowel letters sixteen's sound nearby abiding.

Letter's form also that's sound whatever arisen, stacked—for example:

KA KĀ—

Within, life A existing: called A Ā.

From Symbolic Expression:

A is all letters' supreme—

Within, arisen, birthless.

Thus:

KI KĪ is Ī.

KU KŪ is Ū.

KE KAI is E AI.

KO KAU is O AU.

KRA KRA is RA RA.

RI RĪ and LI LĪ: life A existing, KI KĪ gathered—

AṂ AḤ is KAṂ KAḤ called.

Those root letters: double-stacked KKA said is great.

GHA JHA DHA BHA: said like thick.

Triple-stacked and five-stacked, etc.:

Called "sphere's single letter."

Suitable ones read as one.

Unsuitable ones read separately.

Channels in thus existing: body, speech, mind, wisdom five, etc., in different spheres.

Letters and similar: light five's appearance having—just brush-strokes, essence white-red, in wind's center, self-clear existing.
