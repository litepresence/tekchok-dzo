[2526] Sutra, Discipline, Abhidharma—Great Vehicle: all Word gathered in three baskets, mantras being manifest great—Sutra, Discipline, Abhidharma, those three equal: twenty-one thousand; eighty-four thousand dharma heaps. From those divided, regarding each antidote taming beings' thought accumulations, extensively many, inconceivable, transcending expression.

[2538] Here, from categorizing vehicles as nine: first, explaining hearer vehicle—five aspects: Essence: mind desiring sole self-liberation from samsara, method realizing person-selflessness. Definition: hearing dharma from teacher, proclaiming to others, depending on teachers, accomplishing last-existence enlightenment: hearer. Purpose: definitely exiting samsara. Effect: obtaining foe-destroyer with or without remainder. Division: Sautrantika and Vaibhasika, two.

[2549] Vaibhasika: gross relative truth appearing gathered as one, like yak-horn and conch; ultimately: part-possessed dust-atoms, separate pieces. Knowledge stream: relative. Instant: ultimate, partless, like pot-water. From aggregates, elements, sense-bases, gross appearance surrounding fine dust, with intermediate. Accepting person-selflessness, yet accepting dharma-self as dust-atoms and instants. Those knowing four truths' dharmas as cause-effect abandon-accept: cause, origin, cessation, focusing on four condition types; knowledge type: suffering, known; origin: to abandon; path: to rely upon; cessation: to obtain—method to know; suffering: to abandon.
