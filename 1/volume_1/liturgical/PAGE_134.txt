KKA GHA JHA ḌHA DHA BHA—

Abodes thirty-four letters all gathered shown.

Those meanings joined, definite:

FROM RIGPA RANGSHAR:

A HO, nearby retinue, listen!

I explain well—hold in mind.

Buddhas all's intention is:

Words and letters not existing—

Words and letters appear like.

Yet words' meaning greatly liberates.

That also gathered in letters' syllables.

Those meanings explained thus:

Cause producing all letters:

KA: perfectly complete, famous.
