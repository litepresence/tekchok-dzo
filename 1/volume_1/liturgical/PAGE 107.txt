[3959] First:

[3960] Revealing and emerging view and conduct, if meditation is concealed,

[3961] That nature in union without beginning or end, saṃsāra-nirvāṇa's beginning and end are cut.

[3962] That nature's two not different, in bindu without outside or inside,

[3963] Without dependence on characteristic, causeless openness.

[3964] If conduct is concealed and the other two are revealed,

[3965] Dwelling in emptiness, through emptiness pure, thus called.

[3966] If view is concealed and the other two are revealed, through grasping-free knowledge directionlessly arising,

[3967] Object-free awareness concept-free liberated, thus called.

[3968] Second, concealing two, one is revealed:

[3969] Meditation revealed, aim-free awareness aim-free arises, causeless result dwells in itself, thus called.

[3970] Conduct revealed, body cast to precipice, mind sent to dharmatā, thus called.

[3971] View revealed, mind delivered to emptiness, body and speech brought to vital point, thus called.
