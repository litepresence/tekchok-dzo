[1370-1374]

RECOGNITION:
Mutual gazing, touching, laughing desire arose, beings increasing greatly from womb birth. At this time merely self-clear light as food, clothing, tree use, and magical wish-fulfilling cow and horse existed. Then merit decreased, anger arose, mutual killing karma, previous qualities subsided. All increasing from gazing, laughing, touching, traveling four inches above ground without touching.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Human devolution

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- lta reg dgod = gazing, touching, laughing
- rang gsal = self-clear light
- bsod nams chung = merit decreased

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Devolution narrative
- Self-sustaining light
- Karma-based change
