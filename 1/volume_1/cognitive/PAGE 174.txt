[6939-6954] The purpose of this preparation involves ripening the body into the deity's form, the speech into the natural sound of ali kali, and the mind into emptiness beyond conception. The intense aspiration to definitely obtain the desired empowerment produces signs of accomplished masters appearing in dreams, giving teachings and prophecies. The result of definite necessity establishes that through serving the teacher and supplicating in this manner, all desired qualities arise like wish-fulfilling jewels and trees, turning one back from the battle of samsara.

[6955-6987] The fourth aspect of how empowerment is conferred presents five topics: benefits of empowerment, faults of not receiving empowerment, locations for empowerment, timings for empowerment, and the sequence of the ganacakra celebration. The benefits section cites Rang shar presenting the essential qualities: unimpeded entry into great wisdom light, appearance shown as friendly companion, and all activities accomplished. The Rin po che'i rgyud citation establishes that completing the empowerment ritual with a teacher who has abandoned desires definitely leads to becoming a great vajra holder in this life. The gsang snying citation emphasizes that empowerment with the five essences makes one a victorious one's son with no lower rebirths and attainment of the lord of high liberation.
