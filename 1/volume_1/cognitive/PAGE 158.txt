[6197-6261] The Sgra thal 'gyur citation presents the near-causes of teaching destruction through specific individuals with particular physical characteristics who will appear in various regions. The detailed descriptions of their physical marks, locations, and the durations of darkness they cause structure the prophetic narrative. Each figure is associated with specific directions and intervals of sixty years of darkness. The systematic enumeration of these obstacles to the teaching establishes the pattern of cyclical emergence and withdrawal that characterizes the tantric presentation of historical process.
