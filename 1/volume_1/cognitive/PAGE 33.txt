[1140-1145]

RECOGNITION:
Formless realm expansion. Infinite consciousness, sphere, wheel entry. Knowing merely as self-grasping of knower. No gathering entity.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Formless realms

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- rnam shes mtha' yas = infinite consciousness
- 'khor lo 'jug pa = wheel entry
- formless = no form, only consciousness

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Four formless realms
- Consciousness-based
- Highest samsaric realms
