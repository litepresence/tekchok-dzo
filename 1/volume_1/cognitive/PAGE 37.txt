RECOGNITION:
Brahma reading before them. Before them reciters exist, thus named. Body 1 yojana, lifespan 1 intermediate kalpa. Below, Siktaka jewel space, cut from above called Brahma. Practicing Brahma conduct through virtue, thus named. Body half, lifespan half intermediate kalpa.
