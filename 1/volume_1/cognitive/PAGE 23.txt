RECOGNITION:
Ocean wave-like arising. From upper sky countless wind particles falling like snow in space. Becomes 100,000 yojanas. Above that "earth-supporting space" (gseng byed sa'i nam mkha') blue like turquoise. Space particles falling, gathering through natural action.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric cosmology / Sequential formation

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- dpag tshad 'bum = 100,000 yojanas
- gseng byed sa'i nam mkha' = earth-supporting space
- rang bzhin gyi byed pa = natural action

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Yojana = traditional Indian measure
- Sequential element formation
- Natural action, not creator
