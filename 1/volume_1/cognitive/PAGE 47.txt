From four great kings to great Brahma similarly. Water destruction: clouds gathered rain fell flooded, below wind maṇḍala supported not sank. Beings there all died. Then fire destruction. Wind destruction: wind maṇḍala upper and lower upper and lower very grinding one above one struck completely destroyed. Last: then container-contents all destroyed. That abiding destruction empty three. Uncommon vehicle here 60 intermediate kalpas equal 180, called first duration kalpa. 180 duration kalpas two intermediate kalpas called second. Intermediate kalpa number infinite said. Thus vessel-content four eons measure elaborated. Now explaining abode vessel-content taming beings retinue.
