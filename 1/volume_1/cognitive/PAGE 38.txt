RECOGNITION:

Buddhist cosmology / Heaven realm

- rgyang grags bzhi gsum = 4,500 fathoms
- dpag bsam gyi shing = wish-fulfilling tree

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Time dilation in heavens
- Wish-fulfilling tree = cosmological feature
