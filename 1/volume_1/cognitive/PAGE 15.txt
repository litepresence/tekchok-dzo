RECOGNITION:
Enumeration of third and fourth nirmanakāya manifestations. Third: warm-moist gathering light-cluster place, teacher Jigs pa skyob pa'i yid, retinue 200,000 ḍākiṇīs, teaching five tantras of three gates, lifespan 100 million years. Fourth: retinue Dong sprugs and Rma bya mjing bsnol, teaching four elements exhaustion tantras, lifespan 100,000 years.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric cosmology / Nirmanakāya enumeration

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- drod gsher 'dus pa = warm-moist gathering
- rgyud lnga = five tantras
- 'byung bzhi zad pa = four elements exhaustion

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Enumeration continues nirmanakāya manifestations
- Lifespans indicate kalpa variations
- Specific tantra titles
