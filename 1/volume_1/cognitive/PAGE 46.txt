[1479-1483]

RECOGNITION:
Countless passed, thunder-decreasing not yet far called Not-yet-far. In between beings' merit appearance from bliss, virtue, Buddha, Dharma. Twenty eons decreasing, standing eon. Twenty eons when humans first appear, increasing eon.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Eon progression

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- ma thog ring mo = not-yet-far eon
- 'phel 'grib = increase-decrease
- mi snyed = first humans

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Eon stages
- Merit-appearance correlation
- Cyclic cosmology
