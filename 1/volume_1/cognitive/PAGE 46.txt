[1479-1512]
Countless passed thunder-decreasing not yet far called ma thog ring mo. In between beings merit appearance from bliss, virtue, Buddha, Dharma. Twenty eons decreasing, standing eon. Twenty eons when humans first appear, increasing eon. Similarly from four great kings to great Brahma. Container destruction: one sun sky extremely hot burning grass trees. Two suns dried rivers ponds. Three suns dried great rivers. Four suns dried lakes. Five suns dried ocean 100 yojanas. Six suns great ocean completely dried. Seven suns Meru etc. mountains smoke fire kindling burning. Then fire maṇḍala one below one burning. Beings there all died, wind kissed fire one place gathered, everything burned ashes dust also not remained, completely empty like space.
