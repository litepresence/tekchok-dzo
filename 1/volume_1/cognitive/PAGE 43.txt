[1393-1397]

RECOGNITION:
Body brilliance power possessing, sense door engaging desire enjoying. Some throwing sticks, clubs, earth, stones, shame arising. Propping with wood and stones. From nectar rain falling, roofs thatched. Early homes called Roof-houses, Round-houses arose.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Human civilization

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- dbang po sgo = sense doors
- ngo tsha = shame
- bdud rtsi = nectar

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Civilization development
- Shame as moral marker
- Housing evolution
