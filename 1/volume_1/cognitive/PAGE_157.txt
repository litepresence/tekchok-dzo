[6139-6196] The continuation of the prophecy moves through the seven periods with increasing specificity. The holder Blo gros bzang po and the five hundred fifty years represent the current period according to the text's reckoning. The distinction between those who remain on this earth and those who retreat to khecara realms preserves the view of simultaneous manifestation in multiple dimensions. The citation from Ati bkod pa chen po provides scriptural authority for the specific predictions about the degenerate age, including the shortening of lifespans, internal disputes among practitioners, and the eventual disappearance of the teachings into non-manifestation.
