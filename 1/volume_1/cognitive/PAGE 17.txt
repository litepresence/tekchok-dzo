Body inner and secret four activities. Inner four: abandoning retinue and enjoyment, best horse, abandoning vehicle, going to Bodhi essence, opening light rays, transforming weapon rain to flowers—all intending abandonment. Secret four: intending final teaching samādhi taming, doing asceticism, samādhi sound hundred-thousand twenty in Gser ldan mountain in single moment manifesting, always abiding in rin po che za ma tog samādhi, vajra-like samādhi attaining enlightenment. Detailed samādhi names require technical precision.
