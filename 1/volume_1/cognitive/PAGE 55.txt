[1782-1787]

RECOGNITION:
Beyond view. Beyond extreme means not grasping self in all existents. Beyond view means inexpressible.

VIEW REGISTER:
Madhyamaka / Transcendence

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- lta ba las 'das pa = beyond view
- mtha' las 'das pa = beyond extreme
- brjod du med pa = inexpressible

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Madhyamaka influence
- Extreme transcendence
- View negation
