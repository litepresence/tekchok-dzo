[1059-1066]

RECOGNITION:
Equal height like four gardens. Tree in north-east called Yong 'dus brtol, roots 5 yojanas deep, 1,000 branches rising like parasols spreading, circumference 50 yojanas, jewel nets, divine clothes hanging. White stone slab like armonic jewel, 50 yojanas, divine throne, flower rain.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / World tree

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- yongs 'dus brtol = cosmic tree name
- dpag tshad = yojana measurements
- armo ni = armonic jewel

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Cosmic tree = axis mundi
- Precise measurements = symbolic precision
- Divine assembly place
