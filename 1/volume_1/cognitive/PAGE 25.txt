RECOGNITION:
Seven golden mountains enumerated from inside outward: Gñā' shing 'dzin, Gshol mdā' 'dzin, Seng ldeng can, Blta na sdug, Rta rna, Rnam 'dud, Mu khyud 'dzin. All rising from water boundaries upward.
