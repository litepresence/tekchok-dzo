RECOGNITION:
Verse description of cosmological arrangement. Southern-east-left world direction. Beyond six world realms. Sound called "Thal ba'i sgra" (Ash sound). Protected by savior 'Phan. Raised by victory banner, umbrella. Flower rain, scent, beauty forms surrounding teacher and teaching. Bliss play arising.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric cosmology / Directional symbolism

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- drug 'das = beyond six
- thal ba'i sgra = Ash sound = cosmological marker
- rgyal mtshan gdugs = victory banner and umbrella

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Directional cosmology
- Royal symbols = Buddha as supreme sovereign
- Verse form = summary condensation
