[1321-1328]

RECOGNITION:
Light lotus extremely beautiful, 1,000 petals, wide spacious cavity, miraculously born. Three gazes seeing beings. Above-born divine son Sun Radiant and Moon Stainless born. Method and wisdom depending, arising as teacher taming beings sign. At that time on each lotus petal Buddha and corresponding cause came.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Buddha manifestation

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- 'dab ma stong = 1,000 petals
- gzigs stangs rnam pa gsum = three gazes
- thabs dang shes rab = method and wisdom

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Lotus cosmology
- Three gazes = compassion display
- Method-wisdom union
