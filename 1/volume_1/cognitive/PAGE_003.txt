Definitive determination of place specified as luminous-clarity vajra-essence teaching. Characterized as unsurpassable mind-secret of all Tathāgatas, the great completely-complete vajra place of wondrous marvelous self-nature. Establishes tantric authority through epistemological definiteness. Gnas nges pa denotes technical term for definitive specification of ground. Thugs kyi gsang ba marks innermost dimension requiring transmission. Ngo mtshar rmad du byung ba functions as standard tantric epithet of wonder. The self-nature as marvelous signals primordial perfection without contrivance.

[61-64]
Explicit citation from Guhyagarbha Tantra. Threefold cosmogonic presentation: primordial protector's manifest enlightenment and field arrangement, peaceful attainment in this field through Vajradhara's emanation, Dharma-wheel turning through emanation-body elaboration. Sequential trikāya emanation structure. Guhyagarbha serves as supreme Nyingma tantric authority warranting this cosmogony. Threefold manner denotes process description, not ontological assertion. Trikāya emanation sequence follows dharmakāya to sambhogakāya to nirmāṇakāya.

[65-71]
Detailed cosmogony narrative. First stage before all, without division of saṃsāra-nirvāṇa. Complete sovereign teacher Samantabhadra. Self-arising wisdom of expanse from primordial basis arising as basis-appearance. Moment of transcending basis recognizing self-appearance. Three self-arising dharmas spontaneously accomplishing precious cavity. Primordial exhaustion-place of great primordial purity. Youth vase body field conquering ground seized. Abandonment-realization qualities complete, becoming Buddha in dharmakāya nature, abiding in inner clarity. This presents primordial enlightenment preceding all manifestation. Gzhi snang as basis-appearance signals spontaneous display from ground. Youth vase body denotes form of spontaneous presence. Inner clarity marks dharmakāya abiding mode.

Sambhogakāya field description pervaded by five wisdoms. Self-appearing spontaneous mandala to limits of sky. Five families as individual self-appearances beyond trainee objects. Five wisdoms manifest as five Buddha aspects. Sambhogakāya field appears as spontaneous display, not constructed visualization. Beyond trainee objects signals transcendence of all conceptual practice. The field extends to nam mkha'i mtha' klas pa, sky's limit.

[73-75]
Compassion narrative. Long after primordial awakening, from basis-appearance potency seeing beings move like dreams without cause of confusion. Compassion arising for beings' benefit. Field arrangement from self-appearing enjoyment-body appearance aspect. Five-family Buddhas as great ocean bodies pervading to sky limits. Temporal gap de nas ring zhig na separates primordial enlightenment from compassionate response. 'Khrul rgyu med without cause of confusion is key Dzogchen point. Compassion arises from recognition, not from suffering. Sambhogakāya display functions as compassionate response to non-existent confusion.
