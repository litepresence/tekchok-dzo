[842-849]

RECOGNITION:
Form and color like golden wheel with thousand spokes. Self-nature movement power arising, churning earth-water maṇḍalas. Like butter from milk, dust impurity becomes gold, heavy sinks becoming ground, clear rises becoming Mount Meru.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric alchemy / Cosmological formation

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- gser gyi 'khor lo = golden wheel
- 'o ma = milk = transformation simile
- ri rab = Mount Meru = cosmic mountain

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Mount Meru = cosmic center
- Measurements: precise but symbolic
- Alchemical transformation symbolism
