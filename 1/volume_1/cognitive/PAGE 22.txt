[781-788]

RECOGNITION:
Wind formation description. "Suppressing-appearance wind" (rnam par srub byed kyi rlung) arising like mouse breath, clouding sky like mist. Various vajra cross shape center marked with YAM. Four wind goddesses holding wind banners. Green wind element like Indra's form, vast and thick.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric cosmology / Element formation

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- rnam par srub byed = suppressing-appearance
- YAM = seed syllable of wind
- rlung gi lha mo = wind goddesses

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Seed syllables mark elements
- Element formation: subtle to gross
- Cosmological symbolism
