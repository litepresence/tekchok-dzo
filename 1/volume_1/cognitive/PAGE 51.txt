[1653-1658]

RECOGNITION:
Detailed enumeration of six consciousness elements. Eye consciousness holding name and color. Ear consciousness accomplishing grasping. Nose consciousness grasping others. Tongue consciousness experiencing objects. Mind consciousness pervading all existent and non-existent. Body consciousness gathering all objects.

VIEW REGISTER:
Abhidharma / Consciousness analysis

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- mig gi rnam par shes pa = eye consciousness
- yid kyi rnam par shes pa = mind consciousness
- lus kyi rnam par shes pa = body consciousness

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Six sense consciousnesses standard
- Each has specific function
- Abhidharma framework
