[955-962]

RECOGNITION:
Four seasons' sequential processes: summer rains, autumn harvest, flower ripening, winter cold, spring warmth. Four wind goddesses performing these functions. These beings superior to common sentient beings, accomplished through collective karma.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Seasonal deities

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- dbyar char 'bab pa = summer rains
- dgun bsil = winter cold
- spyi mthun gyi las = collective karma

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Seasonal changes as karmic result
- Wind goddesses = environmental deities
- Collective karma = shared experience
