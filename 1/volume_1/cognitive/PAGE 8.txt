[219-221]

RECOGNITION:
Detailed sambhogakāya cosmological architecture. Sixty perfect abodes in light-point basis ('od kyi thig le). Immeasurable Buddha bodies appearing as self-appearance (rang snang). Five Buddha families' light rays ('od zer) from tongues as six self-arising syllables (rang byung gi yi ge drug). Effortless compassion (mdzad pa med pa'i thugs rje) pervading directions.

VIEW REGISTER:
Dzogchen view - Self-arising display / Effortless compassion

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- Line 219: 'od kyi thig le = light-point = bindu
- Line 220: rang snang = self-appearing, not external
- Line 221: rang byung gi yi ge drug = six self-arisen syllables

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Self-appearance = not constructed by mind
- Effortless compassion = thugs rje without intentional agent
- Six syllables = natural resonance of dharmatā

Tantric/Dzogchen view - Five families as unity

- Great Perfection body = transcends individual forms
- Unity of diversity in ground

VIEW REGISTER:
Dzogchen view - Non-action / Self-arising ground

TRANSLATION NOTES:

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Negations = freedom from fabrication
- Self-arising = spontaneous presence
- King of rigpa = sovereign awareness
