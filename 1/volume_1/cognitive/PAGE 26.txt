[916-923]

RECOGNITION:
Enumeration of lower and higher realms. Lower realms first: hells below Gang ri ti se (Kailash), 20,000 yojanas down, eight hot hells including Reviving, eight cold hells including Blisters. Nearby exist without fixed location in four elements.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Six realms

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- gangs ri ti se = Mount Kailash
- tsha dmyal brgyad = eight hot hells
- grang dmyal brgyad = eight cold hells

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Standard Buddhist cosmology
- Mount Kailash as reference point
- Hells as spatial locations
