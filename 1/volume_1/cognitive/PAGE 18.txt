[565-573]

RECOGNITION:
Conclusion of activity enumeration as single Vajradhara complete qualities. Called Victor's intention transmission sequence (rgyal ba dgongs pas brgyud pa'i rim pa). Single Vajradhara appearing at different times, contained in one intention or tantra. Future Good Eon guides different emanations but single essence Vajradhara nature.

VIEW REGISTER:
Dzogchen view - Single essence / Multiple emanations

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- dgongs pas brgyud pa = mind transmission
- bskal bzangs = Good Eon = Bhadrakalpa
- ngo bo gcig pa = single essence

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Single essence = multiple emanations unified
- Mind transmission = dgongs brgyud
- Future Buddhas = same essence
