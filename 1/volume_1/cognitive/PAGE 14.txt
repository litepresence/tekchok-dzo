RECOGNITION:
Detailed analysis of emanation-body supports and dwelling places. Mind support vajra (thugs kyi rten rdo rje) as wish-fulfilling jewel curing beings' diseases. Speech support lotus at navel level with vowels-consonants. Body dwelling in various locations, not fixed to one earth.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric cosmology / Emanation supports

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- rten = support/base
- sman g.yog = curing diseases
- A-li KA-li = Sanskrit vowels and consonants

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Three supports = body-speech-mind
- Not fixed location = pervasive presence
- Symbolic anatomy
