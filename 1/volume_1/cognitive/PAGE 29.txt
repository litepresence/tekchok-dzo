[1041-1048]

RECOGNITION:
Divine sons and daughters, song and music filling. Beautiful common city, various jewel pavilions, 4,450 yojanas each face. Inside ground and streets beautiful, flower rain in four seasons. Seven jewel walls, filled with flowing water, lotus flowers, bird songs. Jewel boats present.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Heaven architecture

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- dpag tshad bzhi bzhi stong lnga brgya = 4,450 yojanas
- rin po che sna bdun = seven jewels
- gru = boats

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Measurements precise but symbolic
- Architecture = pure land features
- Transition between realms
