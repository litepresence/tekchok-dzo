RECOGNITION:
Nirmāṇakāya emanation analysis. Twelve emanations (sprul sku bcu gnyis) from blessing of Vajradhara complete qualities. Body forms arisen from 101 precious causes without maker. Three emanations of body-speech-mind indestructible. Blessing self-arisen, immeasurable miracles and qualities. Contact liberation activity.

VIEW REGISTER:
Tantric view - Twelve emanations / Nirmāṇakāya

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- sprul sku bcu gnyis = twelve emanations
- byin rlabs las rang shar = self-arisen from blessing
- 'phrin las mnga' ba = possessing liberating activity

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Twelve emanations = standard set
- Self-arisen = not constructed
- Liberation through contact = blessing power
