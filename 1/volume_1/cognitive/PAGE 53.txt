[1704-1709]

RECOGNITION:
Ethics aggregate for purifying stains. Liberation aggregate beyond all conceptualization. Sense-bases: what arises and expands from what. Six objects like form for eye.

VIEW REGISTER:
Abhidharma / Sense-base analysis

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- tshul khrims kyi phung po = ethics aggregate
- skye mched = sense-bases
- 'dzin byed = apprehender

PRESERVATION FLAGS:

- Dual arising
