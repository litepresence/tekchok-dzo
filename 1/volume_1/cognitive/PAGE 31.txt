[1083-1090]

RECOGNITION:
Higher world divine beings: four wind goddesses, many water goddesses dancing, four powerful nāga sons. Padma's speech sound pervading all directions. Formless potential consciousness awakened from latency. Emanation body of samādhi consciousness established. Self-appearance pervading, not lacking body aspect.

VIEW REGISTER:
Buddhist cosmology / Divine emanation

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- rlung gi lha mo bzhi = four wind goddesses
- nam mkha' nas = from space
- rnam par shes pa = consciousness

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Divine creation narrative
- Sound as creative force
- Samādhi emanation body
