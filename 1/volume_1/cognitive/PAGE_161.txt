The explanation of how emanated images pervade the six realms according to beings' faith and the teacher's blessing establishes the universal accessibility of the teachings. The actual teachings of the two kayas—dharmakaya and sambhogakaya—manifest as the teacher's own pure appearance, existing eternally in the pure realms. The differentiation between common vehicles and uncommon secret essence vehicles maintains the hierarchical structure of the path while affirming the unique efficacy of this specific transmission.
