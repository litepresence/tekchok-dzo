Those who deviate from partial similarity to insiders are called tirthikas, deviating from similarity to Buddhists through mistaken Buddhist philosophical positions, merely flesh. Those who think all appearance is mind's magical display, teaching all is magical display. Some say these deluded appearances are true, ultimate is nonexistent. Some, anger arising, demons are finished, son of good family should dwell in anger. Some say mind is self-arisen, self-liberated, therefore uncorrectable. Some say deity body meditation alone is sufficient. Some, being tirthikas, deviate from tirthika debate speech. Some say this alone is permanent, therefore nothing to do.
