[6488-6511] The clarification explains that these six hundred and forty thousand represent the emanations of the first lord, with actually nineteen hundred thousand buddhas appearing for disciples. The description of this establishment as existing in the heart-center of Vairocana within the pure realm of Samantabhadra presents a mandala structure that defies ordinary spatial conception. The citations from Uttaratantra and Dkon mchog brtsegs pa establish scriptural authority for the inconceivability of buddha activity, emphasizing that even tenth-level bodhisattvas cannot fathom these dimensions, much less ordinary intellect.

[6512-6546] The enumeration of those liberated in Jambudvipa through this secret essence teaching presents specific numbers: sixty years of age, one hundred thousand liberations through natural dissolution of phenomena, five hundred with the mark of the dge sbyong gzugs, and so forth. The distinction between those liberated in this life and those in the intermediate state requires precise terminology to preserve the technical distinctions. The citation from Bkod pa chen po provides the scriptural basis for these enumerations, establishing the teaching as the heart of all results.
