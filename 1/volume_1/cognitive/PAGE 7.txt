Scriptural citation from Meeting of Father and Son. Great hero skillful means, for completely ripening sentient beings, becoming victorious one, appearing as Buddha billions of times, still you guide, showing many Buddhas. Sutra proof of emanation principle: Buddhas appear multiple times while remaining essentially unchanged.

[200-206]
Scriptural citation from Avataṃsaka. Having attained enlightenment-ocean, for ripening sentient-being-ocean, showing bodhicitta-ocean generation, showing non-decreasing conduct-ocean at all times. Thus Sugatas' emanations. The ocean metaphor signals vastness and depth. Non-decreasing conduct maintains continuous activity despite enlightenment.

[207-210]
Historical narrative introduction. Therefore primordial protector, thinking to tame this field, emanated manner of generating mind. Referring to Good Eon, immeasurable eons before, field called naturally arranged, Buddha called Great Beautiful Flower, offered golden vajra, first mind generation supreme bodhicitta. Specific historical reference grounding emanation theory in narrative. Bskal pa bzang po as Good Eon frames temporal context.

Second birth narrative. Then second birth in north called Wind-Movement Arrangement, became householder Legs-sbyin's son called Jewel-Garland. Near Buddha called Wheel-Beautiful-Practice, heard Dharma. Then transferred, born in Bliss-Intoxicated Island as son called Lightning-Light, brahmin youth called Legs-byin. Sequential rebirths demonstrating progressive cultivation. Geographic names signal cosmological scope.

[215-218]
Enlightenment narrative. Then for five years heard Dharma from Buddha dGe-ba'i-blo-gros, seven years single-pointed samādhi equipoise. Seventy-five years on peak of Jewel-Pile-Arrangement Mountain showed manner of completely manifestly becoming Buddha. From dharmakāya intention abiding one great eon, victorious one sambhogakāya ornament and color wheel self-arisen. From light rays' projection and gathering, thirty-six tantras self-arisen. The thirty-six tantras enumerate specific teachings arising spontaneously. Self-arisen signals non-human composition.
