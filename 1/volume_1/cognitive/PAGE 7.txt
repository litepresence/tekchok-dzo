RECOGNITION:
Citations from Yabsras mjal ba and Phal po che sūtras. Heroic great skillful means (thabs mkhas) manifesting as many Buddhas for maturing beings (sems can yongs su smin pa'i phyir). Skillful display of Buddhahood while remaining as guide. Emanation as pedagogical display.

VIEW REGISTER:
Mahāyāna view - Emanation pedagogy / Skillful means

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- Line 192: yab sras mjal ba = Father-Son Meeting Sūtra
- Line 200: phal po che = Avataṃsaka Sūtra
- byed pa'i phyir = for the purpose of maturing

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Sūtra citations establish scriptural authority
- Skillful means = pedagogical adaptation to capacity
- Emanation not separate from ultimate reality

VIEW REGISTER:
Dzogchen view - Primordial bodhicitta / Emanation biography

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- Line 208: bskal pa bzang po = Bhadrakalpa = Good Eon
- Line 210: gser gyi rdo rje = golden vajra offering
- thugs dang po byang chub mchog tu sems bskyed = first mind generation

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Emanation biography = pedagogical narrative framework
- Bodhicitta generation = apparent beginning of path
- Tantric symbolism (vajra offering)
