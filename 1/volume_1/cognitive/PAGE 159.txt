[6262-6295] The prophecy continues through the remaining periods, specifying the holders and their patrons through the seven cycles. The holder Shakya dzI ka appears in the central region, maintaining the teaching for ten years like a blazing lamp. The completion of the seven cycles of waxing and waning leads to the teaching supports departing for the southern realm of Mdzes ldan bkod pa after the human lifespan reaches ten years. The subsequent passage through other world systems—Yangs pa skyob pa and Gtsug phud dbyangs yangs—with their respective durations establishes the continuation of the teaching beyond this world system's dissolution.
