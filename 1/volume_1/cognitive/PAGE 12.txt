[350-365]
Completion of Sambhogakāya activities. Face union purity perfecting all-knowing Dharma enjoyment. Four outer mind activities: non-composite samādhi, pure wisdom vision samādhi, dharmatā purity samādhi, samādhi of suchness. Four inner: mirror-like wisdom, equality, discriminating, all-accomplishing. Four secret: dharmatā clarity-emptiness non-dual, appearance-emptiness non-dual, awareness-emptiness non-dual, object-mind non-dual. Non-dual formulations are structural unities, not sequential stages. Clarity-emptiness dbyer med as key Dzogchen formulation signals primordial unity.

[366-380]
Nirmāṇakāya emanation analysis. Twelve emanations from blessing of Vajradhara complete qualities. Body forms arisen from one hundred one precious causes without maker. Three emanations of body-speech-mind indestructible. Blessing self-arisen, immeasurable miracles and qualities. Contact liberation activity. Twelve emanations as standard set in tantric cosmology. Self-arisen negates constructed nature. Liberation through contact demonstrates blessing power.

[381-397]
Detailed analysis of emanation-body supports and dwelling places. Mind support vajra as wish-fulfilling jewel curing beings' diseases, fulfilling desires, connecting to liberation. Speech support self-arisen syllable, contact and hearing sound alone liberates. Written book holding also effortless liberation activity. Body contact liberates body into clear light. Three supports located differently according to body-speech-mind teaching times. These dwelling various locations not fixed to one earth.
