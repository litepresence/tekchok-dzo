RECOGNITION:
Completion of Sambhogakāya mind activities. Face union purity (zhal gyi sbyor ba dag pa) perfecting all-knowing Dharma enjoyment. Four outer mind activities: non-composite samādhi (mngon par 'du mi byed pa), pure wisdom vision (ye shes kyi mthong ba dag pa), dharmatā purity (chos nyid rnam par dag pa), samādhi of suchness. Four inner: stainless Dharma, two truths, joy-emptiness, realization. Four secret: dharmatā clarity-emptiness, appearance-emptiness, awareness-emptiness, object-mind non-duality.

VIEW REGISTER:
Dzogchen view - Mind wisdoms / Fourfold non-duality

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- mngon par 'du mi byed pa = non-composite = uncompounded
- gsal stong dbyer med = clarity-emptiness without separation
- Non-dual formulations = structural, not sequential

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Non-dualities = unified, not sequential
- Mind activities = wisdom displays
- Clarity-emptiness = key Dzogchen formulation
