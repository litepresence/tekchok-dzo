You enter and obtain! But obtaining is recognizing. Like finding what was never lost.

Four empowerments ripen each example at lines 8834-8839—field good seed faultless plant result ripen like, knowing field first elaborate seed plant, unelaborate sprout arise, very unelaborate flower shine, utterly unelaborate four body certain result ripen.
