These two substance different not, individually not changing, essence one, aspect different without, yes-not, vow-possessed yes, renunciant yes, two non-contradicting! These objections also...

[10801-10820] *Klong drug pa*: "Essence primordially pure, beings' samsara this not logical! Ignorance stain primordially exhausted, Teacher said Bodhisattva great! Nature spontaneously accomplished, also not-ceasing play appears! Bodhisattva himself again asked: Nature spontaneously accomplished, beings' samsara not logical! Primordially spontaneously accomplished, said beings these! Essence primordially pure, liberated ones also play appear! Bodhisattva also asked: Essence primordially pure, spontaneous presence need not becomes! Also spontaneously accomplished, primordially pure not becomes! Teacher said existence mode..."

Do you see? The debate itself is confusion's play! Emaho!

—Patrul Rinpoche, fireside
