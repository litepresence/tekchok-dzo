[9546-9548] Five perfections. Field and emanation, two—making eight. These are the results of the eight empowerments.

Listen, dear practitioner! Perfection is not something you achieve—it is what you recognize. Five perfections, eight results—all are already present in your own mind.

[9549-9558] The emanation body's teacher, sixth Vajradhara. Abode is Tushita—"Ogmin," different in name only. Teaching is nature Great Perfection. Retinue is worldly and transcendental. Time is when the form-body tames. Field is vast Brahma. From that, emerging clearly: the four continents with Meru, a hundred variations. Dharma taught: actual and reflected images. As what tames whom through emanation—emanation body's self and other's benefit, two perfections, activity, and eight.

[9559-9565] The nature body's teacher: inexpressible, utterly pure. Abode: inexpressible, sign and basis free. Teaching: dust-free, stainless. Retinue: wisdom ocean. Field: great bliss, pure. Dharma: no discrimination. Activity: mind itself transcends mistake—eight.

[9566-9574] The nature of all these, four authentic bodies: The dharmakaya's teacher: nature unaltered. Abode: emptiness, all-pervasive. Teaching: pure taming conduct. Retinue: nature pure. Time: indefinite, various. Dharma: contaminated self-depleted. Through dharma-aggregates gathering—dharmakaya. Activity: acts without self-agent—eight.

[9575-9583] The form-body's teacher: form-appearance without nature, face completely open. Abode: no outer or inner, everywhere inside. Teaching: distinguishes dharma and non-dharma. Retinue: visible and invisible. Time: four concepts, mere words. Field: looking from outside, clear inside. Dharma: concepts basis pure. Through purifying form-atoms and particles—form body. Depleting the karma of those with karma-form—eight.

[9584] Incidentally, the peace body's teacher: self-knowing awareness king, unconditioned, abode citta jewel palace.

Emaho! The kayas are not somewhere else—they are your own body, speech, and mind when recognized!

—Patrul Rinpoche, fireside
