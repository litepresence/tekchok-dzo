[14505-14509] Ta and La at the vajra, upper and lower—press these two, and the bindu's movement stops. The syllable Sha at the vajra tip—pressing these two abandons desire. Listen! When yogic illness strikes the body, repeatedly press and release all these.

[14510-14519] Gathering diseases are extracted from the root by this. Tsa and Tsa are below the navel, right and left. Ta and Ta are at the armpits, right and left. To and To are at the hips, right and left. Ja and Ja are below the navel, right and left. Ta and Ta are at the kidneys, right and left. Do not press these inward—recite and release upward, and the elements become balanced.

[14520-14526] Below the waist, however it may be, reciting these syllables with effort, element diseases certainly dissolve in their own place. Right and left are the key points of the syllables themselves. Hot and cold—white and red colors. These are the colors of the syllables. Other than these, root syllables have no benefit or harm.

[14527-14545] Second, the heart dharma wheel—from four: first, generally showing the numbers. From eight root vessels, thirty-two branch into countless leaf channels. From the wheel location: The dharma wheel's channels—not past thirty-two. Channels of karma and wisdom dwell. Know these! From the heart dharma wheel: Thirty-two major channel leaves, one thousand nine hundred minor channels dwell. From their forms: four gathering channels become twenty-eight. Twenty-eight gathering channels, fourteen awareness channels, five body channels, four light and wisdom channels. The location of these major channels having three wheels as above. The first wheel is as above; the second will be explained here.

[14546-14559] Twenty-five wisdom channels, five gathering channels. Two mind channels. The third wheel will be explained. Five bindu channels, eight prajna channels, two ignorance channels, fifteen gathering channels, five light-ray channels, element channels likewise. Two are empty channels. The first wheel will be explained: five awareness channels, five body channels—each having two. Ten gathering channels, three phlegm channels, two wind channels, two bile channels. The rest are gathering. Four light and wisdom channels are called actual things.

[14560-14581] The second wheel is thus: twenty-five wisdom channels are five dharmadhatu wisdoms. Mirror-like is likewise equal. Five are equality wisdom. Likewise five discriminating, five all-accomplishing. Five gathering channels are the five abiding elements. From two mind channels: wind and consciousness abiding. The third wheel explained: five bindu channels—two conventional bindus, two awareness bindus, one wisdom bindu. Eight prajna channels: two discern, two gather, two liberate, the rest maintain. Two ignorance channels self-place press. Fifteen gathering channels: four phlegm-mind, five delusion-bile, four anger-wind, two desire-gathering. Five light-ray channels: base light-ray, path light-ray one, the rest eye light-ray.

[14582-14605] From the citta jewel palace, three wheels possessing channel leaves ninety-six.

Do not make this into philosophy, dear practitioner! These channels, these wheels, these bindus—they are your very body. Press, recite, release—this is the path of subtle body. Balance the elements, and liberation is at hand.
