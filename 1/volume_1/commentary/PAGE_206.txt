You who purify the three! Lines 8546-8564 describe three struck mudra—great body speech mind three bind liberate, self-liberated great mudra. You strike the seals! But the seals bind and liberate. Like a lock—keeps out, but also defines the space within.

Lotus opening PHAT close at lines 8548-8549—great bliss wind leave abandon key. You open and close! But this directs the flow. Like a faucet—controls the water.

Father vajra opening HRIH close at lines 8550-8556—great bliss essence enjoy. You open and enjoy! But enjoyment is the point. Like eating when hungry—satisfaction.

Three body mind purify at lines 8560-8572—three vajra tip waist draw, root draw mind great bliss expand, navel draw body mind non-dual. You draw up! But drawing up mixes energy and mind. Like stirring a pot—blends the ingredients.

Action mudra at lines 8563-8572—seal three strike bliss, clarity, emptiness three experience. You experience three! But they are one taste. Like sugar, salt, and spice—different, yet one meal.

Thus purify consort channel at lines 8572-8576—release, father bodhicitta release, two time one arise. You release together! But simultaneity is key. Like two instruments playing in unison—harmony.

Channel release samadhi at lines 8575-8578—not-done like bliss not separate, channel released time blood water-yellow not flow, body pain suffering liberate. You know release by signs! But the signs confirm the state. Like smoke indicating fire—evidence.
