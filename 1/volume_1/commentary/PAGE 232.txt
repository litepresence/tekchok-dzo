Listen, you who control elements! Lines 16-18 describe fifth ground—empowered seven skies, arisen meditating emptiness. You empower over skies! But emptiness is the key. Like space holds all—by being empty.

Sixth ground at lines 19-20—empowered seven fires, arisen previous empowerment knowledge. You control fire! But knowledge controls. Like knowing fire—can use or avoid.

Seventh ground at lines 21-25—empowered seven two-from-one grounds, arisen meditating element sequence, empowered seven waters, arisen going preliminaries. You meditate elements! But sequence matters. Like steps in ladder—each leads to next.

Eighth ground at lines 27-28—empowered seven winds, arisen purifying four cakras' winds. You purify winds! But purification reveals. Like cleaning—shows what was there.

Ninth ground at lines 30-31—move seven bodhi-limbs, arisen preliminary union. You move with limbs! But limbs of enlightenment. Like seven branches—support the tree.

Tenth ground at lines 33-34—empowered seven dharmas-to-do, arisen meditating deity. You do dharmas! But doing is practice. Like exercise—builds strength.

Eleventh ground at lines 36-37—emanate seven horse-cloud-speed, arisen beginning effort. You emanate speed! But effort produces. Like wind—moves clouds.
