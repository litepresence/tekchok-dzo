[14003-14009] Pure and impure ripening basis—these are distinctions made by the mind that clings to distinction. Drop the clinging, where is impurity?

[14010-14021] Male right, female left—tantras reverse this. Why? To break your certainty in directions. Certainty is the disease. The medicine is doubt cut through.

[14022] Awakened-mind producing—yes, in the one who hears this. Not in the channel. Never in the channel.

—Patrul Rinpoche, fireside
