PAGE 236 COMMENTARY

Listen, you who attain the four bodies! Lines 9508-9515 describe through yellow Ratnasambhava, red Amitabha, green Amoghasiddhi mandala spontaneous-accomplishment. You accomplish spontaneously! But spontaneous means effortless. Like breathing—happens naturally.

Those mandalas at lines 9511-9515—disciple field activity four emanation, east Abhirati, south Glorious-with-possess, west Padma-assemble, north Action-complete, beings ripen. You ripen beings! But ripening is maturation. Like fruit ripening—natural process.

Two unimaginable divide at lines 9516-9525—body four field perfect-possession five five divide, first Dharmakāya teacher Glorious Samantabhadra, abode dharma-space, teaching Buddha all mind, retinue unconditioned, time reality change-transformation without. You teach all mind! But mind is all. Like space—contains everything.

Perfect-possession five at lines 9526-9532—express without, spontaneous-accomplishment two eight. You possess eight! But eight is two fours. Like directions—four cardinal, four intermediate.

Empowerment four at lines 9530-9531—seed four, result four eight. You have eight! But seed and result are one. Like acorn and oak—same essence, different time.
