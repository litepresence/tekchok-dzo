[9623-9631] Thirty-two marks arisen from thirty-two empowerments! Then prajna's seeing purification—six clairvoyances appear! Tamed prajna's seeing purification's six wisdoms arisen—all words' sign-meaning understood! Trained prajna's seeing purification's six clairvoyances—mind itself enters dharmatā specially born! Seeing purification applied to all—heard prajna's six clairvoyances know all sound and meaning! Conceptual prajna's six know all dharmas' aggregates! Meditation-born prajna's six clairvoyances—dharmas and mind mixed, known like space! Thus thirty are from thirty-two empowerments' transformation—Buddha's tamed thirty-two virtues from seeing arise!

[9632-9642] Then wisdom's seeing purification's six clairvoyances appear—at this time, wisdom's eye dust-free and stainless found! Mirror-like wisdom's seeing purification's six clairvoyances—dharmas' characteristics and intent directly known! Equality's six know samsara-nirvana as non-dual! Discriminating's six know six realms' faculties! All-accomplishing's six know all dharmas effort-free and Buddha-fields! Dharmadhātu's six know all Buddha-virtues! Non-abiding wisdom's six know non-abiding in any dharma! Thus thirty-six wisdom clairvoyances from thirty-two empowerments arise!

Do you see? Each empowerment opens an eye—and that eye is already open! Emaho!

—Patrul Rinpoche, fireside
