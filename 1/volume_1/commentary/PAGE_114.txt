
You who would know the tantras—attend! From the Diligent Meaning-Possessing Tantra, lines 4423-4425: All teachings' supreme essence: Dharmatā direct-path called. From the Vajra-Sky-Equality Tantra, lines 4427-4432: Basis, essence, flower: All Buddhas' nature-tantra. Supreme secret distinguished, essence gathered. This to samsara's far shore, like an arrow-shot person.

The supreme essence of all teachings: dharmata, the direct path. Not roundabout, not gradual, direct! Basis, essence, flower—three aspects of the nature tantra. All Buddhas' tantra. The essence gathered, distinguished as supreme secret. This takes you to the far shore of samsara—like an arrow shot, swift, direct, no hesitation.

From the Supreme Golden Light, lines 4434-4437: Basis, nature, methods: Relying, even to the Buddha-field, effortless, easy—so placed as tantra.

Basis, nature, methods—three aspects. Relying on these, even to the Buddhafield. Effortless, easy—not difficult, not requiring eons of practice. This is why it is called tantra.

The Determination of These Meanings. From the Pearl Garland, lines 4440-4449: Nature-tantra is this: Basis, essence, quintessence itself. Basis: essence, nature, compassion pervading all, arisen, possessing self-nature of three primordial wisdoms, primordially pure from stains, the unique one of indivisibility. All Buddhas' great secret. Kāya: perfected essence-awareness. Kāya's tantra: pervading emptiness. Clarity itself is self-appearance. Non-appearance possessing clarity's nature. Perfect essence's nature-tantra.

Basis, essence, quintessence—the three. The basis has essence (emptiness), nature (clarity), compassion (pervading all). Three primordial wisdoms. Primordially pure—never stained. Indivisible—never separate. The great secret of all Buddhas.

Kaya—form body, but here perfected essence-awareness. Pervading emptiness. Clarity itself is self-appearance—it appears to itself. Non-appearance possessing clarity's nature—even when there is no appearance, the nature of clarity is present.

Lines 4450-4457: All appearances are tantra, all interconnected and clinging. In the mandala of natural citta, unproduced, perfected—great essence. Thus, gathered: essence, since become life's breath of all. Explained as essence-tantra.

All appearances are tantra! Not just some special visions or meditative states. Everything! All interconnected, all clinging—wait, this is interesting. Even clinging is tantra. Even delusion is tantra. In the mandala of natural mind, unproduced, perfected. The essence, the life breath of all.

From the Tantra of All Buddhas' Blissful Secret, lines 4460-4470: All Buddhas gathered into one, all essences extracted, essence gathered—thus called essence, gathered into empty-clear awareness's essence. All Buddhas' great blissful secret, possessing basis, essence, flower, the very essence of all self-awareness. Called the Great Perfection's essence, renowned among other tantras.

All Buddhas gathered into one—one essence, one nature. All essences extracted, gathered—this is the essence. Empty-clear awareness's essence—the nature of your mind. The Great Perfection's essence—famous, renowned, supreme among tantras.

The secret tantra explained, lines 4469-4482: Scripture held by definite ones. Nature empty, all-pervading. That too: essence's nature. Essence gathered, three clarities. Three clarities, pervading cognition. Three confidences, three non-returns—obtain definiteness of unchanging essence. Three appearances, three emptinesses: Essence: pure nature. Established as tantra. Basis, path, result—held definitively: nature-tantra. Thus explained, tantra established. Established through natural descent. Thus called nature-itself tantra.

Held by definite ones—those with certainty. Nature empty, all-pervading. Three clarities, three confidences, three non-returns, three appearances, three emptinesses—these are the details of practice and realization. Basis, path, result held definitively—all are the nature tantra. Established through natural descent—not made by anyone, naturally present.
