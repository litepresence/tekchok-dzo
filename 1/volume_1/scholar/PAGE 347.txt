[14172-14205] [STRUCTURE] Three Channels' Functions and Nature
The text presents the functions and nature of the three channels. The structure establishes: (1) ro ma's function of sustaining bliss, (2) rkyang ma's function of sustaining emptiness, and (3) kun 'dar ma's function of sustaining non-dual wisdom.

[14172-14205] [PHILOLOGY] Channel Function Terminology
The term ro ma'i ngo bo bde ba (ro ma's nature is bliss) identifies its essence. The compound stong pa (emptiness) describes rkyang ma's nature. The phrase bde stong gnyis su med pa (bliss-emptiness non-dual) establishes kun 'dar ma's realization.

[14172-14205] [CONTEXT] Channel Wisdom
The text presents channels as wisdom vehicles when recognized. Each channel carries a specific wisdom: ro ma carries mirror-like wisdom, rkyang ma carries emptiness wisdom, kun 'dar ma carries non-dual wisdom. This reflects the integration of physiology and enlightenment.

[14172-14205] [CONCEPT] Bliss-Emptiness Unity
