[3838-3840] [CONCEPT] Free-from-Action Expanse
**Analysis:** Lines 3838-3840 present the Free-from-Action expanse:

- *bya ba dang bral ba'i klong* — Expanse free from action
- *ma byas rang grol* — Uncreated, self-liberated dharmatā
- Liberation through dharmatā
- Called: Liberated through liberation without action

[3841-3858] [STRUCTURE] Ornament, Play, and Dharmatā Expanses
**Analysis:** Lines 3841-3858 present three additional expanse categories:

**Ornament Expanse (*rgyan gyi klong*) [3841-3843, 3844-3846]:**
- Appearances mind's continuum
- Mind emptiness without reference-recognition
- Called: Ornament
- Quote: "Unproduced, unceasing, not thinking anything"

**Play Expanse (*rol pa'i klong*) [3847-3852]:**
- Without existence aspect, unceasing play arises
- Appearance-mind without change
- Quote: "Completely liberating great pure path"

**Dharmatā Expanse (*chos nyid kyi klong*) [3853-3858]:**
- Unfabricated self-arisen placing
- From nowhere, nothing
- Unfabricated emptiness ground
- Seeing-knowing non-dual
- Quote: "Emptiness transcends Samantabhadra"

[3859-3869] [STRUCTURE] Introduction to Instruction Section
**Analysis:** Lines 3859-3869 transition to the Instruction Section (*man ngag gi sde*):

**Characterization [3859-3860]:**
- Superior to previous, directly speaking path
- Like meeting crucial fire-fang
- *ngo bo gnad du 'gro ba gnad la thebs pa* — Essence going to crucial point, striking crucial

**Three Divisions [3861-3864]:**
1. *kha 'thor ba'i tshul du gsungs pa* — Spoken in scattered manner
2. *kha gtam pa'i tshul du gsungs pa* — Spoken as advice/discussion
3. *rgyud rang gzhung gi tshul du bka' sts pa* — Spoken as tantra own-text

**Scattered Essence [3865-3866]:**
- Beyond action-doing mind, making path

**Two Divisions [3867-3869]:**
- Placing path cutting extreme instruction
- Liberation-force-pure path manifest instruction
