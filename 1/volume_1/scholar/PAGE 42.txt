[1370-1371] [PHILOLOGY] The Arising of Sexual Desire and Womb Birth
**Analysis:**

**Three Desires:**
- *blta ba* — Looking
- *reg pa* — Touching  
- *dgod pa* — Laughing/Smiling
- *'dod chags byung* — Desire arose

**Birth Transition:**
- *mngal nas skyes pa* — Womb-born (replacing spontaneous birth)
- Population explosion (*mang du 'phel*)

**Material Conditions:**
- Light merely self-clear (*'od rang gsal tsam*)
- Food and clothes from trees
- Domesticated animals: *'dod 'jo'i ba* (desire-satisfying cow) and *cang shes kyi rta* (sensible horse)

[1372-1377] [CONTEXT] The Arising of Violence and Physical Decline
**Analysis:**

**Merit Depletion:** *bsod nams bri* — Merit decreased

**First Violence:**
- *zhe sdang* — Anger/hatred arose
- *gcig gis gcig gsod pa* — Killing one another
- *sngar gyi yon tan rnams nub* — Previous qualities submerged

**Physical Deterioration:**
- Could no longer travel without touching earth (*sa la sor bzhis ma reg par 'gro*)
- Lost ability to fly/levitate
- Light became dim (*mog mog por song*)
- Form and youth no longer beautiful

**New Diet:**
- Earth's fat essence (*sa'i tshil zhag*)
- Honey-like taste
- Soft tree fiber for clothes

[1378-1379] [STRUCTURE] The Origin of Theft and Famine
**Analysis:**

**First Theft:**
- Someone stored earth essence (*sa zhag*) without taking only when needed
- Hoarded for days, months, years
- Others imitated, saying "good"
- Evening/night (*nub*) = beginning of concealment

**Consequence:**
- *mu ge dang mun pa'i dus* — Famine and darkness time
- Many years of suffering
- Karmic consequence of greed and hoarding

[1380-1386] [CONTEXT] The Origin of Sun and Moon
**Analysis:**

**Sun's Arising:**
- From beings' common merit (*'gro ba spyi mthun gyi bsod nams las*)
- Light arose in sky
- *nyi ma* (sun) etymology: benefit-doing near (*mnye bar phan 'dogs pa*)
- No concept of "near/far" before sun

**Night's Definition:**
- *de nub pa* — When sun sets
- Named "night" (*mtshan mo*)

**Moon's Arising:**
- From hope that darkness be cleared (*mun pa sangs par smon pa*)
- Similar to sun
- *zla ba* (moon) etymology: sits as companion (*zla bor 'dug*)

[1387-1391] [PHILOLOGY] The Waxing and Waning of the Moon
**Analysis:**

**Initial State:**
- Moon possessed heat throughout entire night
- Too hot for beings

**First Wish:**
- *bsil bar gyur cig* — May this become cool
- Heat disappeared, became cool

**Second Problem:**
- Some tormented by excessive coolness
- *ha cang bsil bas gdungs pa ma bzod* — Could not endure torment from very cool

**Second Wish:**
- *mi bsil bar gyur cig* — May this not be cool
- Result: Waxing and waning (*yar skye mar 'grib*)

**Cosmological Principle:** Celestial mechanics arise from collective karma and wishes of beings.

[1392] [CONTEXT] Self-Grown Grain Agriculture
**Analysis:**

**Agricultural Revolution:**
- *ma rmos rang 'khrungs* — Unplowed self-grown
- *sA lu* grain (a type of wild rice/millet)
- *zhing nang rngas nang skye* — Grows inside day-seed in field
- *nub rngas nub skye* — Evening-seed grows in evening

**Immediate Results:**
- Food available without cultivation
- Still relatively effortless sustenance
- Marks transition from gathering to early agriculture

**Decline Pattern:** Each epoch shows progressive loss of spontaneous blessings, requiring increasing effort for survival.
