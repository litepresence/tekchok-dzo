[7446-7563] [STRUCTURE] Siddhi Classifications and Historical Validation

[7446-7507] **Detailed Siddhi Analysis:**
From the tantra, the eight siddhis elaborated:

**Longevity/Enjoyments:**
- Jewels and food (*nor dang zas*)
- Grains and desired purposes (*'bru dang 'dod pa'i don*)
- Fruits increase and expand (*shing thog lo thog gzhad pas mchog tu smin zhing rgyas*)
- No exhaustion through use (*spyad pas 'dzad med*)

**Gatherings (*'du ba*) - Four Types:**
1. Humans: Communities and women
2. Non-humans: At charnel grounds, thousands of ḍākinīs gather showing forms, signs, and prophecies
3. Gods, nāgas, yakṣas: Miraculous powers, brilliance, strength
4. Those delighting in Buddha's teaching

**Food Gatherings:**
- Delicious supreme tastes in this land
- Other lands' foods, fruits, essences, medicinal preparations
- Marvelous foods gather to the fortunate one

**Wealth:**
- Whatever desired forms of wealth
- By mere thought (*bsam pa tsam gyis*), gathering occurs
- Clothes, bedding, seats, etc.

[7508-7543] **Additional Siddhis:**
- Cakravartin merit and fame without harm
- Swift-footed siddhi and sky-walking
- Eye medicine and life control
- Empowerment and life control
- Glory and wisdom perfection
- Dharma and enjoyment perfection
- Merit possession and accumulation completion
- High rebirth after death

**Obtaining through dharmatā:**
- Equipoise
- Not afflicted by emotional afflictions
- Self-appearance sent forth as path
- Miraculous powers and display
- Various emanation bodies
- Body becoming vajra-like
- Buddha marks and signs arising
- No harm from earth, water, fire, wind
- Flying in space
- Body becoming insubstantial

[7544-7563] **Historical Validation:**
From *Bskod pa chen po*, the narrative of *Tshogs bdag nag po* (Black Lord of Gatherings):
- Received utterly without elaboration empowerment (*rab tu spras pa med pa'i dbang*)
- Made feast for five hundred yogins
- A ḍākinī prophesied accomplishment
- One hundred thousand riders on elephants achieved sky-walking siddhi
- Feast and celebration accomplish purpose

[7564-7642] [PHILOLOGY] Mandala Architecture

**Three Mandala Classification:**
From *Rang shar*:
1. **Nature mandala** (*rang bzhin dkyil 'khor*): Self-arisen wisdom in all beings
2. **Root mandala** (*rtsa ba'i dkyil 'khor*): Supporting the path
3. **Form reflection mandala** (*gzugs brnyan dkyil 'khor*): The created representation

**Root Mandala Details:**
- Abides in each individual's mind (*rang rang tsitta'i dkyil na gnas*)
- Self-arisen awareness as mandala center (*rang byung rig pa dkyil yin*)
- Five lights' appearance as retinue ('od lnga'i snang ba 'khor du 'dod)

**Three Great Root Mandalas:**
1. **First**: Pure bindu in white conch shell heart (*duṅ khaṅ dkar po*) with radiant bindu particles
2. **Second**: Vajra chain body (*rdo rje lu gu rgyud kyi sku*) in space vajra's hollow
3. **Third**: Body mandala unmoving, speech mandala unceasing, mind mandala non-conceptual clarity

**Symbol Mandala (*brda dkyil 'khor*):**
- Painted powder mandala (*rdul tshon gyi dkyil 'khor*)
- Thread and color mandala (*thig dang tshom bu*)
- Depending on symbol mandala for generation and completion meditation

[7643-7711] [CONTEXT] Mandala Construction Details

**Peaceful Mandala Architecture:**
- Central four-spoked wheel (*'khor lo rtsibs bzhi*)
- Outer vajra fence (*rdo rje ra ba*)
- Jewel pinnacle (*rin chen pha gu*)
- Garland of sharp weapons (*za ra tshags kyi phreng ba*)
- Doors and door frames (*sgo dang sgo khyud*)

**Wrathful Mandala Architecture:**
- Central eight-spoked wheel (*rtsibs brgyad ldan*)
- Triangular palace (*pho brang gru gsum*) dark blue
- Sequential arrangement of activities

**Resultant Nature Mandala:**
From *Bskod pa chen po*, after entering symbol powder mandala:
- Accomplishing generation and completion with recitation
- Utterly without elaboration: directly viewing dharmatā's meaning
- Four visions (*snang ba bzhi*) reaching measure
- Youthful vase body (*gzhon nu bum pa sku*) manifesting
- Dharmakaya and self-appearance Saṃbhogakaya mandalas complete
- Both peaceful and wrathful natures spontaneously accomplish

**Akanṣṭha Dharmadhātu Palace:**
- Central wheel hub (*'khor lo'i lte ba*)
- Vairocana as center (*rnam par snang mdzad dkyil*)
- Four directional families' lords and goddesses
- Vajrasattva east, Ratnasambhava south, Amitābha west, Amoghasiddhi north
