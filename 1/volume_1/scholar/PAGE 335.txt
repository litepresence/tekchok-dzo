## PAGE 335 [13716-13751]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **three vajra completion** and **four wheels location**. Lines 13716-13751 establish:

1. **Three vajra formation** (13716-13726): OM AH HUM with parents in three channels
2. **Four wheels and four locations** (13726-13730): Navel, heart, throat, crown
3. **Sound production** (13730-13733): Wind-vibration creates speech
4. **Voice determination** (13733-13751): Channel-width determines voice-quality

The structure completes **embryological formation** with vocal physiology.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Technical terminology:**

- **ཡབ་ཀྱི་ཐིག་ལེ་ཡི་གེ་ཨཱོཾ་ཨཱཿཧཱུྃ་གི་སྒྲ་དང་བཅས་པ་འཐོན་ཏེ།** (13716): "Father's bindu-letter OM AH HUM with sound emerges"—thig le yi ge (bindu-letter) as **syllable-essence**.

- **རོ་རྐྱང་དབུ་མ་གསུམ་དུ་གྱུར་པའི་དབུས་མའི་ནང་དུ།** (13718): "Taste-nerve-open three becoming, in the center of central"—ro (taste), rkyang (nerve), dbu ma (central)—**three main channels**.

- **རྩ་བའི་ནང་གི་ཡི་གེ་རྣམས་རླུང་གི་སྒྲ་གཡོས་པ་ལས་སྐད་འབྱུང་ལ།** (13726-13727): "Letters in the root, from wind-sound vibration, voice arises"—**phonation theory**: speech from wind moving syllables.

- **རིགས་ཅན་གྱི་རྩ་མེད་པ་དང་རྩ་སྦུབས་ཡངས་པས་སྦོམ་པའོ།** (13734): "Without caste channels and wide channel-hollows makes coarse"—voice quality determined by **anatomy**.

**Particle constructions:**
The **ablative of source** ལས། (13718, 13727) establishes **emanation**. The **causative** བྱེད། marks **production**.

### [CONTEXT]
**Mantric cosmogony:**
The three syllables (OM AH HUM) represent:
- OM = body (sku)
- AH = speech (gsung)
- HUM = mind (thugs)

This is standard **Vajrayāna** trikāya symbolism.

**Nāḍī physiology:**
The three channels (ro ma, rkyang ma, kun 'dar ma) correspond to:
- Left = moon, relative
- Right = sun, absolute
- Central = union, wisdom

**Phonetics and spirituality:**
The voice analysis (13733-13751) establishes **sacred phonetics**: voice quality reveals spiritual capacity. This parallels **Vedic** śikṣā (phonetics) and **tantric** mantra science.

### [CONCEPT]
**Three vajras in three channels:**
The text presents **somatic mandala**:
- Right channel: OM + white bindu + father-kāyas = absolute
- Left channel: AH + light + mother-kāyas = relative
- Central channel: HUM + bindu = union

**Speech as wind-syllable vibration:**
Line 13726-13727 establishes **mantric phonation**: speech arises when wind vibrates syllables in channels. This is **sacred physiology**: ordinary speech is **energized mantra** when recognized.

**Voice as spiritual indicator:**
The text specifies (13734-13745) that voice reveals:
- Channel-width (rtsa sbubs)
- Caste-channels (rigs kyi rtsa)
- Affliction-imprints ('dod chags, ma rig pa, zhe sdang, nga rgyal, phrag dog)

Voice is **diagnostic tool** for spiritual capacity.
