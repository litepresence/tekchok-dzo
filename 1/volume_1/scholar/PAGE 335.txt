[13648-13687] [STRUCTURE] Crown Wheel and Sense Purification
The text presents the crown wheel (rtsé mo rnam par bkod pa'i 'khor lo) with 360 channels and sense purification. The structure establishes: (1) 360 channels with letters, lights, and deities, (2) 720 letters and light masses, and (3) 1,080 subtle elements as the basis.

[13648-13687] [PHILOLOGY] Crown Center Terminology
The term rtsé mo rnam par bkod pa'i 'khor lo (crown wheel of peak arrangement) identifies the sahasrāra chakra. The compound ka ka ki ki (Sanskrit vowels) represents the seed syllables. The phrase 'od kyi gong bu (mass of light) describes luminous manifestations.

[13648-13687] [CONTEXT] Crown Center Mysticism
The text presents the crown center as the seat of highest realization. The 1,000+ channels represent the complete subtle body potential. This reflects the tantric understanding of the crown as the gateway to highest awakening.

[13648-13687] [CONCEPT] Crown as Wisdom Gateway
The text conceptualizes the crown center as the gateway through which wisdom descends. The channels, letters, and lights are not merely symbolic but the actual architecture of enlightenment. When recognized, the crown becomes the seat of non-dual awareness.
