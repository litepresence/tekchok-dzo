[3736-3749] [CONCEPT] Variegated Expanse: Three Subdivisions
**Analysis:** Lines 3736-3749 introduce the Variegated (*khra bo*) Expanse:

**Essence [3736-3739]:**
- Variegated existence (*yin sna tshogs*)
- Non-existence aspectless, universally self-arisen
- Play without negation-affirmation

**Three Divisions [3740-3743]:**
1. *yod par smra ba sems sde dang mthun pa* — Asserting existence, agreeing with Mind Section
2. *med par smra ba rang gnad dang mthun pa* — Asserting non-existence, agreeing with own crucial point
3. *yod med man ngag dang mthun par smra ba* — Asserting existence-non-existence, agreeing with Instruction

**First: Existence-Asserting [3744-3749]:**
- Essence: Self-pure abiding-liberation, nowhere divided
- Nature not established as negation-affirmation
- Beyond convention, action-effort
- Non-dual characteristics
- Not transcending one mind's wheel
- Unceasing play
- Called: Appearance-mind non-dually liberated

**Third: Existence-Non-Existence [3754-3761]:**

  2. Unseeable, unaccustomed
  3. Abiding-change non-dually liberated
- Mind without change
- Samsara-nirvana's aspect arises
