[12011-12070] [STRUCTURE] Three Aspects as Unity

**Indivisible Three:**
Essence, nature, compassion—not three separate things.
Three aspects of one ground—indivisible, inseparable.

From *Rang shar*:
"Three aspects one
Not three separate
Indivisible, inseparable
Unity thus"

**Interpenetration:**
Essence not separate from nature—emptiness-luminosity union.
Nature not separate from compassion—luminosity-power union.
Compassion not separate from essence—power-emptiness union.

From *Thal 'gyur*:
"Essence nature union
Emptiness luminosity
Nature compassion union
Luminosity power
Compassion essence union
Power emptiness
Interpenetration thus"

**Practical Implication:**
Cannot meditate on essence without nature.
Cannot meditate on nature without compassion.
Cannot meditate on compassion without essence.
All three complete in every moment.

From *De nyid*:
"Cannot meditate essence
Without nature
Cannot meditate nature
Without compassion
Cannot meditate compassion
Without essence
All three complete
Every moment"
