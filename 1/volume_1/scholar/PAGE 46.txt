[1496-1512] [CONTEXT] The Twenty Eons of Destruction
**Analysis:**

**Structure:**
- 19 eons: destruction of essence (*bcud 'jig*)
- 1 eon: destruction of container (*snod 'jig*)

**Destruction Process:**
1. Lower realms empty: old beings die, no new births
2. Evil-doers born in other world-hells
3. Humans decline to 10-year lifespan
4. Disease, weapons, famine calamities
5. One being achieves second dhyana
6. Proclaims "joy of solitude-born bliss"
7. Others follow, progress to dhyana
8. Gods from Four Great Kings to Brahma follow suit

**Complete destruction of sentient beings achieved.**
