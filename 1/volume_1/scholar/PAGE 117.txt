[4358-4392] [CONCEPT] Three Clarities and Confidence
**Analysis:** Lines 4358-4392 present the complete path structure:

**Three Clarities (*gsal ba gsum*) [4358-4366, 4381-4387]:**
1. External object appearing: awareness as body clear, expanse as color clear, space expanse-awareness non-separate clear
2. Three "gdings" (confidence): view, object, delusion ceased certainty
3. Three non-reversals: samsara primordially Buddha, awareness no cause, awareness body appears delusion pure
4. Three appearances: day-night without, awareness clear-dim without, dharmatƒÅ transformation without
5. Three emptinesses: awareness no reverse, mind no condition, light no going-coming

**Liberation [4381-4387]:**
- Delusion appearance self-pure, samsara cause non-existent
- Purity appearance self-arisen, condition self-ceases
- Chod bardo appearance, samsara result self-dissolved

**Three Tantras [4388-4392]:**
- Ground, essence, flower separately shown
- Only aspect difference, nature one
- Entity nature, cause grandmother tantra or ground tantra
