[15186-15222] [STRUCTURE] Crown Wheel Channels Detailed
The text presents the detailed structure of the crown wheel channels. The structure establishes: (1) thirty-two petals as the main structure, (2) the thousand-petaled lotus as the complete form, and (3) the specific channels radiating to different body parts.

[15186-15222] [PHILOLOGY] Crown Structure Terminology
The term spyi gtsug tshangs pa'i bu ga (crown Brahma aperture) identifies the fontanelle. The compound padma lta bu'i rtsa chen (lotus-like great channel) describes the central channel. The phrase 'khor lo'i rim pa (wheel stages) establishes hierarchical structure.

[15186-15222] [CONTEXT] Crown Mysticism
The text presents the crown as the most subtle and important center. The thousand-petaled lotus represents complete opening and full enlightenment. This reflects the highest yoga tantra understanding of the crown as the gateway to liberation.

[15186-15222] [CONCEPT] Crown as Enlightenment Gateway
The text conceptualizes the crown as the gateway through which consciousness exits at death and wisdom enters in awakening. The thousand channels represent the complete subtle body; their opening is the completion of the path.
