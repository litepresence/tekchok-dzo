## [CONTEXT]

**Six Afflictions (Nyon mongs drug):**
Standard list of **six root poisons** (lines 1-9):
1. 'Dod pa (desire)
2. Rmugs pa (torpor)
3. Sdang ba (hatred)
4. Chags pa (attachment)
5. Zhen pa (fixation)
6. Mi shes pa (ignorance)

Each with: rgyu (cause), rkyen (condition), ngo bo (essence), bye brag (particularity)

**Sixth Movement: 'Gyu byed 'dzin pa:**
**Movement-maker grasping** (lines 10-15):
- 'Das pa (past)
- Ma 'ongs (future)
- Da lta'i dngos po (present entities)
- Rjes su 'gyus pas: By following these
- Produces: 'Khor ba'i las (samsaric karma), rnam smin (ripening), etc.

**Dran pa Analysis:**
**Memory etymology** (lines 16-17):
- Bag la nyal sna tshogs pa'i: Various latent tendencies
- Rgyu rkyen dang byed pa du ma las: From many causes, conditions, and activities
- De ltar btags: Thus designated

**Critique of Reductionism:**
Lines 18-22 present **dialectical argument**:
- Position: "Yid ces gcig brjod pas chog" - Saying "mind" alone suffices
- Objection: "Dbye ba du mas mi rigs" - Multiple classifications unsuitable
- Rebuttal: Same applies to sems byung (mental factors)
- Conclusion: Dbye bsdu depends on cha shas - no contradiction

**Dbyings rnam dag gi sgron ma:**
**Field-lamp as visionary structure** (lines 23-41):
- **Definition**: 'Od dang sku'i 'char byed - emergence of light and form
- **Structure**: Five colors gathered, pervasive blue unchanging
- **Enclosure**: Rdo rje'i ra ba - vajra fence
- **Support**: Rdo rje lu gu rgyud - vajra lattice (no support)
- **Content**: 'Od kyi sku - light body radiating
- **Function**: Chos nyid bar do chod - cuts dharmata bardo

**Visionary Unfolding:**
**Progressive manifestation** (lines 32-41):
1. **Gyen 'greng**: Vertical rising
2. **Rtsibs shar**: Horizontal spreading  
3. **Mu khyud**: Peripheral rim
Result: 'Khrul snang rang 'gags - deluded appearances self-cease

**Detailed Structures** (lines 25-41):
Sequential appearance of:
- Mig tshags (eye-network)
- Mig mangs (many eyes)
- Mig mangs ris (eye-patterns)
- Dra ba (net)
- Dra ba phyed pa (half-net)
- Me tog (flower)
- Padma stong ldan (thousand-petaled lotus)
- Mkhar thab (fortress-tower)
- Za 'og gi gur khyim (lower-tent house)

**Soteriological Function:**
**Chos nyid lam du byed pa** (line 41): Making dharmata the path

**Rgyang zhags Result:**
**Epistemological reversal** (lines 42-43):
- Chos nyid mngon sum gyi snang ba mthong: Seeing dharmata directly
- Grub mtha' 'dod rgyal du 'dzin pa: Grasping philosophical tenets self-arrogantly
- Blo ldog: Mind reverses/converts

## [CONCEPT]

**Affliction Ontology:**
The **six poisons** are not merely to be abandoned but **analyzed structurally**:
- Each has rgyu (cause), rkyen (condition), ngo bo (essence), bye brag (particularity)
- This is **Abhidharmic analysis** applied to Dzogchen context
- Understanding structure → natural release

**Temporal Grasping:**
**'Gyu byed 'dzin pa** (lines 10-15) - grasping-maker:
- Past/future/present temporal triad
- Following these produces karma and ripening
- This is **samsaric engine**: temporal grasping → karmic production

**Dran pa as Latency:**
**Memory contains latent tendencies** (lines 16-17):
- Bag la nyal: Karmic latency
- Not active memory but **storage of traces**
- Multiple causes/conditions/activities → designated as memory

**Dbyings rnam dag Visionary Phenomenology:**
The **field-lamp** is **space of manifestation** (lines 23-41):
- **'Char byed**: That from which emergence occurs
- **Five colors**: Pañca-jñāna (five wisdom) manifestation
- **Blue**: Ākāśa-dhātu (space element) - pervasive, unchanging
- **Vajra enclosure**: Protected, indestructible space
- **No support**: Self-standing, non-dependent

**Light Body ( 'Od kyi sku):**
The **content of the field** (line 16):
- Radiating light rays
- Not physical form but **luminous manifestation**
- Within this: chos nyid bar do 'chod - cuts bardo
- **Mngon par rdzogs par sangs rgya**: Complete buddhahood

**Progressive Dissolution:**
**Visionary structures indicate stages** (lines 32-41):
1. **Gyen 'greng/rtsibs shar/mu khyud**: Basic threefold structure
2. **'Khrul snang rang 'gags**: Delusion self-ceases
3. **Ye shes su gsal**: Appears as wisdom
4. **Detailed structures**: Increasing complexity = increasing recognition

**Bar mtshams med pa:**
**No interval between dbyings** (line 24):
- Phyi nang 'dres: Outer/inner mixed
- No gap between inner and outer fields
- **Non-dual spatiality**

**Rtags tshad:**
**Signs and measures** (line 41):
- Sgron ma bzhi determine lam gyi snang ba bzhi'i rtags tshad
- Four lamps → four path lights → their signs/measures determined
- **Diagnostic function**: Lamps indicate progress

**Grub mtha' 'dod rgyal:**
**Philosophical arrogance** (lines 42-43):
- 'Dod rgyal: Self-arrogant, presumptuous
- Grasping tenets (grub mtha') arrogantly
- Rgyang zhags seeing reverses (ldog) this
- **Epistemological humility** through direct perception
