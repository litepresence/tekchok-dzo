[4488-4501] [PHILOLOGY] Five Names, One Nature
**Analysis:** Lines 4488-4501 establish the unity of five names:

**Five Names [4488-4489]:**
- Ground (*gzhi*)
- Essence (*snying po*)
- Essence (*bcud*)
- Three, named differently five
- Meaning nature not transcending one

**Mañjusrinamasamgiti Quotation [4491-4501]:**
- Ground one essence distinction
- Method and nature tantra
- Merely imputed, appearing
- Nature ground one nature empty
- Non-different, unchanging
- Example: person with many names
- Called from anywhere, thus appears
- Unchanging meaning likewise

**Key Insight:** All five names refer to the same nature, distinguished only by aspect.

11. **Introduction Ornament** — Mirror example meaning joined, mirror-like
