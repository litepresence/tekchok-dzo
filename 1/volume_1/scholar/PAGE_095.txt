[3510-3512] [PHILOLOGY] Result Expanse-Wisdom
**Analysis:** Lines 3510-3512 conclude the result-level analysis:

**Line 3510-3511:**
- *mtha' bral stong pa lta sgom las 'das pa'i ye shes shar* — Wisdom beyond view-meditation of extreme-free emptiness shines
- *chos thams cad bde sdug ngo mnyam du rtogs* — All dharmas realized as bliss-pain equal

**Line 3512:**
- Called: Dharmatā beyond action (*chos nyid bya ba las 'das pa'i dbyings dang ye shes*)

**Technical Note:** This represents the culmination of Anuyoga's path structure—realization that transcends deliberate practice.

[3513-3517] [CONCEPT] Union (zung 'jug) in Anuyoga
**Analysis:** Lines 3513-3517 present the union level:

**Sequential Process:**
- Expanse-wisdom entering gradually (*rim gyis zhugs pa*) [3513]
- Abiding (*gnas pa*) [3514]
- Emptiness (*stong pa*) [3515]
- Bliss entering/capture (*bde ba bzung 'jug*) [3516]

**Result:**
- Without arrogance (*bla nga ba med*)
- Without rejection (*spang du med*)
- Called: Union (*zung 'jug*)

[3518-3522] [CONCEPT] Three Entry Modes Detailed
**Analysis:** Lines 3518-3522 elaborate three of the five entry modes:

**1. Simultaneous (cig car) [3518]:**
- Expanse-wisdom entering simultaneously
- Understanding: beyond view of self-arisen dharmatā
- Called: Cig car — liberation without cause (because nothing needs to be liberated)

**2. All-at-once (thar chags) [3519-3520]:**
- Understanding: everything is, yet nothing is particular
- Not rejecting all (*kun mi spong*)
- Nothing to obtain (*thob rgyu med*)
- Called: Thar chags (completely liberated)

**3. Thodgal (thod rgal) [3521-3522]:**
- Entering leap-over manner
- Appearance (*mthong snang*) and liberation together
- Without needing action or effort
- Called: Thodgal Anuyoga (wisdom seeing dharmatā arises leap-over)

**Critical Distinction:** Thodgal here is distinct from Dzogchen thodgal—it's the Anuyoga version emphasizing rapid transformation.

[3523-3528] [CONCEPT] Five Views of Anuyoga
**Analysis:** Lines 3523-3528 enumerate five views (*lta ba*):

1. **Non-dual Equality** (*gnyis med ngo mnyam*) [3523]:
   - Expanse-wisdom without difference

2. **Non-change from Abiding** (*gnas pa las gzhan du 'gyur ba med*) [3524-3525]:
   - No going-coming in expanse-wisdom

3. **Primordially Abiding** (*ye gdod ma nas gnas pa*) [3526-3527]:
   - Beyond partiality (*rgya chad*) and falling (*phyogs lhung*)

4. **Free from Dependent Basis** (*ltos gzhi dang bral ba*) [3528]:
   - Beyond mere words
   - No mutual dependence

[3529-3533] [STRUCTURE] Summary: Ground, Path, Result, Superiority
**Analysis:** Lines 3529-3533 synthesize the Anuyoga framework:

**Ground-Path [3529-3530]:**
- Expanses as ground (*don gyi dbyings gzhi yin*)
- Wisdom as path (*ye shes lam yin*)
- Path-persons liberate through effort

**Cause-Result [3531-3532]:**
- Expanse as cause (*dbyings rgyu yin*)
- Wisdom as result (*ye shes 'bras bu yin*)
- Sharp faculties liberate without depending on external cause-result

**Superiority [3532-3533]:**
- Superior to lower vehicles (*'og ma las khyad par du 'phags*)
- Difficult to realize (*rtogs dka' ba*), therefore explained extensively

[3534-3538] [STRUCTURE] Introduction to Atiyoga (Dzogchen)
**Analysis:** Line 3534 introduces the ninth topic — Great Perfection of Instruction (*man ngag rdzogs pa chen po*):

**Defining Characteristics:**
- Self-arisen wisdom (*rang byung gi ye shes*) [3535]
- No establishment in samsara-nirvana (*'khor 'das su grub pa med*) [3535]
- Primordial liberation beyond acceptance/rejection (*ye grol spang blang las 'das*) [3536]

**Ultimate Nature [3537-3538]:**
- Not established anywhere (*gang du'ang ma grub*)
- Non-ceasing wisdom of all-liberation (*thams cad grol ba'i ye shes su mi 'gag*)

**Structural Significance:** This marks the transition from Anuyoga to Atiyoga, the pinnacle of the Nyingma vehicle system.
