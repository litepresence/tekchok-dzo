[5765-5822] [CONCEPT] Common and Uncommon Preambles
**Analysis:** Lines 5765-5822 distinguish common and uncommon preambles (*thun mong gi gleng gzhi* vs. *thun mong ma yin pa'i gleng gzhi*):

**Uncommon Preamble [5765-5778]:**
Awareness-wisdom abiding mode with five perfections. Expressed by saying uncommon preamble. This appears at the beginning of luminosity (*'od gsal*) tantras: "At one time I taught thus" (*'di skad bdag gis bstan pa'i dus gcig*).

**Superiority [5773-5778]:**
This manner shows ultimate truth (*don dam pa'i bden pa*) of entity mode (*dngos po gshis kyi gnas lugs*), surpassing common vehicle sutras and tantras. Citation from *Thal 'gyur*: "Uncommon is distinctive, surpassing all vehicles."

**Two Divisions [5779-5782]:**
1. Entity mode five perfections abiding
2. How they abide in body configuration five perfections

**Entity Mode Five Perfections [5783-5801]:**
1. **Place perfection**: Dharmatā free from proliferation, characteristics not established anywhere, beyond word and letter activity
2. **Teacher perfection**: Spontaneous rigpa, not established as thing or sign, self-arisen awareness
3. **Retinue perfection**: Play of dharmatā, ocean of wisdom assembly
4. **Dharma perfection**: Nature equality, dharmadhatu ultimate mode, Dzogchen tathatā
5. **Time perfection**: All not separate, without one-many duality, inconceivable dharmatā, great luminosity time

**Citations from *Rin po che spungs pa* [5795-5807]:**
- "From non-existent empty dharma-source"
- "First Buddha taught"
- "From wisdom distinction"
- "Dharmatā empty self-sound"
- "Primordially turned dharma-wheel"
- "Without beginning, middle, end"

And from *Gsang ba chen po'i rang dbyings*:
- "In great secret self-expanse"
- "Abiding from primordial"
- "Not grasped place mode"
- "Great play first arose"

[5808-5851] [STRUCTURE] Body Configuration Five Perfections
**Analysis:** Lines 5808-5851 present the five perfections as they abide in the body:

**Place Perfection [5808-5810]:**
In Cittavajra (*tsitta rin po che*) precious palace, center of great channel pure essence.

**Teacher Perfection [5810-5818]:**
Self-arisen rigpa, supreme luminosity unchanging, nature essence, three compassions' identity:
- Five kayas (*sku lnga*)
- Five wisdoms (*ye shes lnga*)
- Five lights (*'od lnga*)
- Five prajñas (*shes rab lnga*)
- Five winds (*rlung lnga*)
- Thigle, thig phran, etc.

**Retinue Perfection [5815-5818]:**
Surrounding with these retinue perfections.

**Dharma Perfection [5819-5821]:**
Channel-wind-thigle key points, key point arisen bliss-clarity non-conceptual wisdom, upadesha practice experience.

**Time Perfection [5821-5851]:**
When accustomed to rigpa meaning, self-arisen self-arising.

**Citation from *Nyi zla kha sbyor* [5824-5831]:**
- "Cittavajra precious palace"
- "Self-arisen wisdom appeared"
- "Great self-arisen teacher prajñā"
- "Kayas and wisdoms thigle"
- "Non-conceptual varieties retinue arose"
- "Upadesha practice teaching manner"
- "That itself accustomed is time"

**Citation from *Thal 'gyur* [5832-5850]:**
Extensive presentation of how enlightened activity spontaneously accomplishes two benefits.
