## PAGE 337 [13787-13830]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **aggregate formation** and **four lamps origination**. Lines 13787-13830 establish:

1. **Final aggregate correspondences** (13787-13792): Four elements → four aggregates
2. **Channel-wind-karma** (13792-13796): Physiological basis of action and wisdom
3. **Lymph-thigle formation** (13796-13800): Defiled essence transformation
4. **Four lamps origination** (13803-13827): Ultimate elements produce four lamps

The structure transitions from **defiled physiology** to **enlightened anatomy**.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Technical terminology:**

- **དྲོད་ལས་རྣམ་ཤེས།** (13789): "From heat, consciousness"—fire-element produces **vijñāna**.

- **དབུགས་ལས་འདུ་བྱེད།** (13790): "From breath, formations"—wind produces **saṃskāra**.

- **ལས་ཀྱིས་འཁོར་བ་སྡུད།** (13795): "Karma gathers saṃsāra"—las (karma) as **binding force**.

- **ཡེ་ཤེས་ཀྱིས་མྱ་ངན་ལས་འདས་པ་སྡུད་དོ།** (13796): "Wisdom gathers nirvāṇa"—ye shes as **liberating force**.

- **དོན་དམ་པའི་འབྱུང་བ་བཞི།** (13803): "Four ultimate elements"—distinguishing **absolute** from relative elements.

- **རྒྱང་ཞགས་ཆུའི་སྒྲོན་མ།** (13805): "Far-reaching Water Lamp"—rgyang zhags (far-reaching/fishing line) as **visionary capacity**.

**Particle constructions:**
The **causative** བསྐྱེད། appears for **productive origination**. The **ablative** ལས། establishes **elemental derivation**.

### [CONTEXT]
**Dzogchen lamp theory:**
The four lamps (sgron ma bzhi) are **Tögal** (thod rgal) terminology:
1. Rgyang zhags chu'i sgron ma (Far-reaching Water Lamp)
2. Thig le stong pa'i sgron ma (Empty Bindu Lamp)
3. Dbyings rnam dag gi sgron me (Pure Expanse Lamp)
4. Shes rab rang byung gi sgron ma (Self-arising Wisdom Lamp)

**Ultimate vs. relative elements:**
The text distinguishes (13803-13814):
- **Relative elements**: solid, liquid, heat, gas
- **Ultimate elements**: color-radiance producing lamps

This is **Dzogchen** ontology—ordinary matter is **solidified light**.

**Karma and wisdom as gatherers:**
Lines 13795-13796 present **dual gathering**:
- Karma gathers saṃsāra (binding)
- Wisdom gathers nirvāṇa (liberating)

Same verb (sdu), different agents, different results.

### [CONCEPT]
**The four lamps from ultimate elements:**
The text presents **sophisticated light-physiology**:
- White brilliance → Far-reaching Water Lamp (from earth channels)
- Red brilliance → Empty Bindu Lamp (from water channels)
- Yellow brilliance → Pure Expanse Lamp (from fire-wind channels)
- Green brilliance → Self-arising Wisdom Lamp (from wind channels)

This demonstrates **chromatic enlightenment**: the four colors of **Tögal vision** arise from channel-elements.

**Karma and wisdom as binding/liberating:**
The parallel structures (13795-13796) establish:
- **Las** (karma) + **'khor ba** (saṃsāra) + **sdu** (gather/bind)
- **Ye shes** (wisdom) + **mya ngan las 'das pa** (nirvāṇa) + **sdu** (gather/liberate)

Same gathering-function: karma **compresses** into embodiment; wisdom **expands** into liberation.

**Lymph as thigle substrate:**
Line 13797-13800 presents **kuṇḍalinī physiology**: lymph (chu ser) produces thigle (bindu), which produces sense-faculties (dbang po lnga). The **essence is in the fluids**.
