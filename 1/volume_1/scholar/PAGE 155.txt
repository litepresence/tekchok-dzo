[6197-6281] [CONTEXT] Detailed Prophecy of Degeneration
**Analysis:** Lines 6197-6281 present extensive prophecies from *Thal 'gyur* about teaching decline:

**Citation from *Thal 'gyur* [6198-6201]:**
"Teaching destruction's near cause, those individual places, person called wrong desire, each all teaching, destroying near cause is."

**First Destruction Period [6202-6216]:**
This first dark time, Jambudvipa west, king six-fingered hand, before green-faced so-lha, right hand forearm, black mole wicked shape, extremely fearful possessing, Buddha teaching darkness becomes. Sixty years becomes.

Then Glorious-holder (*dpal 'dzin*) first holds. Thousand three hundred passed after, again Jambudvipa north, Luminous (*gzi brjid ldan*) mountain root, ascetic form, loving mind lacking, right two ears, eyes three-cornered upward-covering, this and tirthika factions fighting, sixty years teaching darkness becomes.

**Second Period [6221-6235]:**
After that Bliss-god (*bde ba'i lha*) called, hundred years flourishing makes. After that Jambudvipa north-east, great unfathomable river flowing root, king's body merit-endowed, eye only one not having, limbs coarse body strong, throwing and art adorned, this Dharma-doers snatched, sixty years teaching. Here wanes, elsewhere becomes.

**Third Period [6236-6254]:**
After that Pratyekabuddha form, from Vajrasana west arises. By this supreme teaching, hundred twenty years resides. After that below Jambudvipa center, Golden City (*gser gyi grong khyer*) called, abiding body two tongue-tips, size small limbs short, faction fighting weapon holding, this arising also darkness, sixty years becomes.

**Fourth Period [6243-6254]:**
After that below Jambudvipa east, river great Loki-ta, right side banks, Glorious Lion (*dpal gyi seng ge*) called, thousand four hundred years resides.

**Fifth Period [6255-6267]:**
Then again Jambudvipa center, great river Ganga's bank, king's son shoulder, cloud-colored similar, ordained four-fingered measure, this arising also darkness, sixty years becomes.

After that at Kama-la, Sila called monastery, its master Youth's Protector (*gzhon nu'i mgon*), this thousand years resides.

**Sixth Period [6259-6267]:**
After that Jambudvipa south, Brahma's continent called, village, people's family lion-head, this born three months, sixty to darkness becomes.

**Seventh Period [6268-6281]:**
After that Vulture Peak, monk Beautiful-youth (*mdzes byed gzhon nu*), five hundred fifty years resides.

After that below Jambudvipa border, Kashmir land called, sound-endowed city, bird body human face, low caste family born, this born ten years, sixty to darkness becomes.

Thus seven stages Jambudvipa flourishing and decline makes.
