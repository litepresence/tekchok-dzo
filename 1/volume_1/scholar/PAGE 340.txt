[13824-13860] [STRUCTURE] Second Wheel: Earth Element Functions
The text presents the second wheel as dominated by earth element functions. The structure establishes: (1) earth supporting water and subsequent elements, (2) earth producing flesh and body tissues, and (3) earth as the foundation of physical structure.

[13824-13860] [PHILOLOGY] Earth Element Terminology
The term sa'i byed las gsho ba (earth function is dominant) establishes elemental hierarchy. The compound gnyen sha (flesh) describes bodily tissue. The phrase 'khor lo gnyis pa (second wheel) identifies the navel chakra.

[13824-13860] [CONTEXT] Elemental Physiology
The text presents an elemental physiology where earth provides stability and form. The navel chakra as earth center grounds the entire system. This reflects Ayurvedic and tantric understanding of the navel as the vital center.

[13824-13860] [CONCEPT] Earth as Foundation
The text conceptualizes earth as the foundation of practice. Just as earth supports all that grows, the navel center supports all spiritual development. Stability in earth element creates stability in meditation.
