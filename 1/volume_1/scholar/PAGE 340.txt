## PAGE 340 [13909-13950]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **defiled essence physiology** and **absolute bindu mandala**. Lines 13909-13950 establish:

1. **Sequential transformation** (13909-13918): Blood → flesh → channels → lymph → thigle
2. **Four knots in rkyang ma** (13918-13926): Ultimate bindus in four locations
3. **Kun 'dar ma knots** (13931-13947): Absolute bindus producing mandalas
4. **Summary and transition** (13947-13950): Right-relative, left-absolute functions

The structure completes **three-channel theory** with mandala physiology.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Critical terminology:**

- **ཁྲག་ལས་ཤ། ཤ་ལས་ཆུ་བ། ཆུ་བ་ལས་ཆུ་སེར།** (13909-13912): "From blood, flesh; from flesh, channels; from channels, lymph"—**alchemical chain** of embodiment.

- **རྒྱང་ཞགས་ཆུའི་སྒྲོན་མའི་ས་བོན་མིག་ཆུང་རབ་ཏུ་འཚེར་བ་གཉིས་གནས་སོ།** (13919): "Far-reaching Water Lamp seeds, two pupils radiating, abide"—**visual apparatus** as lamp-support.

- **ཤེས་རབ་རང་བྱུང་གི་སྒྲོན་མའི་རྟེན་མེའི་ཐིག་ལེ།** (13921): "Self-arising Wisdom Lamp support, fire bindu"—wisdom-lamp linked to **fire-element**.

- **དབྱིངས་རྣམ་དག་གི་རྟེན་འོད་ལྔའི་ཐིག་ལེ།** (13923): "Pure Expanse support, five-light bindu"—five colors in **throat chakra**.

- **ཐིག་ལེ་སྟོང་པའི་རྟེན་འོད་ཀྱི་ཐིག་ལེ་སོ་སོའི་ཚོན་གང་དུ་གནས་སོ།** (13925): "Empty Bindu support, light bindus each abiding in their color"—**rainbow body** as color-separation.

- **སྒོ་དག་པས་སྒྲོན་མ་བཞི། མ་དག་པས་འབྱུང་བ་བཞིར་འགྱུར་རོ།** (13929-13930): "Four lamps when gates pure; four elements when impure"—**recognition transforms elements into lamps**.

**Particle constructions:**
The **sequential ablative** ལས། creates **transformation chains**. The **conditional** ན། establishes **practice-results**.

### [CONTEXT]
**Alchemical physiology:**
The blood-flesh-channels-lymp-thigle chain (13909-13918) reflects **rasaśāstra** (Indian alchemy) adapted to **Dzogchen**. The body is **refinery** producing essences.

**Tögal lamp theory:**
The four knots in rkyang ma (13918-13926) correspond to **Tögal** practice:
- Pupils = Water Lamp vision
- Heart = Wisdom Lamp
- Throat = Expanse Lamp
- Crown = Empty Bindu Lamp

**Impure/pure transformation:**
Lines 13929-13930 present **core Dzogchen** doctrine: same channels/gates produce either:
- **Impure**: Four elements (saṃsāra)
- **Pure**: Four lamps (nirvāṇa)

Recognition is the **switch**.

### [CONCEPT]
**Defiled essence as path-substrate:**
The sequential chain (13909-13918) demonstrates **how saṃsāra forms** through coarsening. Yet each stage contains **potential for reversal**: lymph (chu ser) produces thigle; thigle produces senses; senses can produce **wisdom**.

**Four lamps in four knots:**
The text maps **Tögal vision** onto physiology:
1. **Navel/eyes**: Water Lamp (vision)
2. **Heart**: Wisdom Lamp (understanding)
3. **Throat**: Expanse Lamp (space)
4. **Crown**: Empty Bindu Lamp (formlessness)

Each lamp has **bindu-support** in specific channel-knot.

**Pure/impure as recognition:**
The crucial lines 13929-13930 establish **Dzogchen** non-duality:
- **Sgo dag pa** (pure gates) = four lamps
- **Ma dag pa** (impure gates) = four elements

Same gates, different recognition. **Practice is purification of recognition**, not of substances.
