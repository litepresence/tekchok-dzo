[5539-5584] [CONCEPT] Dharma Body and Wisdom Analysis
**Analysis:** Lines 5539-5584 analyze the Dharma body (*chos sku*) and wisdom systems through mantra decoding:

**Stacked Letters 5 Continued [5539-5546]:**

**Decoding DHARMA KĀYA:**
- Pure body shown by actual sound (*dag pa'i lus sgra dngos kyis bstan*)
- Impure body shown by constructed sound (*ma dag pa'i lus bsgrubs pas bstan*)

**Wisdom Body Analysis [5547-5550]:**
- Heart-center awareness (*snying ga'i rig pa*)
- Empty-awareness objective wisdom (*stong rig don gyi ye shes*)
- Dharmatā: permanent, unchanging, reversed from delusion
- Awareness wisdom: transcending the world
- Awareness compassion: without ceasing-arising, benefiting beings

**Stacked Letters 6 [5551-5584]:**
Mantra: 'E DE GE MAṂ LHA NYA / 'AD MA KO TSA GRA BA LO KI

**Light Analysis [5556-5563]:**
Three lights entering into dharmadhatu:
1. Awareness light (*rig pa'i 'od*)
2. Kaya light (*sku'i 'od*)
3. Wisdom light (*ye shes kyi 'od*)

All are non-dual wisdom nature, not transcending great equality dharmatā.

**Emptiness-Appearance Relationship [5565-5569]:**
- Emptiness stacked with emptiness
- Sambhogakaya equality meaning
- Body transcending words
- Pervaded by clear light
- Without partiality anywhere

**Three Paths [5570-5579]:**
1. *stong pa ye shes kyi lam* — Emptiness wisdom path — Bodhipath lamp (*byang chub lam gyi sgron ma*)
2. *gsal ba 'od kyi lam* — Clear light path — Wisdom maya body (*ye shes kyi sgyu lus*)
3. *mi 'gyur ba rig pa'i stong lam* — Unchanging awareness empty path — Precious body (*rin po che'i lus*)

These three are of one taste (*ro gcig pa*) in dharmadhatu expanse, not transcending the supreme unchanging great equality.

[5585-5593] [CONTEXT] Wisdom and Timelessness

**Stacked Letters 7 [5585-5593]:**
Mantra: ŚAṂ PRAJÑĀ MU TA DA YA BYA NYA PRA PA RA DUS TA NA KA HĪḤ

**Decoding:**
