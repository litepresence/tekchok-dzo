[14107-14137] [STRUCTURE] Rkyang Ma: Speech Channel
The text presents rkyang ma as the channel of speech and emptiness. The structure establishes: (1) rkyang ma generating speech, (2) straight channel producing clear communication, and (3) branches affecting voice quality.

[14107-14137] [PHILOLOGY] Speech Channel Terminology
The term rkyang ma (single/isolated channel) describes its direct nature. The compound ngag skyes (speech generated) establishes the channel's function. The phrase dbyangs dang 'brel ba (connected to melody) identifies vocal quality.

[14107-14137] [CONTEXT] Voice and Channel Connection
The text presents the connection between channel structure and vocal quality. Straight channels produce clear speech; branches produce variations. This reflects the tantric understanding of speech as energetic expression.

[14107-14137] [CONCEPT] Speech as Energy
The text conceptualizes speech as the energetic expression of the rkyang ma channel. Voice quality, clarity, and power depend on channel condition. Purifying the channel purifies speech; purified speech becomes mantra.
