## PAGE 332 [13600-13647]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **affliction numerology** and **channel-expansion**. Lines 13600-13647 establish:

1. **Eighty-four thousand afflictions** (13600-13612): Mathematical derivation from maturation marks
2. **Kun gzhi as basis** (13612-13623): Ālaya containing immeasurable factors
3. **Fifty-seven night completion** (13613-13628): Crown wheel with 360 channels
4. **Channel functions** (13623-13647): Seventy-two wind functions enumerated

The structure creates **computational affliction theory** integrated with physiology.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Technical terminology:**

- **ཉོན་མོངས་པ་བག་ལ་ཉལ་སྟོང་ཕྲག་བརྒྱད་ཅུ་རྩ་ལྔ།** (13602): "84,000 affliction latencies"—bag la nyal ( latency) indicates **subconscious tendencies**.

- **ཚོགས་པ་དཔག་ཏུ་མེད་པ་ཡོད་པས་ན་ཀུན་གཞི་ཞེས་བྱའོ།** (13612): "Called 'all-ground' because it has immeasurable gathered dharmas"—etymology of kun gzhi (ālaya) as **container of infinite factors**.

- **རྩ་འདབས་སུམ་ཅུ་རྩ་བརྒྱད་དང་དྲུག་ཅུ་ཐམ་པར་མཐོན་ནས།** (13620): "360 channel petals ascending"—rtsa 'dab (channel-petals) like **lotus petals** in chakra iconography.

- **འགྱུར་བྱེད་དང༌། རིང་ཐུང་དང༌། འཕར་བའི་ལས་བྱེད་པ་ནི་བདུན་ཅུ་རྩ་གཉིས་ཏེ།** (13636-13639): "Transformation, long-short, leaping functions are seventy-two"—enumerating **wind-functions** (rlung gi las).

**Numerical taxonomy:**
Complex enumeration:
- 84,000 afflictions
- 84,000 causes (me'i srin bu)
- 360 + 60 = 420 channels
- 72 wind functions

### [CONTEXT]
**Abhidharma affliction theory:**
The 84,000 afflictions correspond to:
- 21,000 from desire
- 21,000 from anger
- 21,000 from delusion
- 21,000 from equal combination

This is standard **Sarvāstivāda** numerology.

**Chakra anatomy:**
The 360 channel-petals reflect **Indian chakra theory**:
- 360 degrees in circle
- 360 days in year
- Channels as **temporal-physiological** correspondence

**Wind-function taxonomy:**
The 72 wind-functions (13636-13647) establish detailed **vāyu physiology**:
- Gyur byed (transforming)
- Ring thung (long-short)
- 'Phar ba (leaping)
- 'Ghyu ba (moving)
- 'Phrig pa (trembling)
- etc.

### [CONCEPT]
**Afflictions as somatic:**
The 84,000 afflictions each have:
- A cause (me'i srin bu)
- A letter (yi ge)
- A kāya (sku)
- A wisdom-being (ye shes sems dpa')

This establishes **afflictions are embodied** in srin bu (subtle channels), not merely mental.

**Ālaya as computational ground:**
The etymology at 13612 analyzes **kun gzhi** (ālaya) as containing **tshogs pa dpag tu med pa** (immeasurable gatherings). The ālaya is not merely "storehouse consciousness" but **computational substrate** containing all possible phenomenal factors.

**Channel-expansion as time:**
The 360 channels (13620) reflect **chronobiology**—the body contains **temporal cycles**. Channels expand like **lotus petals** from the crown, demonstrating **physiological unfolding in time**.
