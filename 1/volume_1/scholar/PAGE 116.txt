[4328-4357] [CONCEPT] Nature Tantra: Ground, Path, Result
**Analysis:** Lines 4328-4357 present the Nature Tantra (*rang bzhin gyi rgyud*):

**Essence [4329-4330, 4334-4336]:**
- Ground-path-result meaning
- Empty-clear awareness essence
- Ground-path-result gathered
- Pervading all, Buddha accomplishing nature

**Etymology [4332-4333, 4336]:**
- "Ground, path, result"
- "Definite as held"
- "Nature tantra"

**Three Divisions [4337-4340]:**
1. Ground nature tantra
2. Essence nature tantra
3. Essence nature tantra

**Ground Nature Tantra [4341-4346]:**
- Primordial pure awareness
- Essence, nature, compassion three
- Essence primordially pure: no ignorance name
- Delusion never even imputedly existed
- Nature spontaneously accomplished: awareness light clear
- Compassion pervading all: arising-source unceasing
- Samsara-nirvana any arising expanse abides

**Essence Nature Tantra [4347-4349]:**
- Wisdom three-tiered heart-essence manner arising
- Ground nature tantra potency
- Basis appearance eye
- Four lamps arise
- Path four appearances actually seen

**Essence Nature Tantra (continued) [4350-4357]:**
- Awareness appearance experienced result ripening
- Essence empty: not sign-having
- Permanent extreme-grasping not: entity established non-existence
- Empty self-appearance: extreme destroyed not
- Empty-clear indivisible: awareness essence
- Measure ripened: essence gathered
- Three clarities (*dangs ma gsum*)
