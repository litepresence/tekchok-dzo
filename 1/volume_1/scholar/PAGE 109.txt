[4005-4009] [CONCEPT] Thigle Self-Abiding and Two Lineages
**Analysis:** Lines 4005-4009 conclude the Instruction Section:

**Thigle Self-Abiding [4005-4006]:**
- Nature: without many, beyond word-proliferation
- Dharmatā universally pervading
- Characteristics: introduced recognition, certainty-confidence

**Two Divisions [4007-4009]:**
- *snyan rgyud* — Aural lineage
- *bshad rgyud* — Explanation lineage

[4010-4024] [STRUCTURE] Aural and Explanation Lineages
**Analysis:** Lines 4010-4024 present the two lineage types:

**Aural Lineage [4010-4014]:**
- Essence: one-to-one connection, particularly close
- Nature: ear faculty connection, uninterrupted by others
- Characteristics: unwavering, confident liberation
- With and without letters
- Dharmatā meaning instruction

**Explanation Lineage [4015-4024]:**
- Essence: causeless, no ground to reverse three realms
- Nature: luminosity without stain
- Characteristics: beyond investigation, three bodies five wisdoms intent
- Four circles:
  1. *phyi'i skor* — Outer circle
  2. *nang gi skor* — Inner circle
  3. *gsang ba'i skor* — Secret circle
  4. *gsang ba bla na med pa'i skor* — Unsurpassed secret circle

[4025-4039] [CONCEPT] Four Circles Detailed
**Analysis:** Lines 4025-4039 present the four circles:

**Outer Circle [4025-4028]:**
- Essence: afflictions not rejected, five poisons path-continuum
- Nature: no effort-accomplishment, whatever appears dharmatā
- Characteristics: no parts, emptiness nowhere divided

**Inner Circle [4029-4033]:**
- Essence: not material, signless dharmatā
- Nature: no going-coming, always abiding wisdom
- Characteristics: from firm aspect like root arising
  - From rolled aspect like trunk arising
  - From clear aspect like flower arising
  - From ripened aspect like fruit arising

**Secret Circle [4034-4038]:**
- Essence: introduction and realization simultaneous
- Hear-study-meditate all dependent
- Nature: breath-cease and buddhahood simultaneous
- No effort-accomplishment, no familiarization-force
- Characteristics: buddhahood and compassion simultaneous
- No accumulation, no merit
