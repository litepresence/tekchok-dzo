[14525-14561] [STRUCTURE] Pure Realm Lamp
The text presents the pure realm lamp (dbyings rnam dag gi sgron ma). The structure establishes: (1) essence as holding rigpa's own field, (2) display as the space-pervading expanse, and (3) recognition of the pure realm in space.

[14525-14561] [PHILOLOGY] Pure Realm Terminology
The term dbyings rnam dag (pure realm/expanse) describes the purified space of awareness. The compound rig pa'i rang yul (rigpa's own field) identifies its subjective aspect. The phrase nam mkha' (sky/space) establishes its objective aspect.

[14525-14561] [CONTEXT] Space Visualization
The text presents space itself as the pure realm lamp. When awareness is pure, space is recognized as the display of wisdom. This reflects the Dzogchen understanding that space is not empty but luminous.

[14525-14561] [CONCEPT] Space as Wisdom Display
The text conceptualizes space as the display of wisdom. What appears as empty space is actually filled with the light of awareness. The pure realm lamp reveals that space is the body of the Buddha.
