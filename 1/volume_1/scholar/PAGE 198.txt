**No Need for Effort:**
Ground naturally perfect—no need to create.
Ground primordially pure—no need to purify.
Ground spontaneously present—no need to accomplish.

From *Rang shar*:
"Ground no effort need
Naturally perfect
Primordially pure
Spontaneously present
Effortless thus"

**Why Practice Then?**
Practice not to create ground—practice to recognize ground.
Practice not to change ground—practice to manifest ground.
Practice: recognition, not production.

From *Thal 'gyur*:
"Practice not creates
Practice recognizes
Practice not changes
Practice manifests

Not production

From *De nyid*:
"Effort obstacle

Resisting natural

Subject object
Effort thus"
