[15001-15037] [STRUCTURE] Crown Wheel: Fruition Center
The text presents the crown wheel as the center of great bliss fruition. The structure establishes: (1) 360 channels of the crown wheel, (2) 2,009 subtle petals, and (3) the detailed enumeration of channels for wisdoms, body, rigpa, and thigle.

[15001-15037] [CONTEXT] Crown as Fruition Gateway
The text presents the crown as the gateway through which the result manifests. When practice matures, the crown channels fully open and wisdom flows. This reflects the tantric understanding of the crown as the seat of highest realization.

[15001-15037] [CONCEPT] Fruition as Channel Fullness
The text conceptualizes fruition as the fullness of channels. When all channels are open and wisdom flows freely, that is enlightenment. The result is not something new but the full manifestation of what is already present.
