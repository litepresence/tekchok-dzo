[13601-13647] [STRUCTURE] Afflictions and Channel Maturation
The text presents the 85,000 latent afflictions and their channel basis. The structure establishes: (1) 85,000 fire nāḍīs as the cause of afflictions, (2) three great collections totaling 306,666 phenomena, and (3) the ālaya as the basis of immeasurable collections.

[13601-13647] [PHILOLOGY] Affliction Terminology
The term nyon mongs pa bag la nyal (latent afflictions) describes dormant karmic traces. The compound srin bu (embryonic nāḍī) refers to subtle channels containing karmic seeds. The phrase 'bum phrag gsum dang khri brgya drug cu rtsa drug (306,666) provides precise numerical calculation.

[13601-13647] [CONTEXT] Karmic Physiology
The text presents a karmic physiology where physical channels store mental afflictions. The 85,000 channels correspond to the 84,000 categories of afflictions taught in Buddhism. This establishes the physical dimension of karma.

[13601-13647] [CONCEPT] Afflictions as Channel Blockages
The text conceptualizes afflictions not merely as mental states but as blockages in the channel system. Purifying channels purifies afflictions; clearing blockages liberates consciousness. The physical and psychological are integrated dimensions of the same reality.
