[15260-15296] [STRUCTURE] Result: Complete Fruition
The text presents the complete fruition of channel practice. The structure establishes: (1) bringing consciousness into the four-element net, (2) drawing wind inward as the pith instruction, and (3) the arising of bliss, stability, and special wisdom.

[15260-15296] [PHILOLOGY] Fruition Terminology
The term 'bras bu (result/fruition) establishes the completion phase. The compound bde ba skye ba (arising of bliss) identifies a fruition mark. The phrase khyad par gyi ye shes (special wisdom) describes the ultimate attainment.

[15260-15296] [CONTEXT] Completion Stage Fruition
The text presents the fruition of completion stage practice as the integration of bliss, clarity, and non-conceptuality. The four elements are gathered and transmuted. This reflects the tantric path of transformation reaching its goal.

[15260-15296] [CONCEPT] Fruition as Natural State
The text conceptualizes fruition not as achievement but as recognition of the natural state. Bliss, clarity, and non-conceptuality are not produced but revealed. The result is simply the natural state unobscured.
