[14340-14376] [STRUCTURE] Throat Wheel: Enjoyment Center
The text presents the throat wheel (mgrin pa longs spyod kyi 'khor lo) as the enjoyment center. The structure establishes: (1) throat as the water element center, (2) sixteen channels of the throat wheel, and (3) throat as the center of nourishment.

[14340-14376] [PHILOLOGY] Throat Center Terminology
The term mgrin pa longs spyod kyi 'khor lo (throat enjoyment wheel) identifies the viśuddha chakra. The compound chu'i 'byung ba (water element) establishes its elemental nature. The phrase longs spyod (enjoyment/use) describes its function.

[14340-14376] [CONTEXT] Throat as Nourishment Center
The text presents the throat as the center of nourishment and enjoyment. Food passes through the throat; so do words and mantra. This reflects the tantric understanding of the throat as the gateway of enjoyment.

[14340-14376] [CONCEPT] Throat as Enjoyment Gateway
The text conceptualizes the throat wheel as the gateway of enjoyment. Just as food is enjoyed through the throat, spiritual enjoyment (saṃbhoga) flows through this center. The throat transforms ordinary consumption into enlightened enjoyment.
