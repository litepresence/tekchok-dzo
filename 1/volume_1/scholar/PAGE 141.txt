[5414-5464] [STRUCTURE] Pronunciation Rules and Doubled Letters

**Analysis:** Lines 5414-5464 present detailed pronunciation rules for Tibetan stacked letters.

**Short Letter Rule [5414-5415]:**
*sa la nga btugs thung ba nyid / da la na btugs thung bar bshad* — "SA with ṄA affixed is short; DA with NA affixed is explained as short"

**Doubled Letter Pronunciation [5409-5412]:**
- *na ro gnyis brtsegs byung na stod de smad pa zhes bya* — "When two na-ro stacked, called 'top then bottom'"
- *ko'u zhes gcig zhabs kyur bod do* — "*ko'u* — one read with zhabs-kyu"
- *'greng bu gnyis brtsegs byung na 'greng ste bkug pa zhes bya* — "When two 'greng-bu stacked, called 'stretched then curved'"
- *ke'i zhes gcig gu gur bod do* — "*ke'i* — one read with gu-gu"

**Stacked Letter Class 3 [5513-5520]:**
*gsum pa brtsegs ma'i brjod lugs ni / srog mthun pa la sogs 'dra la sgra brjod na* — "Third: stacked pronunciation — vital force similar, etc., pronounce sound"

**Mantra Example [5520]:**
*o ko sho mi tri tsaṃ zhes pa'i* — OṂ KO ŚO MI TRI ṬAṂ
