[1393-1397] [PHILOLOGY] The Origin of Shelter and Shame
**Analysis:**

**Context:** Beings with vital force (*lus mdangs stobs*) engaging in sense-pleasure attachment (*dbang po sgo sbyor pa'i 'dod chags*)

**First Shame:**
- *dbyug pa dang boong ba* — Clubs and sticks
- *sa dang rdo ba* — Earth and stones
- *'phangs pa la ngo tshar* — Shame at throwing [them]

**First Construction:**
- *shing dang rdo* — Wood and stone
- *sk yor ba* — Piling up/protective barriers
- *'jag ma bkab pa* — Covered with thatch

**Etymology:**
- *'jag khyim skyil po* — Thatch-house dome
- First human dwellings arose from protection needs

[1398-1402] [CONTEXT] The Origin of Property, Theft, and Social Decline
**Analysis:**

**Agricultural Crisis:**
- Storing grain (*gsog 'jog*) led to scarcity
- Seeds failed to grow (*ma skyes pa*)

**First Ego/Ownership:**
- *nga rgyal can* — Arrogant one claimed: "This is mine"
- *ris su bcad pa* — Divided boundaries

**Karmic Consequences:**
- Merit-depleted beings (*bsod nams chung ba*) had poor fields
- Theft arose (*gzhan la brkus pa*)
- Five poisons manifested: desire, hatred, delusion, pride, jealousy
- Previous qualities submerged (*de yang nub bo*)

[1403-1406] [STRUCTURE] The First King
**Analysis:**

**Merit-Based Leadership:**
- *rigs bzang ba* — Noble-class being
- Divided surplus grain fairly (*'bras lhag ma cha bgos*)

**Election:**
- *rang gi skal ba chad pa* — Apportioned own share
- *thams cad mgu* — All pleased
- *rgyal por bskos* — Appointed as king

**Title:** *mang pos bkur ba'i rgyal po* — King honored by many

**Political Philosophy:** Kingship arises from merit and service, not force.

[1407-1412] [CONTEXT] Agricultural and Urban Development
**Analysis:**

**Technological Advances:**
- *shing gi thong pas ri la brkos* — Dug mountains with wooden plows
- *gze ka btab* — Sowed seeds
- *so ga* — First shoots appeared

**Urbanization:**
- *grong dang ljongs* — Villages and districts
- *grong khyer dpag tu med pa* — Countless cities

**Population Density:**
- Jambudvīpa covered with cities like "spreading ducks" (*bya gag spar ba'i tshod*)
- Good harvests (*lo legs pa*)
- Filled with beings and people

[1413-1419] [PHILOLOGY] The Origin of Lower Realms from Human Karma
**Analysis:**

**Mechanism:** *mi'i rten la dug gsum gyi las bsags pa'i dbang gis* — By power of accumulated three-poison actions on human basis

**Hell Beings:**
- Intense hatred (*zhe sdang las lhag par bsags*) → First hell-being
- *thog mar mnar med pa'i gnas su gshin rje'i rgyal por skyes* — Born as Yama, king of death, in Avīci hell

**Retinue:**
- Birds, dogs, hell-guardians (*bya dang khyi dang dmyal srung*)
- Many beings experiencing hell-suffering directly

**Eight Hot Hells:** Complete enumeration
**Eight Cold Hells:** Peripheral to hot hells

**Doxographical Note:** Lower realms don't exist independently—they manifest from collective human karma as the world-system evolves.
