[16000-16036] [STRUCTURE] Rainbow Body Fruition
The text presents the rainbow body ('ja' lus) as the supreme fruition of conduct. The structure establishes: (1) dissolution of the physical body into light, (2) attainment of deathless wisdom body, and (3) the highest accomplishment of Dzogchen.

[16000-16036] [PHILOLOGY] Rainbow Body Terminology
The term 'ja' lus (rainbow body) identifies the supreme attainment. The compound 'od kyi sku (light body) describes the nature of the result. The phrase 'chi med ye shes kyi sku (deathless wisdom body) establishes the ultimate form.

[16000-16036] [CONTEXT] Supreme Attainment
The text presents the rainbow body as the highest attainment in Dzogchen. It represents the complete transmutation of the physical into the luminous. This reflects the Dzogchen understanding of the body as the vehicle of enlightenment.
