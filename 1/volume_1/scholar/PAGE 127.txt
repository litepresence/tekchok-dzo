[4728-4758] [STRUCTURE] Method Division and Tantra Completeness

**Analysis:** Lines 4728-4758 present the threefold division of those made definite (*gtan la phab pa*) and establish tantra completeness.

**Progressive Listening Result [4728]:**
*mnyan pas gnas skabs kyi ye shes la spyod pa* — "By listening, acting in temporary wisdom"

This describes the immediate benefit of receiving teachings.

**Instruction Proliferation Warning [4729-4730]:**
- *man ngag gi spros pa tshig gi man ngag tshig tu 'chor bas don rang thog tu mi phebs* — "Instruction proliferation word instruction cast into words, meaning doesn't strike own peak"
- *gnad 'dus don gyi man ngag gis tshig la ma brten par sangs rgya ba* — "Crucial gathering meaning instruction without depending on words, Buddhahood"

**Key Distinction:**
- Word-dependent instructions (*tshig gi man ngag*) — conceptual
- Word-independent instructions (*don gyi man ngag*) — direct realization

[4785-4797] [CONCEPT] Alternative Organization by Capacity

**Analysis:** Lines 4785-4797 present alternative organizational schemes based on practitioner capacity.

**Five-Fold Organization:**

| Level | Organization | Description |
|-------|--------------|-------------|
| With elaboration | Ground and essence tantras | All drop sections |
| Middling | Seventeen tantras | Standard enumeration |
| Excellent | Self-arisen seed tantra alone | Single essence |
| Summit | Three liberations | Before-after explained |
| Ati Great Exposition | Multiple categories | Most comprehensive |

**Ati Great Exposition Categories [4789-4797]:**
- Meaning-word distinctions
- With elaboration: ground-essence
- Without elaboration: seventeen enumerations
- Very without elaboration: Vajra arrangement
- Very beyond elaboration
- Before-after three liberations

**Pedagogical Implications:**

The multiple organizational schemes demonstrate:
1. **Flexibility** — teachings adapted to different capacities
2. **Comprehensiveness** — multiple entry points to same truth
3. **Progressivity** — from elaborate to direct
4. **Authority** — grounded in *Thal 'gyur* and other scriptures

**Soteriological Function:**

| Capacity | Appropriate Teaching |
|----------|---------------------|
| Conceptual | Elaborate ground-essence tantras |
| Standard | Seventeen tantras |
| Direct | Self-arisen seed tantra |
| Supreme | Three liberations |

This accommodates the *three capacities* (*dbang po gsum*) framework: inferior, middling, and superior.
