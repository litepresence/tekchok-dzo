[4211-4223] [STRUCTURE] Method Tantra: Indicated Meaning
**Analysis:** Lines 4211-4223 present the Method Tantra (*thabs kyi rgyud*):

**Two Divisions [4211-4214]:**
1. Method tantra
2. Nature tantra

**First: Method Tantra [4215-4223]:**
- Awareness and its meaning experience instructions
- Two divisions:
  - Indicated meaning method tantra
  - Indicating method tantra

**Indicated Meaning Essence [4219-4220]:**
- Pervading all beings: awareness bodhicitta

**Etymology [4221-4223]:**
- Meaning showing, realization basis, liberation method
- Therefore called indicated meaning method tantra

[4224-4270] [CONTEXT] Mañjusrinamasamgiti Quotation
**Analysis:** Lines 4224-4270 quote extensively from *Mañjusrinamasamgiti*:

**Two Pervasions [4224-4225]:**
- Empty and clear

**Empty Pervasion [4226-4256]:**
- Awareness essence: all empty of entity and sign dharmas
- Abiding in great freedom from extremes
- Clear: empty not destroyed, self-light five wisdoms clear
- Warm, cool, light, spacious nature
- Five elements spontaneously accomplished
- Compassion tone unceasing, potency arises
- Awareness pervading primordially

**Clear Pervasion [4257-4270]:**
- Nature unceasing
- Clarifying appearance, pervading
- Clear, warm, cool, spacious, moving nature
- Pervasion and primordial tone self-clear
- Liberating ripening seed
- From empty reverse, clear pervades
- Simultaneous and self-liberated
- Self-arisen clear, body connected
- Stainless and individually clear
- Non-grasping clear object appears
- Five door consciousnesses self-clear perfect
- Body clear pervades, therefore tantra

**Key Verses:**
- "All Buddhas' method tantra"
- "From ground one essence distinction"
- "Indicated and indicating"
- "Indicated tantra pervading all"
