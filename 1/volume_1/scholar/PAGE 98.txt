[3650-3660] [CONCEPT] Second Mind-Aspect: Beyond Error and Obscuration
**Analysis:** Lines 3650-3660 present the second subdivision of mind-aspect:

**Core View [3650-3651]:**
- Eight stages (*rim pa brgyad*) conceptually examined (*blo brtag gzhi*) — erroneous dharmas
- Depending on spontaneously accomplished non-existence aspect (*med pa phyogs nyal*)
- Fall into existence aspect with extreme-grasping

**Line 3653-3654:**
- Fall into non-existence cause without self-arising
- This establishes mere word-desire (*'dod pa tshig tsam*)
- Nature beyond mind

**Key Insight [3655-3660]:**
- Through crucial point of reversing in cause-conditions
- Self-arisen without creation
- Unproduced cause, primordially pure
- Free from obscurations
- Called: Dharmatā

**No Departure from Dharmatā [3658-3660]:**
- No change-place from dharmatā
- Ground beyond causeless result
- Path beyond obscuration
- Unstained by good/evil actions
- Called: Ground (*gZhi*)

[3661-3671] [CONCEPT] Third Mind-Aspect: Reasoning that Collapses Samsara-Nirvana
**Analysis:** Lines 3661-3671 present the third subdivision:

**Characterization [3662]:**
- *sems kyi phyogs nges par shes pas 'khor 'das khungs brdibs pa* — Mind-aspect knowing definitively collapses the source of samsara-nirvana

**Fourfold Recognition (*ngo shes*) [3663-3670]:**

1. **Ground Nature [3663-3665]:**
   - Unfabricated in ground nature (*gZhi gnas lugs*)
   - Self-arisen wisdom shines universally
   - Nature from unproduced mind
   - Recognizing: no good/evil action

2. **Unceasing Play [3666-3667]:**
   - From unproduced mind, various appearances appear
   - Unceasing play (*rol pa 'gag pa med*)
   - Recognizing: dharmas of samsara-nirvana without acceptance

3. **Non-Dual Characteristics [3668]:**
   - Sign-characteristics of play-ground non-dual reasoning
   - Nature without distinction

4. **Naturally Liberated [3669-3670]:**
   - Non-dual dharmas not mentally imputed
   - Naturally liberated reasoning
   - All not departed from dharmatā

**Line 3671:**
- Ground mind-itself unfabricated
- Considered obscured by reasoning

[3672-3680] [CONCEPT] Fourth Mind-Aspect: Self-Arisen Wisdom Without Partiality
**Analysis:** Lines 3672-3680 present the fourth subdivision:

**Core View [3672-3674]:**
- Self-arisen wisdom's play pervades samsara-nirvana without partiality-falling
- Appearances imputedly force-arisen, unceasing
- No establishment of samsara-lower realms

**Nature Purity [3675-3677]:**
- Appearances different, not departed from nature-aspect-reasoning
- Called: Naturally pure
- Samsara expelled (*dong sprugs*)
- Pervasion of primordial perfection view

**Line 3678-3680:**
- Not falling into existence-non-existence, appearance-emptiness aspects
- Called: Dharmatā beyond action, bliss-pain

[3681-3691] [CONCEPT] Fifth and Sixth Mind-Aspects
**Analysis:** Lines 3681-3691 present the final two subdivisions:

**Fifth: Aspect-Holding Self-Established [3681-3685]:**
- No seeing in mind-aspect
- Appearances not divided into samsara-nirvana
- Not rejecting any aspect-holding entity-ground
- Abiding in expanse of view free from action-effort
- Neither existence nor non-existence, yet variously appearing
- Called: Self-liberated beyond action and conduct

**Sixth: Tenets Beyond Mind [3686-3691]:**
- Beyond mind, not established as entity
- Self-arisen appearances
- Self-liberated conceptual thought
- Appearance-meaning not established as true-false
- Abiding in extreme-free existence-non-existence
- Called: True-false not established as anything
