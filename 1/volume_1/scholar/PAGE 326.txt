## PAGE 326 [13380-13411]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **crown wheel completion** and **channel numerology synthesis**. Lines 13380-13411 establish:

1. **Mathematical correspondences** (13380-13407): 62 channels, 62 letters, 62 light-spheres, 124 parents
2. **Three hundred ten wisdom movements** (13406-13407)
3. **Wisdom-wind functions** (13407-13411): śamatha and vipaśyanā operations
4. **Existence-body as first** (13411): Srid pa lus kyi gzhi yin pas dang po

The structure creates a **computational cosmology** enumerating the subtle body's components.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Technical terminology:**

- **བརྒྱ་ཉེར་བཞི།** (13381): "One hundred twenty-four"—the sum of 62 channels × 2 (left-right). The term establishes **bilateral symmetry** in channel anatomy.

- **ཡེ་ཤེས་འགྱུ་བའི་ཁྱད་པར་སུམ་བརྒྱ་དང་བཅུ་ཐམ་པ།** (13386): "Three hundred ten varieties of wisdom-movement"—'gyu ba (movement) indicates the **dynamic nature** of wisdom-energies.

- **ཞི་གནས་དང་ལྷག་མཐོང་གི་བྱེད་པ་ངེས་པར་རྟོགས་པར་བྱེད།** (13388): "Definitely realizes the operations of śamatha and vipaśyanā"—byed pa (operation/function) specifies the **active dimension** of meditation.

- **སྐྱེད་བྱེད་ཅེས་བྱ་སྟེ་སྲིད་པ་ལུས་ཀྱི་གཞི་ཡིན་པས་དང་པོ་ཞེས་བྱའོ།** (13389): "Called 'producer' because it is the ground of existence-body, thus called 'first'"—etymological analysis linking generation (bskyed) and existence (srid).

- **རྐྱེན་ཚོགས་པ་བྱེ་བྲག་གི་ཆོས་དགུ་བརྒྱ་དང་སུམ་ཅུ་ཐམ་པ།** (13396): "930 particular condition-gathered dharmas"—chos (dharmas) here means **phenomenal factors** in Abhidharma sense.

**Particle analysis:**
The **enumerative** ཏེ། appears extensively for mathematical summation. The **causal-final** བྱས་པས། establishes **resultative numerology**—numbers emerge from conditions.

### [CONTEXT]
**Abhidharma integration:**
The enumeration of dharmas (chos) reflects **Sarvāstivāda** Abhidharma categories (75 dharmas) expanded to 930 in tantric physiology. The passage integrates **systematic Buddhist phenomenology** with tantric anatomy.

**Nyingma cosmology:**
The numbers reflect **Nyingma** subtle body theory:
- 62 channels = 31 major pairs
- 310 wisdoms = 5 elements × 62 channels
- 930 dharmas = 15 factors × 62 channels

**Embryological completion:**
Line 13389 identifies the **navel wheel** (skye byed 'khor lo) as **first** because it is the ground of existence-body. This establishes the **ontological priority** of the navel in tantric physiology.

### [CONCEPT]
**Numerology as mandala:**
The passage presents **sacred mathematics**: 62 + 62 + 62 + 124 = 310 wisdom-movements. These numbers are not arbitrary but represent **resonant frequencies** of enlightened energy. The body is literally a **mathematical mandala**.

**Śamatha and vipaśyanā as wind-functions:**
Line 13388 specifies that wisdom-wind movements (ye shes rlung gi 'phar ba dang shib pa) **realize** śamatha and vipaśyanā. This subverts standard presentations: meditation is not **done by** the practitioner but **occurs through** wind-energies when channels are open.

**The producer wheel as existence-ground:**
The etymology at 13389 analyzes **skye byed** (producer) as **srid pa lus kyi gzhi** (ground of existence-body). This establishes that **ordinary embodiment itself** is the first wheel—the foundation from which liberation emerges. There is no need to reject the body; it is already the **ground of awakening**.
