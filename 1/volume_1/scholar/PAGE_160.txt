**Analysis:** Lines 6282-6347 complete the cyclical narrative of teaching transference:

**Seven Cycles Completion [6282-6284]:**
Thus seven flourishing and declining cycles completed, after life's ten years, teaching's three supports do not remain in this world.

**Transfer South [6282-6284]:**
From here south to Beautiful-array (*mdzes ldan bkod pa*) Buddhafield, teaching descends, with seven flourishing and declining cycles completing that field's benefit.

**Saha Emptiness [6284]:**
This is the Saha world's empty time — its destruction.

**Transfer North [6285]:**
The three supports transfer north to Vast-protection (*yangspy ob*) world.

**Buddha Utpala-flower-beautiful [6285-6286]:**
This Buddha holds the teaching, and after seven flourishing and declining cycles, transfers west to Crested-ornament-sound-vast (*gtsug phud dbyangs yangs*) world.

**Buddha Cyclic-existence-destroyer [6286-6291]:**
Holds the teaching for seven hundred thousand years of flourishing and declining. Afterward, all beings of that field and Vajradhara's arranged Brahma aeon are liberated in the original ground.

**Dissolution [6290-6291]:**
Vajradhara's arranged Brahma aeon, container and contents with thousand, becomes empty — space becomes one, for eighty-five aeons.

**Three Supports Disappear [6292-6295]:**
Teaching's three supports, Vajradhara's great deeds completed, dissolve into dharmatā's peaceful expanse, becoming invisible.

**Cyclic Renewal [6296-6304]:**
Then again, as beings appear from expanse, emanation arranges Brahma. From six hundred thousand aeons to taming beings' appearance, taming teacher comes. From Samantabhadra's blessing, teaching's three supports self-arise, without flourishing and decline, benefiting beings.
