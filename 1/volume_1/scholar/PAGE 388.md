## [STRUCTURE]

**Text Architecture:**
- **Lines 1-2**: Consequence of missing four lamps - perpetual samsara
- **Lines 2-5**: Sgron ma bzhi as path structure - gzhi/smin/lam/'bras correlation
- **Lines 5-10**: Rin po che bum pa metaphor - treasure vase physiology
- **Lines 10-24**: Alternative rnam grangs (nomenclature) - etymological elaboration

**Sequential Logic:**
1. Negative consequence (lines 1-2): Missing sgron ma → perpetual 'khor ba
2. Positive structure (lines 2-5): Present sgron ma → complete path
3. Physiological metaphor (lines 5-10): Rin po che bum pa
4. Alternative nomenclature (lines 10-24):
   - Rgyang zhags derivation
   - Sgron ma fire element correlation
   - Thig le nature
   - Stong pa emptiness

**Fourfold Correlation:**
- Gzhi smin: Maturation of ground
- Byed dbang bzhi: Four empowering blessings
- Lam snang ba bzhi: Four lights of path
- 'Bras bu sku bzhi: Four kayas as result

## [CONTEXT]

**Soteriological Structure:**
The text presents **complete path architecture**:

**Ground (gzhi)**:
- Sgron ma bzhi present in all beings (line 2)
- Don dam gyi 'byung bzhi (line 12) as basis

**Maturation (smin)**:
- Byed dbang bzhi (line 3) - empowerments ripen capacity
- Rang rgyud sbyangs (line 3) - continuum purification

**Path (lam)**:
- Snang ba bzhi tshad du phyin (line 4) - four lights reaching measure
- Four lamps as path markers

**Result ('bras bu)**:
- Sku bzhi dbyings su 'khyil (line 4) - four kayas dissolved in field
- Thar pa'i byang chub (line 1) - liberating enlightenment

**Physiological Metaphor:**
**Rin po che bum pa** (lines 5-10) maps four lamps to body:
1. **Snying gi bum pa**: Heart vase
2. **Rang byung gi sgron me**: Self-arisen lamp-fire like sky
3. **Bar gyi kha'i 'brel thag**: Throat connection-cord
4. **Phyi'i nam mkha' chen po**: Outer great sky
5. **Ka dag dbyings kyi grol sar ma bu sprad**: Mother-child meeting at liberation site
6. **Bar do rang gsal**: Self-luminous bardo
7. **Rdoz bcas lus kyi bum pa chag**: Material body vase shatters
8. **Lhun grub rin po che**: Spontaneously accomplished jewel
9. **Gsang ba'i sbubs la zang thal**: Direct penetration into secret cavity

**Alternative Nomenclature (rnam grangs):**
The passage provides **fourfold etymological analysis**:

**Rgyang zhags** (lines 13-18):
- Rgyang: Gzugs kyi rnam pa rgyang ring po nas mthong (seeing forms from far)
- Also: 'Byung ba rlung las byung bas skyod pa thogs pa med (unobstructed movement)
- Zhags: Rgyang ring po'i gzugs 'dzin (grasping distant forms)
- Chu: Kha dog dang dbyibs la 'jug pa 'byung ba chu'i cha (entering color/shape through water element)
- Sgron ma: Dbang po dang rigs dang longs spyod la mched (expanding to faculties, families, enjoyments) = me (fire)

**Thig le** (lines 19-22):
- Gzhi gnas kyi ye shes: Wisdom of ground-abiding
- Ka dag gi ye shes su gnas: Abides as pristine wisdom
- Rang bzhin lhun grub: Naturally spontaneous
- Spros bral phyogs ris las 'das: Beyond elaboration/directions
- Kun khyab kyi ye shes: All-pervasive wisdom
- Mi 'gyur ba'i gzhi: Unchanging ground

## [CONCEPT]

**Thag chod (Decisive Certainty):**
The text emphasizes **certainty as prerequisite** (line 1):
- Not merely knowing (ngo shes) but decisive certainty (thag chod)
- Combined with nyams su blangs (taking into experience)
- Result: Thar pa'i byang chub (liberating enlightenment)

**Rin po che bum pa Metaphor:**
This is **body-as-vase** physiology:
- **Snying gi bum pa**: Heart as container
- **Sgron me**: Lamp-fire within
- **Ma bu sprad**: Mother-child union - technical term for recognition meeting ground
- **Zang thal**: Direct penetration - non-obstructed breakthrough
- **Rdoz bcas lus chag**: Material body shatters → lhun grub revealed

**Four Elements and Lamps:**
The **ultimate four elements** (don dam gyi 'byung bzhi, line 12) correlate with four lamps:
- Not coarse elements but purified basis
- Each lamp emerges from specific elemental transformation

**Thig le Ontology:**
**Ground-abiding wisdom** (line 19-22):
- Despite appearing manifest (mngon du snang), abides as ka dag
- Spontaneously accomplished without direction
- All-pervasive, unchanging ground
- This is **thig le as ontological foundation**, not merely practice technique

**Stong pa Introduced:**
Line 23 begins stong pa analysis (continued on next page):
- Mtshan nyid 'dzin pa'i ye shes: Characteristic-grasping wisdom
- Dngos po rang rgyud du 'dzin: Grasping entities as having own-continuum
