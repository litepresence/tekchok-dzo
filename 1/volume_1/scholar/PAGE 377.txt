[15519-15555] [STRUCTURE] Fire Element and Equality Wisdom
The text presents the fire element (me yi 'byung ba) and its result as equality wisdom. The structure establishes: (1) fire channels as the basis for warmth and transformation, (2) fire as the agent of digestion and metabolism, and (3) purified fire becoming the wisdom of equality.

[15519-15555] [PHILOLOGY] Fire Wisdom Terminology
The term me yi 'byung ba (fire element) identifies the fire element. The compound mnyam nyid ye shes (equality wisdom) establishes its enlightened form. The phrase drod (warmth/heat) describes its essential quality.

[15519-15555] [CONTEXT] Fire as Transformation
The text presents fire as the element of transformation and digestion. All metabolic processes depend on fire. When purified, fire becomes the wisdom that recognizes all phenomena as equal in emptiness.

[15519-15555] [CONCEPT] Equality as Fire's Gift
The text conceptualizes the wisdom of equality as fire's enlightened quality. Just as fire treats all fuel equally—burning whatever it encounters—equality wisdom recognizes all phenomena as equal in nature.
