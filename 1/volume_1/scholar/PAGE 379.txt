[15593-15629] [STRUCTURE] Earth Element and Mirror-Like Wisdom
The text presents the earth element (sa yi 'byung ba) and its result as mirror-like wisdom. The structure establishes: (1) earth channels as the basis for solidity and form, (2) earth as the agent of support and stability, and (3) purified earth becoming mirror-like wisdom.

[15593-15629] [PHILOLOGY] Earth Wisdom Terminology
The term sa yi 'byung ba (earth element) identifies the earth element. The compound me long lta bu'i ye shes (mirror-like wisdom) establishes its enlightened form. The phrase brtan pa (stability) describes its essential quality.

[15593-15629] [CONTEXT] Earth as Reflection
The text presents earth as the element that provides the ground for reflection. Just as a mirror requires a stable base, mirror-like wisdom requires the stability of earth. When purified, earth becomes the wisdom that reflects all without distortion.

[15593-15629] [CONCEPT] Mirror-Like as Earth's Gift
The text conceptualizes mirror-like wisdom as earth's enlightened quality. Just as the earth supports all that exists upon it without discrimination, mirror-like wisdom reflects all phenomena without bias or distortion.
