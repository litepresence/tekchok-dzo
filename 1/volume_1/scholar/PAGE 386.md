   - Nang ye shes: internal wisdom

## [CONTEXT]

**Etymological Framework:**
The text uses **component analysis** (sbyor ba) for each lamp name:

**Shes rab rang byung gi sgron ma** (lines 5-11):
1. **Shes** (line 7): Wind abiding - channel-based
2. **Rab** (line 8): Wind numerical - projection/retraction count
3. **Rang byung** (line 10): Self-arisen - three-moment liberation
4. **Sgron ma** (line 11): Wind exhausted in dbyings

**Temporal Structure:**
- **Skad cig gsum** (line 10): Three moments
  - Classical Abhidharma measurement
  - Indicates instantaneous liberation

**Wisdom Taxonomy:**
**Zad par gyi ye shes** (line 19):
- Includes: yi ge, phyag mtshan, sku, sa bcas (letters, hand-symbols, forms, earth, etc.)
- Exhausts with 'khrul pa - cannot appear with delusion to senses
- "Mngon par snang" - directly manifests

**Dbyings rnam dag Structure:**
- **'Od lnga'i 'char gzhi** (line 29): Five-light emergence-basis
- **'Byung ba rdos bcas dag pa** (line 30): Elemental purity with substance
- **Rdoz bcas 'od lus su bsgyur** (line 31): Transform substance into light body

## [CONCEPT]

**Thig le Phenomenology:**
The **stong pa** (emptiness) of thig le is **protective** (line 3):
- 'Byung bzhi'i gnod pa med: No harm from four elements
- Rang snang: Self-appearing (autonomous)
- Bstan yang mi gzhan gyis mi mthong: Even if shown, others cannot see
- **'Od gsal**: Luminosity beyond extremes

**Shes rab Rang byung Mechanics:**
The self-arisen wisdom operates through **wind-channel dynamics**:
1. **Wind abiding** (gnas pa): Stability in channels
2. **Numerical projection/retraction**: Measured energetic movement
3. **Three-moment liberation**: Temporal collapse of binding
4. **Exhaustion in field**: Final dissolution

**Zad par gyi ye shes:**
This is **exhaustion wisdom** - not mere absence but **transformation**:
- All 'khrul snang (deluded appearances) exhausted
- Yi ge (seed syllables), phyag mtshan (hand-symbols), sku (forms) appear
- Sa lasogs pa bcus (ten elements) manifest at exhaustion time
- Cannot appear with 'khrul pa to dbang po (senses)

**Sgron ma Function:**
The lamps as **mun pa sel ba** (darkness clearing):
- Thig le stong pa: 'Khor ba'i mun pa (samsaric darkness)
- Shes rab rang byung: Sems sems byung gi mun pa (mental darkness)
- Dbyings rnam dag: [implied continuation]

**Ontological Distinction:**
- **Rigs pa'i gzhi snang shes rab dang bcas pa** (line 20): Reason-basis appearance with wisdom
- **Gzhan nas ma byung bar rang las shar** (line 20): Not arising from elsewhere, self-arising
- **'Brel pa grub pa** (line 21): Established connection - dependent origination
