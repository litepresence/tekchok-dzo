[1246-1248] [PHILOLOGY] Brahma's Ministers (*tshangs pa mdun na 'don*)
**Analysis:**

**Etymology:** *tshangs pa mdun na 'don* (Brahma Before Recites/Ministers) because:
- *de dag gi mdun na kha don byed pa re yod pas* — There is one doing speech-recitation before them
- These are the assistants/retainers who recite Brahma's praises

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 1 league (*dpag tshad gcig*)
- Lifespan: 1 intermediate eon (*bar bskal gcig*)

This completes the pattern of decreasing size: 2.5 → 1 leagues

[1249-1251] [CONTEXT] Brahma's Retinue (*tshangs pa*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che si ka ta* — "Jewel Sikata"

**Etymology:** *tshangs pa* (Brahma) because:
- *dge ba'i las la tshangs par spyod pas* — Dwelling brahma-like in virtuous action
- This represents ordinary Brahma beings, not the "Great Brahma"

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 0.5 league (*phyed*)
- Lifespan: 0.5 intermediate eon

**Structural Significance:** The fractional dimensions indicate approaching the threshold between form and desire realms.

[1252-1258] [STRUCTURE] Transition to the Desire Realm (*'dod pa'i khams*)
**Analysis:**

**Major Division:** From two [categories], the desire realm has six god races (*lha rigs drug*)

**First Desire Heaven:** *gzhan 'phrul dbang byed* — Other-Emanation-Power (Paranirmitavaśavartin)

**Heaven Name:** *dpag bsam gyi shing rin po che rab tu rgyas pa* — "Wish-fulfilling Tree Jewel Greatly Expanded"

**Arising:** Some Brahma beings expand, falling from previous excellent accumulations (*sngon gyi bsags pa'i khyad kyis lhung ba*)

**Etymology:** *gzhan 'phrul dbang byed* (Other-Emanation-Power) because:
- *lus dang longs spyod kyi 'byor ba gzhan las sprul par snang ba la dbang* — Power over emanated appearances of body and enjoyment from others
- They control what others emanate

**Calculation Method:** The shift to human years indicates transition to the "grosser" desire realm:
- Human years: 1,600
- One human day = one god year
- God lifespan: 16,000 years

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 2.5 leagues (*rgyang grags phyed dang gnyis*)
- Alternative measurement: 625 bow-lengths (*gzhu 'dom*)

[1259-1263] [PHILOLOGY] Emanation-Joy Heaven (*'phrul dga'*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *dpag bsam gyi shing mthong mdzes* — "Wish-fulfilling Tree See Beautiful"

**Etymology:** *'phrul dga'* (Emanation Joy) because:
- *lus kyi 'od las bu brgya'am stong ngam dpag tu med par sprul nas rtse dga' byed pas* — Emanating hundreds, thousands, or measureless sons from body-light, doing play-joy
- They create their own enjoyment through emanation

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 1.25 leagues (*rgyang grags gcig dang bzhi cha*)
- Or: 625 bow-lengths

**Lifespan Calculation:**
- Human years: 800
- God years: 8,000

[1264-1267] [CONTEXT] Joy-Possess Heaven (*dga' ldan*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *dpag bsam gyi shing mig mdzes* — "Wish-fulfilling Tree Eye Beautiful"

**Etymology:** *dga' ldan* (Joy Possess/Tuṣita) because:
- *dga' bde dam pa'i chos la spyod pa* — Dwelling in supreme joy-bliss dharma
- This is where bodhisattvas dwell before final birth

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 1 league (*rgyang grags gcig*)
- Or: 500 bow-lengths

**Lifespan:**
- Human years: 400
- God years: 4,000

**Doxographical Significance:** The Tuṣita heaven is where:
- The future Buddha Maitreya currently dwells
- Śākyamuni dwelt before his final birth
- Bodhisattvas complete their final accumulations

**Transition Pattern:** Notice the consistent halving:
- Lifespan: 16,000 → 8,000 → 4,000 god years
- Body size decreasing progressively
