[15556-15592] [STRUCTURE] Water Element and Discriminating Wisdom
The text presents the water element (chu yi 'byung ba) and its result as discriminating wisdom. The structure establishes: (1) water channels as the basis for circulation and cohesion, (2) water as the agent of fluidity and connection, and (3) purified water becoming the wisdom of discrimination.

[15556-15592] [PHILOLOGY] Water Wisdom Terminology
The term chu yi 'byung ba (water element) identifies the water element. The compound so sor rtog pa'i ye shes (discriminating wisdom) establishes its enlightened form. The phrase gsal ba (clarity) describes its essential quality.
