[2485-2525] [STRUCTURE] Sutra and Mantra Organization
**Analysis:**

**The Three Baskets (*sde snod gsum*):**

All Word (*bka'*) gathered in:
- Sutra (*mdo*)
- Discipline (*'dul ba*)
- Abhidharma (*mngon pa*)

**Mantra as Manifest Great:**
- Sutra, Discipline, Abhidharma — these three equal 21,000 teachings
- Total: 84,000 dharma heaps (*chos kyi phung po*)

**Pedagogical Principle:**

From these, divided regarding each antidote for taming beings' thought accumulations — extensively many, inconceivable, transcending expression.

**Nine Vehicles Framework:**

Longchenpa establishes the detailed nine-vehicle schema:

**Sutra Vehicles (*mdo'i theg pa*):**
1. Hearer (*nyan thos*)
2. Self-enlightened (*rang sangs rgyas*)
3. Bodhisattva (*byang chub sems dpa'*)

**Outer Tantra (*phyi rgyud*):**
4. Kriya (*bya rgyud*)
5. Upa (Charya) (*spyod rgyud*)
6. Yoga (*rnal 'byor rgyud*)

**Inner Tantra (*nang rgyud*):**
7. Maha (*ma hA*)
8. Anu (*a nu*)
9. Ati (*a ti*) — Dzogchen

**Two-fold Simplification:**

Generally taught as:
- Great and small vehicles (*theg pa chen po dang chung ngu*)
- Characteristic vehicle (*mtshan nyid kyi theg pa*)
- Effect Vajra Vehicle (*'bras bu rdo rje'i theg pa*)

This structure provides the organizational framework for the detailed presentations to follow.
