[1107-1110] [PHILOLOGY] The Realm of Nothing-Perception (*'du shes med pa'i snyoms 'jug*)
**Analysis:**

**Technical Designations:**
From the upper realm where "only perception exists" (*'du shes tsam du yod*), beings expand into:
- *ci yang med pa* — Nothing whatsoever
- *cung zad med pa* — Slightly nothing

These correspond to the third and fourth formless absorptions:
- *ci yang med pa* = Nothingness (*akiñcanya*)
- *cung zad med pa* = Neither perception nor non-perception (*naivasaṃjñānāsaṃjñāyatana*)

**Grammar Note:** *tsam* (only/merely) with *du* creates an adverbial phrase indicating exclusive existence of perception without content.

[1111-1117] [CONCEPT] The Mechanics of Cosmic Devolution
**Analysis:**

**Principle:** *ting nge 'dzin gyi snang ba bzang ba ni ting nge 'dzin bzang ba las skye* — Good samādhi appearances arise from good samādhi.

**Progressive Degradation:**
As beings "go increasingly bad below" (*'og mar je ngan du song ba*):
1. Emanation bodies (*lus sprul*) become smaller (*lus bongs chung*)
2. Light brilliance diminishes (*'od mdangs kyang chung*)
3. Designated with "low denying sound" (*dman pa dgag pa'i sgra*)

**Technical Term:** *skye mched* (āyatana) — The sense-bases arise from expanded upper appearances.

**Affliction Status:**
- *nyon mongs pa mngon du rgyu med* — Afflictions not manifestly active
- *phra ba bag la nyal du gnas* — Dwell in subtle latency

This describes the form realm gods whose attachment is subtle but present as latent tendencies.

[1118] [CONTEXT] The Jewel Box Samādhi (*rin po che za ma tog*)
**Analysis:**

These beings dwell in the "Jewel Box" samādhi—an enclosure of subtle enjoyment where:
- Sensory experience is present but refined
- Attachment exists as latency (*bag la nyal*)
- The "box" metaphor suggests containment and limitation

**Dzogchen Parallel:** This corresponds to the "all-ground of habitual tendencies" (*bag chags kyi kun gzhi*) in Dzogchen psychology.

[1119-1128] [CONTEXT] Doctrinal Citations and Synthesis
**Analysis:**

Longchenpa identifies this as "noble tenet arrangement from common vehicle" (*theg pa mthun mong las rnam gzhag 'phags pa'i grub mtha'*), then connects it to Dzogchen:

**Citation 1: Guhyagarbha Tantra (*gsang snying*)**
- Line [1123]: *e ma ho bde gshegs snying po las* — "E ma ho from Sugata heart"
- Line [1124]: *rang gi rnam rtog las kyi sprul* — "Self-thought karma emanation"

**Citation 2: Condensed Perfection of Wisdom (*sdud pa*)**
- Line [1127]: *rkyen rnams 'tshogs na las kyi 'khrul 'khor rab tu 'byung* — "When conditions gather, karma's confusion-wheel arises"

**Synthesis Strategy:** Longchenpa uses common vehicle (abhidharmic) cosmology as the framework, then reveals its Dzogchen interpretation through tantric citations.

[1129-1133] [PHILOLOGY] The Dzogchen Critique: New Birth vs. Primordial Purity
**Analysis:**

**Paradox:** *sems can gsar skye 'dra yang gsar skyes ma yin* — "Like new-born beings, yet not new-born."

**Dzogchen Ontology:**
1. *rig pa la mtshungs ldan ma rig pas* — Ignorance co-endowed (*mtshungs ldan*) with awareness
2. *lhan skyes su dbyings kyi cha de la sgrib pa nyid* — Together-born obscuration of that space-portion
3. *dang po dag pa* — Primordially pure from the first
4. *phyis 'khrul pa ma yin* — Not confused later

**Critical Insight:** Confusion arises through:
- *rkyen glo bur bas* — Adventitious conditions (not inherent)
- *dngos su gsal bar byas pa tsam* — Only made actually clear

**Etymological Note:** *dbyings* (space/expanse) is explained as expressing the four elements (*'byung bzhi*), connecting cosmic space to elemental manifestation.

**Philosophical Significance:** This establishes the Dzogchen view that confusion is adventitious (*glo bur*) and purification reveals primordial purity (*ka dag*) — a position distinct from gradualist views that see purification as removing inherent defilements.

[1134-1139] [STRUCTURE] Descent to the Mind-Activity Realm
**Analysis:**

**Spatial Transition:** 30,000 leagues below (*dpag tshad stong phrag sum cu'i 'og na*)

**New Realm:** *yid 'byed rnam par rol pa* — Mind-activity completely-play

**Cosmic Feature:**
- *'khor lo dpag tu med pa 'phro zhing* — Immeasurable extending wheels
- *gcig la gcig phrad pa'i sgra rang 'byung ba* — Self-arising sound of mutual contact

**Process of Fall:**
1. Upper beings go to "one direction calm abiding" (*zhi gnas phyogs gcig tu song ba*)
2. Some fall below (*'og tu 'ga' tsam lhung nas*)
3. Mutual consciousness-only contact (*gcig la gcig rnam par shes pa tsam*)

**Doxographical Context:** This describes the transition from the highest form realm into progressively grosser states, establishing the mechanism for the arising of dualistic perception through contact (*phrad pa*), a key concept in dependent origination (*pratītyasamutpāda*).

**Structural Transition:** Having explained the philosophical framework for the arising of beings, Longchenpa will now describe the specific evolution of realms and bodies as descent continues.
