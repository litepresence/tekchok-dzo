[3691-3708] [STRUCTURE] Mind Section: Summary and Scriptures
**Analysis:** Lines 3691-3708 summarize the Mind Section's literature:

**Line 3691:**
- *sloka 'bum phrag nyer gcig* — Twenty-one hundred thousand ślokas

1. *chos nyid la brjod du med pa'i klong* — Expanse of dharmatā inexpressible
2. *lta ba ma yin pa med pa'i klong* — Expanse without non-view
3. *spyod pa la yin pa med pa'i klong* — Expanse without existence in conduct

3. *chos nyid ngo mnyam tha dad med pa'i 'phyong* — Chong of dharmatā equal, non-different

[3709-3720] [CONCEPT] Expanse Section: Result as Primordial Liberation
**Analysis:** Lines 3709-3720 introduce the Expanse Section (*klong sde*):

**Essence [3709-3710]:**
- *'bras bu ye grol du smra ba'i rdzogs pa chen po klong gi sde* — Expanse Section Great Perfection asserting result as primordial liberation
- Various existence arising, non-existence result primordially pure

**Four Divisions [3711-3715]:**
1. *klong nag po rgyu med du smra ba* — Black Expanse asserting causeless
2. *khra bo sna tshogs su smra ba* — Variegated asserting various
3. *dkar po sems su smra ba* — White asserting as mind
4. *rab 'byams rgyu 'bras la bzla ba'i sde* — Vast section reversing in cause-result

**Black Expanse Essence [3716-3720]:**
- Unchanging, self-perfect, directionlessly arising
- Not depending on dharmas themselves
- Three divisions:
  - *mdzad pa klong nag gi sde* — Black expanse of activity
  - *thugs rje klong nag gi sde* — Black expanse of compassion
  - *sprul pa klong nag gi sde* — Black expanse of emanation

[3721-3730] [CONCEPT] Black Expanse: Activity and Compassion
**Analysis:** Lines 3721-3730 present first two black expanse divisions:

**Activity Expanse [3721-3724]:**
- Various arising causelessly
- Appearances without border (*ris med*)
- Arising-place empty, appearance-place liberated
- Samsara-nirvana nameless
- Neither existence nor non-existence aspect-reasoning
- Establishes Great Perfection view beyond extremes

**Compassion Expanse [3725-3730]:**
- Arising is wisdom, knowledge not depending on other
- Liberation is mind, reversing in cause-conditions
- Arising-liberation non-dually abiding
- Result primordially liberated
- Harmonious-appearance self-pure reasoning
- Great Perfection liberated at appearance-place
