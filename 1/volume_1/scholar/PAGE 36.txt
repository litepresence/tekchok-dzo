[1221-1222] [PHILOLOGY] Virtue Increase Heaven (*dge rgyas*) Dimensions
**Analysis:**

**Etymology:** *dge rgyas* (Virtue Increase) because arisen from expanded virtue roots (*dge rtsa rgyas pa las byung bas*).

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 64 leagues (*dpag tshad drug cu rtsa bzhi pa*)
- Lifespan: 64 intermediate eons (*bar bskal de snyed*)

**Pattern Continuation:** The 64:64 ratio maintains the numerical correspondence between body size and lifespan, though the halving pattern is now becoming less strict.

[1223-1227] [CONTEXT] Measureless Virtue Heaven (*tshad med dge*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che in dra nI las rnam par spras pa* — "Variously Ornamented by Jewel Indra"

**Etymology:** *tshad med dge* (Measureless Virtue) because:
- Established from measureless virtue roots (*dge rtsa tshad med las grub pa*)
- Arisen from those cut from above realms

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 32 leagues
- Lifespan: 32 intermediate eons

**Variant Reading:** Line [1227] notes that some sources place Measureless Virtue above Virtue Increase, indicating textual variation in cosmological ordering.

[1228-1231] [PHILOLOGY] Small Virtue Heaven (*dge chung*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che pa tang ka ling gis rnam par spras pa* — "Variously Ornamented by Jewel Patangkaling"

**Etymology:** *dge chung* (Small Virtue) because:
- Dwelling in small virtue-fruit compared to above (*gong ma bas dge ba'i 'bras bu chung ba la spyod pas*)
- Progressive diminishment of merit as descent continues

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 16 leagues
- Lifespan: 16 intermediate eons

**Geometric Progression:** The halving pattern resumes: 64 → 32 → 16

[1232-1234] [CONCEPT] Clear Light Heaven (*'od gsal*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *nor bu rin po che ke ke ru rnam par spras pa* — "Variously Ornamented by Jewel Kekiru"

**Etymology:** *'od gsal* (Clear Light) because:
- Body possesses clear light (*lus la 'od gsal ba*)
- Progressive transition from virtue-based to luminosity-based naming

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 8 leagues
- Lifespan: 8 intermediate eons

**Dzogchen Connection:** The term *'od gsal* anticipates the Dzogchen concept of clear light nature of mind, though here referring to bodily radiance.

[1235-1237] [PHILOLOGY] Measureless Light Heaven (*tshad med 'od*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che dpal brtsegs* — "Jewel Glory Pile"

**Etymology:** *tshad med 'od* (Measureless Light) because:
- Body's light is measureless (*lus kyi 'od la tshad med pas*)
- Thematic shift to light-based naming continues

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 4 leagues
- Lifespan: 4 intermediate eons

**Numerical Pattern:** Halving continues: 16 → 8 → 4

[1238-1241] [CONTEXT] Small Light Heaven (*'od chung*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che dpa' ba'i 'od* — "Jewel Brave Light"

**Etymology:** *'od chung* (Small Light) because:
- Body's light is small compared to above (*lus kyi 'od gong ma las chung ba*)
- Diminishment of luminosity correlates with approaching desire realm

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 2 leagues
- Lifespan: 2 intermediate eons

[1242-1244] [PHILOLOGY] Great Brahma Heaven (*tshangs chen pa*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che ke ru dza* — "Jewel Keruja"

**Etymology:** *tshangs chen pa* (Great Brahma) because:
- *'dod pa'i skyon las tshangs pas* — Brahma [free] from desire-fault
- Marks transition to Brahma-class heavens
- Brahma here represents purity from desire realm attachment

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 2.5 leagues (*phyed dang gnyis*)
- Lifespan: 2.5 intermediate eons

**Structural Significance:** The appearance of fractional dimensions (*phyed* = half) indicates approaching the boundary where form realm transitions to desire realm.

[1245] [STRUCTURE] Transition to Brahma's Retinue
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che sa mi ka* — "Jewel Samika"

**Arising:** Some cut from Great Brahma (*tshangs chen las 'ga' zhig chad pa*)

**Doxographical Context:** This begins the sub-heavens of the Brahma realm, indicating hierarchical structure within the lowest form realm heaven before transitioning to the desire realm.

**Summary Pattern:** Page 36 completes the systematic enumeration of form realm heavens from Virtue Increase (64 leagues) down to Great Brahma (2.5 leagues), demonstrating the graduated nature of existence from subtle to gross, from long lifespan to short, from virtue-based to light-based to Brahma-based classification.
