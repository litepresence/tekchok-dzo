[5187-5206] [STRUCTURE] Vowel System Architecture
**Analysis:** Lines 5187-5206 continue the analysis of the vowel system (*'dogs*) architecture:

**Visual Reference [5187]:**
The line *yi ge 'di'i rigs rnams zur du gzigs par zhu* indicates the presence of a marginal illustration (*zur du gzigs pa*) showing letter forms, labeled as "Stacked Letters 1" (*brtsegs yig 1*).

**Technical Definitions:**
- *yi ge'i ngo bo nyis brtsegs ni 'dogs yin pas rtsa ba'i 'dogs la* — double-stacked letter nature is vowel, therefore root vowel
- *na ro la sogs pa ni yan lag ces bya* — na-ro etc. are called branches
- *'dogs kyi 'dogs zhes bya'o* — called appendix of appendix

The text presents complex visual examples of vowelled letters [5186, 5191] showing the graphical representation of Sanskrit letters in Tibetan script.

**Hermeneutical Framework [5207-5227]:**
The text presents the two-fold samsara-nirvana hermeneutic:

**Samsara Explanation (*'khor ba'i bshad lugs*) [5209-5212]:**
- Letters without vowels (*'dogs med pa'i yi ge*) represent samsara's dharmas
- Base letters like KA represent samsara's arising from primordial expanse
- Accompanied by afflictions (*nyon mongs pa'i tshogs*) and ignorance (*ma rig pa*)

**Nirvana Explanation (*mya ngan las 'das pa'i bshad lugs*) [5213-5227]:**
- Whatever vowels and branches arise represent nirvana
- When vowels arise: appearance-emptiness (*snang stong*) as dual pairs
- When branches arise: five kayas (*sku lnga*), five wisdoms (*ye shes lnga*), five lights (*'od lnga*), etc., according to branch count

The citation from *Rang shar* [5216-5227] provides authoritative grounding:
- "All letters' explanation modes are gathered into two"
- "Nirvana letter explanation mode"
- "Samsara letter explanation mode"

[5228-5260] [CONCEPT] Location and Enjoyment of Letters
**Analysis:** Lines 5228-5260 analyze the place (*gnas*) and enjoyment (*longs spyod*) of letters:

**Location Analysis [5228-5239]:**
When vowels and branches are complete:
- Base letter's A represents samsara
- Whatever vowels arise thereafter represent nirvana's place

**Ma-mo Letter Enjoyment [5234]:**
The vowels and branches arise as the enjoyment (*longs spyod*) of the ma-mo (*ma mo'i yi ge*) letters.

**Four Lamp Letters (*sgron ma'i yi ge*) [5235-5240]:**
The four above the ma-mo are called lamp letters:
- *gu gu* — light letter (*'od kyi yi ge*)
- *'greng bu* — path letter (*lam gyi yi ge*)
- *na ro* — expanse letter (*dbyings kyi yi ge*)
- *klad kor* — dharmatā letter (*chos nyid kyi yi ge*)

These four, existing above, are likened to the arising of lamps from the upper opening of the body like eyes.

**Base Letter Analysis [5241-5260]:**
- Lower vowel (*'og gi 'dogs*) — base letter (*gzhir yi ge*)
- *ya btags* — pervading letter (*khyab pa'i yi ge*)
- *ra btags* — arising letter (*'char ba'i yi ge*)
- *zhabs kyu* — compassion letter (*thugs rje'i yi ge*)

The citation from *Rang shar* [5245-5260] enumerates these correspondences in verse form.
