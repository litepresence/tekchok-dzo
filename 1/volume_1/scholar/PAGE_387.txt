## [CONTEXT]

**Practice Structure:**
The text correlates **sgron ma bzhi with gradual path** (rim pa):
- **Sngon du** (line 1): Preliminary - triad preparation
- **Dang po** (line 2): First - body crucial point
- Sequential progression through channels

**Six Gnad Framework:**
These are **six points of entry** for dbyings recognition:
1. **'Char byed sgo**: Door where chos nyid dbyings is seen
2. **Snang ba yul**: Whatever appears has no departure point from chos nyid
3. **Gzer gdab pa rlung**: Thought differentiation ceases
4. **'Char byed 'od gsal**: Coarse/subtle elements reverse
5. **Chos nyid mngon sum**: Lta sgom spyod 'bras continuum cut
6. **Nyams gong 'phel**: Deluded bems rig self-ceases

**Doxographic Superiority:**
**Theg pa kun las 'phags pa** (line 16):
- 'Bras bu lam du byas pas: Using result as path
- Rgyu 'bras gdod kyis rtsol sgrub: From the start causal-result effort-accomplishment
- Dzogchen distinctive: Immediate vs. gradual

**Man ngag bzhi Structure:**
The sgron ma function as **four supports** (mthar rten):
1. **Mi 'gyur ba gsum**: Threefold non-change as basis
2. **Sdod pa gsum**: Threefold abiding measured
3. **Thob pa gsum**: Threefold attainment with peg
4. **Gdeng bzhi**: Fourfold confidence of liberation

## [CONCEPT]

**Gzhi 'chos pa Phenomenology:**
The **triad preparation** establishes recognition:
- **Lus kyi gnad gzir**: Body crucial point torqued → ye shes dbang du 'dus (wisdom gathers)
- **Ngag gi gnad gzir**: Speech crucial point → brjod du med pa'i don rnyed (finds unutterable meaning)
- **Yid kyi gnad gzir**: Mind crucial point → 'gyu ba rang rgyud chod (movement's own continuum cut)

This is **embodied epistemology**: Knowledge arises through somatic preparation.

**Sgo (Door) Metaphor:**
The **six doors** (gnad) are **points of entry** into recognition:
- Not sequential stages but **simultaneous access points**
- 'Char byed (emergence) + sgo (door) = where manifestation appears
- Mngon sum (direct) + rang thog (self-cognized) = non-mediated knowledge

**'Phreng thag chad:**
The cutting of the **continuity-thread** (line 11):
- Lta sgom spyod 'bras (view, meditation, conduct, result)
- Usually presented as progressive
- Here: **Simultaneous** recognition cuts linear progression

**Bems rig Rang 'gag:**
**Materialistic cognition self-ceases** (line 12):
- Bems: Material, substantial (Tibetan philosophical category)
- Rig: Cognition
- Without deliberate rejection ('gag - cessation), samsara ends
- Distinctive Dzogchen: **Self-liberation** not **transformation**

**Sku gsum Rol pa:**
The **three kayas** are not goals but **present enjoyment** (line 14):
- Rig pa tshad phebs: Awareness reaching measure → gzugs sku gnyis la longs spyod
- Chos nyid zad pa: Dharmata exhaustion → sku gsum rang rgyud grol
