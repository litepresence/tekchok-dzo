[14599-14635] [STRUCTURE] Six Lamps Complete System
The text presents the complete system of six lamps (sgron ma drug). The structure establishes: (1) the four lamps plus the flesh lamp (sha'i sgron ma) and far-reaching lasso water lamp, (2) the relationship between the six lamps, and (3) their positions in the body.

[14599-14635] [PHILOLOGY] Six Lamps Terminology
The term sgron ma drug (six lamps) completes the enumeration. The compound sha'i sgron ma (flesh lamp) identifies the physical basis. The phrase mig gi dangs ma (eye's clarity) describes the visual capacity.

[14599-14635] [CONTEXT] Six Lamp System
The text presents a six-lamp system that includes the four main lamps plus two supporting lamps. This expanded system provides a complete framework for thödgal practice. This reflects the systematic nature of Dzogchen presentation.

[14599-14635] [CONCEPT] Six Lamps as Complete Path
The text conceptualizes the six lamps as providing a complete path of thödgal practice. Each lamp corresponds to a stage of vision development; together they lead to full awakening. The six lamps are the roadmap of leapover.
