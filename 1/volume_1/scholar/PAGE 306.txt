[12685-12730] [STRUCTURE] Final Summary of Ground Section
The text presents a final summary of the entire ground (gzhi) section. The structure synthesizes: (1) ground as primordially pure spontaneous presence, (2) ground's manifestation as eight doors, four aspects, and six lights, (3) ground-appearances as the play of wisdom, and (4) the mind-awareness distinction as the key to recognition.

The compound sems rig (mind and awareness) identifies the crucial distinction.

[12685-12730] [CONCEPT] Ground as Complete Foundation
The synthesis conceptualizes ground as the complete foundation containing all elements of the path within it. The eight doors describe how ground manifests; the four aspects describe its dimensions; the six lights describe its luminosity; ground-appearances describe its display; the mind-awareness distinction describes the recognition point. Ground is thus the "container" of the entire path, already complete.
