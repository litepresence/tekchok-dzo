[14270-14302] [STRUCTURE] Navel Wheel: Emanation Center
The text presents the navel wheel (lte ba sprul pa'i 'khor lo) as the emanation center. The structure establishes: (1) navel as the earth element center, (2) sixty-two petals of the navel wheel, and (3) navel as the basis for magical emanation.

[14270-14302] [PHILOLOGY] Navel Center Terminology
The term lte ba sprul pa'i 'khor lo (navel emanation wheel) identifies the maṇipūra chakra. The compound sa'i 'byung ba (earth element) establishes its elemental nature. The phrase rtsa 'dab drug cu rtsa gnyis (sixty-two petals) describes its structure.

[14270-14302] [CONTEXT] Navel as Power Center
The text presents the navel as the center of power and manifestation. From the navel comes the capacity for emanation and magical display. This reflects the tantric understanding of the navel as the storehouse of power.

[14270-14302] [CONCEPT] Navel as Manifestation Source
The text conceptualizes the navel wheel as the source of all manifestation. Just as the navel was the connection to life in the womb, it remains the connection to the source of manifestation. Activation of the navel center enables enlightened activity.
