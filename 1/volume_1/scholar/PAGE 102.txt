[3764-3768] [CONCEPT] White Expanse: Action-Vast
**Analysis:** Lines 3764-3768 present the first White Expanse division:

**Characterization:**
- *las rgya che ba brjod med du bshad pa'i klong dkar po* — White Expanse of vast action explained inexpressibly

**Essence [3767-3768]:**
- *ngo bo mthong ba cer grol* — Essence: seeing, naturally released
- *gnas pa rang dag pa* — Abiding: self-pure

**Key Insight:** Liberation through seeing, not through action or effort.

[3769-3785] [STRUCTURE] Ocean Expanse: Two Divisions
**Analysis:** Lines 3769-3785 present the Ocean Expanse (*rgya mtsho'i klong*):

**Two Divisions [3770-3771, 3775]:**
1. Great Ocean Expanse (*rgya mtsho'i klong che ba*)
2. Small Ocean Expanse (*rgya mtsho'i klong chung ba*)

**Great Ocean Expanse [3772-3782]:**
- Essence: Eternal extreme self-liberated
- Without action, liberated through liberation
- Non-dual variousness
- Mind self-abiding, empty, primordially liberated
- Mind-itself pure in emptiness-reference
- Dharmatā of seeing-appearance self-pure
- Inexpressible from primordial time
- Abiding in one great wheel beyond existence
- Emptiness not established anywhere

**Small Ocean Expanse [3783-3785]:**
- Reference self-pure, mind without stain
- Particularly abiding pure
- Circumstances inexpressible from primordial time

[3786-3796] [CONCEPT] Space Expanse
**Analysis:** Lines 3786-3796 present the Space Expanse (*nam mkha'i klong*):

**Essence [3786-3787]:**
- Dharmatā unfabricated
- Mind-itself self-liberated

**Two Divisions [3788-3790]:**
1. Sun-Moon Expanse (*nyi zla'i klong*)
2. Jewel White Expanse (*rin po che'i klong dkar po*)

**Sun-Moon Expanse [3791-3793]:**
- Essence: completely pure emptiness
- Nature: unproduced, primordially liberated

**Jewel White Expanse [3794-3796]:**
- Qualities completely perfect
- Cause-conditions self-pure
- No action in mind
- Dharmatā spontaneously accomplished
- Cause-conditions self-pure

[3797-3808] [CONCEPT] Vast Expanse: Four Divisions
**Analysis:** Lines 3797-3808 introduce the Vast Expanse (*klong rab 'byams*):

**Essence [3797-3798]:**
- Appearances known by mind
- Non-appearances known by abiding-nature

**Four Divisions [3799-3803]:**
1. *bya ba dang bral ba phyi'i rab 'byams* — Outer Vast free from action
2. *grub mtha' rang gzhung du smra ba nang gi rab 'byams* — Inner Vast asserting tenets in own texts
3. *gegs bsal ba gsang ba'i rab 'byams* — Secret Vast clearing obstacles
4. *gnad spro ba de kho na nyid kyi rab 'byams* — Thatness Vast spreading crucial points

**Outer Vast [3804-3808]:**
- Three crucial points:
  1. Causeless, no reversal
  2. Conditionless, thus appearing not harmed by anything
  3. Both cause-condition non-existent, samsara-nirvana unestablished
