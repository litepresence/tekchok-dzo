[14377-14413] [STRUCTURE] Crown Wheel: Great Bliss Center
The text presents the crown wheel (spyi gtsug bde chen gyi 'khor lo) as the great bliss center. The structure establishes: (1) crown as the wind element center, (2) thirty-two channels of the crown wheel, and (3) crown as the gateway to enlightenment.

[14377-14413] [CONTEXT] Crown as Enlightenment Gateway
The text presents the crown as the gateway through which consciousness exits at death and wisdom enters in awakening. The thirty-two channels radiate like a thousand-petaled lotus. This reflects the highest tantric understanding of the crown center.

[14377-14413] [CONCEPT] Crown as Bliss Source
The text conceptualizes the crown wheel as the source of great bliss. When the subtle winds enter the central channel and rise to the crown, great bliss is experienced. This bliss is the fuel for realization.
