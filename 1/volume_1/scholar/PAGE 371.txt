[15297-15333] [STRUCTURE] Channel Vital Points and Application
The text presents the vital points (gnad) of channel practice. The structure establishes: (1) covering (gtem pa), pressing (gman pa), warming (bskyil ba), spreading (gzir ba), and releasing (gzhil ba) as key techniques, and (2) their application to body, speech, and mind.

[15297-15333] [PHILOLOGY] Vital Point Terminology
The term gnad (vital point/pith) identifies essential techniques. The compound gtem pa (covering/sealing) describes a specific method. The phrase lus kyi gnad (body's vital point) establishes somatic application.

[15297-15333] [CONTEXT] Esoteric Technique
The text presents esoteric techniques for working with channels. These are not merely physical but integrate body, energy, and mind. This reflects the tantric emphasis on direct methods that engage the whole being.

[15297-15333] [CONCEPT] Technique as Recognition Support
The text conceptualizes techniques as supports for recognition. The vital points are not ends in themselves but means to reveal the natural state. When recognition occurs, techniques are transcended.
