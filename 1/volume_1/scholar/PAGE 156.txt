[6282-6295] [CONTEXT] Transference of Teachings
**Analysis:** Lines 6282-6295 explain the transference of teachings to other world-systems:

**Completion of Seven Cycles [6282]:**
Thus seven flourishing and decline completed, after life's ten years, below eighty, teaching's three supports not remaining in this world. From here south Beautiful-array (*mdzes ldan bkod pa*) Buddhafield descends, flourishing and declining seven, that field's benefit completion time.

**World-System Destruction [6284]:**
Saha empty time — world-system destroyed.

**Transfer to Vast-protection [6285]:**
Again those three north Vast-protection (*yangspy ob*) world transfer. Buddha Utpala-flower-beautiful (*utpala me tog mdzes pa*) holds, flourishing and declining seven made after, west Crested-ornament-sound-vast (*gtsug phud dbyangs yangs*) world transfers.

**Seven Hundred Thousand Years [6286-6291]:**
Buddha Cyclic-existence-destroyer (*'khor ba 'jig*) holds, seven hundred thousand years flourishing and declining manner resides after, that field and Vajradhara's arranged Brahma aeon's world all beings liberated in original ground.

**Vajradhara's Arrangement Dissolution [6290-6291]:**
Vajradhara's arranged Brahma aeon container-contents with thousand emptiness, space becomes one, eighty-five aeons resides.

**Teaching Supports Disappear [6292-6295]:**
The three teaching supports, Vajradhara's great deeds completed, dharmatā peace expanse self-dissolved, not visible.

[6296-6341] [CONTEXT] Cyclic Renewal of Teachings
**Analysis:** Lines 6296-6341 describe the cyclic renewal process:

**New Emanation [6296-6304]:**
Then again beings appearing from expanse, emanation arranges Brahma, from aeon six hundred thousand to taming beings' appearance, taming teacher comes, from Samantabhadra's blessing, teaching's three supports self-arisen, without flourishing and decline, beings' benefit arises.

**Citation from *Thal 'gyur* [6299-6341]:**
"Then Beautiful-array Buddhafield, there transfers, lifespans ten below, here not remaining, there visible.

Thus flourishing and declining completed, supreme teaching's essence, Vast-protection world, Buddha teaching abiding, Utpala-flower-beautiful called, teacher Buddha Bhagavan, Bliss-arranged palace, thousand five with retinue, all Bodhisattvas great, unproduced dharma patience obtained, virtue mind-endowed only arises.
