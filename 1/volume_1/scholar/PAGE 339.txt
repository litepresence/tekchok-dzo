## PAGE 339 [13876-13909]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **detailed three-channel pathways** and **four vital knots**. Lines 13876-13909 establish:

1. **Central channel pathway** (13877-13884): Kun 'dar ma to crown, then to right ear
2. **Left channel pathway** (13884-13886): To left ear, wisdom-wind arising
3. **Thigle locations** (13886-13890): Right-relative, left-absolute
4. **Four knots** (13890-13909): Skyed byed, dran pa, ro rnams, rtse mo

The structure provides **surgical-precision anatomy**.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Critical terminology:**

- **དབུས་ན་དྲང་པོར་གནས་ཏེ།** (13877): "Abiding straight in the center"—central channel's **verticality**.

- **ཚངས་པའི་བུ་ག་ལ་སྣང་བར་བྱེད་ལ།** (13878): "Making appear at Brahma's aperture"—cranial fontanelle as **crown opening**.

- **རིག་པ་རྟོག་པ་མེད་པའི་སྐུ་འཆར་རོ།** (13879): "Body of rigpa without thought appears"—crown aperture as **site of rainbow body**.

- **ལས་ཀྱི་རླུང་འགྱུ་སྟེ།** (13881): "Karma-wind moves"—right ear as **karmic outlet**.

- **རླུང་གི་ཏིང་ངེ་འཛིན་སྒོམ་པའི་རྟེན་བྱེད་པའོ།** (13883): "Becomes support for wind-samādhi meditation"—nostril-breath as **meditation basis**.

- **མདུད་པ་ཕྲ་མོ་བཞི།** (13890): "Four subtle knots"—mdud pa (knots) as **blockages** in channels:
  1. Skyed byed (producer/navel)
  2. Dran pa (memory/heart)
  3. Ro rnams (tastes/throat)
  4. Rtse mo (apex/crown)

**Particle constructions:**
The **locative of function** ན། establishes **channel locations**. The **instrumental of agency** ཀྱིས། marks **wind-actions**.

### [CONTEXT]
**Brahma-aperture cosmology:**
Tshangs pa'i bu ga (13878) is the **anterior fontanelle** where the soul exits at death. In Dzogchen, it's where **rigpa exits** to become rainbow body.

**Nāḍī pathway precision:**
The detailed routes (13869-13886) reflect **tantric anatomy** as **surgical knowledge**. Each channel has specific trajectory with **soteriological significance**.

**Knot theory:**
The four knots (13890-13909) correspond to **four chakras**:
- Navel = production knot
- Heart = memory knot
- Throat = taste knot
- Crown = apex knot

These are **obstacles** to be untied through practice.

### [CONCEPT]
**Crown aperture as liberation-gate:**
Line 13878-13879 establishes **tshangs pa'i bu ga** as site where **rigpa without thought** appears. This is **Tögal** theory: when channels open to crown, **spontaneous vision** arises.

**Nostril winds and karma/wisdom:**
The text specifies (13881-13884):
- **Right nostril** = karma-wind (las kyi rlung)
- **Left nostril** = wisdom-wind (ye shes kyi rlung)

Breath-control (prāṇāyāma) thus becomes **karmic purification**.

**Four knots as practice-stages:**
The four vital knots (13890-13909) mark **progressive opening**:
1. Navel-knot: Birth/generation
2. Heart-knot: Memory/consciousness
3. Throat-knot: Speech/taste
4. Crown-knot: Liberation/vision

Untying these through yoga is **the path**.
