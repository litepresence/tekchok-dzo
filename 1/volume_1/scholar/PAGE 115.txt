[4271-4327] [CONCEPT] Indicating Method Tantra
**Analysis:** Lines 4271-4327 present the indicating method tantra:

**Three Divisions [4271-4273, 4280]:**
1. Effortless primordial liberation taught
2. With effort gradual taught

**Essence [4272-4273]:**
- Indicated meaning reasoning realization method instruction advice
- Self-abiding awareness wisdom showing
- Realization method practice crucial condensed
- One direction shown

**Etymology [4274-4276]:**
- Therefore called indicating method tantra

**General vs. Instruction Tantras [4277-4279]:**
- Explanatory tantras: long words, difficult
- Instruction tantras: clear words, easy
- Great Perfection tantras: instruction tantras

**First: Effortless [4281-4286]:**
- Ground cutting through (*khregs chod*)
- Faculties simultaneous
- Primordially liberated, no repetition

**Second: Gradual [4287-4307]:**
- Path leap-over (*thod rgal*)
- Faculties gradual
- Relying on channels-winds crucial
- Three preliminaries, direct placing
- Body, door, object crucial
- Wind-awareness crucial
- Four appearances reaching measure
