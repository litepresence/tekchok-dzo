MISREADING:
Completing Thogal is a status or achievement conferring special rank.
WHY IT ARISES:
Hierarchy of accomplishment. Spiritual titles.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Claims of completion for status.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Elitism; competition.

Licentiousness; spiritual apathy.
