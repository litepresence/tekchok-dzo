[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [OBSTACLES AS EXTERNAL]
MISREADING:
Obstacles (laziness, agitation) are personal failings or external forces.
WHY IT ARISES:
Moral judgment of practice difficulties.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Self-blame or discouragement.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the self-liberated nature of obstacles.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PSYCHOLOGIZATION ERROR].

MISREADING:

WHY IT ARISES:
"Completion" suggests goal reached.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking confirmation of completion.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Pride or inadequacy.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
