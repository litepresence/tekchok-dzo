[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [CONFUSION AS REAL]
MISREADING:
Confusion ('khrul pa) is an actual force, entity, or thing that obstructs enlightenment.
WHY IT ARISES:
"Ignorance" personified as demon or force.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Externalizing confusion as enemy to defeat.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Aggressive spiritual warfare mentality.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].

WHY IT ARISES:
Conventional language of "liberation" suggests exit.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Missing the non-dual identity of samsara and nirvana.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Spiritual materialism of seeking "better place."
