[PRACTICE ERROR] [THOGAL AS VISUALIZATION]
MISREADING:
Thogal involves visualizing lights, colors, and mandalas.
WHY IT ARISES:
"Vision" practice suggests imagination. Creative visualization culture.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Making up visions rather than direct perception.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Fantasy; hallucination; self-deception.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR].

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [VISIONS AS REAL ENTITIES]
MISREADING:
The visions (thigle, rainbows, Buddhas) are actual external objects or beings appearing.
WHY IT ARISES:
Literal interpretation of visionary experience.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Reification of visions as objective realities.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
External worship of visionary deities.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[REIFICATION ERROR].
