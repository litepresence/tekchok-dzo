[EPISTEMIC ERROR] [RIGPA AS EXPERIENCE]
MISREADING:
Rigpa is a special experience, state of consciousness, or peak mystical state.
WHY IT ARISES:
"Awareness" suggests subjective experience. Peak experience chase.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Rigpa becomes another experience to attain.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Disappointment when "ordinary" awareness is pointed out.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [RIGPA AS ENTITY]
MISREADING:
Rigpa is an actual thing, substance, or entity that one possesses or discovers.
WHY IT ARISES:
"Nature of mind" suggests entity with nature. Reification tendency.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Rigpa substantialized as "true self" or "pure consciousness."
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Eternalistic interpretation of Dzogchen.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ETERNALISTIC ERROR].

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [LHUNGRUB AS EFFORTLESS]
MISREADING:
Spontaneous presence (lhun grub) means "effortless" in the sense of easy or relaxed.
WHY IT ARISES:
"Spontaneous" suggests casual ease. Psychological interpretation.
PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Lhundrub reduced to psychological state.
SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
"Just relax" as spiritual advice.
CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PSYCHOLOGIZATION ERROR].
