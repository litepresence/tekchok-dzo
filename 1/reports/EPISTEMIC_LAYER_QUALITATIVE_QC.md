- [ ] Classifies FROM WHERE statements are spoken, not what is true

- [ ] No devotional language
- [ ] No practice advice
- [ ] No assertions of "ultimately true"

- [ ] Read against Tibetan source

- [ ] Risk flags appropriately applied
