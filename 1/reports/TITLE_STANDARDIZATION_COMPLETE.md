## Why These Changes?

### Chapter 9: "Spontaneous Presence Appearance"
- **Tibetan:** ལྷུན་གྲུབ་སྣང་ཚུལ་གྱི་སྒོ་ལས་འཁོར་འདས་ཀྱི་གྱེས་མདོ་བསྟན་པ
- **Literal:** "Showing the Turning Point of Saṃsāra and Nirvāṇa through the Door of Spontaneous Presence Appearance"
- **Rationale:** The chapter's core teaching is about spontaneous presence (*lhun grub*), not merely delusion. The delusion arises from NOT recognizing spontaneous presence.

### Chapter 25: "Spontaneous Fruition"
- **Tibetan:** འབྲས་བུ་ལྷུན་གྲུབ་ཏུ་བསྟན་པ
- **Literal:** "Showing Spontaneous Fruition"
- **Rationale:** འབྲས་བུ ('bras bu) specifically means "fruition" or "result" in the soteriological sense—enlightenment as fruit. "Results" is too generic.
