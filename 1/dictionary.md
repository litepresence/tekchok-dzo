| Tibetan | English | Context | Literal | Liturgical | Notes |

| gzhi | ground / basis | Fundamental ground | "ground" or "basis" | "Ground" or "Base" | NEVER translate as "cause" - avoid causal fallacy |
| lam | path | Method | "path" | "Path" | Distinguish: causal vehicle vs result vehicle |
| 'bras bu | fruition | Result | "fruition" | "Fruition" or "Result" | Result vehicle context |
| rgyu | cause | Causal condition | "cause" | "cause" | In rgyu 'bras = cause and result |
| snod | vessel / container | Universe as vessel | "vessel" | "vessel" or "container" | Cosmological term |
| bcud | essence / contents | Sentient beings as essence | "essence" | "essence" or "quintessence" | Contents of the vessel |
