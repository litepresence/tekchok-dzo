## GROUND, PATH, FRUITION

| Tibetan | English | Context | Literal | Liturgical | Notes |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|------------|-------|
| gzhi | ground / basis | Fundamental ground | "ground" or "basis" | "Ground" or "Base" | NEVER translate as "cause" - avoid causal fallacy |
| lam | path | Method | "path" | "Path" | Distinguish: causal vehicle vs result vehicle |
| 'bras bu | fruition | Result | "fruition" | "Fruition" or "Result" | Result vehicle context |
| rgyu | cause | Causal condition | "cause" | "cause" | In rgyu 'bras = cause and result |
| snod | vessel / container | Universe as vessel | "vessel" | "vessel" or "container" | Cosmological term |
| bcud | essence / contents | Sentient beings as essence | "essence" | "essence" or "quintessence" | Contents of the vessel |

## KEY PARTICLES (Literal Layer)

| Tibetan | English | Function | Literal | Notes |
|---------|---------|----------|---------|-------|
| kyis | by / through / with | Instrumental | "by" / "through" / "with" | Instrumentality over simple agency |
| las | from / out of / than | Ablative | "from" / "out of" / "than" | Default to ablative unless grammar dictates instrumental |
| la | to / in / at | Dative/Locative | "to/in" | Use slash for ambiguity: "to/in" |
| na | if / when / in | Conditional | "if" / "when" | Temporal/conditional marker |
| ste | and so / thus / -ing | Continuative | "thus" / "and so" | Connective particle |
| yang | also / moreover / again | Emphatic | "also" / "moreover" | Additive/emphatic |
| zhig | -ing / imperative | Imperative/Suspensive | "do it!" / "-ing" | Context-dependent urgency |
| cig | one / a / single | Indefinite/Numeral | "one" / "a" | Can function as indefinite article |
