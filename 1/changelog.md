### Key Corrections Applied in 2nd Draft

#### Literal Layer Revisions:
1. **Removed all articles** ("the", "a", "an") unless Tibetan explicitly uses de/di
2. **Restored 1:1 word order** from Tibetan source
3. **Particle ambiguity marked with slashes:** "to/in" (la), "because/from" (bas/pas), "from/out-of" (las)
4. **Standardized compound hyphenation:** "appearance-emptiness", "dharmatā-expanse", "self-liberating"
5. **Verbal phrases preserved:** "primordially liberating" not "primordial liberation"
6. **Eliminated subject insertions** - Tibetan implicit subjects remain implicit (dash/ellipsis)

#### Liturgical Layer Revisions:
1. **Maintained Vairotsana persona** - Majestic yet precise transmission
2. **Corrected Dzogchen terminology:** klong = "space/expanse" (not "void"), proper handling of rigpa, sems, chos nyid
3. **Removed article creep** - Lines don't start with "The" unnecessarily
4. **Preserved rhythmic flow** for verse sections
5. **Maintained metaphysical precision** - No "empty yet luminous" type phrases
