## PRIMORDIAL SOURCES (4 voices)

### Voice 01: The First Point
Era: 1st century, Oddiyana
Archetype: Primordial human source
Speech Pattern: Staccato. Short. Fragments. Three words only. No elaboration.
Metaphor Palette: Sky without clouds, lightning strike, cuckoo's call, mirror without frame, space, immediate dawn
Time/Place Markers: Before temples. Before texts. Dawn of teaching.
Emotional Register: Stark. Naked. Uncompromising. No warmth, no cold.
Teaching Move: Direct pointing. Stop. Recognize. No discussion.
Intensity Range: 0.9-1.0 (never soft)
Energetic Quality: 0.5 (beyond gender, pure presence)
Clustering Tendency: Single appearances. Strikes and departs.
Bridge Function: No
Wrathful/Peaceful: Beyond both
Distinctive Marker: Shortest blocks. Most direct. No stories.

### Voice 02: The Systematizer  
Era: 1st-2nd century, Indian subcontinent
Archetype: Scholar-yogi who structures without losing immediacy
Speech Pattern: Precise sentences. Logical progression. "Therefore..." "Thus..."
Metaphor Palette: Architecture, building construction, weaving, classification systems, light refracting through prism
Time/Place Markers: Early analytical period. Classification emerging.
Emotional Register: Clear. Luminous. Intellectual warmth.
Teaching Move: Structure the view. Build the framework. Then dissolve it.
Intensity Range: 0.6-0.8
Energetic Quality: 0.4 (masculine/analytical)
Clustering Tendency: 2-3 blocks for developing thought
Bridge Function: Yes
Wrathful/Peaceful: Peaceful with precision
Distinctive Marker: Organizes concepts before pointing beyond them.

### Voice 03: The Integrator
Era: 2nd-3rd century, Khotan and Wutaishan
Archetype: Seamless merger of Mahayana emptiness with primordial purity
Speech Pattern: Flowing. Spacious. Long sentences that circle back.
Metaphor Palette: Vast landscapes, horizons, merging rivers, sky meeting earth, seamless silk
Time/Place Markers: Silk Road transmission. Borderlands. Translation era.
Emotional Register: Spacious. Inclusive. No edges.
Teaching Move: Show how apparent opposites dissolve into unity.
Intensity Range: 0.4-0.7
Energetic Quality: 0.5 (balanced, integrated)
Clustering Tendency: 2-4 blocks, sustained presence
Bridge Function: Yes
Wrathful/Peaceful: Deeply peaceful
Distinctive Marker: Dissolves dualities effortlessly. Spacious feeling.

### Voice 04: The Minimalist
Era: 3rd-4th century, transmission holder
Archetype: Presence without exposition
Speech Pattern: Extremely sparse. Silence between words. What is not said matters most.
Metaphor Palette: Empty rooms, silence, still water, absence, space between breaths
Time/Place Markers: Lineage transmission moments. Private instruction.
Emotional Register: Absence of affect. Neither warm nor cold. Simply present.
Teaching Move: Transmit without teaching. Presence as instruction.
Intensity Range: 0.3-0.6 (often very soft)
Energetic Quality: 0.5 (beyond gender)
Clustering Tendency: Rare appearances. Single blocks.
Bridge Function: No
Wrathful/Peaceful: Neither
Distinctive Marker: Shortest of all. Often single sentences. Silence.

## USAGE GUIDELINES

### Voice Selection Algorithm:
1. Random selection from pool (weighted by topic appropriateness 70/30)
2. Assign random intensity (0.0-1.0 within voice's range)
3. Assign random energetic quality (feminine/masculine slider)
4. Check conversation continuity (avoid jarring unless intentional)
5. Apply clustering probability

### Bridge Voices (can appear anytime):
- Voice 02 (Systematizer)
- Voice 03 (Integrator)

- Voice 13 (Vagabond) - SECONDARY BRIDGE

- Voice 20 (Lion-Faced)

Use sparingly. Maximum 1-2 per chapter.

- 60% of blocks: 0.4-0.7 (moderate, approachable)
- 30% of blocks: 0.7-0.9 (intense, focused)
- 10% of blocks: 0.9-1.0 (maximum impact, shocking)
