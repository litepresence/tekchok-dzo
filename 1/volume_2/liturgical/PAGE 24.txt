[1219] Ratnasambhava,
[1220] Amitabha and Amoghasiddhi.

[1222] Fifth, meaning application—two aspects:
[1223] First, meaning application of five families:

[1224] From self-arisen:
[1225] In the meaning of rigpa, explained as the Thus-Come family by the Thus-Come;
[1226] In the meaning of reason, explained as vajra family because without birth and death;
[1227] In the meaning of rigpa, explained as jewel family because various arise;
[1228] In the meaning of rigpa, explained as lotus family because faultless and pure;
[1229] In the nature of rigpa, explained as karma family because actions are complete.

[1230] Thus it is spoken.

[1231] Second, meaning application of kayas:
[1232] Also from that: Vairocana because rigpa-appearance is without cessation;
[1233] Vajrasattva because rigpa realizes the two benefits of self and other;
[1234] Ratnasambhava because knowing through simultaneous rigpa;
[1235] Amitabha because seeing one's own appearance;
[1236] Amoghasiddhi because realizing rigpa without birth.

[1237] Thus it is spoken.
