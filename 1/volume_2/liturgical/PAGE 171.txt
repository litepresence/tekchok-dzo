[7265] Kye Speech Vajra:
[7266] By vital point of no going-coming in awareness primordial wisdom:
[7267] Person with various dharmata signs arising in body-speech and
[7268] Two persons not training mind even moment in hearing-thinking—
[7269] I, Samantabhadra, show: not exist distinction even dust in seeing things.

[7270] Kye Speech Vajra:
[7271] By vital point of no birth-ceasing in dharmata:
[7272] To person experiencing hot-cold hells and
[7273] Two persons experiencing bliss of all Buddhas—
[7274] I, Samantabhadra, show: not exist distinction even dust at realization door.

[7275] Kye Speech Vajra:
[7276] By vital point of no change in awareness:
[7277] Person cutting mind dharma elaborations and
[7278] Two persons viewing self as permanent—
[7279] I, Samantabhadra, show: not exist distinction even dust.

[7280] Kye Speech Vajra:
[7281] By vital point of dharmakaya existing in self:
[7282] Person making various outer offerings, praises, requests and
[7283] Two persons abiding separate from doing-actions—
[7284] I, Samantabhadra, show: not exist distinction even dust at result obtainment.
