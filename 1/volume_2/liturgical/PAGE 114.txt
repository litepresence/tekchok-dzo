From Letterless:
Direct buddhas without birth,
See vision pure without field.

Thus said, and:
From Illusion:
Jewel, wish-fulfilling, desire-glory—
All buddhas' direct.

Thus it was said.

Definitive-meaning: see three-bodies' vision.
From Jewel Heap Tantra:
Direct called see;
Three called three-bodies;
Certain arrival at field realm.

Thus it was said.

Purpose: liberate without error obscuration, this main-point.
That same, from:
Dharmata direct main-point not existing—
Similar to going to precipice without eye.

Thus it was said.

Main-point from Thalgyur:
Main-point: arrow not moving;
Apply three gaze manners this time.

Thus said, and:
From Letterless:
Make fully place eye;
Dhatu emerges from eye corner itself;
Gaze eye at sky element.

Thus it was said.

Vision is:
From Thalgyur:
Dharmata direct vision is;
Certain emerge from sense-power gate;
Clear in sky without clouds.

Thus said, and:
Also channel and delusion-wheel:
Five wisdoms abide in nature,
See bubble by eye.

Thus said, and:
From Self-Dawn:
Body of light possessing five wisdoms,
Fully clear in hanging-cord,
That also: go and come,
Move and shake.

Thus said, and:
From Lion Power Complete:
That entity of self-vision awareness
Abides in wisdom hanging-cord.

Thus said, and:
From Jewel Display:
Said: arrow, water lamp—fully perfect buddha,
Awareness hanging-cord body fully clear.

Manner of taking experience:
First: many number short sessions;
Second: extend session, extend duration;
Third: take experience without day-night,
Make awareness not move, place in dhatu fence.
