[869] Samayatara—five.
[870] Five clusters: surrounded by five—Akshobhya and others—periphery ornamented by five lights' enclosure.
[872] Five primordials:
[873] Five paths, primordially complete,
[874] Five kayas, primordially established,
[875] Rigpa, primordially arisen,
[876] Five lights, primordially resonant,
[877] Defilements, primordially liberated.
[878] Five knowledges:
[879] From realization's extent,
[880] Know as five kayas,
[881] Know as five wisdoms,
[882] Know as five lights,
[883] Know as self-appearance,
[884] Know as duality-free great liberation.
[885] Five realizations:
[886] Realize field as expanse,
[887] Realize beings as kayas,
[888] Realize appearance as light,
[889] Realize arise-liberation category-free,
[890] Realize wisdom as rigpa.
[891] Five bases:
[892] Light-clear basis: rigpa,
[893] Phenomena basis: kunzhi,
[894] Delusion basis: sems,
[895] Path basis: lamp,
[896] Thought basis: wisdom.
[897] Five common paths:
[898] Accumulation path,
[899] Application path,
[900] Seeing path,
[901] Meditation path,
[902] No-more-learning path—five.
[903] Five uncommon paths:
[904] Dharmata path: expanse,
[905] Rigpa path: knowledge,
[906] Light-clear path: two lamps,
[907] Trekcho path: arise-liberation,
[908] Thogal path: expanse-rigpa.
[909] Five fruitions:
[910] Wisdom fruition: liberated in rig,
[911] Light fruition: delusion-appearance ceases,
[912] Realization fruition: delusion-thought clears,
[913] Self-appearance fruition: brought to exhaustion,
[914] Final fruition: dissolves into expanse.
[915] Five abodes:
[916] Thigle abode: tsitta,
[917] Wisdom abode: crown,
[918] Lamp abode: eyes,
[919] Expanse-appearance abode: sky,
[920] Liberation-place abode: primordial purity.
[921] Five fields:
[922] Rigpa's field: expanse,
[923] Lamp's field: sky,
[924] Wisdom's field: bardo,
[925] Light's field: self-appearance,
[926] Sambhogakaya's field: cluster.
[927] Five essences:
[928] Rigpa's essence: primordial purity,
[929] Wisdom's essence: spontaneous accomplishment,
[930] Light's essence: five aspects,
[931] Wisdom's essence: realization,
[932] Lamp's essence: empty-clarity.
