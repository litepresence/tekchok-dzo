[9315] Realizing appearance-emptiness as equality without ceasing.
[9316] Separated individually by sense-power.
[9317] From Power-Perfect:
[9318] At the time when sun-moon equal gap intention penetrates expanse,
[9319] For best, middle, and last three sense-powers, three view-signs arise.
[9320] The view of best sense-power:
[9321] Realizes wisdom as open.
[9322] Intention: not separate from wisdom river's channel without ceasing.
[9323] Conduct:
[9324] Conducts display without ceasing by great wisdom conduct.
[9325] Fruit:
[9326] Obtaining fruit of method and wisdom two practice and release two, four aspects without gathering or separation.
[9327] Also, three view-signs of middle sense-power:
[9328] Realizes view without method and wisdom two.
[9329] Intention: abides in manner like sun-moon arising in space-between, nature of method and wisdom.
[9330] Conduct: conducts to white-red secret bindu of great bliss.
[9331] Fruit:
[9332] Obtaining fruit of Buddha-hood in womb of five Buddha-families' practice.
[9333] Three view-signs of last sense-power:
[9334] Realizes view indicating awakening mind seed by father-mother practice.
[9335] Intention:
[9336] Abides in clear samadhi manner, remembering god's nature heart-essence.
[9337] Conduct:
[9338] Conducts to five medicines, five nectars.
[9339] Fruit:
[9340] Seizing tutelary deity's illusory body in intermediate,
[9341] Obtaining fruit of pure appearance. Thus it is spoken.

[9342] Fifth, the essence of ocean expanse-penetration intention:
[9343] Abiding in force-samadhi of nature as Dharma-nature equal-clear state.
[9344] Separated by individual sense-power.
[9345] From Power-Perfect:
[9346] At the time of ocean expanse-penetration intention,
[9347] Also, three view-signs arise for three sense-powers.
[9348] The view of best sense-power:
[9349] Realizes whatever appears by great wisdom's activity as self-appearance itself.
[9350] Intention:
[9351] Like being intoxicated by alcohol with small attachment to appearance.
