[11501] Breaking great samaya toward vajra guru.
[11502] Signs of birth as hungry ghost, animals, asura, god, human.
[11503] First, breaking great samaya:
[11504] To break samaya with the vajra guru is the greatest downfall,
[11505] For the guru is the embodiment of all Buddhas.
[11506] Thus breaking great samaya.
[11507] Second, signs of birth as hungry ghost:
[11508] Attachment to giving arises, desire for food and drink intensifies,
[11509] The mouth constricts, the throat narrow.
[11510] Thus the signs of birth as hungry ghost.
[11511] Third, signs of birth as animal:
[11512] Delusion increases, stupidity prevails,
[11513] Fear arises, confusion of direction.
[11514] Thus the signs of birth as animal.
[11515] Fourth, signs of birth as asura:
[11516] Jealousy arises, competitive mind intensifies,
[11517] Aggression toward others, desire for superiority.
[11518] Thus the signs of birth as asura.
[11519] Fifth, signs of birth as god:
[11520] Pride increases, attachment to bliss,
[11521] Complacency arises, neglect of Dharma.
[11522] Thus the signs of birth as god.
[11523] Sixth, signs of birth as human:
[11524] Equanimity toward the six objects,
[11525] Desire to practice virtue, aspiration for liberation.
[11526] Thus the signs of birth as human.
[11527] Herein, the purpose of knowing signs:
[11528] Recognizing the signs, one can purify.
[11529] Through purification, better rebirth is attained.
[11530] Thus the purpose of knowing signs.
[11531] Herein, the method of purification:
[11532] Confession, restoration, and accumulation of merit.
[11533] Through these, samaya is repaired.
[11534] Thus the method of purification.
[11535] Thus breaking great samaya and its signs are explained.
