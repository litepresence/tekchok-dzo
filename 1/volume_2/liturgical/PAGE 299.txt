Second, purifying on corpse: before that corpse, doing mandala of two cubits, etc., sprinkling with good scents like saffron, etc., in center of eight-petaled lotus written in beautiful colors, putting jewel or if clay, vase transformed by white earth with mouth ornament, neck binding, mantra thread, inside filled with clean water and vase substances, four directions written with four A's by vermilion or madder.

Thinking vase as light five's measured palace, inside teacher Samantabhadra blue-green with samadhi, father-mother's direction-corners race five's buddhas; above, thinking master lineages; inviting by moment, inserting. Doing approach by om ah hum swaha and a hundred-thousand; interval by om a bhaya rgya tam kem, "So-and-so's evil and obscuration all shantim ku ru ye swaha," thus reciting.

Holding from mantra thread, self thinking as vajra-mind, light-ray wrapping on mantra thread, gone, thinking vase fills by nectar falling from master and gods' individual body parts. Praising with worship and praise of what is known; at end, reciting A A, wisdom-pa goes; thinking samaya-pa dissolves in water.

From that, holding vase in armpit, reciting kem, washing, send from asura. From that, holding vase on crown, reciting tam, washing, send from god's place.
