[13801] Purifying on corpse.
[13802] Maṇḍala of two cubits.
[13803] Vase transformation.
[13804] Inviting by moment.
[13805] Reciting A age amount, washing, sending from hell, hungry ghost, animal, asura, god, human.
[13806] First, purifying on corpse:
[13807] Purify oneself upon the corpse,
[13808] Recognizing death as the nature.
[13809] Thus purifying on corpse.
[13810] Second, maṇḍala of two cubits:
[13811] Create a maṇḍala of two cubits,
[13812] As the abode of the deity.
[13813] Thus maṇḍala of two cubits.
[13814] Third, vase transformation:
[13815] Transform the vase into the deity's abode,
[13816] Filled with nectar and blessings.
[13817] Thus vase transformation.
[13818] Fourth, inviting by moment:
[13819] Invite the wisdom beings in an instant,
[13820] Through the power of samādhi and mantra.
[13821] Thus inviting by moment.
[13822] Fifth, reciting A age amount:
[13823] Recite the letter A according to age,
[13824] Purifying the lifespan obscurations.
[13825] Thus reciting A age amount.
[13826] Sixth, washing:
[13827] Wash with nectar, purifying defilements,
[13828] Cleansing the obscurations of body and mind.
[13829] Thus washing.
[13830] Seventh, sending from hell:
[13831] Send consciousness from the hell realms,
[13832] Liberating from intense suffering.
[13833] Thus sending from hell.
[13834] Eighth, sending from hungry ghost:
[13835] Send consciousness from the hungry ghost realm,
[13836] Liberating from hunger and thirst.
[13837] Thus sending from hungry ghost.
[13838] Ninth, sending from animal:
[13839] Send consciousness from the animal realm,
[13840] Liberating from stupidity and darkness.
[13841] Thus sending from animal.
[13842] Tenth, sending from asura:
[13843] Send consciousness from the asura realm,
[13844] Liberating from jealousy and conflict.
[13845] Thus sending from asura.
[13846] Eleventh, sending from god:
[13847] Send consciousness from the god realm,
[13848] Liberating from pride and complacency.
[13849] Thus sending from god.
[13850] Twelfth, sending from human:
[13851] Send consciousness from the human realm,
[13852] Liberating from ordinary existence.
[13853] Thus sending from human.
[13854] Herein, the purpose:
[13855] Through this practice, beings are liberated.
[13856] Through liberation, they attain higher realms.
[13857] Thus the purpose.
[13858] Herein, the result:
[13859] All six realms are purified.
[13860] All beings are established in happiness.
[13861] Thus the result.
[13862] Thus purifying on corpse is explained.
[13863] Thus the maṇḍala, vase, and invitation are complete.
[13864] Thus reciting, washing, and sending are finished.
[13865] Thus the instructions on purification are complete.
