[12908] [13809] Thus purifying on corpse.
[12909] [13810] Second, maṇḍala of two cubits:
[12910] [13811] Create a maṇḍala of two cubits,
[12911] [13812] As the abode of the deity.
[12912] [13813] Thus maṇḍala of two cubits.
[12913] [13814] Third, vase transformation:
[12914] [13815] Transform the vase into the deity's abode,
[12915] [13816] Filled with nectar and blessings.
[12916] [13817] Thus vase transformation.
[12917] [13818] Fourth, inviting by moment:
[12918] [13819] Invite the wisdom beings in an instant,
[12919] [13820] Through the power of samādhi and mantra.
[12920] [13821] Thus inviting by moment.
[12921] [13822] Fifth, reciting A age amount:
[12922] [13823] Recite the letter A according to age,
[12923] [13824] Purifying the lifespan obscurations.  [13825] Thus reciting A age amount.  [13826] Sixth, washing:  [13827] Wash with nectar, purifying defilements,  [13828] Cleansing the obscurations of body and mind.  [13829] Thus washing. [13838] Ninth, sending from animal:  [13839] Send consciousness from the animal realm,  [13840] Liberating from stupidity and darkness.  [13841] Thus sending from animal. [13846] Eleventh, sending from god:  [13847] Send consciousness from the god realm,  [13848] Liberating from pride and complacency.  [13849] Thus sending from god.  [13850] Twelfth, sending from human:  [13851] Send consciousness from the human realm,  [13852] Liberating from ordinary existence.  [13853] Thus sending from human.
