[12501] Possessing retinue, enjoyment, prosperity.
[12502] Where to be born depends on present thought-conduct.
[12503] Sutra Section Trunk Arrangement quotation.
[12504] First, possessing retinue:
[12505] When possessing a retinue of disciples,
[12506] One has gathered the accumulations and benefits beings.
[12507] Thus possessing retinue.
[12508] Second, enjoyment:
[12509] The enjoyment of Dharma, the supreme bliss,
[12510] Not worldly pleasure, but the joy of liberation.
[12511] Thus enjoyment.
[12512] Third, prosperity:
[12513] The prosperity of virtue and wisdom,
[12514] The wealth of the seven noble riches.
[12515] Thus prosperity.
[12516] Fourth, where to be born depends on present thought-conduct:
[12517] The realm of rebirth is determined by present mind and action,
[12518] Virtuous conduct leads to higher realms,
[12519] Non-virtuous conduct leads to lower realms.
[12520] Thus where to be born depends on thought-conduct.
[12521] Fifth, Sutra Section Trunk Arrangement quotation:
[12522] As stated in the Sutra Section Trunk Arrangement:
[12523] "Through virtuous thought and conduct,
[12524] One attains the higher realms and liberation.
[12525] Through non-virtuous thought and conduct,
[12526] One falls into the lower realms of suffering."
[12527] Thus the quotation.
[12528] Herein, the importance of thought:
[12529] Thought is the forerunner of all actions,
[12530] Pure thought leads to pure results.
[12531] Thus the importance of thought.
[12532] Herein, the importance of conduct:
[12533] Conduct manifests thought in action,
[12534] Virtuous conduct purifies the mind.
[12535] Thus the importance of conduct.
[12536] Herein, the result:
[12537] Through pure thought and conduct,
[12538] One attains the higher realms and liberation.
[12539] Thus the result.
[12540] Herein, the summary:
[12541] Retinue, enjoyment, and prosperity arise from virtue.
[12542] Rebirth depends on present thought and conduct.
[12543] Thus the summary.
[12544] Thus possessing retinue, enjoyment, and prosperity is explained.
[12545] Thus the dependence of rebirth on thought-conduct is established.
[12546] Thus the Sutra Section Trunk Arrangement quotation is complete.
