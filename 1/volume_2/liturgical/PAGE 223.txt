[9538] Though there are many indicative-example aspects, among all examples, call the supreme become precious crystal.
[9539] By demonstrating that crystal naturally pure complete, clear without outer-inner,
[9540] Know Dharma-body essence.
[9541] Relying on crystal's interior light,
[9542] From wisdom becoming life's wind possessing four limbs,
[9543] Know wisdom-body's clear manner essence.
[9544] Relying on outer-clear light,
[9545] Know emanation body's light-arising manner essence.
[9546] Relying on and demonstrating crystal, know base-abiding wisdom's essence.
[9547] Know wisdom holding within-clear's characteristic essence.
