Completing samadhi twenty-one days, own-appearance impure as samsara door beings six appear, emanation kaya whatever tamed emanates from pure door, doing benefit—like illusion doing illusion benefit, own-appearance doing own-appearance benefit.

What need for already liberated before emitting emanation? If self not liberated, self-benefit not complete, no occasion for other benefit arising.

That emanation explained as only shooting star running, longer than that, time long-short uncertain.

Confusing this about able-not-able emit emanation, many focus mind on able-not-able do other benefit having buddha—great error.

Not wanting buddha not doing benefit after buddhahood in Great Vehicle, impossible.

To those liberated into primordially-pure without long spontaneously accomplished bardo door appearing, no emanation—because impure emanation field not appearing at that time.

From spontaneously accomplished door appearing impure appears, if emanation not doing benefit there, samsara appearance not setting, buddha activity not completing own-appearance, completing by opening emanation appears, that doing benefit, samsara emptying, again that emanation appearance dissolving into spontaneously accomplished eight doors, going to primordially-pure ground.

That primordially-pure ground beyond appear not-appear, three kayas only arise-basis, actually without difference from individual three kayas. Know way of other benefit arising again from that as ground-appearance.

This place is supreme vajra great secret—if from now on not-wrong speaking know arises, know my power.
