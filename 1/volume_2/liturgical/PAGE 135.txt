[5753] Second, holding measure with three abidings has three parts:
[5754] Through three body abidings, separation from action;
[5755] Through three wind abidings, holding measure of non-arising;
[5756] Through three vision abidings, faith of non-returning.

[5757] First three:
[5758] Abiding not abiding in body action, impossible to increase samsara's delusion.
[5759] Abiding limb emitting and gathering, delusion-wheel continuum ceases.
[5760] Placing action at the basis, certain separation from accumulated action-ripening.

[5761] Second three:
[5762] Abiding wind not going outward, no harm from different condition-vision.
[5763] Abiding eye opening inward, no support for different conceptuality.
[5764] Abiding without outer-inner going and coming, empty basis of holding samsara-nirvana as two.

[5765] Third, three vision abidings:
[5766] Abiding without movement in light, recognition of not returning to three realms.
[5767] Abiding without movement in awareness, exhaustion of action and ripening end.
[5768] Abiding without shaking in body, body's contaminations naturally exhaust.
