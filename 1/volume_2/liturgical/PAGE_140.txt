[5911] First, at the time of seeing truth, joy arising to that is first ground: Very Joyful.
[5912] Recognizing that as self-vision is
[5913] Second ground: obtaining Stainless.
[5914] Then, habituation to that is
[5915] Third ground: obtaining Light-Doing Ground.
[5916] Then, habituating to that and seeing light-vision is fourth: obtaining Light-Emitting Ground.

[5917] Then, having habituated to seeing wisdom-vision,
[5918] Purifying all afflictions by nature,
[5919] Seeing wisdom is fifth: obtaining Difficult-to-Purify Ground.

[5920] Then, that effulgence body manifesting from light is sixth: obtaining Manifest Ground.

[5921] Then, habituation arriving at the end, afflictions gone far, is seventh: obtaining Far-Going Ground.
[5922] Then, unmoving from that manner is
[5923] Eighth: obtaining Unmoving Ground.

[5924] Then, qualities complete is
[5925] Ninth: obtaining Good Intelligence Ground.

[5926] Then, having caught self-knowing at wisdom-vision, vision dawning naturally,
[5927] Habituating seeing all dharmas like clouds is
[5928] Tenth: obtaining Dharma-Cloud-Like Ground.

[5929] That is obtaining without abandoning this very shell of truth-seeing.
[5930] Those persons who abandon the shell obtain thus.

[5931] Vision ceasing delusion-vision, dawning as heap-vision is eleventh: obtaining All-Light Ground.

[5932] Then, not attaching to that vision also, unstained by all outer-inner dharmas is
[5933] Twelfth: obtaining Non-Attaching Lotus-Possessing Ground.
