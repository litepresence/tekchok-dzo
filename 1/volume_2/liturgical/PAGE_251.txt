Heart not desiring human association,
Thinking of flying in sky,
Perception of joy when this appearance ceases,
No attachment to body and life,
Mind not entering whatever appearance,
Clear awareness not dull,
Awareness naturally slow,
Joy when associating,
Not able to be aroused by any affliction,
Not holding thought even when affliction arises,
Attachment not arising to beautiful forms,
Not hating ugly forms,
Perception of food and drink not arising through samadhi power,
Making others' minds not accord when associating with humans.

These are signs before complete nirvana.

Of the third, signs of familiarity with bindu—five aspects:
Bindu essence, definition, division, characteristic, and result.

Essence: not different, dwelling as one, without duality.

Definition: bindu not-change, LE vast, greatly pervading all.

Division: body-dwelling channel bindu, nature path bindu, complete result bindu—three.

Of the first, conventional cause bindu white and red two, ultimate wisdom bindu five—two categories.

Wisdom bindu five and ground bindu good, light upper-bur, dwelling unmoved in general body channel.

From Result Exposition:

In crown and heart channel,

Path bindu good, moved by wisdom wind from within channel, goes.

Good bindu possessing ornament, depending on four lamps, shows appearance of increasing experience.
