[10710] Definition: appearing clear, therefore light.
[10711] Division: own-characteristic daytime-holding light, wisdom five own-tone, and light of measure of familiarity from practice increasing above direct experience until reaching measure—two aspects.
[10712] Characteristic: self-illuminating without depending on other illuminators.
[10713] Result: becoming condition for buddhahood in inconceivable dharma-expanse state.
[10714] Familiarizing with such light, confusion-appearance liberates into clear-light, special signs of body and speech appear.
[10715] From Blazing Relics:
[10716] Who is familiar with light,
[10717] In self-seen activity-field,
[10718] Earth, water, fire, and wind—
[10719] Four-elements appearance self-dissolves,
[10720] Gradually enjoying five colors.
[10721] Signs not separate from this,
[10722] In others' seen activity-field,
[10723] Like madness, obstacle-free,
[10724] Without attachment to whatever,
[10725] Transparently penetrating mountain rocks,
[10726] Likewise appearing to enter ground depths,
[10727] Transparently penetrating all waters,
[10728] Body without clothes,
[10729] Naked, obstacle-free to whatever,
[10730] Food and desires self-free—
[10731] This upon this appearance,
[10732] Elements dissolve to self-place,
[10733] Contaminated aggregates not appearing,
[10734] Without doubt obtain result.
[10735] As outer appearance experience-signs,
[10736] So inner awareness arises.
[10737] Signs of nirvana arise when person enters path without error.
[10738] From Self-Arisen:
[10739] Ride changeless awareness horse,
[10740] Always look at correct light,
[10741] Teacher's words indicate great meaning,
[10742] Correctly hold this measure of seeing awareness king,
[10743] Obtain happiness dwelling alone,
[10744] Because own-appearance arises as companion,
[10745] Accord with others' minds,
[10746] Because not separate from own that,
[10747] Become without attachment and clinging,
[10748] Because going to own ground,
[10749] Not become hostile to whatever,
[10750] Because gaining own meaning,
[10751] Even like cotton leaf,
[10752] Because worms and insects are free.
