Doing certainty by clear situation, from Sun-Moon Union:

Exclamation! Conqueror, you listen:
By condensing all those,
The drop remaining as one is obtained.
And what and what are they?
By putting, self-spread is obtained;
By intentions, time is shown;
By exhaust-patterns, intention is shown;
By stable confidence, the not-turning sign.
Thus meanings are condensed.

These are that result:
Who sees the space-lamp
Manifestly exercises in dharma-space;
Who sees the drop-lamp
Obtains the hope-base of those with effort;
Who sees the wisdom-lamp
Realizes general and self-characteristic;
Who sees the water-lamp
Obtains power over faculties;
Who is habituated in lu-gu-rgyud,
Awarenesses obtain balance.
By that result of three bodies,
All these are self-awareness ground.

In the intermediate state of nature-remaining,
The birth-being habituated to these,
All elements as self-vanish,
Become in this without doubt.
These dharmas I proclaimed,
