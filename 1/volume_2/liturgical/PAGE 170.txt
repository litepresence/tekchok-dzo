[7231] Kye Speech Vajra:
[7231] Complete accumulations at one time without practicing six paramitas—ha ha!

[7233] Look to all-equal primordial wisdom of awareness just-place great emptiness—
[7234] Wonder: all actions dawn as ornament;
[7235] Ha ha: liberate all abandoning-accepting by view.

[7237] Look to primordially empty great of empty empty—
[7238] Wonder: all Buddhas abide again in place;
[7239] By doing-action meditating:
[7240] Ha ha: become downfall.

[7242] Look to thing-possessing empty of not-empty—
[7243] Wonder: vehicle grasping as self to non-existent;
[7244] Ha ha: obtain birth-non-existent by being born.

[7245] Thus said: just looking at self-know, twelve laughters abide in nature—effort exertion self-destroy.
[7246] Should echo in great dharma-exhaust mind-transcendence.

[7247] That also: explain complete great self-voice straight in nature, make confidence in mind-depth.
[7248] Thus: should echo also in intention of samsara-nirvana dharma-exhaust object-non-existent by eight great wonder words.

[7249] Also from Jewel Heap:
[7250] E ma ho:
[7251] You listen, speech vajra heart essence!
[7252] By vital point of self-know itself separate from birth-death:
[7253] Cutting million beings' lives and
[7254] To person always practicing ten paramitas—
[7255] I, Samantabhadra, show: not exist distinction even dust.

[7256] Kye Speech Vajra of All Buddhas:
[7257] By vital point of elaboration-non-existent in dharmata:
[7258] Person always familiarizing-meditating emptiness and
[7259] Two persons not making emptiness in mind even for moment—become Buddha: I, Samantabhadra, show not exist distinction even dust.
