[1376-1404] Third: manner of appearance in intermediate state. In dharmata-bardo, five wisdom lights dawn; from final of twenty-one sense-powers, dharmata-bardo sets. In existence-bardo, relying upon impure light within oneself. There too, resonance dawns as six individual types. From Rang-shar: six impure delusion lights—at final of final sense-power, white dawns and yellow dawns, red dawns and green, likewise blue-green dawns, flickering manner regarded to dawn. That impure delusion light. Thus lights, when summarized: delusion-light for unrealized beings and wisdom-light for realized ones, two gather. From that itself: light distinction beyond-thought. Pure wisdom light and impure delusion light regarded. Expanse possesses light, thus body-wisdom-appearance clear. In delusion-time now too, appearance of five elements five colors beyond not gone, all beings dwell in that nature. Buddhas' exhaustion-meditations, rays, measureless bodies emanate, also from that arisen.

[1405-1419] Third meaning: dependent wisdom distinction in three—essence, distinction, definite-words distinction explanation. First essence: primordially uncompounded awareness. From expanse-rigpa primordially-pure dharmata-emptiness nature dawns. From Rigpa Rang-shar Great Tantra: kye-ma! Secret-lord, certainly retain—non-dharma wisdom self-arisen, all dharmas within born become, from that wisdom arising. Second distinction: five wisdoms, when with defilement time, included by being-mindstream, called naturally-spontaneously-accomplished wisdom.

Emaho! Wisdom lights in bardo!
