Listen, dear practitioner! Signs and familiar fruit definite understanding [10257-10321]: From Body Lineage Blaze - who purified Buddha body, at that forehead expanse, conch right coil or top three frown upward, or letter OM mark, line or self mark!

Whoever has these signs, before purified Buddha body! This yogi who purified is inseparable from Buddha body! With confidence, in two lives will obtain! Without intermediate obstacle entering, effort in meditation - this itself is supremely precious!

For all Buddhas' speech - that person before familiar, suitable vessel possessed, at that throat right-left, lotus petal eight or conch right coil, or banner spread, hook or sword, AH letter mark, line or protrusion!

Whoever has this, for all Buddhas' speech, before purified! In two lives, one speech definite fruit obtained! This too without intermediate obstacle is precious! Meditate uninterrupted speech, certainly obtain!

For Buddhas' minds - whoever before familiar, this body sign at heart place: top three vajra upward raise, or wheel spokes four, or three sequence complexion, or jewel shape, HUM letter mark!

Whoever has this, for Buddha mind too, this meditation suitable possessed! Effort in meditation, without intermediate, in three lives in Buddha mind mandala - without doubt Buddha!

Though signs exist, if Dharma not practiced, beginningless habit residue long, immediate non-virtue arises near, karma momentum fierce - merely having past signs does not protect!

Emaho!
