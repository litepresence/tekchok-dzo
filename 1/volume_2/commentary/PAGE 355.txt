Listen! That self-appearance recognizing, gradually certainty obtaining, has seven: Twenty-first, primordially pure liberation. Not dissolved's gap there, emanation opened, primordially pure dissolved. Though twenty-one instants equal, samadhi's days many few existing, faculties sharp-dull posited. Those days instants joined, faculties' appearance-manner and days' enumerations and emanation's dividing manner different.

From Self-Arising: Some white color liberated. Some yellow color liberated. Some red color liberated. Some green liberated. Some dark blue liberated. Some five colors liberated. Some bindu rays liberated. That is end's liberation manner.

End faculties' liberation manner thus: End ground held, fourteen days needed. That too gathered upper-lower six. Pristine awareness five. Spontaneously accomplished three days abiding's end, own appearance seeing, Buddha. Emanation also forty days capable of benefit.

Emaho! The colors of liberation! They correspond to the five Buddha families—white Vairocana, yellow Ratnasambhava, red Amitabha, green Amoghasiddhi, blue Akshobhya. Some see one, some see all five. Don't grasp at the colors—they're just the natural display of your own awareness recognizing itself!
