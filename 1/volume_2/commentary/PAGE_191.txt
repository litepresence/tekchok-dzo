Khregs chod from six faculties' unpurified gates, seeking clarity in mind's dharmas. Thod rgal pure light-luminous gate, wisdom wind urged, four lamps' appearance dawns—surpasses.

From that: "How from gate arising, entering wind urges, from four lamps four activities arise."

Sixth: Khregs chod looking in rigpa self-clear faculty, seeking clarity. Thod rgal outer-inner wisdom wide-open faculty, expanse and wisdom meeting-free appearance, actually analyzing—special.

From Tal 'Gyur: "Pure expanse lamp, thigle body and deluded appearance, nature itself and own-nature, nondual meeting-free appearing."

Seventh: Khregs chod making outer objects inner mind delusion appear, accepting-rejecting-free increase, quickly not liberating. Thod rgal from moment with key, outer-inner deluded appearance with delusion-grasping self-dissolved, not abandoned in-place pure increase not depending.

Wisdom increase-appearing like waxing moon, above increasing, appearance four measures reached, quickly liberating.

From that: "Appearance mode not delusion, pure nature cause-conditions, existent nonexistent self-pure, five gates object-grasping pure, arising coarse pure, wisdom pure increase-appearing shows, from that exhausted dharmata."

These specialties if analyzed finely inconceivable, summarized spoken. Know great difference by wisdom eye possessing!

Emaho! Thod rgal's direct seeing!
