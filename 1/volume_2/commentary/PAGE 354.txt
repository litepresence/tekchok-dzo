Listen! Also own above, pristine awareness body variously radiating one seen. That certainty obtaining. That too shooting star-like. Emanation twenty-seven days capable of benefit.

Middle's supreme: Nine days needed. Again gathered five days. Pristine awareness four days. Then again certainty obtaining. Whatever appearing all mirror-like realizing, seeing, stability obtained. Emanation also thirty days capable of benefit.

Middle's excellent: Ten days needed. Again gathered five. Pristine awareness four days. Spontaneously accomplished appearance one day, then those bodies all non-dual realizing, Buddha. Emanation also thirty-one days capable of benefit.

Middle's excellent: Eleven days needed. Again gathered five. Pristine awareness five. Spontaneously accomplished one abiding. Then again certainty obtaining, all appearances light-house seeing, certainty obtained. Emanation also thirty-four days capable of benefit.

Middle's middle: Twelve days needed. Again gathered five. Separate body wrathful field ground one. Pristine awareness five days. Spontaneously accomplished one day abiding. Then certainty obtaining, stability obtained. Emanation also thirty-seven days capable of benefit.

Middle's end: Thirteen days needed. Again gathered upper-lower six. Pristine awareness five. Spontaneously accomplished two abiding. Then whatever appearing butter-lamp mandala-like seeing, certainty obtained. Emanation also thirty-nine days capable of benefit.

Emaho! The days are metaphorical—they represent stages of recognition, not calendar time! Some recognize quickly, some slowly. The "gathered" days are periods of confusion. The key is that liberation is possible at any stage—don't give up!
