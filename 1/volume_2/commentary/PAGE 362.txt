# Commentary on PAGE 362

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [15077-15121]

My heart-children, understand! Thirteenth, birthplace identifying: Womb-born, egg-born, warmth-moisture-born, miraculously-born four [15077-15081].

Miraculously-born included. Whatever Dharma corresponding, miracles and light existing, god corresponding [15082-15083].

Seven days seven, birth-death suffering engaging, hell corresponding. Thus.

"Intermediate existence miraculously born" [15084-15085].

Cause: From above emanation aspect seeing arising [15086].

Fourteenth, activity: Suchness truth power dissolving, former attachment habit latency arising, former whatever doing, this also doing [15087-15079].

Cause: Suchness time self-display bewildered and surprised arising [15080].

Fifteenth, light: Pure karma upper birth ones, white one mile clear. Impure lower migration birth ones, yellow and black dim merely clear [15081-15083].

Those causes: From suchness self-light arising [15084].

Sixteenth, karma connection: Later good migration Dharma possessing birth, here virtue aspect meeting. Later wealth and retinue and enjoyment possessing birth, pleasant aspect meeting [15085-15087].

Later faculties incomplete and evil-doer and lower migration etc. birth, unpleasant and poverty etc. aspect meeting [15088-15091].

Cause: From suchness attached or anger grasped arising [15092].

Emaho!
