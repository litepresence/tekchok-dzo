# Commentary on PAGE 333

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [14035-14062]

You who practice the Dharma, understand this! Then nose clairvoyance: With divine nose, smell invisible scents [14019]. With pure, smell the scents of coarse material objects [14020]. With extremely pure, smell formless beings' scents [14021]. With supremely pure, smell space-dwellers' scents [14022].

With nose possessing marks, simultaneously smell the scents of the six realms' beings [14023]. With pure, smell shravakas and pratyekabuddhas' scents [14024]. With extremely pure, smell bodhisattvas' scents [14025]. With supremely pure, smell perfectly complete Buddhas' scents [14026].

Then tongue clairvoyance: With the pure element tongue, taste divine nectar [14027]. With pure, taste the flavor of seven thousand nectars [14028]. With extremely pure, taste the great actual nectar of dakinis [14029]. With supremely pure, taste the nectar of the hero who found the swirling nectar [14030].

With pure sense organ tongue, taste the samadhi flavor of great arrangement [14031]. With extremely pure, taste the nectar of five thousand Buddhas' blessings [14032]. With supremely pure, taste the nectar of seven billion Buddhas' blessings [14033].

Then body clairvoyance: With the body of subtle atoms completely purified, touched by gentle melody [14034]. With pure, touch Maitreya's stainless body [14035]. With extremely pure, touch the mind equal to a thousand suns' radiance [14036].

Emaho!
