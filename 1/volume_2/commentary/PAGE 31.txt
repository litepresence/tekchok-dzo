[1588-1648] Second particular explain in four from: First pacifying. From that: white dharmata pacifying—affliction defilement where exists, stain-free mandala. Example crystal pure like, defilement fault by not-stained; likewise pacifying dharmata in, afflictions stain not. Second enriching: from yellow enriching dharmata, all qualities arise. Example precious jewel, all needs-desires fulfill like; by great enriching dharmata, by various qualities dawn, all beings liberate do.

[1649-1664] Third power: also from that, that red power dharmata—not-sought ground complete is, power itself dharma-body essence, all not-sought complete. Example in jewel gold, yellow not-seek need-not like; in great ground-appearance mandala, power mandala seek need-not, ground dharmata power complete. Fourth fierce: also from that, green dharmata fierce—all fierce nature complete, in ground itself pure dharmata, what obstruction-free dawns. Example wind emanation, what obstruction-free move do; likewise from great ground-appearance, wisdom obstruction-free dawn, what need-free wisdom, pure enlightened-activity nature is.

From Rang-shar: great pacifying enlightened-activity, self citta center in dwell; that also soft and gentle and white and rays emanate like; body speech mind itself like, all-pervading wisdom crown-ornament possess, pacifying mandala likewise dwell.
