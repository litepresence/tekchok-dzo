# Commentary on PAGE 340

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [14178-14215]

My devoted ones, understand this distinction! Regarding object, with outflows and without are spoken. The object, the deluded appearance, has outflows; dharmata free of elaboration, unmistaken, is without outflows. The meeting mind of these two is spoken thus [14178-14180].

In nature, the realizing mind of aryas is without outflows, equal; ordinary beings' is with outflows—know thus [14181-14182].

The sixth, Buddha's knowledge extremely without outflows clairvoyance six: Having completed the thirty-six, dwelling as samadhi, seeing and abiding in things' mode without mistake [14183-14186].

Essence expanding as form, see all appearances included in three kayas [14186-14187]. Knowledge expanding as wisdom, simultaneously see previous clairvoyances [14187-14188]. Activity expanding as deeds, see thirty-six perfect marks of emanation body activity [14188-14189].

Thus expanding, engaging in unceasing dharmata wisdom clairvoyance, seeing hidden objects [14189-14190].

Self-purifying signs wisdom, self-existing nature abiding clairvoyance, emptiness appearance [14190-14191].

Invisible faculties, knowledge, mind, wisdom, and wisdom, and compassion's aspects [14191-14196]—previously bound by self-belief in three kayas' hidden ordinary fixation, all liberated by knowledge, so called "great clairvoyance" [14197-14198].

Emaho!
