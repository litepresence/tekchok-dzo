
Ah, precious disciple! Sixth: birth-place signs [14501-14520]—heat upward dissolving, best ka-dag, middle bardo, last nirmanakaya breath-giving certain; ordinary beings born in higher realms, breath-giving. Also: dying time eyes making film, sign-heat gathering, animal birth.

Right hand waving [14521-14540], heat gathering at crown—god. Words confused, heat gathering right armpit—asura. Left foot earth-pounding, heat gathering foot sole—hell. Body yellow, lusterless, heat gathering throat—preta. Words clear, memory not lost, heat gathering eyes—human birth.

Sun-Moon Union [14541-14580]: "Outer appearance like this: if heat upward dissolving, great breath-giving becomes; eyes unclear making film, lower realm places born; right hand waving, that person god-place; mouth words confused, asura likewise born; left foot earth-pounding, hell-place; body color yellow lusterless, preta-realm; mouth sentient-being voice speaking, animal-place; tongue stammering mouth meeting, likewise animal birth-place; words clear memory not lost, human-place."

Outer signs these [14581-14600], inner signs these joined: crown heat gathering, god-place; likewise foot sole heat gathering, hell-place; right armpit heat gathering, asura-realm; likewise throat heat gathering, preta-realm; likewise sign heat gathering, animal birth-place; eyes heat gathering, human birth.

Thus signs possessed [14601-14620]—certain, definite. These especially animals: sign heat gathering, mouth meeting, or mouth various animal voices speaking, or eyes film-appearance covered—outer-inner signs complete.

Emaho!
