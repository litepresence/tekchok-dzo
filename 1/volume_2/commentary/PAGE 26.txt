[1303-1326] Drawing outer light inward, seizing ground, gathering crystal light inward—like abiding as ground of arising without clear although clarity. From Self-Arisen: light, kaya, and wisdom; mother, element, and aggregate; ground and family—thus explained. To all beings, thus; abiding in each own body. That appearance of great wisdom abides in center of tsitta. Path: arising from inside ocean; field: empty sky; body: kayas and wisdom; eye: lamp of wisdom; essence: all-clear mirror. Example: stainless lapis crystal kaya; time: dharmata intermediate; arrived at extent of jewel cavity. If these appearances are seen, called 'manner of appearance of pure one-cutting in this intermediate state.'

[1327-1375] When measure arrives—portion liberated from binding of five aggregates, inside tone arising outside, arising of great appearance of five lights in sky above. From that itself—appearance of pure one-cutting: in sky of pure wisdom, five lights unchanging, clear; also five clusters of five, arising in manner of father-mother. Colors: Blue—Vairocana kaya, dharmadhatu wisdom, Akashadhatvishvari mother, empty sky element, form aggregate, ground of completed action, Thus-Come family. White—Vajrasattva kaya, mirror wisdom, Mamaki mother, great river element, perception aggregate, ground of relinquished action, vajra family. Yellow—Ratnasambhava kaya, equality wisdom, Buddha-Lochana mother, great producing ground element, formation aggregate, ground of various arising, jewel family. Red—Amitabha kaya, discriminating wisdom, Pandaravasini mother, burning fire element, consciousness aggregate, ground showing aspects, lotus family. Green—Amoghasiddhi kaya, all-accomplishing wisdom, Samayatara mother, supporting wind element, feeling aggregate, ground of finished action, karma family.

Emaho! Five lights, five colors, five kayas complete!
