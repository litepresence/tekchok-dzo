Now the instruction: awareness-relaxation method. Three realms' primordially pure intention. Afflictions self-liberated result. First: king seated on throne, unmoving; entering commoners' midst, unmoving; minister taken prisoner, unmoving—from three. First: like royal caste consecrated from crown, seated on own royal seat, unmoving—awareness seated in its own place, unmoving from dharmata. Second: like one king leading army to border lands, entering enemy land, that king with retinue without change—five door-consciousnesses arising, basis empty, samsara without name. Third: like a king's excellent minister captured by other king's land, that king must listen to other king's commands—mental consciousness moving, continuum cut...

See the three metaphors? Your awareness is the king—whether on throne, in crowds, or imprisoned, the nature doesn't change!

Emaho! King awareness never leaves his seat!
