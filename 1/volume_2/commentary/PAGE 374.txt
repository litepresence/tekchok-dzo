Qualities limit not exist thus exist! Palace measure this from north direction league five hundred above exists! There born bodhisattvas form realm gods enjoyment equal! North direction emanation body field realm thus exists!

Field realm those front space emanation body wrathful field realm exists! Thus in all ten directions, Buddha fields beyond measure exist for the benefit of sentient beings! Do not think this is mere imagination!

These are the actual display of Dharmakaya's compassionate activity! Through purification of your own mind, you will see such realms directly! Practice with diligence, dear one, and you too will abide in such fields!

Emaho!
