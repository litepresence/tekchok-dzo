# Commentary on PAGE 349

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [14481-14518]

My devoted ones, understand! First: Sharp, medium, dull faculties each have final, medium, supreme, peak, excellent, best, and complete—seven each, making twenty-one [14412-14418].

Wisdom appearance manner one, delusion basis of aspects one, realization measure one, knowing analysis one, self-display liberation manner one—each arise separately, so faculties also explained as twenty-one [14419-14424].

From Self-Arising: "Hey faithful retinue, listen! I have taught beings' ignorance. Since appearance arising modes are twenty-one, understand these well" [14425-14429].

Faculties shown with three types: supreme, medium, supreme has seven—supreme's final, supreme's medium, supreme's supreme, supreme's peak, supreme's excellent, supreme's best, supreme's complete [14430-14440].

This is supreme's division. That also, seeing-return one, wisdom appearance birth manner one, realization measure one, understanding time one, knowing analysis one—arising and abiding stability in respective appearance obtained [14441-14446].

Medium also has seven—medium's final, medium's medium, medium's supreme, medium's peak, medium's excellent, medium's best, medium's complete. That also wisdom birth measure one arises [14447-14456].

This is medium's. Final also has seven—final's final, final's medium, final's supreme, final's peak, final's excellent, final's best, final's complete [14457-14464].

Emaho!
