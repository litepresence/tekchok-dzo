# Commentary on PAGE 363

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [15093-15132]

Listen well, fortunate ones! Seventeenth, body and afflictions' connection: Though form self-characteristic non-existent, dream-like within self-form attached, desire arising. Anger arising, form. Intermediate arising, formless birth [15093-15096].

Those causes: From suchness truth missing arising [15097].

Eighteenth, three realms which included: Desire, form, formless three [15098-15101].

Name four's skandhas illusory mind body existing-yet, later skandhas not taken, not existent. Formless form other, feeling, perception, formations, consciousness latency skandhas abiding, formless realm included [15102-15108].

Formless cause: From above suchness illusory-like seeing arising [15109].

Nineteenth, next womb entering signs: Seven days or two from seven's gap, intermediate experienced karma exhausted, later existence individually first entering time [15110-15111].

God birth place entered, this time mind's appearance divine palace's upper story or jewel house stacked inside abiding thought [15112].

Demi-god womb entered, spark wheel's inside abiding or rain falling center abiding thought coming [15113-15114].

Human womb entered, extremely cold thought, human body merely. Dull center abiding thought, human body pure. People gathered noise's midst abiding thought, human body precious jewel sign [15115-15117].

Emaho!
