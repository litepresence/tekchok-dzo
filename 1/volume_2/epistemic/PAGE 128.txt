[DECLARATIVE FINALITY]

Polemical critique of Nyingtigpa position on primordial purity (ka dag) denying body and wisdom. The epistemic stance asserts correct understanding of dissolution process (thim pa) and boundary (sa mtshams) between inner expanse (nang dbyings) and outer vision (phyir snang). Finality indicated by "without doubt" (nges pa). Distinction made between erroneous and correct interpretation of emptiness.
