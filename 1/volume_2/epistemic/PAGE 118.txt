Technical description of wisdom-palace (ye shes mkhar) visions and five wisdom colors (ye shes lnga'i kha dog) corresponding to Buddha families (rigs lnga). The epistemic stance is tantric methodology using symbolic correspondences for purification (rnam dag). Classification system (dbye ba) for visionary experiences arising from empowerment sequence (dbang rim). Instructional provisionality indicated by "similar" ('dra ba) terminology showing analogical rather than literal nature.
