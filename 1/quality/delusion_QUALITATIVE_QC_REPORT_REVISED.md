## DEEP ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS

### A++ Quality Standards

Each error block must demonstrate:

**1. Misreading (Specific & Realistic)**
- Immediately recognizable to practitioners
- Not generic (e.g., not "misunderstanding emptiness")
- Concrete scenario: "Taking 'spontaneous' to mean 'no effort needed' leads to..."

**2. Why It Arises (Multi-Layer)**

- **Primary:** Immediate practice impact

- **Long-term:** Years of distortion
- **Social:** Community/lineage effects

## BYTE RATIO RECALIBRATION

**Previous Error:** Using byte ratio as target instead of diagnostic.

**New Approach:**
- **<0.5x:** ALERT - Review for missing coverage (not automatic expansion)
- **0.5x-1.0x:** NOMINAL - Verify quality, don't expand for ratio alone
- **1.0x-2.0x:** TYPICAL - Normal range for well-covered sections
- **>2.5x:** REVIEW - Check for redundancy (don't assume quality)

**Key Insight:** A 0.6x file with deep, comprehensive analysis is better than a 1.5x file with shallow, repetitive blocks.
