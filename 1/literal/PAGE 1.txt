[11] To Samantabhadra, homage.

[12] The five aggregates of self-nature, complete in qualities and powers, the All-Good—

[13] The five lineages of the regents, the great ocean-like, together with the teachings of the supreme lord—

[14] The five awareness-holders of the aural transmission, together with the ornaments of the three worlds—

[15] By-means-of the glorious, auspicious, blissful activity, all are delighted and venerated.

[16] From the beginning, the unobstructed, completely pure expanse, in which all elaborations are pacified—

[17] The self-nature abiding as the eternal, luminous, seven-horse chariot, the three bodies, qualities, and activities—

[18] In the world, all non-abiding, gathered without effort, spontaneously accomplished—

[19] To the peak of the vajra, the supreme, blissful, all-endowed, the essence of the Sugatas, homage.

[20] The dharma's manner, like the sun, unstained, vast, infinite, the ornament of space—

[21] The supreme secret vajra vehicle, arising from all, the glory of the wish-fulfilling jewel—

[22] Within that, the supreme, self-nature, perfectly complete peak—

[23] The essence of direct perception, by-means-of which the long-standing conceptuality of the self is protected—

[24] The abode of the ocean-like teachings, the place of the nāga-king's treasure—

[25] The expanse of the vajra essence, supreme among all vehicles—

[26] The profound and vast abodes, clearly manifesting—

[27] The explanation of the "Precious Treasury of the Supreme Vehicle"—

[28] At the time when the nectar of the dharma-body's essence is extracted,

[29] Complete enjoyment and
[30] The nirmāṇakāya manifesting—

[31] The manner of the dharma, the ocean-like teachings, and the abodes, all taught,
[32] And the abodes—

[33] By-means-of the power of the teachings, the three aspects are gathered and established as:

[34] The abode, complete in qualities, the basis of dharmatā, luminous clarity,

[35] The qualities, not gathered, spontaneously accomplished, arising from the field of the precious, spontaneously accomplished, pure realm.
