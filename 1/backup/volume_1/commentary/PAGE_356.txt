[14606-14617] Look here! These channels—some have water-yellow essence, some blood essence, some wind and earth elements, some five elements' essence. Each one has essences dwelling in sequence! Wind and bindu dwelling—each channel has one of each. These spreading as wind, merely dwelling in form. Do you see? It's all the play of essences, practitioner! Water, blood, wind, earth—your body is a mandala of elements!

[14618-14630] Their function and enumeration—from the dharma wheel's petals: five go upward, vitality radiance and wisdom sending. Five go left, prajna flow and sense faculties holding. Five dwell in joints and center, secret continuity's basis. Five dwell in directions and center, arising-sending and setting-gathering. Thus channel forms—two existent secret channels... Do you see the pattern? Up, left, center, joints, directions—channels weave through your body like threads in a tapestry! And who weaves? No weaver! Just wisdom-display! Emaho!
