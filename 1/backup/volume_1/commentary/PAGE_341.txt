[13954-13970] Thalgyur speaks to those who must count channels before they trust what never moves. The body has no life-pillar. That which thinks "this is the central channel"—find that one first.

[13971-13985] Eight topics, one hundred twelve channels, sixty-four at the peak—numbers for children who need fingers to count. The vital-point is not found in any of them. It is the one who counts.

[13986-13997] Roma, Kyangma, Kundarma—left, right, center. Where is the one who knows left from right? Rest there. Do not search for these in the body. The body is already a channel for confusion.

[13998-14001] Three channels named like old friends. You have never met them. Yet you are never separate from what they indicate.

[14002-14021] A-OM-HUM abide—where? In the space where the letter A has no form. That space is your face right now. Recognize it or chase channels for another eon.
