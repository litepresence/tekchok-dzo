The ripening! Speech as ali kali—self-sound. Mind as emptiness meditation—without face. When you supplicate with fierce aspiration, dreams explain dharma, prophecies arise, signs of accomplishment appear.

Why rely on guru? Because whatever you desire is accomplished—like a wish-fulfilling tree, like a wish-fulfilling jewel, like a cow of desires. Numberless qualities obtained. Samsara's battle reversed!

Five aspects of empowerment action: benefit of empowerment, defect of not receiving, where to empower, when to empower, celebration sequence.

Benefits! Great wisdom appearance. Obstacles removed. Self-appearance shown as companion. All actions accomplished. This life becoming Vajradhara! No lower realms. Becoming a master of liberation.

Special qualities: in bardo, self-appearance face-known. Stable attainment. Able to separate emanation kaya in twenty-one days. Supreme faculties, no suffering or fear. Wisdom appearance never ceasing, always clear.

Common qualities: knowing dakini voice, receiving dakini blessing, honor from humans and gods, loving-kindness from all, beautiful form, great merit, birth as cakravartin, jewel-like qualities, pure conduct like lotus, worthy like stupa, suppressing beings' common suffering like parasol, liberating with wisdom like fire mass, benefiting like sword, holding beings with compassion like vajra hook, realizing sphere-wisdom like dharma cloud.

Hundred eight qualities! All actions accomplished. Obstacles and wrong-leadings cannot harm. Children, enjoyments—all signs of obtained empowerment.
