[2391-2400] Similarly, perishable composite views numberless, included in two: eternalism and nihilism. From this, countless perishable composite views proliferate. Thus all teachings are included in four: viewing extremes, end of extremes, worldly Lokayata, and diffuse.

[2401-2424] Viewing extremes means asserting permanent eternalism. End of extremes means asserting both: permanent is permanent, impermanent is impermanent. Worldly Lokayata means asserting eternalism-nihilism half-and-half. Diffuse means asserting completely cut off nihilism. This itself included in eternalism-nihilism two. From the previous: all those mistaken views are included in four aspects. Viewing extremes, end of extremes, worldly Lokayata, and diffuse. This explanation system: viewing extremes is permanent eternalism. End of extremes is half-permanent half-nihilism. Diffuse is nihilism completely cut off. Worldly Lokayata is result-cutoff. Thus three hundred sixty also are included in four aspects. These also included in eternalism-nihilism two.
