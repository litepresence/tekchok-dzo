[2150-2160]

RECOGNITION:
Examination of self and aggregates. Analysis of personal identity. Arguments against permanent self.

VIEW REGISTER:
Madhyamaka / Selflessness

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- bdag dang phung po = self and aggregates
- bdag 'dzin = self-grasping
- rtag pa = permanent

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Selflessness doctrine
- No permanent self
