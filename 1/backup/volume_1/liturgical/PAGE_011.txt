[323] [323] By perfected wisdom face-hand, the body's self-nature is perfected.
[324] [324] By clear light face-hand, self-appearance is liberated.
[325] [325] By clear discerning face-hand, knowledge is perfected.
[326] [326] The four of the inner:
[327] [327] By appearance of the thirty-two marks, the body's qualities are perfected.
[328] [328] By possessing the eighty signs, reality is perfected.
[329] [329] By emanating masses of light-rays, special qualities are perfected.
[330] [330] By face pervasively residing in all directions, the characteristic of enjoyment is perfected.
[331] [331] The four of the secret:
[332] [332] By residing without front or back, the aspect of appearance is perfected.
[333] [333] Looking from inside, by outer clarity, the quality of empty aspect is perfected.
[334] [334] Looking from outside, by inner clarity, the quality of non-duality is perfected.
[335] [335] By tip pervasively residing in all directions, the quality of non-dwelling without cessation is perfected.
[336] [336] The four outer deeds of speech:
[337] [337] By six self-arisen syllables emanating from tongue, immeasurable reality deeds are perfected.
[338] [338] By entering the father's face, enjoyment of markless dharma is made.
[339] [339] By emerging from mother's space, engagement with non-dual reality is made.
[340] [340] By that same emanating and gathering, deeds without nature are perfected.
[341] [341] The four of the inner:
[342] [342] By self-nature explained, control of inexpressible sound is made.
[343] [343] By non-word, enjoyment of reality beyond speech and thought is made.
[344] [344] By appearing as understood meaning, enjoyment of non-composite reality is made.
[345] [345] By many light-rays emanating from face, enjoyment of disciple and discipliner dharma is made.
[346] [346] The four of the secret:
[347] [347] By pure body practice, engagement with self-arisen dharma is made.
[348] [348] By pure speech practice, engagement with outflow-free dharma is made.
[349] [349] By pure mind practice, enjoyment of wisdom knowing all aspects is made.
