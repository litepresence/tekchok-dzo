[REIFICATION ERROR] [LAYERS AS LEVELS]

MISREADING:
The light layers (snam bu, dum bu) are hierarchical levels of attainment.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Layer" suggests stratification. Spiritual progress conceptualized as ascent.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking to access higher layers.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Hierarchical view of experience.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR] seeking advanced states.

[16651-16670]
[PRACTICE ERROR] [VAJRA COVERING REMOVAL]

MISREADING:
The vajra covering (rdo rje gur) must be removed or penetrated to see the mandala.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Covering" suggests obstruction. "Tent" (gur) implies something hiding inside.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Effort to break through or dissolve covering.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Aggressive manipulation of experience.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR] effortful striving.

[16671-16690]
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [MANDALA AS STRUCTURE]

MISREADING:
The mandala is a geometric structure or arrangement of deities in space.

WHY IT ARISES:
Visual arts tradition of mandala drawing. Architectural metaphor.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Externalizing mandala as object of contemplation.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing that mandala is one's own nature.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[REIFICATION ERROR] [TANTRIC TRANSFORMATIVE VIEW] literalism.
