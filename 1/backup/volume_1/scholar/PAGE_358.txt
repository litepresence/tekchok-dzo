## DOXOGRAPHICAL NOTES
- **Anatomical Precision:** Channel locations specified to finger-width measurements
- **Therapeutic Range:** Head to hand channels for systemic conditions
- **Method:** bsreg (cauterization) for upper body points
- **Naming Convention:** Descriptive names often indicate function or morphology

## KEY TERMS
- gshong bu: hollow/cavity
- zur: corner/edge
- rgyab dkyil: back center
- mtheb mdzub: thumb-index (measurement unit)
- bzlas shing gdon: recite and extract
