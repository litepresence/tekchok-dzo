## PAGE 333 [13647-13681]

### [STRUCTURE]
The passage presents **crown wheel completion** and **seventy-two day development**. Lines 13647-13681 establish:

1. **Wind-disease functions** (13647-13656): Differentiating pure/impure; pleasure/pain/neutral
2. **Channel-wheel luminosity** (13656-13661): Colorful light wheels from channel resonance
3. **Wisdom-channel pervasion** (13661-13669): Ye shes as pervading channels like oil or sunlight
4. **Seventy-two day completion** (13669-13681): Movement and feeding begin

The structure completes **crown chakra formation** with wisdom integration.

### [PHILOLOGY]
**Technical terminology:**

- **དངས་སྙིགས་འབྱེད་པའི་ལས་བྱེད་པ།** (13649): "Function of differentiating pure/impure"—dangs (pure essence) vs. snyigs (dross). **Alchemical terminology**.

- **བདེ་བ་དང༌། སྡུག་བསྔལ་དང༌། བཏང་སྙོམས་ཀྱི་ལས་བྱེད་པ།** (13653-13655): "Functions of pleasure, pain, and equanimity"—three **vedanā** (feelings) produced by wind-functions.

- **ཏིལ་འབྲུ་མར་གྱིས་ཁྱབ་པའམ།** (13663): "Pervading like sesame oil"—simile for **wisdom-pervasion** of channels.

- **ཉི་ཟེར་བུ་གར་ཤར་བ་བཞིན་ནོ།** (13663): "Like sunlight shining in a child's body"—simile for **natural wisdom-presence**.

- **རྩའི་རང་ངོ་ཡེ་ཤེས་ཀྱི་རྩལ་ལས་ལྟ་སྒོམ་སྤྱོད་པའི་རྟེན་དུ་གནས་སོ།** (13664): "The channel's own face abides as support for view-meditation-conduct from wisdom-display"—rtsal (display/dynamism) as **active wisdom**.

**Particle constructions:**
The **similative** ལྟར། and བཞིན། establish **analogical reasoning**. The **ablative of source** ལས། marks **wisdom-origin**.

### [CONTEXT]
**Vedanā theory:**
The three feelings (bde sdug btang snyoms) correspond to **standard Buddhist** vedanā analysis, here **somatized** as wind-functions.

**Dzogchen pervasion:**
The similes (sesame oil, sunlight) illustrate **Dzogchen** doctrine: wisdom **naturally pervades** channels without effort. This is **ka dag** (primordial purity) as **innate presence**.

**Embryological milestones:**
The seventy-two days (13669) mark **10+ weeks** of gestation. Movement ('gul ba) and feeding (kha zas) begin, reflecting **Western embryology** where week 10 marks fetal movement.

### [CONCEPT]
**Wind-functions as determinants of experience:**
The text presents **psychosomatic determinism**: specific wind-functions produce:
- Differentiation (dangs snyigs 'byed pa)
- Pleasure/pain/neutral (bde sdug btang snyoms)
- Disease (nad kyi 'dus mched)
- Afflictions (nyon mongs pa)

This establishes **vāyu** as the **master regulator** of experience.

**Wisdom as natural pervasion:**
Lines 13662-13663 specify that wisdom abides in channels **not as something planted** but like oil in sesame or sunlight in space—**naturally present**. This is the **Dzogchen** view: enlightenment is not achieved but **recognized**.

**Channel-face as practice-support:**
Line 13664 identifies **rtsa'i rang ngo** (channel's own face) as **support for practice** (lta sgom spyod pa'i rten). The channel itself, when recognized, becomes the **ground of path**. No need for external supports.
