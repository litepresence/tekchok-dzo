## [CONTEXT]

**Wisdom Taxonomy:**
The text integrates **standard five wisdoms** with Dzogchen emphasis:

**Mirror-like wisdom foundation** (lines 1-2):
- Stong pas nyams pa nyid: Emptiness → sameness
- Phyogs/ris/dbus/mtha' mi dmigs: No observation of spatial categories

**Discriminating wisdom** (line 2):
- So sor rtog pa'i ye shes not ceased
- Blo rtog tha dad kun nub: Different thoughts dissolved
- Recognition still functions without reification

**All-accomplishing wisdom** (line 3):
- 'Bad rtsol las 'das: Beyond effort
- Bya ba grub pa: Activities accomplished
- Spontaneity emphasized

**Dharmadhatu wisdom** (line 4):
- Dkyil 'khor las ma g.yos: Unmoving from center
- 'Dus pa: Gathered/synthesized
- Chos kyi dbyings: Field of phenomena

**Wisdom of non-elaboration** (line 5):
- Spros bral: Beyond conceptual proliferation
- Fivefold taxonomy completes

**Shes pas bsdus pa'i ye shes Five Types:**
The text distinguishes **five modes of knowing** (lines 6-12):

1. 'Jig rten pa'i shes pas bsdus: Worldly knowing summary
2. 'Das pas bsdus: Beyond summary
3. Shes byas bsdus: Known objects summary
4. Lta bas bsdus: View summary
5. Mkhyen pas bsdus: Omniscience summary

**Shes rab rang byung Structure:**
Four mental activities (lines 12-13):
1. 'Gyu ba: Movement
2. Dran pa: Memory
3. Rtog pa: Conceptualization
4. Bsam pa: Thought

**Mind Movement Classification:**
**Sixfold yid kyi 'gyu ba** (lines 21-38):
1. Kun tu tshol ba: All-pervasive seeking → las/nyon mong different causes/conditions
2. Gtan la 'bebs pa: Establishing certainty → nga/bdag/gzhan/gnyis ka/gnyis ka ma yin
3. Nges pa 'jog pa: Setting certainty → gcig/gnyis distinctions
4. Yid kyi rnam par shes pa: Mental consciousness → yul mi 'dra different objects
5. Nyon mongs pa can gyi yid: Defiled mind → 'dod pa/rmugs pa/sdang ba/etc.
6. 'Gyu byed 'dzin pa: Movement-maker grasping → 'das pa/ma 'ongs/da lta'i dngos po

## [CONCEPT]

**Stong pa as Ground:**
The text presents **emptiness not as negation but as ground of wisdom** (lines 1-5):
- Emptiness → five wisdoms emerge
- Not merely absence but **generative capacity**
- Each wisdom has specific function:
  - Mirror-like: Uniformity beyond spatial categories
  - Discriminating: Recognition without reification
  - All-accomplishing: Spontaneous activity
  - Dharmadhatu: Unmoving synthesis
  - Non-elaboration: Beyond conceptual proliferation

**Sgron ma as Wisdom:**
The lamps are **wisdom modalities** (line 6):
- Not external lights but **modes of knowing**
- Shes pas bsdus: Summarized by knowing
- Five types cover all epistemological domains

**'Gyu ba Phenomenology:**
**Movement as fundamental analysis** (lines 13-38):
- Wind (rlung) and mind (yid) both move
- Six types of mental movement classified by **function**:
  1. **Seeking**: Produces karma and afflictions
  2. **Certainty-establishing**: Produces self/other dualities
  3. **Certainty-setting**: Produces one/many distinctions
  4. **Mental consciousness**: Varied appearances from single cognition
  5. **Defiled mind**: Afflictive emotions
  6. **Grasping-movement**: Temporal grasping (past/future/present)

**Critique of Reification:**
Lines 31-34 critique **over-aggregation**:
- Kha cig: Some people
- "Yid ces gcig brjod pas chog": Saying "mind" alone should suffice
- Rebuttal: Dbye bsdu (analysis/summary) depends on cha shas (parts)
- No contradiction in multiple classifications
