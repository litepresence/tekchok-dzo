[2428-2463] [CONTEXT] Transition to Transcendent Vehicles
**Analysis:**

The Buddha's statement marks the shift from outsider (*phyi rol pa*) to Buddhist (*nang pa*) doxography:

*rigs kyi bu khyod phyin log gi lam du zhugs par 'jigs pas na / 'jig tshogs kyi lta ba mang po 'di dag bsdus nas bshad do* — "Son of the clan, fearing you enter wrong paths, I gathered and taught these many views of aggregates."

**Three-Part Structure:**

1. **Superior Method** (*phyi rol pa las khyad par du 'phags pa'i thabs*)
2. **General Explanation** (*spyi bshad pa*)
3. **Extensive Particular Explanation** (*bye brag tu rgyas par bshad pa*)

**Four Dharma-Seal Mudras:**

The transcendent vehicle accepts:
1. All compounded things impermanent (*'dus byas thams cad mi rtag pa*)
2. All with defilements suffering (*zag bcas thams cad sdug bsngal ba*)
3. All dharmas empty and selfless (*chos thams cad stong pa bdag med pa*)
4. Liberation from suffering peaceful (*sdug bsngal las grol ba zhi ba*)

**Threefold Refuge:**
Going for refuge to the Three Jewels (*dkon mchog gsum*) — absent in outsider paths.

[2464-2484] [STRUCTURE] Vehicle Classifications from Rigpa Rangshar
**Analysis:**

**Primary Division:**
- Sutra (*mdo*)
- Great Extensive (*rgya chen*)

**The Great Extensive Vehicle includes:**
- 84,000 inconceivable
- Extensively numbered
- Many great
- Inexpressible
- Thought-transcending
- Great victor

**Ninefold Division:**

```
Listener and self-enlightened,
And thus bodhisattva.
Kriya and Upa,
And thus explained as Yoga vehicle.
Maha, Anu, Yoga,
And thus Dzogchen Ati.
```

**Threefold Faculty Classification:**

- **Inferior faculties** (*dbang po dma' ba*): Listener, self-enlightened, bodhisattva vehicles
- **Medium faculties** (*dbang po 'bring*): Kriya, Upa, Yoga
- **Sharp faculties** (*dbang po rno ba*): Maha, Anu, Ati (Dzogchen)

This hierarchical structure organizes the vehicles according to capacity, with Dzogchen as the pinnacle for the sharpest faculties.
