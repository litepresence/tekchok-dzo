[4560-4569] [CONCEPT] Three Essences of Tantra
**Analysis:** Lines 4560-4569 present the first section — three essences of tantra:

**Three Essences:**
1. *rig pa rang shar* — Self-Arisen Awareness
2. *rang grol* — Self-Liberated
3. *yi ge med pa* — Without Letters

**Purpose [4562-4564]:**
- Knowing these three like king controlling subjects
- Controls tantra general meaning
- Without letters shows tantra general instruction

**Analogy [4565-4569]:**
- Like minister steering
- Self-arisen holds view-meditation-conduct ground
- Like subjects doing work
- Self-liberated liberates that meaning
- Three gathered like conquering kingdom
- Controls all tantra sections

[4570-4582] [CONCEPT] Three Clarities and Three Flowers
**Analysis:** Lines 4570-4582 present the second and third sections:

**Second: Three Clarities (*dangs ma gsum*) [4571-4576]:**
- Samantabhadra Heart Mirror
- Vajrasattva Heart Mirror
- Jewel Ornament
- Knowing like reaching three mountain peaks
- Purpose: seeing all lower valleys
- Knowing all tantra sections' inner meaning

**Third: Three Flowers (*me tog gsum*) [4577-4582]:**
- Pearl Garland
- Auspicious Beauty
- Lion Power Perfection
- Like three suns in sky
- Purpose: dispelling world darkness
- No ignorance of tantra meaning and words
