## [CONTEXT]

**Epistemological Framework:**
The text establishes **criteria for valid cognition** (lines 10-11):
- **Shes rab dang ldan pa rnams kyis dpyad na rtogs par bya'o**: Those endowed with wisdom must analyze to understand
- **Rten dang brten pa**: Basis (pure perception) and supported (deluded appearance) distinction

**Doxographic Distinction:**
- **'Khor ba'i 'khrul snang** vs. **'das pa'i cha** (line 13-14):
  - Samsara: deluded appearance and deluded mind (non-distinct)
  - Liberation: grasps the transcendent aspect

**Transmission Note:**
- **Gnad 'di gal po che'o** (line 15): "This crucial point is extremely important" - teacher emphasis marker
- **Kho bo'i gnad** (line 16): "My own crucial point" - first-person teacher attribution

**Canonical Structure:**
The exposition follows standard **Dzogchen pedagogical progression**:
1. Definition (lines 17-18)
2. Tripartite analysis (lines 19-28)
3. Common errors (lines 29-32)
4. Phyi/nang distinction (lines 33-41)

---

## [CONCEPT]

**Rgyang Zhags Phenomenology:**
The distant-snare lamp operates as **dual-aspect perception** (lines 7-11):
1. **'Od rtsa dag pa ye shes kyi snang ba 'dzin**: Grasps pure wisdom light
2. **De'i rten gyi 'khrul snang gi snang ba mthong**: Sees the deluded appearance that serves as its basis

This creates a **phenomenological double-vision**: simultaneous perception of pure and impure aspects.

**Shes rab rang byung Tripartite Anatomy:**
1. **Ngo bo** (line 25): When gazing at thig le, internal awareness appears clear and self-luminous
2. **Rtsal** (line 26): External clarity - sharp, swift wisdom knowing objects
3. **Gdangs** (line 28): Vajra chain appearance - golden cord spreading

**Dbyings kyi sgron ma Spatial Topology:**
- **Phyi** (lines 33-35): External space - blue radiance in pure sky
- **Nang** (lines 35-36): Internal dimension - five-colored lights expanding
- **'Ja' 'od kyi khur khyim** (line 75): Rainbow light house - threshold space

**Soteriological Mechanics:**
The passage presents **deconstructive practice** (lines 17-18):
- Thig le emerges from las rlung dag pa (purified karmic wind)
- From thig le emerge two subsequent lamps
- This is not linear causation but **dependent co-arising** (rten 'brel)
