[3317-3321] [CONCEPT] Mahayoga Superiority Through View
**Analysis:** Lines 3317-3321 complete the argument for Mahayoga's superiority:

**Key Distinctions:**
- *blang dor dang gtsang smre med pas 'phags* — Surpassing through no acceptance/rejection, no pure/impure [3317]
- *og ma s lha zhal sbyor du sgom par mi nus* — Lower cannot practice deity-face union [3318-3319]
- *nang pas nus pas kyang 'phags* — Inner capable, therefore superior
- *phyi pa'i lta pa bden pa dbyer med ma rtogs* — Outer doesn't realize non-dual truth [3320]
- *nang pas rtogs pas kyang 'phags* — Inner realizes, therefore superior [3321]

The crucial factor is realization of non-dual truth (*bden pa dbyer med*), not merely different conduct.

[3322-3327] [CONCEPT] Mahayoga: Fourfold Definition
**Analysis:** Lines 3322-3327 present the standard fourfold definition of Mahayoga:

**1. Essence (*ngo bo*) [3323-3324]:**
- *bden pa dbyer med dag mnyam ye shes kyi rol pa* — Play of wisdom of non-dual pure equality
- *tshe gcig gis grol ba thob pa'i rang bzhin* — Nature of liberation in one lifetime

**2. Etymology (*nges tshig*) [3325]:**
- *tshegs chung ngu'i thabs kyis don chen po 'grub pa* — Great aim accomplished through small effort
- Therefore "Inner Method Vehicle" (*nang pa thabs kyi theg pa*)

**3. Purpose (*dgos pa*) [3326]:**
- *tshe gcig gis dngos grub gnyis thob par byed* — Accomplishing two siddhis in one life

**4. Result ('bras bu) [3327]:**
- *tshe 'di nyid du rdo rje 'chang gi sar bgrod* — Proceeding to Vajradhara's level in this life

[3328-3334] [STRUCTURE] Threefold Division of Mahayoga
**Analysis:** Mahayoga divides into three:

1. **Tantra Mahayoga** (*rgyud ma ha yo ga*) [3328]
2. **Scripture Anuyoga** (*lung a nu yo ga*) [3329]
3. **Instruction Atiyoga** (*man ngag rdzogs pa chen po a ti yo ga*) [3330]

**Scriptural Quotation [3331-3334]:** From *Rang shar*:
- "Inner is threefold: Maha, Anu, Ati"

This is the Nyingma classification of Inner Tantras, with Atiyoga (Dzogchen) as the pinnacle.

[3335-3343] [CONCEPT] Tantra Mahayoga: Ground, Path, Fruition
**Analysis:** Tantra Mahayoga practice [3335-3336]:
- *thabs bskyed pa'i rim pa gtso bo* — Generation stage as primary method
- *ting nge 'dzin gsum* — Three samadhis as door
- *bden pa dbyer med chen pos gsal stong 'od gsal gyi ye shes sgom pa* — Meditating on clear-empty luminosity wisdom through great non-dual truth

**Three Approaches [3338-3342]:**
1. **Ground-path-fruition focused** (*gzhi lam 'bras bu gtso bo*)
2. **Accomplishment-focused** (*sgrub pa gtso bo*)
3. **Various-path-transform-focused** (*sna tshogs lam 'gyur du 'dod pa*)

**Simile [3343]:** Ground-essence and mind are non-dual like gold (*gser*) and its yellowness (*ser po*).
