[3809] [PHILOLOGY] Outer Vast: Crucial Point
**Analysis:** Line 3809 concludes the Outer Vast:

- *sems la byar med pas byas pa'i chos kyis sangs mi rgya ba'i gnad* — Crucial point: without action in mind, created dharmas don't become Buddha
- *sems la rgyu mtshan med pas tha dad pa ma yin pa'i gnad* — Crucial point: no cause-reason in mind, non-different

**Key Insight:** No reliance on causes, conditions, or created phenomena for awakening.

[3810-3821] [CONCEPT] Inner Vast: Beyond Eight Vehicles
**Analysis:** Lines 3810-3821 present the Inner Vast:

**Core View [3810-3812]:**
- Not depending on eight vehicles
- Sequence-effort falls into mind
- Various appearances not transcending one liberation
- Nature arises

**Great Perfection Tenets [3813-3816]:**
- *gnas lugs ye rdzogs kyi rdzogs pa chen po'i grub pa'i mtha'* — Established tenet of Great Perfection of primordially perfect abiding-nature
- Self-appearance directionless, universally self-arisen
- Without abandoning anything, completely perfect
- Entity-ground of all entities not falling in any direction

**Line 3817:**
- Dharmatā variously arising, India not exhausted

**Secret Vast [3818-3821]:**
- Appearance-mind error's obstacle self-face primordially pure
- Appearances not abandoned, completely perfect
- Changing mind's obstacle recollection-thought self-pure
- Recollection-thought dharmatā perfect
- Changing empty purity

[3822-3829] [CONCEPT] Thatness Vast and Summary
**Analysis:** Lines 3822-3829 conclude the Vast Expanse:

**Line 3822:**
- Appearance-knowing entering different, free from connection
- Samsara itself arises as nirvana

**Summary [3823-3829]:**
- Self-arisen desire Great Perfection:
  - View: no existence, beyond convention
  - Meditation: no non-existence, no acceptance-rejection
  - Conduct: no existence-non-existence, action-conduct dharmatā liberated
  - Result: no obtaining, non-obtaining placed in self

**Division [3828-3829]:**
- Immeasurable enumerations

[3830-3837] [CONCEPT] Thatness Vast: Crucial Point
**Analysis:** Lines 3830-3837 present the fourth Vast division:

**Essence [3830-3831]:**
- Samsara-nirvana dharmas appearance-knowing body-wisdom arise
- Dharmatā not divided anywhere

**Crucial Points [3832-3837]:**
1. Unchanging crucial point: nail-appearance-knowing two proliferations, samsara reverses
2. Self-face clear-seeing crucial point: recollection-thought self-other place proliferations, mind pure
3. External-internal no object: bliss non-conceptual pure
4. Free from action: beyond mind
5. Self-place knowing: bliss-pain equality

**Summary [3837]:**
- These gathered in three
