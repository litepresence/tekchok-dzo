**Philological Significance:**

The elaborate phonetic system demonstrates:
1. **Precision** — accurate transmission requires correct pronunciation
2. **Authority** — grounded in Sanskrit and Uḍḍiyāna sources
3. **Power** — mantras effective only when properly articulated
4. **Continuity** — link between Indic source and Tibetan translation

**Soteriological Function:**

Correct pronunciation (*ngag*) serves as:
- **Support** for meditation (*sgom*)
- **Cause** for accomplishment (*dngos grub*)
- **Connection** to lineage blessings (*byin rlabs*)
- **Expression** of ultimate meaning (*don*)
