[1269-1272] [PHILOLOGY] Conflict-Free Heaven (*'thab bral*)
**Analysis:**

**Etymology:** *'thab bral* (Free from Conflict/Yāma) because:
- *lha ma yin dang rtsod pa bral ba* — Free from conflict with asuras
- This heaven is free from the constant warfare between gods and demigods

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 0.75 league (*bzhi gsum* = 3/4)
- Or: 375 bow-lengths

**Lifespan:**
- Human years: 200
- God years: 2,000 (one human day = one god year)

**Structural Position:** Fourth of the six desire heavens, marking the descent toward the lower celestial realms.

[1273-1277] [CONTEXT] The Thirty-three Gods (*sum cu rtsa gsum*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** Located on ground decorated by the wish-fulfilling tree *yongs 'dus brtol*

**Arising:** Beings expanded from falling from "limitless" (*mtha' bral* = Yāma heaven)

**Central Symbol:** *glang po che rab brtan* — Great Steadfast Elephant
- 32 gold heads (*gser gyi mgo sum cu rtsa gnyis*)
- 1 turquoise head (*g.yu'i mgo gcig*)
- 3 roots exercising power and near-power

**Thirty-three Abodes:** Including:
- *ma 'dres* — Separate/Exclusive
- *phreng 'bar ba* — Blazing Necklace
- And 31 others

**Thirty-three Powers:** The *dbang po sum cu rtsa gsum* (33 powers/activities) give this heaven its name.

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 0.5 league (*bzhi cha gnyis* = 2/4 = 1/2)
- Or: 250 bow-lengths

**Lifespan:**
- Human years: 100
- God years: 1,000

[1278-1284] [STRUCTURE] The Four Great Kings (*rgyal chen ris bzhi*)
**Analysis:**

**Location:** On the peak of Meru and bases like *gnya' shing 'dzin* (yoke-holder)

**Function:** 
- Born as lords of the four continents (*gling bzhi so so'i bdag po*)
- Exercise control and protection (*dbang bsgyur la bsrung ba*)

**The Four Kings:**
1. *spyan mi bzang* — Virūpākṣa (West)
2. *rnam thos kyi bu* — Vaiśravaṇa (North)
3. *yul 'khor srung* — Dhṛtarāṣṭra (East)
4. *'phags skyes po* — Virūḍhaka (South)

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 0.25 league (*bzhi cha gcig* = 1/4)
- Or: 125 bow-lengths

**Lifespan:**
- Human years: 50
- God years: 500

[1285] [SUMMARY] The Six Desire God Classes
**Analysis:**

*'dod lha rigs drug* — Complete enumeration:

| Heaven | Tibetan | Sanskrit | Body (leagues) | Human Years |
|--------|---------|----------|----------------|-------------|
| 1 | gzhan 'phrul dbang byed | Paranirmitavaśavartin | 2.5 | 1,600 |
| 2 | 'phrul dga' | Nirmāṇarati | 1.25 | 800 |
| 3 | dga' ldan | Tuṣita | 1.0 | 400 |
| 4 | 'thab bral | Yāma | 0.75 | 200 |
| 5 | sum cu rtsa gsum | Trayastriṃśa | 0.5 | 100 |
| 6 | rgyal chen ris bzhi | Cāturmahārājika | 0.25 | 50 |

**Pattern:** Consistent halving of both body size and lifespan as descent progresses.

[1286-1292] [CONTEXT] The Four Asura Classes (*lha min la ris bzhi*)
**Analysis:**

**1. Life-Glory God Type (*tshe dpal lha'i ris*):**
- Equal to Thirty-three gods in body and lifespan
- Highest class of asuras

**2. Great-Power Yakṣa Type (*stobs chen gnod sbyin gyi ris*):**
- Equal to Four Great Kings in body, life, and enjoyment
- Powerful nature spirits

**3. Fierce Nāga Type (*gdug pa klu'i ris*):**
- Equal to nāgas
- Serpent/dragon beings

**4. Killer Rākṣasa Type (*gsod byed srin po'i ris*):**
- Accord with pretas (hungry ghosts)
- Man-eating demons

**Additional Classes:**
- Cruel Māra type (*rtsub 'gyur bdud kyi ris*)
- Cloth-beautiful type (*thags bzangs ris*)
- Equal to Paranirmitavaśavartin in enjoyment type

**Doxographical Note:** This classification shows the hierarchical nature of the demigod realm, parallel to but distinct from the god realm, with beings ranging from nearly divine to nearly hellish in their characteristics.

**Structural Completion:** Having enumerated all six desire heavens and four asura classes, Longchenpa has completed the survey of the upper realms. He will now turn to the lower realms (animals, pretas, hells) and then to human existence.
