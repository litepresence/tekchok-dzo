[1199-1201] [PHILOLOGY] The Not-Sorrow Heaven (*mi gdung ba*)
**Analysis:**

**Etymology:** *mi gdung ba* (Not Sorrow/Free from Suffering) derives from:
- *dga' bde'i snang ba* — Joy-bliss appearance arising
- *gdung ba med pa* — Without sorrow/affliction

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 2,000 leagues (*dpag tshad nyis stong*)
- Lifespan: 2 intermediate eons (*bar gyi bskal pa gnyis*)

**Pattern:** The 2:2 ratio continues the form realm numerical correspondence.

[1202-1206] [CONTEXT] The Not-Great Heaven (*mi che ba*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che shel gyi khang bu brtsegs pa* — "Jewel Crystal House Pile"

**Etymology:** *mi che ba* (Not Great) because:
- *snang ba la 'dod sred mi che ba* — Desire-attachment to appearance is not great
- This marks decreasing attachment as we descend toward the desire realm boundary

**Dimensions:**
- Lifespan: 1 intermediate eon (*bar bskal gcig*)
- Body: 1,000 leagues (*dpag tshad stong*)

**Philosophical Significance:** The decreasing attachment (*'dod sred*) correlates with decreasing lifespan and body size, demonstrating the inverse relationship between subtlety and duration in form realm cosmology.

[1207] [STRUCTURE] The Five Pure Abodes (*gtsang ma ris kyi lha lnga*)
**Analysis:**

**Classification:** *gnas ris lnga po* — Five place classes

**Qualification:** *nyon mongs pa'i dring ba 'ga' zhig med pas* — Without some affliction trace

**Designation:**
- *gtsang ma ris kyi lha lnga* — Five Pure Class Gods
- *'phags pa'i gnas gzhan lnga* — Five Other Noble Places

**Doxographical Note:** The Pure Abodes (śuddhāvāsa) are exclusive to āryas (noble beings) who have attained the path of seeing (*darśana-mārga*) or higher. Ordinary beings (*so so skye bo*) cannot be born here because they still possess affliction-traces (*nyon mongs pa'i dring ba*).

**The Five Pure Abodes:**
1. *'og min* — Neither Below Nor Above (Akanistha)
2. *shin tu mthong* — Clear Sight (Sudṛśa)
3. *gya nom snang ba* — Excellent Appearance (Sudarśana)
4. *mi gdung ba* — Not Sorrow (Atapa)
5. *mi che ba* — Not Great (Avṛha)

[1208-1210] [PHILOLOGY] The Ordinary Being Heavens Begin
**Analysis:**

**Transition:** Below the Pure Abodes (*so so skye bo'i gnas gzhan gsum gyi gong ma*), we enter heavens accessible to ordinary beings.

**Sixth Heaven:** *rin po che rab mdzes rnam par brdal ba* — "Jewel Supreme Beauty Various Spread"

**Arising:** Some "not great" gods (*mi che ba'i lha 'ga'*) fall from above and expand

**Name:** *'bras bu che* — Great Fruit
- **Etymology:** Established from great virtue-fruit of merit-share-equal (*bsod nams cha mthun gyi dge ba'i 'bras bu che ba*)

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 500 leagues
- Lifespan: 500 intermediate eons

**Pattern Continuation:** The 500:500 ratio maintains the body-lifespan correspondence.

[1211-1214] [CONTEXT] Merit-Born Heaven (*bsod nams skyes*)
**Analysis:**

**Heaven Name:** *rin po che ka li mdog mdzes kyis rnam par spras pa* — "Variously Ornamented by Jewel Kali Beautiful Color"

**Arising Mechanism:** Some "great fruit" gods fall in "dot manner" (*thig pa'i tshul du chad pa*) — suggesting particulate or gradual descent

**Etymology:** *bsod nams skyes* (Merit Born) because:
- *sngar bsod nams mang du bsogs pa* — Previously accumulated much merit
- Merit itself becomes the direct cause of birth

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 250 leagues (*dpag tshad nyis brgya lnga bcu*)
- Lifespan: 250 intermediate eons

[1215-1220] [PHILOLOGY] Cloudless and Light-Emitting Heavens
**Analysis:**

**Eighth Heaven:** *rin po che sprin rnam par stug po* — "Jewel Cloud Various Thick"

**Arising:** Merit-born beings fall and expand into *sprin med 'od* — Cloud Without Light

**Characteristics:**
- *dal 'jam dro ba'i tshad dang ldan pa* — Possesses slow, gentle, warm measure
- Form: Like sun without clouds (*sprin med pa'i nyi ma'i rnam pa*)

**Etymology:** Named for appearing like unobstructed sunlight

**Dimensions:**
- Body: 125 leagues (*dpag tshad brgya nyi shu rtsa lnga*)
- Lifespan: 125 intermediate eons

**Ninth Heaven:** *rin po che 'od rnam par 'byin pa* — "Jewel Light Various Emit"

**Arising:** From Cloud Without Light (*sprin med*) fall into *dge rgyas* — Virtue Increase

**Structural Pattern:** Notice the consistent halving of dimensions:
- 500 → 250 → 125 leagues
- 500 → 250 → 125 eons

This geometric progression reflects the systematic nature of Buddhist cosmological mathematics.

**Transition:** Having enumerated all nine form realm heavens accessible to ordinary beings (plus the five Pure Abodes), Longchenpa will now complete the form realm description and transition to the desire realm heavens.
