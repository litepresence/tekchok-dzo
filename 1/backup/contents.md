## KEY DOCTRINAL FRAMEWORKS

### Three Aspects of Ground (*gzhi'i cha gsum*)
1. **Essence** (*ngo bo*): Primordial purity (*ka dag*)
2. **Nature** (*rang bzhin*): Spontaneous presence (*lhun grub*)
3. **Compassion** (*thugs rje*): Unceasing display (*ma 'gags pa*)

### Three Non-Recognitions (*ma rig pa gsum*)
1. Causal non-recognition (*rgyu ma rig*)
2. Simultaneously-arisen non-recognition (*lhan cig skyes pa'i ma rig*)
3. Imputed non-recognition (*kun brtags pa'i ma rig*)

### Four Lamps (*sgron ma bzhi*)
1. Far-reaching Water Lamp (*rgyang zhags chu'i sgron ma*)
2. Empty Bindu Lamp (*thig le stong pa'i sgron ma*)
3. Completely Pure Sphere Lamp (*dbyings rnam dag gi sgron ma*)
4. Self-Arising Wisdom Lamp (*shes rab rang byung gi sgron ma*)

### Four Visions (*snang ba bzhi*)
1. Direct perception of dharmatā (*chos nyid mngon sum*)
2. Increasing experience (*nyams gong 'phel*)
3. Awareness reaching full measure (*rig pa tshad phebs*)
4. Exhaustion into dharmatā (*chos nyid zad pa*)

### Four Empowerments (*dbang bzhi*)
1. Vase empowerment (*bum dbang*)
2. Secret empowerment (*gsang dbang*)
3. Wisdom-knowledge empowerment (*shes rab ye shes kyi dbang*)
4. Word empowerment (*tshig dbang*)

### Sixteen Grounds (*sa bcu drug*)
1. Great Joy (*rab tu dga' ba*)
2. Stainless (*dri ma med pa*)
3. Luminous (*'od byed pa*)
4. Radiant (*'od 'phro ba*)
5. Difficult to Cultivate (*sbyang dka' ba*)
6. Manifest (*mngon du gyur pa*)
7. Far-Going (*ring du song ba*)
8. Immovable (*mi g.yo ba*)
9. Good Intelligence (*legs pa'i blo gros*)
10. Cloud of Dharma (*chos kyi sprin*)
11. Universal Light (*kun tu 'od*)
12. Lotus Holder (*padma can*)
13. Letter-Wheel (*yi ge 'khor lo*)
14. Vajra Holder (*rdo rje 'dzin pa*)
15. Wisdom Sun (*ye shes nyi ma*)
16. Wisdom Guru (*ye shes bla ma*)
