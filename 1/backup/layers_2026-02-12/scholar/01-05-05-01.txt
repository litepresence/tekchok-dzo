[6759-6800] [STRUCTURE]
Empowerment Ground Framework

[6759-6763]
**Five Difficult Points Section:**
The text transitions to the fifth section (*rim khang lnga pa*) on difficult points (*dka' ba'i gnas*) in the definitive meaning of Secret Great Vehicle (*gsang chen nges pa'i don*). This establishes the framework for entering unsurpassed vehicle (*bla med pa'i theg par 'jug pa'i sgo*) through empowerment (*dbang*).

[6764-6768]
**Empowerment Ground Division:**
The ground for empowerment divides into four:
1. Teacher's characteristics (*ston pa'i mtshan nyid*)
2. Student's nature (*slob ma'i rang bzhin*)
3. Common practices (*thun mong du bya ba'i chos*)
4. How to confer empowerments (*ji ltar bskur ba dbang gi bya ba*)

[6769-6800]
**Teacher Characteristics:**
Drawing from *Rig pa rang shar* and *Thal 'gyur*, the qualified teacher (*bla mar 'os pa*) possesses:
- Complete virtues (*yon tan thams cad rdzogs*)
- Freedom from all faults (*skyon thams cad dang bral ba*)
- Great waves of compassion (*thugs rje rgyal ba chen po*)

Six specific characteristics (*mtshan nyid drug ldan*):
1. Renouncing cyclic existence ('khor ba'i chos rgyab tu dor ba)
2. Few desires, contentment ('dod pa chung zhing chog shes pa)
3. Expert in methods, possessing experience (lag len la mkhas shing nyams myong yod pa)
4. Expert in tantra words and meaning, diligent in practice (rgyud kyi tshig don la mkhas shing sgrub pa la brtson pa)
5. Expert in view meaning, complete power (lta ba'i don la mkhas shing nus pa rdzogs pa)
6. Great compassion, delighting in generosity (snying rje che zhing gtong la dga' ba)
