---

<Integration with the Treasury structure demonstrates Longchenpa's architectural mastery in encoding>
<recognition-instructions within comprehensive doctrinal presentation. This page contributes to the systematic>
<exposition of TEMPORARY FRUITION while simultaneously pointing beyond conceptual grasping to direct>
<recognition of rig pa. The apparent specificity of the content—whether describing visionary experiences, bardo>
<processes, fruition stages, or transmission lineages—serves the single purpose of supporting practitioners in>
<recognizing their own nature. The rang byung (self-arisen) quality pervades the entire presentation,>
<precluding constructed attainment while accommodating all apparent practices as natural expressions of>
<awareness.>

---

<Practical implications for fruition realization emphasize the non-attainment nature of complete enlightenment.>
<The rainbow body ('ja' lus) and other signs of realization manifest spontaneously when fixation on materiality>
<dissolves, not as accomplishments to be achieved. The text describes various degrees of fruition—from complete>
<dissolution of the body to partial signs—while maintaining that all are expressions of the same recognition.>
<Practitioners are reminded that fixation on attaining specific signs constitutes another form of grasping to>
<be naturally liberated.>
