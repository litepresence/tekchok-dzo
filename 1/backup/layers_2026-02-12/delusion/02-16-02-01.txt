[1021-1035] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [CHAKRA PHYSIOLOGY]
MISREADING:
Chakras are actual physical structures or plexuses in the body.

WHY IT ARISES:
Correspondence with nerve plexuses suggests physical basis.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Reducing chakras to anatomy; literal location hunting.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the energetic and wisdom aspects.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR].
