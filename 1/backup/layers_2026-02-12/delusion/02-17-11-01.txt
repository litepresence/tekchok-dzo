[3354-3393] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [GROUND AS BASIS]
MISREADING:
Ground (gzhi) is a basis or foundation that supports or underlies phenomena.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Ground" terminology suggests metaphysical foundation.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Dzogchen reified as metaphysical system.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Ground-phenomena dualism.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].
