[10766-10809] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [BEYOND CONCEPTS AS MYSTERY]
MISREADING:
"Beyond concepts" means a mysterious ineffable reality.

WHY IT ARISES:
Romantic mysticism. Ineffability tropes.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Mystery-mongering; obscurantism.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Refusing to explain; elitism.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[EPISTEMIC ERROR].

[10810-10825] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [INTEGRATION AS ECLECTICISM]
MISREADING:
Integration means combining Dzogchen with other traditions or methods.

WHY IT ARISES:
Syncretism fashionable. Pick-and-choose spirituality.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Dilution of Dzogchen view.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Confused practice; mixed methods.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[VIEW COLLAPSE].
