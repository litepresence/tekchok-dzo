[13304-13305] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [TANTRA CLASSIFICATION RIGIDITY]
MISREADING:
Tantra classes are fixed, mutually exclusive categories.

WHY IT ARISES:
Classificatory thinking. Academic taxonomy.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Rigid categorization.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the fluidity and overlap.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR].
