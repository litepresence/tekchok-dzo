[1861-1893] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [INTERNAL-EXTERNAL DUALISM]
MISREADING:
External elements are physical world; internal elements are body/mind—two separate domains.

WHY IT ARISES:
Subject-object dualism.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Missing the non-dual nature of inner-outer.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Environment seen as separate from practitioner.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[DUALISTIC ERROR].
