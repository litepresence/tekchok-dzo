[15275-15310] [PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [DEDICATION AS RITUAL]
MISREADING:
Dedication prayers are ritual formulas without actual intent.

WHY IT ARISES:
Prayer as rote recitation.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Mouthing words without meaning.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Empty ritualism.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PEDAGOGICAL ERROR].
