MISREADING:
The kriya_upa_yoga presentation is understood as establishing fixed ontological categories rather than pedagogical skillful means.


WHY IT ARISES:
Literal interpretation of teachings. The practitioner wants definitive truths. Religious traditions often posit metaphysical realities.


PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Categories become reified. The practitioner treats fluid descriptions as fixed realities.


SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Flexibility is lost. The practitioner forces experience into categorical boxes.

[3890-3892] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [VIEW COLLAPSE]
================================================================================


MISREADING:
Systematic analysis of kriya_upa_yoga is interpreted as requiring comprehensive intellectual mastery before practice can begin.


WHY IT ARISES:
Academic conditioning. The practitioner assumes knowledge precedes realization. Fear of missing important points.


PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Study replaces practice. The practitioner becomes expert without realization.


SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Intellectual pride develops. Recognition is deferred indefinitely.
