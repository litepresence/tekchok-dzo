MISREADING:
Aspiration prayers are wishes for future attainment.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Aspiration" suggests future orientation.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Deferring realization to future.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the always-present.

[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].
