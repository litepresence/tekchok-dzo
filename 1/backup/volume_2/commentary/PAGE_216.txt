Listen carefully to the fourth doctrine! [9315-9332] Sun Moon Equal Measure doctrine's nature: realizing appearance-emptiness exhaustion-equal non-duality. Differentiated by faculty: From Tsal Dzog—"When Sun Moon Equal Measure's doctrine overflows, excellent middling lesser have three seeing aspects. Excellent view realizes wisdom zangthal. Meditation: inseparable from unceasing wisdom river. Conduct: great wisdom conduct playing ceaselessly. Result: attainment of method-wisdom joining, two liberations—four aspects without separation."

[9333-9341] Middling three aspects: view realizes method-wisdom non-dual. Meditation: abiding in method-wisdom's nature like sun-moon in space. Conduct: enjoying great bliss secret bindu white-red. Result: attaining buddha families' five joining practice womb buddhahood. Lesser three aspects: view realizes father-mother joining bodhicitta lake-symbol view. Meditation: deity's nature essence remembered, abiding in clear samadhi. Conduct: enjoying five medicines, five nectars. Result: attaining pure display result, intermediate state taking yidam deity's illusory body.

[9342-9371] The fifth Ocean Depths Opening doctrine's nature: abiding in equal-pure dharmata natural potency spontaneous meditation. Differentiated by faculties: From Tsal Dzog—"When Ocean Depths Opening's doctrine, excellent middling lesser have three seeing aspects. Excellent view: whatever appears is own display—realizing great wisdom's activity. Meditation: less attachment to displays, like being intoxicated with alcohol. Conduct: acting in samsara and nirvana. Result: attaining wisdom arising one-pointedly without abiding in displays."

[9372-9384] Middling three aspects: view realizes clarity zangthal wisdom body. Meditation: having forceful short duration like waterfall. Conduct: possessing non-common conduct like deer-conduct. Result: attaining final certainty, reaching wisdom essence. Lesser three aspects: view wishes to abandon deluded display gradually. Meditation: abiding in god and human mind-holding. Conduct: possessing artificial dualistic grasping conduct. Result: attaining god and human status. These five doctrines bind all Secret Heart Essence yogins—Mountain Summit for view, Wisdom Pouring for ground, Thigle for examining liberation, Sun Moon for carrying to exhaustion, Ocean for carrying to kadag ground.
