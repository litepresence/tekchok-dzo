From here north direction world realm, dear practitioner! Thus in four directions, four emanation body field realms exist with immeasurable qualities! Each with its own particular excellence, yet all displaying the same Buddha activity!

Do not think these are merely stories! These are real fields of pure appearance, where practitioners who have purified their perception will take birth! Through the power of devotion and practice, you too can obtain such a birth!

Emaho!
