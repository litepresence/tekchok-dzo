# Commentary on PAGE 339

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [14163-14197]

Listen well, you fortunate ones! With characteristicless dharmata realization, see hungry ghost realms, lifespans, sufferings, karma, and karmic results, and emanate three measures of countless forms [14151-14153].

With all phenomena realized as self-luminous nature, see animal realms, sufferings, lifespans, karma, and karmic results, and emanate five measures of countless forms [14154-14158].

With all phenomena realized as equality, see human realms and so forth, and emanate hundreds of billions [14158-14159].

With all phenomena realized as empty of self or mine, see asura realms and characteristics, and emanate ninety thousand [14159-14161].

With all phenomena realized as free of words, see gods' realms, lifespans, and fall times, and emanate nine hundred thousand [14161].

Thus, through self-arising knowledge of others' benefit, the different types of beings—twenty-one hells, four hungry ghosts, two animals, four humans, three asuras, and though gods have many types, included in three realms—six birthplaces of gods, their forms, enjoyments, conduct, perceptions, and characteristics [14162-14175].

The six clairvoyances see and know faculties and powers [14175].

Thus thirty with outflows—the six sense faculty ways of seeing—and thirty without outflows—the six faculties' seeing others—sixty [14176-14177].

Emaho!
