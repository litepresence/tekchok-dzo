# Commentary on PAGE 332

**Tibetan Reference:** Lines [14005-14034]

My heart-children, continue listening! With purest purity, simultaneously see seventy billion sambhogakaya realms [14000].

Then with the all-aspects-knowing eye, simultaneously see inconceivable dharmakaya realms [14001]. With pure, simultaneously see inconceivable sambhogakaya realms [14002]. With extremely pure, simultaneously see inconceivable nirmanakaya realms [14003].

Then ear clairvoyance: With divine ear, upon hearing actual sounds, you also hear the voices of hidden beings [14005]. With pure, hear up to a thousand miles [14006]. With extremely pure, five thousand [14007]. With supremely pure, two hundred thousand [14008].

With flesh ear, hear the sounds of hell realms up to a hundred thousand miles [14009]. With pure, hear the voices of asuras [14010]. With extremely pure, hear gods' voices [14011]. With supremely pure, simultaneously hear the voices of inconceivable beings in continents without number [14012].

With liberated ear, hear shravakas and pratyekabuddhas' voices [14013-14014]. With pure, hear bodhisattva servants' voices [14015]. With extremely pure, hear the speech of countless Buddhas [14016]. With supremely pure, hear the dialogue of seventy countless Buddhas [14017-14018].

Emaho!
