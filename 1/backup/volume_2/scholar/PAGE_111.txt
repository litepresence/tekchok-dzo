**Analysis:** The section transitions to the second subtopic (*gnys pa*): individual natures. The analysis of *mngon sum* (direct perception) at 4914-4931 presents four types:
1. **Sense-power direct** (*dbang po'i mngon sum*): Forms, etc., five objects
