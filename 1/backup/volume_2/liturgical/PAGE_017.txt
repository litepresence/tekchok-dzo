[871] [872] Five primordials:
[872] [873] Five paths, primordially complete,
[873] [874] Five kayas, primordially established,
[874] [875] Rigpa, primordially arisen,
[875] [876] Five lights, primordially resonant,
[876] [877] Defilements, primordially liberated.
[877] [878] Five knowledges:
[878] [879] From realization's extent,
[879] [880] Know as five kayas,
[880] [881] Know as five wisdoms,
[881] [882] Know as five lights,
[882] [883] Know as self-appearance,
[883] [884] Know as duality-free great liberation.
[884] [885] Five realizations:
[885] [886] Realize field as expanse,
[886] [887] Realize beings as kayas,
[887] [888] Realize appearance as light,
[888] [889] Realize arise-liberation category-free,
[889] [890] Realize wisdom as rigpa.

[891] [892] Light-clear basis: rigpa,
