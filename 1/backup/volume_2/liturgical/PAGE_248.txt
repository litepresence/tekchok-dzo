[10662] Of the second meaning, particular explanation of main signs—two aspects:
[10663] General showing of common view-meditation signs;
[10664] Particular explanation of direct clear-light signs.
[10665] Of the first:
[10666] View free of extremes, trained like sky, going through sky, circumambulating Jambudvipa in an instant;
[10667] Solid things dissolving when gazed upon.
[10668] Meditation of self-settled clarity, trained samadhi, wondrous signs of body, speech, and mind arise.
[10669] From Blazing Relics:
[10670] Signs of familiarity with view:
[10671] Upon the fortunate person,
[10672] These likewise arise.
[10673] Because emptiness expands body and mind,
[10674] Like dakinis in sky,
[10675] Turning self-control over wind,
[10676] Able to travel all Jambudvipa
[10677] In six hundred instants like wind,
[10678] Because own-mind realizes emptiness,
[10679] Able to dissolve whatever is gazed upon.
[10680] This gains great confidence in view.
[10681] Signs of training in meditation:
[10682] No insects on body,
[10683] Because compassion completes other benefit,
[10684] Because concept mind-continuum is empty,
[10685] Body becomes like cotton,
[10686] Because awareness possesses deathless meaning,
[10687] Body becomes like sixteen years passed.
[10688] Because reality moisture pervades mind,
[10689] Hair becomes oily.
[10690] Thus mind does not wander,
[10691] Sits where placed,
[10692] Cannot enter confusion objects.
[10693] This gains meditation oneself,
[10694] Obtains buddhahood in two lives.
[10695] These are certain signs of conceptual object beings.
[10696] Of the second, particular explanation of direct clear-light signs—five aspects:
[10697] Light, expanse, bindu, kaya, and awareness trained to measure.
[10698] Of the first, light—five aspects:
[10699] Essence, definition, division, characteristic, and result.
[10700] Light's essence shows awareness tone.
