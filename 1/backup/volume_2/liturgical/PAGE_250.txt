[10761] Signs of familiarity with expanse—five aspects:
[10762] Essence, definition, division, characteristic, and result.
[10763] Essence: vast, pervading great, clear by nature, self-clear.
[10764] Definition: called center, called expanse—the nature of reality things, vast, without end.
[10765] Division: outer expanse sky, inner expanse lamp, secret expanse light and bindu, unsurpassed expanse great appearance of confusion-appearance self-ceasing.
[10766] According to rank: awareness, wisdom, three kayas, result object.
[10767] Characteristic: vast and opening, able to appear, pervading, appearing as clear object, like clear mirror expanse.
[10768] Briefly: not reality thing, produces power to clearly appear as object making reality appear.
[10769] Result: depending on not-mistakenly understanding reality object, seeing reality own essence, making direct non-change into path, buddhahood.
[10770] Familiarizing with such expanse, wondrous outer signs appear as experience.
[10771] From Blazing Relics:
[10772] Who is familiar with expanse,
[10773] Outer-inner breath movement ceases,
[10774] Goes like bird in sky,
[10775] Likewise like fish in water—
[10776] This fortunate one goes,
[10777] And sees objects not seen before,
[10778] Continent and Meru together,
[10779] Sees from one sitting place,
[10780] Likewise buddha-fields
[10781] Not seen before,
[10782] All these sees at one time.
[10783] Thus possessing signs,
[10784] From this in sixteen months,
[10785] Dharma-expanse also sees.
