## PERFECTION STANDARDS (A++ Requirements)

### 1. Voice Work (40% of grade)
- **Minimum 6 distinct voices** per file
- **Voice 01 (Garab Dorje)**: Stark, three-word pointing
- **Voice 13 (Patrul)**: Earthy humor, self-deprecating
- **Voice 10 (Longchenpa)**: Vast synthesis, bridges others
- **Voice 06 (Padmasambhava)**: Visionary, poetic, fierce-tender
- **Voice 20 (Lion-Faced)**: Shocking cutting (use 1-2x)
- **Plus 1-2 rotating visitors**
- Each voice recognizable by speech pattern alone
- NO explicit attribution (no "Garab Dorje said...")

### 2. Metaphor Quality (30% of grade)
- **100% fresh metaphors** (no repetition within 5 sections)
- **Specific details**: Not "mountain" but "wind-scored ridge above tree line"
- **Seasonal/time context**: Vary throughout
- **Rotate categories**: Geography, practice, vision, domestic
- **Avoid retired metaphors**: yak hair tent, butter tea, frozen butter lamp

### 3. Doctrinal Engagement (20% of grade)
- **Specific line ranges** [start-end] for every block
- **Engage actual Tibetan content**, not generic commentary
- **Advance understanding**: Each block adds something
- **Catalyze recognition**: Commentary should point beyond itself

### 4. Format & Polish (10% of grade)
- **40+ lines minimum** per file
- **8-15 commentary blocks** per file
- **2-6 lines per block** (not too long, not too short)
- **Read aloud test**: Should sound like living masters speaking
