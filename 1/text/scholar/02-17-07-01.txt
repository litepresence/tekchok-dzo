. Wheel of existence: understanding the six realms.

[3005-3019] <philology>
The term srid pa'i 'khor lo (wheel of existence) carries its technical sense as the visual representation of samsara. Instrumental particles (-kyis, -gis) indicate the causal mechanisms of rebirth. Causative constructions establish relationships between karma and result.

[3018-3028] <structure>
The passage demonstrates Longchenpa's systematic presentation. Structural markers rim pa organize the wheel: hub (three poisons), spokes (six realms), rim (twelve links of dependent origination). The dbye ba distinguishes the wheel as object of contemplation.

[3020-3034] <context>
Citations from Thal 'gyur, Rang shar, and Nyi zla kha sbyor establish doctrinal authority. The zhes so formula indicates transmission lineage. The wheel of existence represents the foundation for renunciation.
