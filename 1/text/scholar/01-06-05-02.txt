Six Yogas and Karma Purification

The posture derives from:
- Six Yogas of Naropa: Postural stability for karma purification
- Cakrasamvara: Lion posture in deity yoga
- Guhyagarbha: Integration into Nyingma system
- Dzogchen rushen: Preliminary practices for recognizing rigpa

The karma-latency connection refers to:
- Las (karma): Active deeds
- Bag chags (latencies): Subtle imprints
- 'Brel (connection): Binding mechanism
- Chod (sever): Liberation method
