[3870-3882] [CONCEPT]
Cutting the Root of Samsara

**Analysis:** Lines 3870-3882 present the first scattered instruction — cutting the extreme through placing.

**Essence

[3870-3874]
:**
The instruction reveals that:
- Great emptiness pervades (*stong pa khyab che ba*)
- Action universally plays without reference (*bya ba kun tu rol ba'i lam la ltos pa med pa*)
- No distinction made from ground (*gzhi las khyad par du 'jog pa'i dus na*)
- Proliferation basis words do not exist (*spros gzhi'i tshig tsam du yang ma grub pa*)
- Therefore, proliferating names impossible (*spros byed ming du mi srid*)

**Key Formulation

[3870-3882] [CONCEPT]
Two Crucial Instructions
**Analysis:** Lines 3870-3882 present the two instruction types:

**First: Placing-Path Extreme-Cutting

[3870-3876]
:**
- Great pervasive emptiness, without action-reference
- Not particularly placed in ground
- Proliferation basis mere word unestablished
- Proliferator name impossible
- Inexpressible dharmatā, no letter
- Reasoning: causeless action
- Expelling samsara
- Past recitation

**Second: Dharmatā Liberation

[3874-3876]
:**
*brjod du med pa'i chos nyid la yi ge med pa zhes bya ste* — "Inexpressible dharmatā is called 'without letters'"

**Soteriological Mechanism

[3875-3876]
:**

| Aspect | Function | Result |
|--------|----------|--------|
| *bya ba rgyu med pa'i gtan tshigs* | Action-without-cause reasoning | *'khor ba dong sprugs pa* — Expelling samsara |
| *'das pa'i khong 'don pa* | Expressing past (understanding) | Liberation achieved |

**Second Instruction Preview

[3877-3882]
:**
*chos nyid gnas pa'i cha nas sems la 'gyur ba med la* — "From dharmatā abiding aspect, no change in mind"

When three kāyas appear on the path:
- Nothing unliberated (*ma grol ba gang yang med*)
- At result striking crucial point (*'bras bu gnad la phebs pa'i dus na*)
- Dharmatā meets mother (*chos nyid ma dang phrad par 'dod pa*)

**Philosophical Significance:**

These instructions establish the Atiyoga path as:
1. **Non-progressive** — no stages to traverse (*lam du byed pa* is metaphorical)
2. **Immediate** — recognition (*ngos 'dzin*) rather than transformation
3. **Non-dual** — ground-path-result are not different substances
4. **Self-liberating** — delusions liberate themselves upon recognition
