[1589-1598] <concept>
Pacifying Activity Detailed
**Analysis:** "Particular explain in four, first pacifying" (*bye brag tu bshad pa la bzhi las / dang po zhi ba*). From *de las*:
- "White dharmata pacifying" (*dkar po chos nyid zhi ba*)
- "Affliction defilement where exists" (*nyon mongs dri ma ga la yod*)
- "Stain-free mandala, example crystal pure like, defilement fault by not-stained" (*gos pa med pa'i dkyil 'khor ni / dper na shel gong dag pa bzhin / dri ma'i skyon gyis ma gos pa*)
- "Likewise pacifying dharmata in, afflictions stain not" (*de bzhin zhi ba'i chos nyid la / nyon mongs gos pa med pa*)

[1600-1609] <context>
Enriching Activity Citation
**Analysis:** From *yang de las*, the enriching (*rgyas pa*) activity:
- "Yellow enriching dharmata-from, excellence quality all arising" (*ser po rgyas pa'i chos nyid las / yon tan thams cad 'byung ba*)
- "Example jewel precious, need desire all fulfill like" (*dper na nor bu rin po che / dgos 'dod thams cad bskang ba ltar*)
- "Dharmata enriching great by, excellence quality various dawn by, beings all liberate do" (*chos nyid rgyas pa chen po des / yon tan sna tshogs shar ba yis / sems can thams cad grol bar byed*)
- "Excellence quality enriching great regarded" (*yon tan rgyas pa chen por 'dod*)

[1610-1614] <concept>
Power Activity Introduction
**Analysis:** "Third power" (*gsum pa dbang*). From *yang de las*: "Red power dharmata that, not-sought ground complete is" (*dmar po dbang gi chos nyid de / ma btsal gzhi la grub pa yin*). "Power itself dharmakaya essence" (*dbang nyid chos sku'i ngo bo*).
