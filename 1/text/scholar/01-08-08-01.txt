[11334] <concept>
**Analysis:** The seven views on the basis represent seven *limited* (*tha dad*) perspectives on the single basis (*gzhi gcig*), all of which are ultimately partial. Each view captures one aspect of the basis while missing others: the first view (primordial purity) captures emptiness but may miss spontaneous presence; the second (spontaneous presence) captures luminosity but may miss primordial purity; and so forth. The complete realization requires transcending all seven partial views to recognize the *gzhi mchog* (supreme basis) beyond conceptual extremes. This marker initiates the analytical (*dpyad pa*) phase of the chapter wherein each view is examined and its limitations demonstrated.
