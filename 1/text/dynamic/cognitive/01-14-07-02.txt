Second light-vision stage. Gnyis pa—second—ordinal progression. 'Od kyi zer thag tu gnas pa—abiding as continuum of light-rays. Sequential development: first stage (snambu—continuum), second stage (zer thag—ray-continuum). Technical distinction: snambu is diffuse light field, zer thag is structured ray-emanation. Structural function: marks intensification and organization of visionary appearance. Preserve the progressive terminology without implying temporal stages are ultimately real.
