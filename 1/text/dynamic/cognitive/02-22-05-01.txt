Ringsel (relics) - colors, sizes, locations, causes. Colors: blue-black, yellow, red, green, white luster, dark-blue, red-green-black. Size: big-small—equal to mustard seed, white mustard, others between poppy seed and small mustard seed, oily, round, gathered. Location: head, rib joint, liver top, kidney, lung top. Causes: two from peaceful relics—pure bone, heat, blood, elements, awareness gathered arise in body. Wrathful relics: skull luster head, pure blood, joint, foot, from pure five elements. Five relics very round shape, oily, gathered. Structural note: Dzogchen tantric physiology of relics (rdul phran/ringsel) as manifestation of spiritual accomplishment.
