Third stage: bindu-circle vision. Gsum pa—third—thig le'i mu khyud—circle of bindu—dawning. Snying nang gi thig le—inner heart bindu—kha dog lnga—five colors—gsal ba—clear—tshon sna lnga'i dru bur—like five-colored pea. Che chung—size—nya'i mig—like fish eye. 'Od kyi tshad khra tshang—light measure complete—nyal ba'i mig—sleeping eye. Gsal ba'i tshad—clarity measure—mkha' 'gro ma yid 'gyu ba'i mig—ḍākiṇī moving mind eye. Ye shes kyi rlung—wisdom wind—bskyod—moved—rtsa ro rkyang—channel tips extended—kun 'dar ma—vajra tent—shel smug—gray crystal—'gyu zhig gnas—abide trembling. Goms pa las byung ba'i thig le—bindu arisen from familiarity—ye shes dngos kyi snang ba—actual wisdom appearance—ye shes kyi phyogs tsam dag pa'i snang ba—partially pure wisdom appearance—'char—dawning. Mtshan nyid 'dzin pa'i ye shes—mark-grasping wisdom—phyogs gcig dag pa—one side purified—dbang po'i spyod yul—sense object sphere. Sequential progression: 'od kyi snam bu—light continuum—dum bu—drops—gru chad—interrupted—ban bun—bubbles—lang long—diffuse—all appearing.
