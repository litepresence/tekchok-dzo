Five Buddha lineages and mandala in this Jambu continent. Holding lineage of five Buddhas, five crystal stupa thousand-spans, nature of five wisdoms, lineage of five sky-expanses—expanding to pervade sky without appearance, individually clear colors unmixed. Three aspects: essence (pure ground without ceasing), nature (five great elements), compassion (demonstrated form-body). From Vajra-space mandala, spontaneous complete body, water-moon body possessing five wisdoms, self-nature of unclear-clear. Five crystal stupa descends to Vajra-Holder's left, three aspects of tone enters corner in northeast direction. Nighttime direction sees daytime. Wisdom placed near right corner becomes seeing single Buddha. Structural note: tantric mandala and Buddha family system applied to Dzogchen Thögal.
