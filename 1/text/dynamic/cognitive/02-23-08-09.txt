Liberation through primordial purity. Interval between liberated but not yet dissolved—emanations displayed. Day of emanation equivalent to one day of meditative concentration. Five instants correlated with each day—manner of liberation through appearances. Various liberations: into Expanse of Five Lights, Five Bodies, through Five Spheres, Expanse of Mirror, Expanse of Periphery, through upper and lower spheres, into empty periphery—modes for medium faculties. Definitive realization for medium: in seven days attained. Five days in appearances of spheres, one day in Dharma-Expanse, one day in Mirror-Like Jnana. From heart center, single Body emerges—Bodies without number emanate. Awareness becomes empowered like shooting star. Emanation able to accomplish benefit on twenty-sixth day. Structural note: Dzogchen bardo liberation timelines for different faculties.
