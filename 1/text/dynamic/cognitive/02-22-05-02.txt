Grounds (sa) enumeration - Buddhist path stages. Inferior grounds: seeing, reducing, realizing accomplishment—Śrāvaka grounds, Pratyekabuddha grounds. Stream-enterer, once-returner, non-returner, Arhat—four grounds. Bodhisattva grounds: Perfect Joy, Stainless, Luminous, Radiant, Difficult to Cultivate, Gone Afar, Immovable, Excellent Wisdom, Dharma Cloud—ten causal grounds. Universal Light, resultant ground—eleven. After death, in six days trembling south indicates Bodhisattva ground; in nine days trembling from above with sound indicates Vidyādhara ground. Structural note: standard Mahāyāna ground system for comparison with Dzogchen.
