[1693-1709]
Ethics aggregate for purifying stains. Liberation aggregate beyond all conceptualization. Sense-bases: what arises and expands from what. Six objects like form for eye, sound for ear, etc. Arising (*skye*) and expanding (*mched*) as mutual dependent origination. Twelve sense-bases: six internal apprehenders, six external objects. Not creator-creation but simultaneous conditionality. Preserve Abhidharma framework.

[1710-1731]
Elements as basis: five great elements as ground of appearance. Earth-solid, water-moist, fire-hot, wind-moving, space-accommodating. Not material substances but qualities of experience. Eighteen dhatus: six sense-bases plus six objects plus six consciousnesses. Complete phenomenological analysis. Preserve enumeration structure without losing forest for trees.
