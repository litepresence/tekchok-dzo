Thögal practical technique - thumb-finger pressing. Sitting cross-legged, pressing thumb and finger on red-clear light-appearance, taking as experience. Sessions short, many in number. Release effort, look slowly—seeing appearance by pressing again called dharmatā directly manifest appearance. Then five-light progression: first like smoke, dull illusion, hovering clouds, fire sparks, sun and moon, star forms, intermediate-space rainbow, white clear light. Structural note: specific Thögal visualization technique for generating visionary appearances. Register: practical meditation instruction terminology.
