Bardo experience categories and clairvoyances. Second and third have twenty-four each. Remaining three have six each—divided into 134 portions, gathered into six groups of six. Thirty-six's fifth: six faculties with outflows. First group's six faculties seeing beyond—thirty without outflows. Last, utterly without outflows. Six clairvoyances: knowledge of previous realms, death and transmigration, knowledge of birth, knowledge of others' minds, knowledge of all hidden appearances, seeing and knowing six realms—clairvoyance of purified faculties. Fourth: attainment of retentive powers—previously heard Dharma all appearing simultaneously, never-before-heard Dharma also arising—called dharmata bardo. Structural note: enumeration of bardo experiences and supernormal powers in Dzogchen system.
