Buddha manifestation enumeration. Twelve enumerations of Buddhas, aeons, teachings, retinues. First: Hero holding thousand worlds, strife-possessing aeon. Second: Victory-banner of merit, light-receiving aeon. Third: Luminous self-arisen Brahma, ancient aeon. Fourth: Sun-appearance thousand, sun-rising aeon. Fifth: Net-holder Buddha, conch aeon. Sixth: Flower garland holder, excellent aeon. Seventh: Sun-rays thousand encircling protector, light-victorious aeon. Eighth: Red-sun, sage earth-pressing aeon. Ninth: Self-arisen crystal moon, conjunction aeon. Tenth: Jewel moon, enjoying aeon. Eleventh: Supreme guide horse-vehicle king, moon-world aeon. Twelfth: Jewel-appearance holder, renowned aeon. Each with specific retinue liberation manner. Technical cosmological enumeration requiring precise rendering of temporal vastness without literalizing.
