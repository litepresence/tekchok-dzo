Difficulties of obtaining leisure and training. Three aspects: contemplating difficulty of obtaining, train in Dharma without leisure intermission; contemplating cause of certain decay, produce definite emergence and weariness; contemplating simile of certain death, meditate on profound emptiness when time comes. Difficult to be born: born in Dharma place from karma-ground of Jambu continent. Difficult to obtain: obtaining leisure with endowments. Difficult to meet: meeting virtuous friend. Difficult to enter: entering door of holy Dharma. Difficult to know: knowing unsurpassed instruction exactly.
