Liberation criteria - momentary recognition vs meditation. Momentary recognition divided in half—meditation days comprise abiding portion. Classification of aspects of momentary recognition pervaded by recognition of own face, yet meditation days not pervaded by realization—even without realization, one may abide in natural contemplation, like end of spontaneous presence. Momentary recognition not pervaded by meditation—for in moment of realization, one liberated into primordial purity. Limit connected with meditation from peak supreme to final. Meditation days not pervaded by momentary recognition like final delusion—limit unconnected like middle. Therefore dividing momentary recognition and meditation days is most crucial. Structural note: key Dzogchen distinction for understanding bardo liberation.
