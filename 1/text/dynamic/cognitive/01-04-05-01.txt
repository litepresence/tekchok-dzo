False conventional establishment. All phenomena including container world and contained beings arranged by intellect are log pa'i kun rdzob—erroneous conventional. Appearance manifests as deluded object's essence, appearing as intellect's falseness. These imputed appearances function as deluded intellect's accordant objects with causal power, yet when analyzed are empty by own essence. Structure establishes problematic status of ordinary perception: functional yet fundamentally mistaken. Preserve tension between don mthun par snang ba—appearance accordant with purpose—and ultimate emptiness.

Eight examples of illusion citation. Dream, illusion, gandharva city: arising as non-existent yet clear-appearing. Analogical structure demonstrates dependent arising without inherent existence. Sngon ma las indicates these examples were established in previous discussion. Verse form signals authoritative transmission, not mere philosophical argument. Preserve eight examples as complete set: dream, illusion, gandharva city, mirage, echo, reflection, magical creation, and water-moon. Each illustrates specific aspect of emptiness-appearance identity.

[2862-2869]
True conventional definition. Yang dag kun rdzob—authentic conventional—appears deluded yet serves as cause for blissful migration and liberation. White virtuous actions of ten virtues, merit collections of generosity, path of truth through four aspects of seeing and meditating. Uttaratantra citation anchors this in tathāgatagarbha literature: all paths of truth are compounded, all compounds are merely conventional. Distinction between log pa'i kun rdzob and yang dag kun rdzob is crucial—false vs true conventional, not merely conventional vs ultimate.

[2870-2872]
Ultimate truth exposition. Don dam bden pa: emptiness essence abiding as inherent nature of dharmatā, together with subsequent measuring wisdom, peaceful aspect free from elaboration. Two-fold division: rnam grangs pa—nominal ultimate expressed through words and signs, and rnam grangs pa ma yin pa—non-nominal ultimate of true authentic nature. First serves as gateway; second is actual realization. Preserve structural distinction without privileging either as "more ultimate"—both are aspects of don dam, differing only in relation to linguistic expression.
