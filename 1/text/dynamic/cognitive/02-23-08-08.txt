Fourth: realizing as self-appearance through the power of truth. Structural marker concluding fourfold bardo sequence. View register: culmination—self-manifestation (rang snang) accomplished through truth's inherent power. Translation: preserve bzhi pa, rang snang du rtogs, bden pa'i stobs key terms. Note: "power of truth" indicates liberation not through effort but through reality's own force—this is lhun grub dimension. Translation risk: treating stobs (power) as personal capacity misses the spontaneous accomplishment aspect. Preservation: the fourth moment represents full realization, not merely recognizing but embodying self-appearance such that truth accomplishes liberation.
