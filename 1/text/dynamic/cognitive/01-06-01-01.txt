[6801-6871]
Student qualification framework. Six-family seed emptying, six affliction purification, six pristine cognition arising. Root and branch guru distinction: root with four marks and empowerment basis, branch with two or three qualifications. Guru to be avoided: ignorance, pride, foolishness, external attachment, inattention to Secret Mantra, critical arrogance, wrong paths, no empowerment, casual samaya, unable to answer, little learning. Ten student dharmas: devotion, faith, desire for definitive meaning, generosity, diligence, few attachments, good nature, wisdom, samaya, harmony. Guru devotion as Buddha or surpassing. Vessel of Great Perfection as qualified student.

[6872-7098]
Student to be abandoned. No honor, careless Secret Mantra conduct, no lineage, small wisdom, disregarding kindness, speaking of own lineage, bodily ornamentation. Guru-student relationship as core of tantric practice. Risk of guru abuse in modern contexts; preserve traditional framework without anachronism. Scriptural authority from Self-Arisen tantra.
