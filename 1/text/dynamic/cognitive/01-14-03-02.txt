Wisdom nature exposition—second section. Opens with negation: yeshes is not conceptual thought. Threefold classification: nges tshig (definitive meaning), ro snang (appearance of emptiness), and sems tsam (Cittamātra). Definitive meaning refers to direct non-conceptual realization. "Knowing by knowing itself" indicates self-aware knowing without mediation. Three divisions correspond to Madhyamaka, Yogācāra, and epistemological approaches. Register maintained at highest philosophical level. Preserve technical terms: nges tshig, ro snang, sems tsam.
