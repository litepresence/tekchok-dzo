Fruition citation from Mu tig phreng ba. Grol sa nyid ni thog ma'o—liberation place itself is the primordial. Establishes non-progressive view: fruition is recognition of primordial state, not achievement of new condition. Structural function: scriptural warrant for exhaustion doctrine (zad pa). Preserve the paradox: thog ma—beginning—as grol sa—liberation place—collapses temporal sequence.

[15638-15644]
Exhaustion point definition. Ka dag primordial purity recognized through rig pa rang ngo shes—self-recognition of awareness—rgyur mi ldog—non-returning as cause—'khrul gzhi zad pa—exhaustion of delusion basis. Serves as 'bras bu—fruit—through name transformation. Dri ma dang bral bas—through separation from stains—differentiation from ground exposition. Dbyings su grol—liberation into expanse—as threefold: dbyings su grol tshul—manner of liberation, dbyings su gnas pa'i rang bzhin—nature of abiding, dbyings su chos nyid kyi dgongs klong—dharmatā intention-vastness coiling.

[15645-15654]
Universal liberation equality. Dbang po rno rtul 'bring gsum—sharp, dull, medium faculties—grol ba'i tshe 'bras bu la khyad par med—all equally without fruition distinction. Dus gsum gyi sangs rgyas thams cad—all Buddhas of three times—brjod bral chos sku—inexpressible Dharmakāya—ngo mnyam tha dad med—equal nature without difference—thig le nyag gcig—single bindu—sangs rgyas—awakened. Tshe 'dir—this life, bar dor—in bardo, sprul pa'i zhing—in emanation field—all equally valid. Lam snang gi sku dang ye shes—path-appearance body and wisdom—phyir gsal—outer clarity—dbyings su nub pa—dissolves into expanse—shel 'od—crystal light—nang ba stong par grol—liberation into empty appearance.

[15655-15677]
Space analogy systematic. Dbyings las rig pa gud du bcad—awareness separately cut from expanse—bum nang gi nam mkha'—space inside vase—de kha'i bu ga—vase opening—nam mkha' che ba—great space—'brel ba—connection. Sgron ma bzhi—Four Lamps—thog ma'i ka dag—primordial purity—mtshams sbyar—connected. Bum pa chag dus—when vase breaks—phyi nang bar gsum—outer, inner, middle—dbye med—without distinction. Rig pa lnga—five wisdoms—phyi med—without outer—nang dbyings ka dag chen po—inner expanse great primordial purity. Skal ldan—fortunate ones—rig pa rang sa zin—awareness takes own place—rgyal ba 'od mi 'gyur ba'i sangs rgyas—Buddha of unchanging light—kun tu bzang po ye shes lhun grub—Samantabhadra spontaneously accomplished wisdom. Analogies: nyi ma nub pas zer rang la 'dus—sun setting rays self-gathered, chu la chu thim—water into water, mar la mar thim—butter into butter, nam mkha' nam mkha' thim—sky into sky.

Final fruition state. Ka dag gi nang dbyings su thim pa—exhausted into inner expanse of primordial purity. Rang shar las citation: rtogs pa'i skyes bu—realized being—mi 'gyur mnyam nyid ngang du thim—dissolved into unchanging equality state—rtog med shes rab rang shar—thoughtless wisdom self-arisen. Sa chen—great ground—ma bkod nor bu'i phung po—unarranged jewel heap—sna tshogs 'byung zhing ngang du thim—variously arising and naturally dissolving. 'Od mi 'gyur ba—unchanging light—grub pa'i mtha' 'jig—destruction of establishment extreme—bla ma'i man ngag nub—guru instruction dissolved—lta sgom spyod pa'i mtha' stongs—emptiness of view-meditation-conduct extremes—chos su snang ba med—no appearance as dharma—sku dang ye shes rgyun chad—continuum of body and wisdom ceased—sangs rgyas med cing sems can med—no Buddhas, no beings—gang yang gnas pa med—nothing abiding. Rang shar las: snying po 'di la gnas pa'i skyes bu—being abiding in this essence—kun tu bzang po dang mnyam—equal to Samantabhadra.

[15711-15734]
Beyond existence-nonexistence. Ka dag rang ngo la yod med kyi mtha' dang bral ba—free from existence-nonexistence extremes in primordial purity's own face. Sku dang ye shes—body and wisdom—yod med las 'das—beyond existence and nonexistence. 'Char gzhi ma 'gags pa tsam—mere non-ceasing arising-basis—shel gong—crystal shell—'od dang kha dog gang du'ang ma grub—light and color unestablished anywhere. Thal 'gyur las citation: 'bras bu brjod par mi nus—fruit inexpressible—chos rnams zad sar phyin pa—phenomena arrived at exhaustion point. Klong drug pa: ye shes med cing sku yang med—no wisdom, no body—'od med kha dog rnams dang bral—free from light and color—gang gi ngo bo grub med pas—essence unestablished—ma rig pa dang nyon mongs kyang—ignorance and afflictions also—smos ci dgos te yod ma yin—needless to say, do not exist. Structural function: radical negation as completion, not nihilism.
