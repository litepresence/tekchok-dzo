Peaceful and wrathful kayas. To nirvana possessing method—contaminated remainder, aggregates trace burned and purified—two kayas: Peaceful Kaya and Wrathful Kaya. Wrathful cannot empower emanation without peaceful. Continuum: two from essence-cause—all beings primordially abiding as clear-light kaya; from existing relics, creation-completion shows manifest cause arising from gathered pure elements. Latency not-yet-ripened nature; manifest ripened appearing as relics. Place: greatly arising from head or spine—clear-light-familiar arising-kaya from those two. Structural note: Dzogchen system of peaceful/wrathful kaya manifestation.
