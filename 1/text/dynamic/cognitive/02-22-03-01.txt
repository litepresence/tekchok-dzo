Bindu essence, definition, division, characteristic, result. Essence: not different, dwelling as one, without duality. Definition: bindu not-change, vast expanse, pervading all. Three divisions: body-dwelling channel bindu, nature path bindu, complete result bindu. Body-dwelling: conventional cause bindu white and red two, ultimate wisdom bindu five—two categories. Wisdom bindu five and ground bindu good, light upper-burden dwelling unmoved in general body channel. Path bindu: moved by wisdom wind from within channel, makes four lamps arise, shows appearance of increasing experience. Nature path bindu: reality bindu without fabrication pervading all; Samantabhadra bindu appearing without good/bad/big/small; expanse bindu clear without outer/inner; pure expanse bindu arising without deviation; wisdom bindu dwelling without primordial making; great wisdom bindu appearing directly unobscured—six. Structural note: detailed bindu taxonomy in Dzogchen.
