Dzogchen practice at time of death. Obtain samadhi of non-thinking in dharmatā Bardo—certain to become Buddha. Performing benefit of others, compassion grasping self—open three doors of body, speech, mind. Breath: hold, draw in, throw—insert three syllables, produce strength of body, speech, mind. Doing again and again, appearance becomes like this. If obtaining strength of body, bestow instructions. Consciousness becomes clear, place equally in own meaning. Doors of faculties in own presence, place looking at thusness of eye. Mind of equal remaining arises, obtain samadhi possessing non-thinking. Instruction called "hitting as robe-shake"—essential point of Great Perfection. Gathering faculties, also hitting clarity by key of reversing death. Structural note: critical Dzogchen death practice instructions for achieving buddhahood in bardo.
