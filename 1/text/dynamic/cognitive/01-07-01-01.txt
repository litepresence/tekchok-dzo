Samaya definition from Supreme Samaya Tantra. Binding pledge, vajra cross difficult to traverse, certain in aim. Mind itself unbroken, free from deception guile. Two classifications: root and branch. Root three: body speech mind. Branches twenty-five: accept, not abandon, practice, accomplish. Specific classification: view, conduct, practice, common, particular body-speech-mind, branches, crucial certain samaya. Hundred-thousand-fold spoken samayas briefly taught. Basis teacher and student, vajra siblings. Teacher protection cause, student protection maker. Vajra siblings four: general, close, mixed, maṇḍala-vase-mixed. General siblings all entered teachings, close one Dharma-family, mixed one Guru, extremely mixed one empowerment one maṇḍala one lamp one vase. Sevenfold strictness objects. Teacher four: general honored, drawing to connect, samaya-empowerment bestowing, instruction-transmission giving. Teacher definition: not-knowing training to liberate through knowing, connecting to Dzogchen meaning. Student definition: training through listening undistracted, vessel for instruction. Meaning-endowed teacher and student abide equally upon right ground.
