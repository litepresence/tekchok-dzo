Five wisdoms exposition—extensive section. First: appearing wisdom manifests as five elements while remaining fundamentally empty. Second: dharmadhatu wisdom—beyond conceptual grasping, like space, cutting five sensory attachments. Third: mirror-like wisdom—knowing all appearances without grasping. Fourth: discriminating wisdom—knowing distinctions among phenomena while remaining in equality. Fifth: all-accomplishing wisdom—activity without effort. Each wisdom follows formula of "wisdom is X, arising from Y, producing Z." Longest Chapter 14 section at 693 lines—core tantric curriculum. Register: highest tantric technical precision with standard five-wisdom taxonomy.
