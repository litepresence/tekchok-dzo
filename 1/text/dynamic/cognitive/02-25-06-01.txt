Emanation bodies. First: hunters and butchers, etc.—emanations benefiting sentient beings. Second: whatever tames whom—six Shakya emanations. Third: Akanistha, Sudarshana, Glorious-Endowed, Lotus-Stack, Karma Perfect in five aspects—appearing as five families' pure budddas. Considering disciples' power—Emanation Body, Dharmakaya nature, pure field, Teacher of five families. In Jambudvipa, Glorious Great Vajradhara—arising as body of four births, completing thirty-six deeds. Pure field: three-thousand three-hundred-million worlds, possessing one great Brahma aeon. Structural note: Dzogchen understanding of emanation bodies manifesting for benefit of beings.
