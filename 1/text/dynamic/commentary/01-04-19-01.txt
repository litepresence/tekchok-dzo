Three times of purity—not that error is destroyed, but that it was never there.
Previously, not experienced. Now, not dwelling. Finally, impossible. This is not

They all received the vase, the bell, the empowerment. But only three recognized. The rest got souvenirs. Lineage is not democratic. It doesn't care about numbers. It cares about recognition.

The Mind Section (Sems sde), Space Section (Klong sde), and Instruction Section (Man ngag sde) are not three different vehicles. They are three different doorways into the same room. Some enter through analytical mind, some through vast spaciousness, some through direct pointing. The room is the same. The recognition is the same.

[178-178]
Patrul Rinpoche's practical advice: Don't get lost in the classifications. Nine spaces, twelve thousand chapters, thirty-five sections— these numbers are meant to show the vastness of the teaching, not to create a curriculum to complete. You don't need to read every text. You need to recognize your nature. One verse that leads to recognition is worth more than twelve thousand chapters that lead to scholarship.

Mountain hermit's confession: I spent twenty years collecting texts. I had the Mind Section complete, most of the Space Section, many Instruction Section teachings. My cave was full of books. Then one winter, a fire burned them all. I wept for three days. Then I laughed. The fire taught me what the books couldn't—knowledge burns, recognition doesn't.

Singing yogi's melody: The lineages are like rivers flowing to the same ocean. The ear lineage is a swift mountain stream—direct, clear, cutting through rock. The explanation lineage is a broad river—meandering, nourishing many fields. The secret cycle is underground water—hidden, pure, emerging where least expected. The unsurpassable secret is the ocean itself—no distinction of streams, all water returning to source.

ESSENCE OF ALL LINEAGES:

Whether you receive ear lineage from a living master, study explanation lineage from texts, enter secret cycle through ripening, or rest in unsurpassable secret beyond all methods— the essence is recognition. Without recognition, ear lineage becomes whispered gossip. Explanation lineage becomes academic study. Secret cycle becomes mystical experience. Unsurpassable secret becomes nihilistic void.

With recognition, all become the path. The whisper points directly. The study illuminates nature. The experience liberates spontaneously. The void is luminous clarity.

This is Longchenpa's gift—showing that dzogchen is complete, vast, profound, yet immediately accessible. The nine spaces contain all possible teachings. The three sections present them according to capacity. The four cycles accommodate different ripeness. But the recognition is one.

Lion-faced dakini's fierce blessing: Cut through lineage pride! "My lineage is older." "My lineage is purer." "My lineage is shorter, more direct." All nonsense! Lineage is not a pedigree. Recognition is not inherited. Stop comparing lineages and start recognizing!

Final metaphor: Lineage is like a dream you tell upon waking. The dream was vivid, detailed, real-seeming. Upon waking, you know it was mind's display. Telling the dream to another who also woke—they understand without needing the details. Lineage transmits between the woke. The dream details don't matter.

This completes the commentary on lineages and cycles. May all who encounter these words find the lineage that suits their capacity. May all enter through the appropriate gate. May all arrive at the same recognition.
