[Final] Voice 10 (Longchenpa): Five aggregates: form, feeling, perception, formations, consciousness. The prison and the key. Analyze them to understand samsara. Recognize them to achieve liberation. Form appears—recognize it as display. Feeling arises—see it as feeling. Perception grasps—watch it liberate. Formations create—know their emptiness. Consciousness knows—rest in that knowing. Five aggregates, five opportunities for recognition. The prison becomes the palace. The chains become the decorations. The aggregates are not enemies. They are the path. Form is the body, the world, all physical phenomena. Feeling is pleasant, unpleasant, neutral—the three gates. Perception is the grasping, the labeling, the "I like" and "I don't like." Formations are the karmic seeds, the volitional imprints, the choices that create future. Consciousness is the knowing, the aware, the base of everything. Five waves of one ocean. Five fingers of one hand. Don't separate them—see them as one experience, recognized. When form appears, it's an opportunity. When feeling arises, it's an opportunity. When perception grasps, it's an opportunity. When formations create, it's an opportunity. When consciousness knows, it's an opportunity. Five opportunities in every moment. The aggregates are not obstacles. They are the path itself, seen clearly.

Emaho! The five aggregates! The five opportunities! The five recognitions!

What is called delusion is wisdom's play. The treasury is complete.
