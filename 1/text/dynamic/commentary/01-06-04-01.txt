COMPREHENSIVE EXPANSION - DEEPENING THE FIVE AGGREGATES TEACHING:

The Weaver's Five Threads:
Five colors, five textures, five thicknesses—all woven into one fabric. The five
aggregates are like this. Form, feeling, perception, formation, consciousness—
apparently separate, actually one display. The form thread appears solid. The
feeling thread appears emotional. The perception thread appears cognitive. But
all are threads of awareness woven into the fabric of experience.

This old weaver spent years trying to separate the threads, analyze them,
understand each one. Finally she saw: separating threads destroys the fabric.
Analyzing aggregates misses the aggregate-less nature. The fabric is whole. The
awareness is whole. Rest in the wholeness.

[435-534]
The Hot Spring's Five Minerals:
Iron, sulfur, calcium, magnesium, lithium—all dissolved in the water, depositing
as travertine in different colors. The five aggregates are like these minerals—
different aspects, same source, same nature. Form aggregate is iron—solid,
structural. Feeling is sulfur—reactive, changing. Perception is calcium—
building, creating. Formation is magnesium—energizing, activating.
Consciousness is lithium—illuminating, knowing.

But the water doesn't discriminate. It holds all five. It deposits all five. The
nature of the water is not changed by what it carries. So too with awareness.
It holds all five aggregates. It is not changed by what it experiences.

[435-534]

mantra, five sections of one wall. The five aggregates are like this. Each
section complete in itself. Each stone complete in itself. But together they
form the wall of liberation. The form section is Om—the foundation. The feeling
section is Ma—the movement. The perception section is Ni—the recognition.
The formation section is Pad—the transformation. The consciousness section is
Me—the completion.
