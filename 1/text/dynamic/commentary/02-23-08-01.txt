First: signs possessed, recognition. Not looking for external omens, but
recognizing the signs that have always been present. Like knowing spring
has arrived by the quality of light—not by decree, but by direct knowing.

This wanderer once sought signs everywhere. In dreams, in visions, in the
words of others. Then he recognized: the sign was the seeking itself, the
nature of the seeker. Already possessed, already recognized.
