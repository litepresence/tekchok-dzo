The Hot Spring's Natural Flow:
Water bubbles up from deep earth, carries minerals to the surface, deposits them

The Mani Wall's Silent Prayer:

wall, yet without each stone there is no wall. So too with practice. Each moment
of recognition is a stone. The wall of liberation is built slowly, naturally,
without forcing. The wind of awareness turns each stone. The prayer is continuous.

The Cave's Dark Clarity:

all is dark. The eyes adjust slowly. Forms emerge from darkness. Clarity dawns
without light. This is the clarity of awareness, not dependent on external
conditions. The cave is your heart. The darkness is non-recognition. The emerging
forms are the five lights. The clarity is rigpa itself.

All happens naturally, spontaneously, without artificial effort.

The Migration's Seasonal Wisdom:
Birds migrate with the seasons, following inner wisdom they do not understand.

The Animal Tracks' Silent Teaching:
In fresh snow, tracks appear—deer, rabbit, fox, bird. Each track tells a story

the symbols to find the teaching, but do not stop at the symbols.

The Root System's Hidden Connection:
Above ground, trees appear separate, distinct, individual. Below ground, their
roots intertwine, share nutrients, communicate silently. What appears separate
is deeply connected. So too with beings. What appears as individual minds are
branches of the same root system of awareness. The separation is appearance.
The connection is nature.

The Dzo's Patient Labor:
