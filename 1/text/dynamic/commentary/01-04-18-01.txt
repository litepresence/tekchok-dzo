First: vital-point wind in channels, mind focused through instruction. From dharmakaya's source, activity of averting harm. Essential point: severing debate between buddhas and sentient beings.

Wind vital-point descends into body—severing connection between body and mind. Root wind exhausted—arising's self-liberation. Four key points that cut through the root of samsara.

A simple practitioner... once tried to force his winds through channels like water through a blocked pipe. He strained, he pushed, he visualized intensely. Finally exhausted, he let go. The wind moved naturally, the mind settled spontaneously. The forcing was the only obstacle.
