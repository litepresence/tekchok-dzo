Definitive terminology: "kaya" because body is complete. In the Buddha-ground, though there is neither body nor entity, names alone do not contradict. From the Ratnagotravibhaga (Dkon mchog sgron me):

"The dharmakaya is the body of the Victors. By seeing, purifying views, one will know."

Just as it is said. Here's the key point: Dharmakaya isn't a physical body, yet it's called "body." Sambhogakaya has no marks, yet it appears with marks.
