[14753] <ornament> Fifth: liberation through primordial purity.
[14754] <prose> In the interval between having been liberated but not yet dissolved, emanations are displayed.
[14755] <break> <prose> The day of emanation is equivalent to one day of meditative concentration.
[14756] <break> <prose> Correlating the five instants with each day of meditative concentration, the manner of liberation through appearances is taught.
[14757] <prose><tantra> From the Self-Arisen:
[14758] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Five Lights;
[14759] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Five Bodies;
[14760] <verse> Some are liberated through the Five Spheres;
[14761] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Mirror;
[14762] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Periphery; || 10 ||
[14763] <verse> Some are liberated through the upper and lower spheres;
[14764] <verse> Some are liberated into the empty periphery.
[14765] <prose> These are the modes of liberation for those of medium faculties.
[14766] <prose> Thus it is said.
[14767] <prose> Moreover, it is thus:
[14768] <prose> Establishing the definitive realization for those of medium faculties:
[14769] <prose> In seven days, definitive realization is attained.
[14770] <prose> Furthermore, one abides five days in the appearances of the spheres,
[14771] <prose> One day in the Dharma-Expanse,
[14772] <prose> One day in the Mirror-Like Jñana. || 20 ||
[14773] <prose> Moreover, from one's own heart-center, a single Body emerges,
[14774] <prose><tantra> From which Bodies without number emanate.
[14775] <prose> Through awakening to this, one's awareness becomes empowered, like a shooting star.
[14776] <prose> Even emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the twenty-sixth day.
[14777] <prose> The peak of medium faculties:
[14778] <prose> Requires eight days,

[14780] <prose> Abiding three days in the appearances of the Four Jñanas,
[14781] <prose> Moreover, seeing above oneself various Jñana-Bodies emanating,
[14782] <prose> Through attaining certainty regarding these, || 30 ||

[14796] <prose> Abiding one day in the appearances of spontaneous presence,
[14797] <prose> Then, recognizing all those Bodies as non-dual, one is awakened.
