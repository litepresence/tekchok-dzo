<classification> Concluding verses establish causal condition pervading all (རྒྱུ་རྐྱེན་ཁྱབ་འཇུག). Thirteen fields mapped to: (1) general cause, (2) Vinaya, (3) Karma, (4) Abhidharma, (5) Prajnaparamita, (6) mantra, (7) dependent arising—each teaching vehicle corresponds to field-type. From Dzogchen view: these are not external places but recognition-states (ཤེས་པའི་གནས་སྤྱོད་) where appearance and emptiness are undifferentiated. Declarative assertion: all thirteen fields are none other than the nature of rigpa—self-appearing luminosity (རང་སྣང་འོད་གསལ་) manifesting as diverse recognition-fields.
