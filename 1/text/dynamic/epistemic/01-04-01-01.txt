[1920-1943]
<view>dzogchen-rigpa
<pedagogy>polemical-distinction
<risk>nihilism
<classification> Refutation of nihilist position (chad par lta ba) represented by Lokayata/Carvaka materialism denying previous/future lives, karma efficacy, and liberation. Two types: cause-nihilism denying mind continuity, result-nihilism denying action effects. Polemical distinction establishing middle way beyond both eternalism and nihilism while recognizing emptiness of all phenomena.

[1944-1967]
<view>dzogchen-rigpa
<pedagogy>polemical-distinction
<classification> Refutation of heterodox conduct: attachment to views, black mantra supremacy, divination reliance. Distinction between authentic soteriology and magical-ritual approaches seeking worldly benefits. Views as fingers pointing at moon—not to be confused with moon itself. Polemical clearing of distractions appearing spiritual but leading away from liberation.

[1968-1991]

[1992-2015]
<view>dzogchen-rigpa

<classification> Conclusion of non-Buddhist refutation summarizing three fundamental errors: eternalist self-grasping, nihilist denial of cause and result, incomplete realization of middle way.
