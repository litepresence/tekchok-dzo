[1] [669] <prose> The first aspect:

[2] [670] ངོ་བོ་གཅིག་ལ་ལྡོག་པ་ཐ་དད་ཀྱི་ཚུལ་དུ་སྐུ་གསུམ་དབྱིངས་ལ་བཞུགས་ཏེ།
[2] [670] <prose> Though essence is one, the Three Kayas abide in the expanse through different aspects.

[3] [671] རང་ཤར་ལས།
[3] [671] <tantra> From Self-Arisen:

[4] [672] སྐུ་གསུམ་ཡེ་ཤེས་སྣང་བ་ནི།
[4] [672] <verse> Three Kayas, wisdom-appearance,

[5] [673] གང་ལ་ཐོགས་པ་མེད་པ་དང༌།
[5] [673] <prose> Without obstruction to anything;

[6] [674] དམིགས་པའི་ཡུལ་ནི་གཅིག་པ་ཡི།
[6] [674] <prose> Perceived objects being one,

[7] [675] །ངོ་བོ་སོ་སོར་སྣང་བའོ།
[7] [675] <prose> Essence appearing individually.

[8] [676] །ཞེས་སོ།
[8] [676] <tantra> Thus it is spoken.

[9] [677] <prose> This is the manner of awareness-itself's appearance.

[11] [679] <verse> Thought-free awareness, Dharmakāya's essence;

[13] [681] <prose> Whatever-appearing awareness, Nirmāṇakāya.
