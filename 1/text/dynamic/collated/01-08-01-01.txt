[13] [10484] <list> The fifth: the view that holds the Basis as transformable into anything whatsoever,

[14] [10485] <list> The sixth: the view that holds the Basis as accepting anything whatsoever that arises,

[15] [10486] <list> The seventh: the view that holds the Basis as various, manifold, and multiplicity itself—these seven views complete.

[16] [10487] <prose> Therefore, these seven are seen through perceiving one limited aspect or another of the single primordial purity, each perceiver accepting their own recognition as complete.

[22] [10493] །རིག་པའི་ངོ་བོ་འཕོ་འགྱུར་མེད་པར་འདོད་པ་ངེས་པ་ཅན་ནོ།

[25] [10496] <list> One holds multiplicity because one holds that the nature of awareness appears without ceasing in its manner of appearance, being naturally variegated and multi-colored.
