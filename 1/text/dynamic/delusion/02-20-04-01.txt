[8970-9001] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [NON-ATTAINMENT NIHILISM]
**Misreading:**
"Nothing to attain" means there is no enlightenment or liberation.

**Why it arises:**
"Nothing" interpreted nihilistically.

**Primary consequence:**
Rejection of path and fruit.

**Secondary consequences:**
Licentiousness; spiritual apathy.

**Cascade effects:**
<nihilistic-error>.
