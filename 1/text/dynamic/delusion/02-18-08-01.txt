[4916-4919] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [THOUGHTS AS ENEMY]
**Misreading:**
Thoughts must be eliminated, stopped, or are obstacles to realization.

**Why it arises:**
"Cutting through thoughts" interpreted as anti-cognitive.

**Primary consequence:**
Thought suppression or blank mind pursuit.

**Secondary consequences:**
Fear of thinking; dullness mistaken for meditation.

**Cascade effects:**
<practice-error>.
