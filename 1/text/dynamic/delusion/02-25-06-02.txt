The five perfections (*phun sum tshogs pa lnga*)—teacher, place, retinue, teaching, time—interpreted as checklist to complete for spiritual accomplishment.

**Why it arises:**
"Five" suggests set. "Perfections" implies ideal conditions. Achievement mentality.

**Secondary consequences:**

- Failing to recognize that perfections are naturally present
- Waiting for right conditions
- Reifying circumstances as prerequisites

**Cascade effects:**
<five-perfections-accumulation> leads to <condition-seeking> [perfect setup] produces <waiting-mentality> [not yet ready] triggers <natural-perfection-miss> [already perfect]
