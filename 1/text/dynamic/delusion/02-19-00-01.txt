[5963-6026] <epistemic-error> [PROGRESS AS LINEAR]
**Misreading:**
Signs of progress indicate linear advancement toward goal.

**Why it arises:**
Goal-oriented thinking. Developmental models.

**Primary consequence:**
Spiritual materialism of measuring progress.

**Secondary consequences:**
Competition; comparison with others.

**Cascade effects:**
<eternalistic-error>.
