[9518-9525] <ontological-error> [DHARMAKAYA AS ENTITY]
**Misreading:**
The description of Dharmakaya teacher Samantabhadra in dharmadhatu is interpreted as establishing an actual being or person who is the Dharmakaya teacher.

**Why it arises:**
Personification reflex. "Teacher" suggests person. Mythic thinking.

**Primary consequence:**
Reifying Dharmakaya as person. The practitioner believes there is a "Samantabhadra being."

**Secondary consequences:**
Theism; praying to Samantabhadra; missing that Dharmakaya is nature, not person.

**Cascade effects:**
<eternalistic-error> through personifying Dharmakaya.
<theism-error> through reifying teacher.
