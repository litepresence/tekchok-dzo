[6420] <structure>
**Analysis:** Third enumeration (*gsum pa*): Protectors of the thousand Brahmā world-systems.

[6420] <philology>
**Key Terms:**
- *gsum pa* (third) — ordinal marker
- *tshangs pa* (Brahmā) — form-realm deity
- *stong gi* (of thousand) — genitive construction
- *ye srid mgön* (world-existence protector)

[6420] <context>
**Cosmological Framework:** Form-realm Brahmās represent profound concentration achievements that remain within saṃsāra. Establishes scope of phenomena before revealing Dzogchen transcendence.

[6420] <concept>
**Doctrinal Significance:** Demonstrates that even highest meditative attainments are limited without recognition of awareness-nature (*rig pa*). All phenomena arise from ground (*gzhi*), encompassed by Dzogchen view.
