<structure>
**Investigation:** Third tier (*bang rim gsum pa*) of the Heaven of the Thirty-Three, occupied by gods intoxicated by honey-wine (*sbrang chang*).

<philology>
**Key Terms:**
- *gsum pa* (third) — ordinal numeral
- *sbrang chang* (honey-wine) — compound: bee (*sbrang*) + alcohol (*chang*)
- *myos pa* (intoxicated) — participle indicating absorbed state

<context>
**Doctrinal Framework:** Third tier represents sensory absorption, the subtlest desire-realm pleasure. Approaches form-realm meditative states while remaining within cyclic existence.

<concept>
**Symbolic Significance:** Intoxication represents altered consciousness—more subtle than aesthetic pleasure, yet still attachment. Marks the limit of desire-realm before form-realm transition.
