[6422] <structure>
**Morphological Breakdown:** Fifth enumeration (*lnga pa*): Web-protector Buddha of the five-family maṇḍala.

[6422] <philology>
**Terminology:** *dr ba mgon* (web/net protector) + *sangs rgyas* (Buddha) — genitive construction indicating Buddha of the protection-web configuration.

[6422] <context>
**Maṇḍala Structure:** Fifth of five Tathāgata families (*de bzhin gshegs pa'i rigs lnga*): Vairocana, Akṣobhya, Ratnasaṃbhava, Amitābha, Amoghasiddhi.

[6422] <concept>
**Five Buddha Framework:** Each corresponds to direction, wisdom, defilement-transformed, and element. Completes maṇḍala structure for subsequent practice instructions.
