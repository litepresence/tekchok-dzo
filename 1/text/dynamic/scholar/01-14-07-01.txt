[19404] <structure>
First: Light Canopy Appearance
Structural marker introducing the first of four wisdom lamps.

[19404] <philology>
*dang po 'od kyi snam bur snang ba dang* — "First: appearance as a mass of light." *snam bu* (field/mass/canopy) with genitive *kyi* indicating light's field; *snang ba* (appearance) as substantive.

[19404] <context>
First of four wisdom lamps (*ye shes sgron ma bzhi*) in Chapter 14. The lamps serve as supports (*rten*) for wisdom recognition: (1) bindu-empty, (2) awareness-expanse, (3) wisdom self-arisen, (4) far-cast water lamp.

[19404] <concept>
The four lamps represent ground-path-result supports for recognizing *rig pa*. First lamp corresponds to *thig le* (bindu) dimension—white and red essences united in heart center (*snying ga*). Field of light (*'od kyi snam bu*) is ground (*gzhi*) for all appearances.
