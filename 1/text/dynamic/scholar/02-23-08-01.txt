[14588] <structure>
First: Recognition
Structural marker identifying the initial stage in a sequential enumeration within the fruition chapter.

[14588] <philology>
*dang po la ngos zin* — "First: recognition." Ordinal *dang po* with locative *la* marking the stage. *ngos zin* (recognition) combines *ngos* (face/aspect) with *zin* (seized/obtained).

[14588] <context>
Located in Chapter 23 (*'bras bu*), marking the beginning of progressive stages in fruition practice—likely the sequence of recognition, realization, dissolving, and liberation.

[14588] <concept>
Recognition (*ngos zin*) as the foundation of Dzogchen fruition. Unlike gradualist paths, recognition reveals the primordially pure nature (*gdod ma nas dag pa*) already present.
