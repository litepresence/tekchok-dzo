[6423] <philology>
**Key Terms:**
- *drug pa* (sixth) — ordinal marker
- *'od zer stong* (thousand light-rays)
- *mu khyud mgon* (rim-protector)

[6423] <context>
**Protector Configurations:** Six-fold protective maṇḍala arrangements (*mgon po drug*) for tantric practice. Extends five-Buddha model with sixth radiance-configuration.

[6423] <concept>
**Radiance vs. Containment:** Web-protector (fifth) represents wisdom/emptiness as spatial containment; radiance-protector (sixth) represents method/compassion as emanating activity. Establishes complete protective apparatus combining both dimensions.
