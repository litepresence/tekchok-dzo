Threefold division introduction. Dang po la gsum—first has three—gzhi lam 'bras bu—ground, path, fruit. Standard Buddhist triadic structure applied to Dzogchen contemplative process. Ground as ground, path as recognition, fruit as exhaustion. Structural function: establish comprehensive framework for thod rgal practice exposition. Preserve categorical clarity while recognizing these are pedagogical distinctions, not ontological separations.
