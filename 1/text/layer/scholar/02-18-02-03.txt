[4406-4424] <concept>
**Vajra Eye:** Non-dual perception—see phenomena as they truly are. Brief recognition leads to liberation.

**Bubble Eye:** Relative perception where phenomena arise and cease. Four appearances must reach final consummation. Dynamic nature—not flaws but natural display.

**Three Supports:** Three lamps support vision.
