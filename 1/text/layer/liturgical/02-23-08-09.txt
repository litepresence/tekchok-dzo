[35179] <ornament> Fifth: liberation through primordial purity.
[35180] <prose> In the interval between having been liberated but not yet dissolved, emanations are displayed. || 35180 ||
[35181] <prose> The day of emanation is equivalent to one day of meditative concentration.
[35182] <prose> Correlating the five instants with each day of meditative concentration, the manner of liberation through appearances is taught.
[35183] <tantra> From the Self-Arisen:
[35184] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Five Lights;
[35185] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Five Bodies;
[35186] <verse> Some are liberated through the Five Spheres;
[35187] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Mirror;
[35188] <verse> Some are liberated into the Expanse of the Periphery;
[35189] <verse> Some are liberated through the upper and lower spheres;
[35190] <verse> Some are liberated into the empty periphery. || 35190 ||
[35191] <prose> These are the modes of liberation for those of medium faculties.
[35192] <prose> Thus it is said.
[35193] <prose> Moreover, it is thus:
[35194] <prose> Establishing the definitive realization for those of medium faculties:
[35195] <prose> In seven days, definitive realization is attained.
[35196] <prose> Furthermore, one abides five days in the appearances of the spheres,
[35197] <prose> One day in the Dharma-Expanse,
[35198] <prose> One day in the Mirror-Like Jñana.
[35199] <prose> Moreover, from one's own heart-center, a single Body emerges,
[35200] <tantra> From which Bodies without number emanate. || 35200 ||
[35201] <prose> Through awakening to this, one's awareness becomes empowered, like a shooting star.
[35202] <prose> Even emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the twenty-sixth day.
[35203] <prose> The peak of medium faculties:
[35204] <prose> Requires eight days,
[35205] <prose> Furthermore, requiring five days in the spheres.
[35206] <prose> Abiding three days in the appearances of the Four Jñanas,
[35207] <prose> Moreover, seeing above oneself various Jñana-Bodies emanating,
[35208] <prose> Through attaining certainty regarding these,
[35209] <prose> This too is like a shooting star.
[35210] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the twenty-seventh day. || 35210 ||
[35211] <prose> The supreme among medium faculties:
[35212] <prose> Requires nine days,
[35213] <prose> Furthermore, requiring five days in the spheres.
[35214] <prose> Four days are required in Jñana.
[35215] <prose> Then, attaining definitive realization,
[35216] <prose> Recognizing all appearances as like a mirror, and seeing thus, stability is attained.
[35217] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the thirtieth day.
[35218] <prose> The transcendent medium:
[35219] <prose> Requires ten days,
[35220] <prose> Furthermore, five in the spheres, || 35220 ||
[35221] <prose> Four days are required in the appearances of Jñana.
[35222] <prose> Abiding one day in the appearances of spontaneous presence,
[35223] <prose> Then, recognizing all those Bodies as non-dual, one is awakened.
[35224] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the thirty-first day.
[35225] <prose> The excellent among medium faculties:
[35226] <prose> Requires eleven days,
[35227] <prose> Furthermore, five in the spheres,
[35228] <prose> Five in Jñana,
[35229] <prose> One abides in spontaneous presence.
[35230] <prose> Then, attaining definitive realization, seeing all appearances as a mansion of light, certainty is attained. || 35230 ||
[35231] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the thirty-fourth day.
[35232] <prose> The intermediate medium:
[35233] <prose> Requires twelve days,
[35234] <prose> Furthermore, five in the spheres,
[35235] <prose> One angled Body in the Wrathful Buddha-Field of Earth-Unity,
[35236] <prose> Five days in the appearances of Jñana,
[35237] <prose> Abiding one day in the appearances of spontaneous presence.
[35238] <prose> Then, attaining definitive realization, stability is attained.
[35239] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the thirty-seventh day.
[35240] <prose> The lowest of medium faculties: || 35240 ||
[35241] <prose> Requires thirteen days,
[35242] <prose> Furthermore, six in the upper and lower spheres,
[35243] <prose> Five in Jñana,
[35244] <prose> Two abide in spontaneous presence.
[35245] <prose> Then, seeing all appearances as like a lamp-maṇḍala, certainty is attained.
[35246] <prose> Emanation is able to accomplish benefit on the thirty-ninth day. Thus it is said.
[35247] <ornament> Third: The six of lowest faculties:
[35248] <prose> Are liberated in twenty-one instants.
