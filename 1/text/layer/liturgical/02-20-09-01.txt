[29830] <ornament> First:
[29831] <prose> The practice chamber, its direction determined, together with sunbeams entering, pure crystal without stain, prepared as the foundation.
[29832] <tantra> From the Tantra of Clear Introduction:
[29833] <prose> The practice chamber's direction should be determined;
[29834] <prose> Having apertures admitting sunbeams,
[29835] <prose> By extending the mirror that clarifies,
[29836] <prose> Light's appearance is seen emerging outward. || 9410 ||
[29837] <prose> Thus it is spoken.
[29838] <prose> Through this, by Vajrasattva's blessing of mirror-introduction shown in former times, the blessing of the Teacher.
