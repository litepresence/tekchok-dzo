Tenth: attainment of the dharma-cloud-like ground. Obtaining without abandoning this very shell of truth-vision. Those who abandon the shell obtain thus. Delusion-vision ceases, dawns as heap-vision—this is the eleventh: attainment of the universal light ground.

Before recognition dawned... once thought he needed to traverse all sixteen grounds to reach Buddhahood. He spent years analyzing each ground, comparing his experience to the descriptions. Finally, he understood: the grounds are not destinations but descriptions of recognition. Like prayer flags describing the wind—the flags are not the wind, and analyzing them won't make you feel the breeze.
