Etymology from Self-Arising—nine reasons it's called emanation-kaya:
1. Appearing suitable to world realm beings
2. Able to perform emanation-kaya's benefit 
3. Performing all actions
4. Able to liberate countless beings into expanse
5. Brief in time in one field realm
6. Compassion arising without direction
7. Wrathful and fierce when needed
8. Peaceful and certain
9. Beings see as beautiful

All these ways compassion appears—not limited to peaceful forms!
