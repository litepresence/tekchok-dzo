[9404] First:
[9405] The practice chamber, its direction determined, together with sunbeams entering, pure crystal without stain, prepared as the foundation.
[9406] From the Tantra of Clear Introduction:
[9407] The practice chamber's direction should be determined;
[9408] Having apertures admitting sunbeams,
[9409] By extending the mirror that clarifies,
[9410] Light's appearance is seen emerging outward.
[9411] Thus it is spoken.
[9412] Through this, by Vajrasattva's blessing of mirror-introduction shown in former times, the blessing of the Teacher.
