[12479] <prose> When the ground appears from the ground, it becomes deluded by the Three Ignorances and Four Conditions.
[12480] <prose> From Self-Arisen Rigpa: || 12480 ||
[12481] <break> <tantra> "Kye! O companions.
[12482] <break> <tantra> Though the Pure Buddha possesses no delusion,
[12483] <tantra> Vajrasattva's manner of delusion from the primordial ground:
[12484] <tantra> From the Field called Broad, one becomes deluded.
[12485] <tantra> From the Place called Beautiful, one becomes deluded.
[12486] <tantra> From the time when destruction occurs, one becomes deluded.
[12487] <tantra> From the Pig Year, one becomes deluded.
[12488] <tantra> From the Sun called Created, one becomes deluded.
[12489] <tantra> From the Star Bird, one becomes deluded.
[12490] <tantra> From the human named Old One Who Possesses a Heap, one becomes deluded. || 12490 ||
[12491] <tantra> From indeterminate lineage, one becomes deluded.
[12492] <tantra> Four friends exist.
[12493] <tantra> Then the five wild-men arise.
[12494] <tantra> Then one back-support arises.
[12495] <tantra> Then one thief arises.
[12496] <tantra> Then one accumulator of all arises.
[12497] <tantra> Then immeasurable collections of armies arise.
[12498] <tantra> Those immeasurable ones become deluded.
[12499] <tantra> That arises from the ground that possesses no delusion."
