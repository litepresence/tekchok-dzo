[8675] Third bliss is mind and gnosis agitate-of time. The structural function places this as the culminating affective state in the sequence of sensory engagement. Bliss represents the heightened experiential quality arising from the integration of ordinary mind and transcendent knowing. The view register maintains the technical distinction between conditioned bliss and the gnosis-agitated state. Translation requires careful handling of the relationship between mental factors and wisdom-awareness. Preservation value lies in documenting the taxonomy of affective states within tantric contemplative psychology.
