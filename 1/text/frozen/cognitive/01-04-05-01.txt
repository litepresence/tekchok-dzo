False conventional establishment. Container world and beings arranged by intellect are log pa'i kun rdzob—erroneous conventional. Appearance manifests as deluded object's essence, intellect's falseness. Imputed appearances function as deluded intellect's accordant objects with causal power, yet empty by own essence when analyzed. Structure establishes ordinary perception as functional yet fundamentally mistaken. Preserve tension between don mthun par snang ba—appearance accordant with purpose—and ultimate emptiness.

Eight examples of illusion: dream, illusion, gandharva city, mirage, echo, reflection, magical creation, water-moon. Arising as non-existent yet clear-appearing demonstrates dependent arising without inherent existence. Verse form signals authoritative transmission. Each illustrates specific aspect of emptiness-appearance identity.

[2862-2869]
True conventional (yang dag kun rdzob): appears deluded yet serves as cause for blissful migration and liberation. White virtuous actions of ten virtues, merit collections, path of truth through four aspects. Uttaratantra citation: all paths of truth are compounded, all compounds merely conventional. Distinction between log pa'i kun rdzob and yang dag kun rdzob crucial—false vs true conventional.

[2870-2872]
Ultimate truth (don dam bden pa): emptiness essence abiding as inherent nature of dharmatā, peaceful aspect free from elaboration. Two-fold: rnam grangs pa—nominal ultimate expressed through words; rnam grangs pa ma yin pa—non-nominal ultimate of true nature. First as gateway; second as actual realization. Preserve structural distinction without privileging either.
