[6759-6800]
Empowerment as gate of entering unsurpassable vehicle. Threefold structure: basis for entering, path of actual empowerment, result of completed empowerment. Guru characteristics: completing all qualities, free from faults, great compassion. Six characteristics specifically: discarding saṃsāra, few desires, skilled in application, skilled in tantra meaning, skilled in view, great compassion. Glorious Vajradhara as guru ideal. Scriptural citations from Rigpa Rangshar and Thalgyur establishing authority. Tantric guru yoga framework.

[6801-6871]
Student qualification through six-family seed emptying, six affliction purification, six pristine cognition arising. Root and branch guru distinction: root with four marks and empowerment basis, branch with two or three qualifications. Guru to be avoided: ignorance, pride, foolishness, external attachment, inattention to Secret Mantra, critical arrogance, wrong paths, no empowerment, casual samaya, unable to answer, little learning. Ten student dharmas: devotion, faith, desire for definitive meaning, generosity, diligence, few attachments, good nature, wisdom, samaya, harmony. Especially Guru devotion as Buddha or surpassing. Vessel of Great Perfection as qualified student.

[6872-End]
Student to be abandoned: no honor, careless Secret Mantra conduct, no lineage, small wisdom, disregarding kindness, speaking of own lineage, bodily ornamentation. Guru-student relationship as core of tantric practice. Risk of guru abuse in modern contexts; preserve traditional framework without anachronism. Scriptural authority from Self-Arisen tantra.
