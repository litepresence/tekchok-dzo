Four empowerments sequence in colored-powder maṇḍala. Preliminary preparation: deity, vase, student ready. Vase activity with eight auspicious substances, seven royal possessions. Blessing peaceful and wrathful forms with tsaka-li, protruding images, or statues. Vajra blessing, name-empowerment, poti of command-seal. Glorious Torma empowerment completes vase. Secret empowerment: two bodhicittas given. Prajñā-Jñāna empowerment: mudrā meditation for unattached, action-seal for attached. Word empowerment: realization wisdom of view, meditation, rigpa. Four empowerments as middling elaboration.
