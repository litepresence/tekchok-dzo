[998-998]
Third tier of Heaven of Thirty-Three: gods perpetually intoxicated by honey-wine (*sbrang chang gis rtag tu myos pa'i lha*). Progression from protection (tier 1) to beauty (tier 2) to sensory indulgence (tier 3) traces desire-realm evolution from coarse to subtle gratification. Intoxication represents subtlest desire-realm pleasure before form realm transition.
