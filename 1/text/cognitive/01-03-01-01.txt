Brief explanation three gates from where. First aggregates: from where immeasurable memory formless realm, from memory separate grasping desire realm. Second sense-bases: internal mind-appearance-skill self-nature appearing. Because rigpa exists, obstructing skill ignorance alaya, depending on that alaya-vijñana mind, mind and five sense consciousnesses arise. Like golden ground palm tree growing branches flowers leaves. Three realms explained: form realm below above-near distinctions well explained, mind only appearance aspects clear rigpa five wisdoms clear light nature appearing. Desire realm five objects appearing desire arising affliction power, support basis alaya all-seed mind, eight consciousnesses various concepts arising conceptualization, like city reflection moon water reflection dream illusion magical display.

[1653-1658]
RECOGNITION:
Detailed enumeration of six consciousness elements. Eye consciousness holding name and color. Ear consciousness accomplishing grasping. Nose consciousness grasping others. Tongue consciousness experiencing objects. Mind consciousness pervading all existent and non-existent. Body consciousness gathering all objects.
