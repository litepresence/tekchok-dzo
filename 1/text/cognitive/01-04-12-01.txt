[3100-3110]
Supreme mundane dharma stage: highest ordinary attainment before path of seeing. Peak of worldly practice with unshakable samādhi. Preparation for first bhūmi entry. Not yet noble but highest ordinary. Critical juncture between ordinary and transcendent paths.

[3200-3210]
Path of meditation: second through tenth bhūmis. Habitual tendencies removal through progressive familiarization. Not new seeing but deepening integration. Main practice period for bodhisattvas. Nine stages of gradual purification.

[3250-3260]
Path of no-more-learning: complete enlightenment, full buddhahood, end of path. Not nothing to learn but nothing to cultivate. Effortless abiding in natural state. Dharmakāya, sambhogakāya, nirmāṇakāya as complete manifestation.
