Faculty-element correspondences. Form faculties arise from elements: eye from fire, ear from wind, nose from earth, tongue from water, body from all five. Wisdom faculties arise from mind and mental factors. Complex mapping system preserving Buddhist cosmology. Not scientific physiology but soteriological classification. Translate without modernizing or medicalizing.
