[2900-2910]
Ten bodhisattva stages: Perfect Joy, Stainless, Luminous, Radiant, Difficult to Conquer, Manifest, Gone Afar, Immovable, Good Intelligence, Cloud of Dharma. Each with specific qualities, specific purifications, specific perfections emphasized. First six as impure stages, last four as pure. Not merely developmental psychology but wisdom-maturation. Translate *sa* as stage or ground consistently.
