[1677-1682]
RECOGNITION:
Thought-free aggregates. Five abiding in self-knowing aspect of aryas beyond world. Samatha-vipasyana nature samadhi aggregate. Liberated from own afflictions wisdom aggregate. Seeing own-common nature discerning aggregate.

VIEW REGISTER:
Abhidharma/Mahayana / Transcendent aggregates

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- rtog pa med pa'i phung po = thought-free aggregates
- zhi gnas lhag mthong = samatha-vipasyana
- so so rang rig = self-knowing

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Two types of aggregates
- Worldly vs transcendent
- Path-related qualities

[1704-1709]
RECOGNITION:
Ethics aggregate for purifying stains. Liberation aggregate beyond all conceptualization. Sense-bases: what arises and expands from what. Six objects like form for eye.

VIEW REGISTER:
Abhidharma / Sense-base analysis

TRANSLATION NOTES:
- tshul khrims kyi phung po = ethics aggregate
- skye mched = sense-bases
- 'dzin byed = apprehender

PRESERVATION FLAGS:
- Standard abhidharma
- Sense-base definition
- Dual arising
