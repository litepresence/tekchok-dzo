[2779-2791] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [ALAYA AS CONTAINER]
MISREADING:
Alaya is a container or storehouse that holds seeds and impressions.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Storehouse" metaphor taken literally.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Alaya-vijnana reified as metaphysical container.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Confusion about how seeds are "stored."

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].

[2956-2993] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [RIGPA AS EXPERIENCE]
MISREADING:
Rigpa is a special experience, state of consciousness, or peak mystical state.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Awareness" suggests subjective experience. Peak experience chase.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Rigpa becomes another experience to attain.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Disappointment when "ordinary" awareness is pointed out.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
