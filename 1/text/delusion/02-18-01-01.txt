[3922-3940] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [KADAG-LHUNGRUB SEPARATION]
**Misreading:**
Primordial purity (ka dag) and spontaneous presence (lhun grub) are two different things.

**Why it arises:**
Two terms suggest two entities. Analytical thinking.

**Primary consequence:**
Dividing the indivisible ground.

**Secondary consequences:**
Privileging one aspect over the other.

**Cascade effects:**
