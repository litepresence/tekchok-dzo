[3922-3940] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [KADAG-LHUNGRUB SEPARATION]
MISREADING:
Primordial purity (ka dag) and spontaneous presence (lhun grub) are two different things.

WHY IT ARISES:
Two terms suggest two entities. Analytical thinking.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Dividing the indivisible ground.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Privileging one aspect over the other.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[VIEW COLLAPSE].

[3965-4003] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [CONFUSION AS REAL]
MISREADING:
Confusion ('khrul pa) is an actual force, entity, or thing that obstructs enlightenment.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Ignorance" personified as demon or force.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Externalizing confusion as enemy to defeat.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Aggressive spiritual warfare mentality.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].

[4132-4155] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [SAMSARA-NIRVANA DUALISM]
MISREADING:
Samsara and nirvana are actually different, and practice aims to escape one for the other.

WHY IT ARISES:
Conventional language of "liberation" suggests exit.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Missing the non-dual identity of samsara and nirvana.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Spiritual materialism of seeking "better place."

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[DUALISTIC ERROR].
