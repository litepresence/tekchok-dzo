[4601-4631] [PRACTICE ERROR] [TREKCHO AS TECHNIQUE]
MISREADING:
Trekcho is a specific meditation technique or method to practice.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Cutting through" suggests active method. Technique-oriented culture.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Trekcho becomes another meditation method.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Trying to "do" cutting through rather than recognizing.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
