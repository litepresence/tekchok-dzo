[12522-12540] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [LINEAGE AS AUTHORITY]
MISREADING:
Lineage is an authority structure validating teachings.

WHY IT ARISES:
Institutional authority patterns. Apostolic succession model.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Bureaucratic lineage validation.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Politics; power struggles.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[SCHOLARLY COLLAPSE ERROR].
