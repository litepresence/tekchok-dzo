[4156-4183] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [SAMSARA-NIRVANA DUALISM]
MISREADING:
Samsara and nirvana are actually different, and practice aims to escape one for the other.

WHY IT ARISES:
Conventional language of "liberation" suggests exit.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Missing the non-dual identity of samsara and nirvana.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Spiritual materialism of seeking "better place."

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[DUALISTIC ERROR].

[4366-4392] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [RECOGNITION AS EVENT]
MISREADING:
Recognition (ngo shes pa) is a special event, insight, or experience that happens in time.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Recognition" suggests cognitive achievement.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Waiting for recognition to "happen."

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Disappointment that it's "just this."

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
