SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Practice becomes about reaching or entering rather than recognizing what already displays. The immanence of realization is obscured.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
Triggers [SPATIALIZATION ERROR] and [ATTAINMENT ERROR].
