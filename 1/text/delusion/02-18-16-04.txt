[5911-5934] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [OBSTACLES AS EXTERNAL]
MISREADING:
Obstacles (laziness, agitation) are personal failings or external forces.

WHY IT ARISES:
Moral judgment of practice difficulties.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Self-blame or discouragement.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the self-liberated nature of obstacles.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PSYCHOLOGIZATION ERROR].

[5935-5962] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [PROGRESS AS LINEAR]
MISREADING:
Signs of progress indicate linear advancement toward goal.

WHY IT ARISES:
Goal-oriented thinking. Developmental models.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Spiritual materialism of measuring progress.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Competition; comparison with others.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ETERNALISTIC ERROR].
