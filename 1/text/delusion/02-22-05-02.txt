[11736-11760] [PEDAGOGICAL ERROR] [OBSTACLES AS PROBLEMS]
MISREADING:
Obstacles are problems to solve or faults to eliminate.

WHY IT ARISES:
Problem-solving mindset. Self-improvement culture.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
War with obstacles; self-aggression.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Missing the self-liberated nature.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[PRACTICE ERROR].

[11761-11802] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [PROGRESS AS MEASURABLE]
MISREADING:
Signs of progress are objective indicators of advancement.

WHY IT ARISES:
Evidence-based thinking. Measurable outcomes.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Spiritual materialism; checklist Buddhism.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Comparison; competition.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ETERNALISTIC ERROR].
