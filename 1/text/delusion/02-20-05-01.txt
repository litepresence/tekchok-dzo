[9027-9042] [VIEW COLLAPSE] [NON-ATTAINMENT NIHILISM]
MISREADING:
"Nothing to attain" means there is no enlightenment or liberation.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Nothing" interpreted nihilistically.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Rejection of path and fruit.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Licentiousness; spiritual apathy.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[NIHILISTIC ERROR].

[9043-9068] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [SPONTANEOUS AS AUTOMATIC]
MISREADING:
Spontaneous perfection means everything happens automatically without effort.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Spontaneous" suggests automatic process.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Waiting for automatic enlightenment.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Passivity; inaction justified.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ONTOLOGICAL ERROR].
