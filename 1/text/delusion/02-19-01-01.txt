[6414-6415] [EPISTEMIC ERROR] [COMPLETION AS ACHIEVEMENT]
MISREADING:
Completion of Trekcho is an achievement or state to reach.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Completion" suggests goal reached.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking confirmation of completion.

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Pride or inadequacy.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[MEDITATIONISM ERROR].
