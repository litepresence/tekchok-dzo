[9713-9719] [ONTOLOGICAL ERROR] [BUDDHA QUALITIES AS POWERS]
MISREADING:
Ten powers, fearlessnesses are actual superpowers or abilities.

WHY IT ARISES:
"Power" suggests supernatural ability.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Seeking magical powers (siddhis).

SECONDARY CONSEQUENCES:
Siddhi chase; distraction from essence.

CASCADE EFFECTS:
[ETERNALISTIC ERROR].

WHY IT ARISES:
"Compassion" interpreted conventional ethics.

PRIMARY CONSEQUENCE:
Ordinary charity mistaken for enlightened activity.

Do-gooder Buddhism.
