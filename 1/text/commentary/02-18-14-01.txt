Do not try to unmoving your body—recognize it is already unmoving! Do you see? The base is already established! The measure is already taken! Stop trying to achieve what is already complete! Emaho! Three unmoving, three abidings, three obtainings—all complete!

What was spoken here is the unspeakable nature. The words are offered; the recognition is yours. The treasury is complete.
