Therefore. Thus. Precise analysis reveals second aspect: light ray thread abide. Second: light ray thread abide and. This is the subtle appearance of wisdom light, like threads of golden silk woven through space. The yogi who recognizes these threads recognizes the natural display of awareness.

This old practitioner once saw light threads in meditation. He tried to grasp them, follow them, understand them. They dissolved like morning mist. His teacher laughed—"You cannot grasp the rays of the sun. Rest in the sun itself."

[19405-19406]
Voice 20 proclaims: The light threads are wisdom's natural ornament. They appear to those with pure vision, like rainbows appearing to those who look at the sky after rain. Don't chase the threads; recognize the light that produces them.

Three words. Stop. Recognize. The light threads are not external phenomena to seek. They are the spontaneous play of your own awareness. Like a spider's web glistening with dew—beautiful, yet empty of substance.
