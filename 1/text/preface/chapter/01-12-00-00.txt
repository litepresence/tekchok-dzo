## CHAPTER 12: Key Points of Channels and Winds

**Tibetan:** རིམ་ཁང་བཅུ་གཉིས་པ། (Twelfth Lecture Hall)  
**Location:** Volume 1, Sections 01-12-01-01 through 01-12-07-01  
**Tibetan Lines:** 13831-16024  
**Total Liturgical Lines:** ~1,950

An extensive examination of **channel systems** throughout the body. Longchenpa maps the twenty-one thousand channels, the four major plexuses (crown, throat, heart, navel), and the intricate networks through which winds and bindus flow.

**The Four Cakras:**
- **Crown** (*spyi bo*): Thirty-two petals, bliss channel
- **Throat** (*mgul pa*): Sixteen petals, enjoyment channel  
- **Heart** (*snying ga*): Eight petals, Dharma channel
- **Navel** (*lte ba*): Sixty-four petals, emanation channel

**Key Points:**
Detailed presentation of channel varieties, interconnections, and their functions in meditation practice.

**Key Contribution:** Provides the complete anatomical map for subtle body practices.
