[5911] First, at the time of seeing truth, joy arising to that is first ground: Very Joyful.
[5912] Recognizing that as self-vision is
[5913] Second ground: obtaining Stainless.
[5914] Then, habituation to that is
[5915] Third ground: obtaining Light-Doing Ground.
[5916] Then, habituating to that and seeing light-vision is fourth: obtaining Light-Emitting Ground.
[5917] Then, having habituated to seeing wisdom-vision,
[5918] Purifying all afflictions by nature,
[5919] Seeing wisdom is fifth: obtaining Difficult-to-Purify Ground.
[5920] Then, that effulgence body manifesting from light is sixth: obtaining Manifest Ground.
[5921] Then, habituation arriving at the end, afflictions gone far, is seventh: obtaining Far-Going Ground.
