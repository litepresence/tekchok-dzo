[5911] <ornament>First:</ornament>
[5912] <prose>At the time of seeing truth, joy arises in that—
This is the first ground, Very Joyful.</prose>
[5913] <prose>Recognizing that as one's own vision—</prose>
[5914] <prose>This is the second ground, Stainless, obtained.</prose>
[5915] <prose>Then, habituating to that—</prose>
[5916] <prose>This is the third ground, Illuminating, obtained.</prose>
[5917] <prose>Then, habituating to that and seeing the vision of light—</prose>
[5918] <prose>This is the fourth ground, Radiant, obtained.</prose>
[5919] <prose>Then, seeing the vision of wisdom and habituating to that,
Purifying all afflictions by nature,</prose>
[5920] <prose>Wisdom seeing is the fifth ground, Difficult to Purify, obtained.</prose>
[5921] <prose>Then, the body of effulgence from light manifestly becoming—
That is the sixth ground, Manifest, obtained.</prose>
[5922] <prose>Then, habituation arriving at the end, affliction gone far—
This is the seventh ground, Gone Far, obtained.</prose>
[5923] <prose>Then, from that manner, unmoving—</prose>
