<!-- Section: Removal of Obstacles -->

[4401] 4401. Such meaning: if the Guru's instruction does not indicate it, it is not realized.
[4402] 4402. If indicated, realizing the indicating method: that is called the method-indicating Tantra.
[4403] 4403. That method transmitted one-to-one: thus called method-indicating.
[4404] 4404. Through indication, Rigpa must be seen—liberation itself must be gained.
[4405] 4405. "Meaning unestablished anywhere, so no Tantra"—if one thinks this, it is not so.
