[2836] First:
[2837] All phenomena gathered by samsara and nirvana are gathered into the self-nature of the two truths.
[2838] As the Root Wisdom states:
[2839] "The Buddhas taught the Dharma,
[2840] Relying correctly upon the two truths:
[2841] The conventional truth of the world,
[2842] And the ultimate truth of meaning."
[2843] Thus it is said.
[2844] From the Illusion Net:
[2845] "One abides in the manner of the two truths."
[2846] Thus.
[2847] As for this: phenomena marked by characteristics that appear as objects of intellect are the conventional truth.
