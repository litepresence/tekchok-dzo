[7099] First, the time and signs for empowerment with elaboration:
[7100] From showing the crucial point of timing for sequentially entering Empowerment,
[7101] The Empowerment with elaboration:
<tantra> [7102] "Rat, dragon, horse, and pig—
<tantra> [7103] Especially in these four years,
<tantra> [7104] The Empowerment ritual is accomplished.
<tantra> [7105] In the four first months,
<tantra> [7106] Accomplished for producing complete result at the complete time.
<tantra> [7107] From the first day of the waxing moon,
<tantra> [7108] Until the time of the full moon.
<tantra> [7109] The time for conferral is midday or midnight.
<tantra> [7110] According to the particular body of the yogi,
<tantra> [7111] Apply to the individual element-year;
<tantra> [7112] The Empowerment ritual becomes auspicious.
<tantra> [7113] At this time, the student possessing Samaya,
<tantra> [7114] Bodies of harmonious elements—
<tantra> [7115] Number without measure,
<tantra> [7116] One hundred, fifty, and so forth,
<tantra> [7117] If harmonious with elements, without contradiction.
<tantra> [7118] If entering the ritual,
<tantra> [7119] Without possessing the lamp flame,
<tantra> [7120] Perform the ritual of repairing Samaya;
<tantra> [7121] Without defilement, siddhi—
<tantra> [7122] Should be taken again and again.
<tantra> [7123] Death without meaning, obstacles arise;
<tantra> [7124] At complete Empowerment, forcefully exert the recitation.
<tantra> [7125] At that time, among gathered relatives,
<tantra> [7126] If disease arises in the body,
<tantra> [7127] Perform prostration and circumambulation;
<tantra> [7128] If chaos arises in speech to anyone,
<tantra> [7129] Contemplate and recite the hundred syllables;
<tantra> [7130] If mind is hungry or memory fades,
<tantra> [7131] Enter the generation and completion stages.
<tantra> [7132] If signs of improvement arise,
<tantra> [7133] From the beginning, exert the Empowerment ritual.
<tantra> [7134] If the substance of accomplishment increases,
<tantra> [7135] Having obtained supreme siddhi,
<tantra> [7136] Become faithful to the meaning of equality.
<tantra> [7137] Having entered the Empowerments,
<tantra> [7138] If good signs are always seen,
<tantra> [7139] Common siddhi is accomplished thereby.
<tantra> [7140] Even possessing experience and knowledge,
<tantra> [7141] Through this also, engage in bliss and happiness.
<tantra> [7142] To the Guru supreme,
<tantra> [7143] Whoever is born with good faith,
<tantra> [7144] Through this also, apply to the supreme mudrā.
<tantra> [7145] If self sees body and speech blissful,
<tantra> [7146] This is the pure Buddha field.
<tantra> [7147] Thus the time of Empowerment with elaboration
<tantra> [7148] And signs are well explained."
<tantra> [7149] Thus it is spoken.

<tantra> [7151] Also from that source:
<tantra> [7152] "Now, without elaboration,
<tantra> [7153] Time and signs should be clearly shown.
<tantra> [7154] In this, there is no distinction of year;
<tantra> [7155] Whoever knows.

<tantra> [7158] Empowerment time at dawn when sounds become clear.

<tantra> [7169] The time and signs
<tantra> [7170] Of empowerment without elaboration are thus explained."
<tantra> [7171] Thus it is spoken.
