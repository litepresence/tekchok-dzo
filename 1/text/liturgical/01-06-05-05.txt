[8677] <verse> Fifth: Emptiness—recognition that neither affirms nor denies, transcending all extremes.
[8678] <tantra> Thus it is spoken:
[8679] <ornament> From before the beginning—

<prose> Recognition has never opened or closed. The two extremes are mere concepts in the face of direct wisdom. Distinctions dissolve into the spontaneous display.
