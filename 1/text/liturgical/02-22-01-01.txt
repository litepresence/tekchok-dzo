[10211] Intermediate, and after liberation: determining signs, concluding by secret.
[10212] First, there are three:
[10213] How the body purifies the three doors of body, speech, and mind—signs.
[10214] How speech becomes familiar—signs.
[10215] How mind trains—signs.
[10216] First:
[10217] In life before, obstacles to purifying body, speech, and mind from the body's gates—now demonstrating four signs.
[10218] Sequence of signs:
[10219] Arising suitability,
[10220] Familiar fruition,
[10221] Not familiar, definitely not obtained.
[10222] First:
[10223] From the Glorious Sky Without Body Blaze Great Tantra:
[10224] How all perfect Buddhas'
[10225] Body signs and speech signs,
[10226] Likewise vajra mind signs,
[10227] How arise before—like that.
[10228] Here also, all sentient beings' bodies
[10229] Become all this appearance.
[10230] That which itself appears to whom,
[10231] Possesses fortune of purification from before.
[10232] From not having ripened before,
[10233] Now, certain time, fruition.
[10234] To the person accomplished by whom,
[10235] Body signs and likewise speech,
[10236] Mind, etc., signs—this arises indeed.
[10237] Thus having,
[10238] Signs of purifying Buddha-body in life before: born as a woman whose forehead center has conch, right swirl, or three-point vertical frown.
[10239] All OM marks,
[10240] Lines or
[10241] The six and so forth,
[10242] Guru seeing, prophesying.
[10243] Speech signs:
[10244] Sweet-voiced, clear, gentle-spoken,
[10245] Pleasant, joyous,
[10246] Like Brahma's melody.
[10247] Unstained by unwholesome deeds of speech,
[10248] Word meaningful.
[10249] Mind signs:
[10250] Faith, compassion, generosity,
[10251] Conduct virtuous.
[10252] Through these three signs,
[10253] Liberation intermediate attained.
[10254] Second:
[10255] Familiarity, three indeed:
[10256] Body-familiarity,
[10257] Speech-familiarity,
[10258] Mind-familiarity.
[10259] Body-familiarity:
[10260] Not abandoning body-vessel,
[10261] Training all faculties,
[10262] Channels, winds, bindus all gathered.
[10263] Speech-familiarity:
[10264] Reciting, reading,
[10265] Receiving explanation, retaining.
[10266] Mind-familiarity:
[10267] Generating mind, making offerings,
[10268] Accumulating collections, purifying obscurations.
[10269] Body-familiarity signs:
[10270] Body light, easily sustained.
[10271] No sickness, long-lived.
[10272] Clear complexion, lustrous.
[10273] Speech-familiarity signs:
[10274] Voice melodious, charming.
[10275] Teachings easily explained.
[10276] Mind-familiarity signs:
[10277] Mind pliant, serviceable.
[10278] Wisdom sharp, clear.
[10279] Dreams auspicious.
[10280] Through these, definitely obtain.
[10281] Third:
[10282] Fruition, three indeed:
[10283] Body-fruition,
[10284] Speech-fruition,
[10285] Mind-fruition.
[10286] Body-fruition signs:
[10287] Marks and characteristics complete.
[10288] Major marks, minor marks perfect.
[10289] Speech-fruition signs:
[10290] Brahma's voice, sixty qualities.
[10291] Mind-fruition signs:
[10292] Ten powers, four fearlessnesses.
[10293] Knowing all knowables.
[10294] These three fruitions,
[10295] From having familiarity.
[10296] Thus the Blessed One spoke.
[10297] From the Sound Tantra:
[10298] "How did former Buddhas
[10299] Body purify, speech purify,
[10300] Mind purify, like that.
[10301] Here also sentient beings all,
[10302] These all appear become."
[10303] Thus it is spoken.
[10304] Signs of body purification in life before:
[10305] Forehead mark, conch-swirl,
[10306] Three points vertical, and so forth.
[10307] Signs of speech purification:
[10308] Voice sweet, clear, gentle.
[10309] Signs of mind purification:
[10310] Faith, generosity, compassion.
[10311] Through these three signs,
[10312] Obtaining liberation.
[10313] Intermediate after liberation:
[10314] Secret signs, concluding summary.
[10315] These are the key points of lamps.
