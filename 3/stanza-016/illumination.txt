## Translator's Illumination (ngo sprod)

Tree-power trunk—not a location, not a time, not an event.

What the Protector knew—not knowledge accumulated, not wisdom achieved.

Victorious Sons—not lineal descendants, not spiritual ancestors.

What they saw—this seeing, not another, not the same.

Single word—not abbreviated teaching, not secret code.

Inexpressible meaning—not awaiting expression, not frustrated by it.

Skilful means—not technique, not method, not approach.

The showing—not display for an audience, not revelation to a recipient.
