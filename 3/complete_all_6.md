kun mkhyen bla ma'i gsung rab la bsngags pa bzhugs so  
na maḥ sarba jñāya gu ra ve  

Homage to the scripture of the All-Knowing Lama  
namaḥ sarva jñāya gurave

Stanza 1 - ༡ 

Tibetan  
གང་ཞིག་རྒྱལ་བའི་ཡོན་ཏན་མཐའ་ཀླས་པ།
།མ་ལུས་ཡང་དག་རྫོགས་པའི་དཀྱིལ་འཁོར་ཅན།
།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མ་རྒྱལ་བ་ཐམས་ཅད་ཀྱི།
།རྒྱལ་ཚབ་སེམས་དཔར་གྱུར་ལ་ཕྱག་འཚལ་ལོ།

Wylie  
gang zhig rgyal ba'i yon tan mtha' klas pa  
ma lus yang dag rdzogs pa'i dkyil 'khor can  
kun mkhyen bla ma rgyal ba thams cad kyi  
rgyal tshab sems dpar gyur la phyag 'tshal lo  

Literal  
which Victorious-One's quality-merit limit-transcended  
without-remainder perfect-complete mandala-circle possessing  
all-knowing Lama Victorious-One all-of  
Victorious-regent heart-son become-to prostration-offer  

Liturgical  
Qualities extending to furthest bounds complete,  
Perfect mandala, whole without remainder,  
All-Knowing Lama of every Victorious One,  
To regent heart-son I offer prostration.  

Commentary  
Bower-bowed-bowing collapse where no division stood.  
Extension itself the boundlessness—no edge to transcend.  
Mandala completing what never lacked completion—container and contained one display.  
Gesture where bower, bowed-to, bowing arise as single movement never divided.

།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མ་ཐམས་ཅད་མཁྱེན་པ་ཡིན།

Literal
primordial ground expanse qualities complete expanse

Liturgical

Dharmatā fully entering great emptiness' womb,

All-Knowing knowing all knowing—omniscience not possession but recognition that separation never occurred.
