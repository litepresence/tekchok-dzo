# Stanza 067: Complete Translation

## Original Tibetan
།ནམ་ཞིག་སྲིད་དང་ཞི་བ་མཉམ་པར་བརྡལ།
།འཁོར་བ་དོང་སྤྲུགས་ཞི་བ་བུམ་སྐུར་བཞེངས།
།འཁོར་འདས་མ་ལུས་ཐིག་ལེ་གཅིག་གི་ཀློང༌།
།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མའི་དགོངས་པར་འདྲེས་གྱུར་ཅིག

## Extended Wylie Transliteration
nam zhig srid dang zhi ba mnyam par brdal/
'khor ba dong sprugs zhi ba bum skur bzhengs/
'khor 'das ma lus thig le gcig gi klong/
kun mkhyen bla ma'i dgongs par 'dres gyur cig
༦༧

## 1. Liturgical Rendering (mchod brjod tshig; Skt. stotra-pada)

When existence and peace are equally pervaded,
Hollowing out samsara, erecting peace in a vase,
May the expanse of one drop without remainder of samsara and nirvana
Become mixed with the realization of the All-Knowing Guru.
༦༧

## 2. Commentary ('grel pa don 'grel; Skt. lakṣaṇa-nirdeśa)

## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This final batch continues the non-dual transmission to the closing stage. The field of praise becomes the field of liberation; the multiplicity dissolves into unity, and the unity becomes the expressible form of liberation itself. Lakṣaṇa: field as ground; unity as the essence; transmission without naming.

## 3. Illumination (ngo sprod; Skt. svarūpa-prakāśa)

00001| Field and ground converge; unity remains the ground that carries all.
00002| Transmission through syntax, not naming, remains the heart of the message.
00003| The field of the mind is the stage of recognition for all beings.

## 4. Literal Translation ('gyur don bsgyur; Skt. artha-anuvāda)

When existence and peace equally pervade/
Samsara hollow-out peace vase-in erect/
Samsara-nirvana without-remainder drop one of expanse/
All-knowing guru of realization mix become may/
༦༧
