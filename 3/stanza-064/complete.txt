# Stanza 064: Complete Translation

## Original Tibetan
།བསྔགས་འོས་ཡུལ་ལ་བསྔགས་པའི་མཆོད་སྤྲིན་འདིའི།
།ལེགས་བྱས་དགེ་བ་སྲིད་ལ་ཞི་རྒྱུ་དང༌།
།ཞི་ལ་སྲིད་དོན་གཉིས་མཐར་མི་གནས་པའི།
།རྒྱལ་དང་དེ་སྲས་ཐུགས་དགོངས་རྫོགས་ཕྱིར་བསྔོ།

## Extended Wylie Transliteration
bsngags 'os yul la bsngags pa'i mchod sprin 'di'i/
legs byas dge ba srid la zhi rgyu dang/
zhi la srid don gnyis mthar mi gnas pa'i/
rgyal dang de sras thugs dgongs rdzogs phyir bsngo/
༦༤

## 1. Liturgical Rendering (mchod brjod tshig; Skt. stotra-pada)

This cloud of offerings of praise to the praise-worthy object,
The well-done virtue that causes existence to become peace,
And dedication for the complete heart-intention of the victorious ones and their sons,
Who do not abide in either extreme of existence or peace.
༦༤

## 2. Commentary ('grel pa don 'grel; Skt. lakṣaṇa-nirdeśa)

## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This batch reinforces the non-dual field reading across the sky-expanse metaphor, reinforcing unity as the ground in which all realms and voices converge. Lakṣaṇa: field-boundary dissolves; the field of recognition becomes the only field; nine vehicles and the commentary are seen as a single, continuous expression.

## 3. Illumination (ngo sprod; Skt. svarūpa-prakāśa)

00001| Sky expanse as field; unity is ground.
00002| Nine vehicles dissolve into one field, no hierarchy.
00003| Transmission occurs as syntax, not as naming.
00004| Recognition arises where boundaries disappear.

## 4. Literal Translation ('gyur don bsgyur; Skt. artha-anuvāda)

Praise-worthy object to praise-of offering-cloud this's/

Peace to existence purpose two extreme not-abiding's/
Victorious and those-sons heart-intention complete for dedicate/
༦༤
