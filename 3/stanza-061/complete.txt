## 1. Liturgical Rendering (mchod brjod tshig; Skt. stotra-pada)

Even in countless realms this field of praise,
For the victorious ones and learned victorious sons,
Became the source for discussion of sacred dharma,
The domain of individual correct knowledge.
༦༡

## 2. Commentary ('grel pa don 'grel; Skt. lakṣaṇa-nirdeśa)

## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This batch reinforces non-dual field perception by reframing the 'field of praise' as an all-encompassing ground. The many realms are not separate spaces but facets of recognition; the domain of individual correct knowledge dissolves into a singular field where the ground and display are inseparable. Lakṣaṇa: field as groundless unity; unity as field; no boundary among realms.

## 3. Illumination (ngo sprod; Skt. svarūpa-prakāśa)

00001| Field of praise—groundless, pervading the whole field.
00002| Unity—field, centerless, without boundary.
00003| Learned ones and victors are facets of the field, not separate beings.
00004| Transmission is the field’s presence, not an external instruction.
00005| Lakṣaṇa: unity in multiplicity; field as the ground of all.
