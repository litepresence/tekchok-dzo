## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This batch tightens non-abiding through the key of method, emphasizing the non-seeking aspect of understanding. The mind’s partial investigations exist as a display within a larger non-dual field; the key is that method reveals and dissolves itself as the ground of recognition. Lakṣaṇa: method as appearance, knowledge as display, and the realization that there was never a separate method to master.
