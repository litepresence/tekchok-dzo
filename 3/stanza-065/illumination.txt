## Translator's Illumination (ngo sprod)

Interval clouds—not weather patterns, not phenomena, but the natural displays that appear between thoughts.

Splendor—not brightness, not shine, but the natural radiance that was never dim.

Marks and examples—not signs, not symbols, but the expressions that point to what was never hidden.

Remembering—not memory, not recollection, but the recognition that was never forgotten.

Wisdom—not knowledge, not learning, but the natural knowing that was never acquired.

Gentle glorious hero—not deity, not being, but the quality of wisdom that is simultaneously soft and courageous.

Three spheres—not categories, not realms, but the triad of subject, object, and action that was never separate.

True existence—not reality, not fact, but the conviction that something is ultimately real.

Complete dedication—not total transfer, not perfect giving, but the recognition that there was never anything to transfer.

Without aspiration—not hopelessness, not despair, but the natural state that needs no wishing.
