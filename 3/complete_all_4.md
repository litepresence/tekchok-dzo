### Stanza 1

**Original Tibetan**  
གང་ཞིག་རྒྱལ་བའི་ཡོན་ཏན་མཐའ་ཀླས་པ།  
།མ་ལུས་ཡང་དག་རྫོགས་པའི་དཀྱིལ་འཁོར་ཅན།  
།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མ་རྒྱལ་བ་ཐམས་ཅད་ཀྱི།  
།རྒྱལ་ཚབ་སེམས་དཔར་གྱུར་ལ་ཕྱག་འཚལ་ལོ།  
༡  

**Extended Wylie**  
gang zhig rgyal ba'i yon tan mtha' klas pa  
ma lus yang dag rdzogs pa'i dkyil 'khor can  
kun mkhyen bla ma rgyal ba thams cad kyi  
rgyal tshab sems dpar gyur la phyag 'tshal lo  

**Literal Translation**  
which Victorious-One's quality-merit limit-transcended  
without-remainder perfect-complete mandala-circle possessing  
all-knowing Lama Victorious-One all-of  
Victorious-regent heart-son become-to prostration-offer  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
Qualities reaching furthest bounds—  
Perfect mandala, complete in every aspect—  
All-Knowing Lama of every Victorious One—  
To you, regent heart-son, I bow.  

**Commentary**  
Bowing bows where no one stands.  
Qualities extend without reaching an edge—extension itself the boundlessness.  
Mandala completes what never lacked completion—container and contained dissolve into single display.  
All-Knowing recognizes what never departed from recognition—Lama appears as Victorious, Victorious as regent, regency inhering without heir.  
Prostration: gesture where bower, bowed-to, and bowing collapse into movement that never divided.  

**Illumination**  
Qualities extending—no edge reached  
Mandala complete—nothing added  
All-Knowing—no one knowing  
Heart-son—no inheritance  
Prostration—no one bowing

### Stanza 2

**Original Tibetan**  
སྐྱོབ་པ་དེ་ཡི་ལེགས་བཤད་ཚིག་གཅིག་གི  
།ཟུར་ཙམ་ཐོས་ཀྱང་སྲིད་པ་ཧྲུལ་པོ་རུ།  
།ངེས་པར་བགྱིད་ན་བསྟན་པ་ཡོངས་རྫོགས་པའི།  
།གསུང་ལ་ཐོས་བསམ་བགྱིད་རྣམས་ལྟ་ཅི་སྨོས།  
༢  

**Extended Wylie**  
skyob pa de yi legs bshad tshig gcig gi  
zur tsam thos kyang srid pa hrul po ru  
nges par bgyid na bstan pa yongs rdzogs pa'i  
gsung la thos bsam bgyid rnams lta ci smos  

**Literal Translation**  
Protector that-of excellent-explained word-one of  
corner-edge merely heard-even existence wandering-into  
certainly do-if teaching complete-completion of  
speech to hearing-contemplation doing-plural see-what mention  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
One word from the Protector's excellent speech—  
Hearing merely at the edge, samsara reels.  
Practice with certainty this complete teaching—  
What need to speak of study, contemplation?  

**Commentary**  
Word speaks without speaker.  
Hearing occurs where no ear receives—edge of sound touches nothing yet recognition arises.  
Samsara reels where no samsara stands—apparent wandering dissolves upon contact, revealing no wanderer ever departed.  
Certainty needs no establishing. Practice needs no practitioner. Complete teaching transmits without distance between speaker and hearer.  
Study contemplates itself already studied. Contemplation studies itself already contemplated. Methods dissolve into methodless recognition.  

**Illumination**  
Word from Protector—speech speaking itself  
Edge-hearing—no receiver, recognition  
Samsara reeling—stability appearing as movement  
Certain practice—accomplishing without practitioner  
Complete teaching—transmission without distance

### Stanza 3

**Original Tibetan**  
རྒྱལ་བ་རྣམས་དང་རྒྱལ་སྲས་ཆེ་རྣམས་ཀྱིས།  
།དཔའ་བར་འགྲོ་སོགས་ཏིང་འཛིན་རྣམ་ཐར་མཐུས།  
།འཕགས་བཞི་སོ་སྐྱེ་དུ་མའི་སྤྲུལ་གཟུགས་ཀྱིས།  
།རྒྱལ་བའི་མཛད་བཟང་ཅི་ཡང་སྟོན་པ་ཡིན།  
༣  

**Extended Wylie**  
rgyal ba rnams dang rgyal sras che rnams kyis  
dpa' bar 'gro sogs ting 'dzin rnam thar mthus  
'phags bzhi so skye du ma'i sprul gzugs kyis  
rgyal ba'i mdzad bzang ci yang ston pa yin  

**Literal Translation**  
Victorious-One plural and Victorious-Son great plural by  
heroic-go etc. concentration liberation power-of  
noble-four diverse birth many-of emanation form by  
Victorious-One's activity good what all show is  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
Victorious Ones and their great Bodhisattva sons—  
Through heroism, concentrations' liberating power—  
In emanation forms of four noble ones, diverse births—  
The Victorious One shows every excellent activity.  

**Commentary**  
Victory without victor.  
Bodhisattva sons manifest as recognition without lineage—greatness without dimension, heirs without inheritance.  
Heroic going goes nowhere yet arrives everywhere—movement without mover, courage without courageous one.  
Concentration gathers without gathering. Liberation liberates liberation itself—power without power-holder.  
Four noble ones appear without fourness—diversity without division, birth without birthing.  
Emanation forms form without forming. Activity accomplishes without accomplishment—showing shows itself.  

**Illumination**  
Victory—no victor  
Recognition—no lineage  
Heroic going—no destination  
Concentration—no gathering  
Liberation—liberating itself  
Diversity—no division  
Forming—no former  
Showing—no show-er

### Stanza 4

**Original Tibetan**  
དེ་སླད་ཀུན་མཁྱེན་མཆོག་གི་སྤྲུལ་པའི་སྐུས།  
།རྒྱལ་བའི་མཛད་པས་སྙིགས་མའི་འགྲོ་སྐྱོང་ཞིང༌།  
།ཐོས་པས་གྲོལ་བ་ཐེག་རྩེའི་ཆོས་འཁོང་གྱིས།  
།རྡོ་རྗེ་སྙིང་པོའི་བསྟན་པ་གསལ་བར་མཛད།  
༤  

**Extended Wylie**  
de slad kun mkhyen mchog gi sprul pa'i skus  
rgyal ba'i mdzad pas snyigs ma'i 'gro skyong zhing  
thos pas grol ba theg rtse'i chos 'khong gyis  
rdo rje snying po'i bstan pa gsal bar mdzad  

**Literal Translation**  
that after all-knowing supreme-of emanation body by  
Victorious-One's activity by degenerate-age beings protect  
hearing by liberate vehicle-peak doctrine treasury-by  
Vajra Essence's teaching clear make  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
After that, through emanation body of supreme All-Knowing—  
By Victorious One's deeds, protecting beings of degenerate age—  
By treasury of peak vehicle doctrine that liberates through hearing—  
He made Vajra Essence teaching clear.  

**Commentary**  
Supreme All-Knowing emanates as body beyond time—"after" appears without subsequency.  
Emanation body manifests without emanator—supremacy knows no comparison, All-Knowing possesses no possessor.  
Victorious deeds protect where no protection needed—degenerate age appears as agelessness, beings appear without beingness.  
Treasury of peak vehicle liberates through hearing—treasury treasures without treasuring, vehicle travels without traveler.  
Hearing liberates without liberation. Sound releases without release.  
Vajra Essence teaching makes clear without clarification—clarity clears what was never unclear, essence essentializes without essentializing.  

**Illumination**  
Emanation—no emanator  
Deeds—no doer  
Degenerate age—agelessness appearing  
Peak vehicle—traveling without traveler  
Hearing liberation—sound releasing itself  
Vajra Essence—piercing without piercing

### Stanza 5

**Original Tibetan**  
ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མ་རྫོགས་པའི་སངས་རྒྱས་དེའི།  
།ལེགས་བཤད་བདུད་རྩི་ཤིན་ཏུ་དག་པ་འདི།  
།ལུང་གི་ཆོས་མཆོག་ཞལ་ནས་གསུང་པའི་བཀར།  
།མི་ཤེས་པ་དེ་བ་གླང་ཡ་སོ་ཅན།  
༥  

**Extended Wylie**  
kun mkhyen bla ma rdzogs pa'i sangs rgyas de'i  
legs bshad bdud rtsi shin tu dag pa 'di  
lung gi chos mchog zhal nas gsung pa'i bkar  
mi shes pa de ba glang ya so can  

**Literal Translation**  
all-knowing Lama perfect Buddha that-of  
excellent-explained ambrosia extremely pure this  
scripture of doctrine supreme mouth-from spoken command  
not-know that ox stall possessing  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
This extremely pure ambrosia of excellent explanation  
From the All-Knowing Lama, that perfect Buddha—  
The supreme doctrine from scripture, spoken from his mouth—  
Those who do not know this are like oxen in a stall.  

**Commentary**  
Ambrosia manifests as extreme purity without substance—purity purifies without purification, achieving without achievement.  
Perfect Buddha perfects without perfection—Buddha manifests without Buddhanature, appearing as Buddha without entity.  
Explained meaning explains without explaining—meaning renders without rendering, communicates without transmission.  
All-Knowing knows without knower—omniscience manifests as knowing that knows all knowing simultaneously.  
Supreme doctrine scriptures without containing—scripture texts without textual vessel, doctrine without doctrinal content.  
Mouth speaks without vocalization—sounds without source, commands without ordering.  
Those who do not know appear as oxen in stall—stall without enclosure, confinement without barrier. Not-knowing manifests as knowing knowing itself as not-knowing.  

**Illumination**  
Ambrosia purity—no substance  
Explained meaning—no explaining  
Perfect Buddha—no Buddhanature  
Supreme doctrine—no containing  
Mouth speaking—no speaker  
Stall confinement—no barrier  
Not-knowing—knowing itself

### Stanza 6

**Original Tibetan**  
སྐལ་བ་བཟང་དག་རབ་དགའ་སྐྱེད་བྱོས་ལ།  
།སངས་རྒྱས་མཐོང་བའི་ངེས་ཤེས་གྱིས་ཤིག་དང༌།  
།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་བླ་མའི་ལེགས་བཤད་འདི་མཐོང་ལས།  
།སངས་རྒྱས་མཇལ་ཡང་ཅུང་ཟད་ཡོད་རེ་ཀན།  
༦  

**Extended Wylie**  
skal ba bzang dag rab dga' skyed byos la  
sangs rgyas mthong ba'i nges shes gyis shig dang  
kun mkhyen bla ma'i legs bshad 'di mthong las  
sangs rgyas mjal yang cung zad yod re kan  

**Literal Translation**  
fortune good great joy produce  
Buddha see-of certainty know do  
all-knowing Lama's excellent-explained this see from  
Buddha meet even slight exist perhaps  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
O you of good fortune, produce great joy—  
And develop certainty of seeing the Buddha!  
From seeing this excellent explanation of the All-Knowing Lama—  
There is perhaps even more than meeting the Buddha.  

**Commentary**  
Good fortune produces great joy without producing—fortune unfolds without acquisition, joy emerges without generation.  
Seeing Buddha develops certainty without developing—certainty establishes without conviction, seeing perceives without perception.  
Buddha appears without being—manifests without entity, appears to sight without sight appearing to sight.  
Excellent explanation seeing exceeds meeting Buddha—seeing explanation reads without reading words, comprehends without comprehending meaning.  
Explanation mediates without medium—bridges without bridging, understanding without understanding.  
The "more" transcends comparison—quantity without measure, superiority without hierarchy. Seeing sees no Buddha yet sees Buddha seeing. Meeting meets no one yet meets meeting itself.  

**Illumination**  
Fortune unfolding—no acquisition  
Joy emerging—no generation  
Seeing Buddha—no Buddha seen  
Explanation reading—no words read  
Meeting meeting—no one met  
More than meeting—no comparison

### Stanza 7

**Original Tibetan**  
ཐུབ་པའི་མཚན་དཔེ་མིག་གི་བདུད་རྩི་སྟེ།  
།སྐལ་བཟང་ཡིད་ལ་དད་གསུམ་བསྐྱེད་པ་ལགས།  
།ལེགས་བཤད་ཡིག་གཟུགས་བཟོ་ཡི་སྤྲུལ་སྐུ་སྟེ།  
།དད་པའི་གསོས་སུ་འདི་ལས་ལྷག་པ་མེད།  
༧  

**Extended Wylie**  
thub pa'i mtshan dpe mig gi bdud rtsi ste  
skal bzang yid la dad gsum bskyed pa lags  
legs bshad yig gzugs bzo yi sprul sku ste  
dad pa'i gso su 'di las lhag pa med  

**Literal Translation**  
Mighty-One's marks-symbols eye-of ambrosia is  
fortune-good mind in faith-three produce is  
excellent-explained letter-form craft-of emanation body is  
faith-of medicine this from superior not  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
The ambrosia for the eyes, the marks and symbols of the Mighty One—  
Is indeed the producer of three faiths in the mind of the fortunate.  
The emanation body of the craft of letter-forms in excellent explanation—  
There is none superior to this as medicine for faith.  

**Commentary**  
Mighty One's ambrosia manifests as marks and symbols for eyes—ambrosia feeds without nourishment, marks indicate without marking.  
Mighty One mights without possessing might—eyes see without seeing. Sensory experience dissolves: marks mark unmarked, symbols signify unsignified.  
Three faiths produce in fortunate mind without production—production produces without causation, faiths believe without believers.  
Fortunate receives without receiving—experiencing without experiencer. Faith manifests threefold yet remains indivisible.  
Letter-form craft emanates as excellent explanation—craft forms without skill, letters without literacy, explanation without clarifying.  
Emanation projects without projection—appearing without source. Excellence excels without comparison.  
No medicine surpasses this as faith's remedy—superiority ranks without hierarchy, medicine cures without healing. Remedy remedies without remedying.  

**Illumination**  
Ambrosia for eyes—seeing without seer  
Marks indicating—no marking  
Three faiths—believing without believers  
Fortunate mind—receiving without receiver  
Letter-form craft—forming without former  
Medicine curing—no illness cured

### Stanza 8

**Original Tibetan**  
སྟོན་པའི་ཚངས་དབྱངས་རྣ་བའི་བདུད་རྩི་སྟེ།  
།བསྐལ་བཟང་ཡིད་ལ་ཤེས་རབ་བསྐྱེད་པ་ལགས།  
ལེགས་བཤད་ཚིག་ཕྲེང་རྣ་བའི་བདུད་རྩི་སྟེ།  
།ཤེས་རབ་གསོས་སུ་འདི་དང་མཉམ་པ་མེད།  
༨

**Extended Wylie**  
ston pa'i tshangs dbyangs rna ba'i bdud rtsi ste  
bskal bzang yid la shes rab bskyed pa lags  
legs bshad tshig phreng rna ba'i bdud rtsi ste  
shes rab gos su 'di dang mnyam pa med  

**Literal Translation**  
Teacher's Brahma-sound ear-of ambrosia is  
Fortune-good mind in wisdom produce is  
Excellent-explained word-garland ear-of ambrosia is  
Wisdom medicine this with equal not  

**Liturgical Rendering**  
The ambrosia for ears, Brahma-sound of the Teacher—  
Wisdom arises in the fortunate mind.  
The ambrosia for ears, garland of words in excellent explanation—  
No equal to this as medicine for wisdom.  

**Commentary**  
Sound sounds without speaker.  
Brahma-sound reaches where no ear stands—reaching without arrival, hearing without receiver.  
Fortunate mind knows without knower—wisdom arises where nothing arises, knowing knows itself.  
Garland of words forms without string—each syllable complete, the whole present in every part.  
Medicine heals what was never ill—wisdom recognizes what never departed from recognition.  

**Illumination**  
Sound without speaker  
Hearing without ear  
Wisdom without knower  
Garland without string  
Medicine without illness

### Stanza 9

**Original Tibetan**

།བསྐལ་བཟང་ཡིད་ལ་བདེན་པ་སྟོན་པ་ལགས།  
ལེགས་བཤད་དོན་བཟང་སྙིང་གི་བདུད་རྩི་སྟེ།  
།ཡིད་ལ་ཡེ་ཤེས་གསོས་སུ་འདི་ཉིད་ཆེ།  
༩

**Extended Wylie**  
rgyal ba'i byin rlabs snying gi bdud rtsi ste

**Literal Translation**  
Victorious-One's blessing heart-of ambrosia is

**Liturgical Rendering**  
The ambrosia for heart, blessing of Victorious Ones—

The ambrosia for heart, excellent meaning in explanation—

**Commentary**  
Blessing blesses without blesser.  
Heart receives what never departed—ambrosia nourishes what was never hungry.  
Truth shows without showing—showering where no surface waits to be wetted.  
Excellent meaning means without meaning—meaning arrives already arrived.  
Pristine awareness knows without knowing—medicine heals by revealing no wound existed.  

**Illumination**  
Blessing without transfer  
Heart without receiver  
Truth without revelation  
Meaning without interpretation  
Awareness without knower

### Stanza 10

**Original Tibetan**  
རྣམ་ཐར་ཏིང་འཛིན་སྟོབས་ཀྱིས་བྱིན་རླབས་པས།  
།འདི་མཐོང་ཡིད་ལ་ཆོས་ཉིད་ཤུགས་ཀྱིས་སྐྱེ།  
ཀ་དག་དགོངས་པ་འཕྲལ་ལ་སྟོན་ནུས་ཤིང༌།  
།ཆོས་ཅན་འཛིན་པ་གཞིག་ལ་འདི་འདྲ་མེད།

**Extended Wylie**
