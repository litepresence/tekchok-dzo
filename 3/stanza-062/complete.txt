།སོ་སྐྱེ་བློ་གྲོས་སྦྲང་བུའི་གཤོག་རླབས་བཅས།
།བསྔགས་པའི་སྙན་གླུ་དར་དིར་སྒྲོགས་པ་འདི།

## Extended Wylie Transliteration
mkha' dbyings bzhin du mkhas pa'i rol gnas/
so skye blo gros sbrang bu'i gshog rlangs bcas/
bsngags pa'i snyan glu dar dir sgrogs pa 'di/
legs bshad tshal la rab dga' skyes nas yin/
༦༢

## 1. Liturgical Rendering (mchod brjod tshig; Skt. stotra-pada)

Like the sky-expanse, the playground of the learned,
With the wing-flutter of bees of newly-arisen intelligence,
This sweet song of praise proclaimed everywhere
Is arisen from great joy in the grove of excellent teaching.
༦༢

## 2. Commentary ('grel pa don 'grel; Skt. lakṣaṇa-nirdeśa)

## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This batch extends non-abiding by emphasizing the unity of the sky-expanse as field. The nine vehicles are shown not as discrete paths but as aspects of a single field in which recognition occurs without boundary. Lakṣaṇa: field of groundlessness; unity that emerges in multiplicity; transmission through syntactic conveyance rather than naming.

## 3. Illumination (ngo sprod; Skt. svarūpa-prakāśa)

00001| Field-boundary dissolves; unity is field itself.
00002| Nine vehicles seen as one field; no ranking.
00003| Ground of all is nondual; transmission is syntax, not naming.
00004| The field holds all, without containment or edge.
