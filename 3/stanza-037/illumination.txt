## Translator's Illumination (ngo sprod)

Naked straight awareness—not uncovered, not direct, but the naked that is the awareness, the awareness that is the naked.

Omniscient Lama's mind—not all-knowing, not intellect, but the omniscient that is the mind, the mind that is the omniscient.

Pith instruction—not essence, not teaching, but the pith that is the instruction, the instruction that is the pith.

Showing bare—not demonstration, not naked, but the showing that is the bare, the bare that is the showing.

Actual Buddha—not real, not awakened, but the actual that is the Buddha, the Buddha that is the actual.

Sacred Dharma—not holy, not teaching, but the sacred that is the Dharma, the Dharma that is the sacred.

Devotion arising—not faith, not emergence, but the devotion that is the arising, the arising that is the devotion.

Virtuous spiritual friend—not good, not kalyāṇamitra, but the virtuous that is the friend, the friend that is the virtuous.
