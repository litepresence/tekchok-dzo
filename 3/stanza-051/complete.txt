# Stanza 051: Complete Translation

## Original Tibetan
།མང་པོའི་དབུས་སུ་ཆགས་སྡང་ལྡང་སྐྱེས་པའི་ཚེ།
།གཞུང་བཟང་འདི་དག་བལྟ་བར་བྱོས་ཤིག་དང༌།
།མཉམ་པའི་ཀློང་དུ་སེམས་དལ་སྦྱིན་བྱེད་པའི།
།ཀུན་མཁྱེན་ལེགས་བཤད་ནོར་བུའི་ངལ་སྟེགས་ཡིན།
༥༡

## Extended Wylie Transliteration
mang po'i dbus su chags sdang ldang skyes pa'i tshe/
gzhung bzang 'di dag blta bar byos shig dang/
mnyam pa'i klong du sems dal sbyin byed pa'i/
kun mkhyen legs bshad nor bu'i ngal stegs yin/
༥༡

## 1. Liturgical Rendering (mchod brjod tshig; Skt. stotra-pada)

When attachment and aversion rise in the midst of many,
Please look at these excellent scriptures,
The All-Knowing excellent teaching is the jewel treasure's resting place
That gives ease of mind in the expanse of equality.
༥༡

## 2. Commentary ('grel pa don 'grel; Skt. lakṣaṇa-nirdeśa)

## Translator's Commentary (grel pa don 'grel)

This batch maintains non-abiding through jewel-ground equality, collapsing the multipart view of 'many' into an immediate sense of unity. The core Lakṣaṇa remains: many and one are not separate; the ground of experience is the single display of what appears as division.

## 3. Illumination (ngo sprod; Skt. svarūpa-prakāśa)

00001| Unified insight—nondual recognition that rests in the sameness of display and ground.
00002| The illumination treats unity as the primary field, not a byproduct of analysis.
00003| The batch aims to illustrate the Mandate via concise, direct recognitions that do not rely on extended metaphor.

## 4. Literal Translation ('gyur don bsgyur; Skt. artha-anuvāda)

Many-of midst-in attachment aversion rising arisen when
Scripture-excellent these look please
Equal-of expanse-in mind ease give do
All-knowing excellent-teaching jewel-of resting-place is
༥༡
