- ✅ Distinction between all-ground (kun gzhi) and Dharmakaya clear

- ✅ Understood as uncaused display, not achievement
- ✅ Unity with primordial purity properly explained
- ✅ Not confused with tantric generation stage

- ✅ Distinguished from ordinary consciousness (sems)
- ✅ Not substantialized as "thing" or "entity"

- ✅ Not sequential ladder but simultaneous recognition
- ✅ First to tenth bhumi as degrees of forgetting
- ✅ No temporalization of realization

- ✅ Essence, nature, compassion triad accurate
